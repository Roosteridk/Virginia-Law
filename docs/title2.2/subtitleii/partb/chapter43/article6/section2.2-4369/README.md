# ยง 2.2-4369 Proscribed participation by public employees in procurement transactions.

<p>Except as may be specifically allowed by subdivisions B 1, 2, and 3 of ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3112/'>2.2-3112</a>, no public employee having official responsibility for a procurement transaction shall participate in that transaction on behalf of the public body when the employee knows that:</p><p>1. The employee is contemporaneously employed by a bidder, offeror or contractor involved in the procurement transaction;</p><p>2. The employee, the employee's partner, or any member of the employee's immediate family holds a position with a bidder, offeror or contractor such as an officer, director, trustee, partner or the like, or is employed in a capacity involving personal and substantial participation in the procurement transaction, or owns or controls an interest of more than five percent;</p><p>3. The employee, the employee's partner, or any member of the employee's immediate family has a pecuniary interest arising from the procurement transaction; or</p><p>4.
