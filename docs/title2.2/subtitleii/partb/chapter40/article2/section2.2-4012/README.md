# § 2.2-4012 Purpose; adoption; effective date; filing; duties of Registrar of Regulations.

<p>A. The purpose of the regulatory procedures shall be to provide a regulatory plan that is predictable, based on measurable and anticipated outcomes, and is inclined toward conflict resolution.</p><p>B. Subject to the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4013/'>2.2-4013</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4014/'>2.2-4014</a>, all regulations, including those that agencies, pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4002/'>2.2-4002</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4006/'>2.2-4006</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4011/'>2.2-4011</a>, may elect to dispense with the public procedures provided by §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4007.01/'>2.2-4007.01</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4009/'>2.2-4009</a>, may be formally and finally adopted by the signed order of the agency so stating. No regulation except an emergency regulation or a noncontroversial regulation promulgated pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4012.1/'>2.2-4012.1</a> shall be effective until the expiration of the applicable period as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4015/'>2.2-4015</a>. In the case of an emergency regulation filed in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4011/'>2.2-4011</a>, the regulation shall become effective upon its adoption and filing with the Registrar of Regulations, unless a later date is specified. The originals of all regulations shall remain in the custody of the agency as public records subject to judicial notice by all courts and agencies. They, or facsimiles thereof, shall be made available for public inspection or copying. Full and true copies shall also be additionally filed, registered, published, or otherwise made publicly available as required by other laws.</p><p>C. Prior to the publication for hearing of a proposed regulation, copies of the regulation and copies of the summary and statement as to the basis, purpose, substance, issues, and the economic impact estimate of the regulation submitted by the Department of Planning and Budget and the agency's response thereto as required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4007.04/'>2.2-4007.04</a> shall be transmitted to the Registrar of Regulations, who shall retain these documents.</p><p>D. All regulations adopted pursuant to this chapter shall contain a citation to the section of the Code of Virginia that authorizes or requires the regulations and, where the regulations are required to conform to federal law or regulation in order to be valid, a citation to the specific federal law or regulation to which conformity is required.</p><p>E. Immediately upon the adoption by any agency of any regulation in final form, a copy of (i) the regulation, (ii) a then current summary and statement as to the basis, purpose, substance, issues, and the economic impact estimate of the regulation submitted by the Department of Planning and Budget, and (iii) the agency's summary description of the nature of the oral and written data, views, or arguments presented during the public proceedings and the agency's comments thereon shall be transmitted to the Registrar of Regulations, who shall retain these documents as permanent records and make them available for public inspection. A draft of the agency's summary description of public comment shall be sent by the agency to all public commenters on the proposed regulation at least five days before final adoption of the regulation.</p><p>1975, c. 503, § 9-6.14:9; 1977, cc. 450, 459; 1981, c. 387; 1982, c. 425; 1983, c. 295; 1984, c. 5; 1989, c. 71; 1992, c. 829; 1993, c. 898; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0938'>938</a>; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0224'>224</a>; 2007, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0873'>873</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0916'>916</a>.</p>
