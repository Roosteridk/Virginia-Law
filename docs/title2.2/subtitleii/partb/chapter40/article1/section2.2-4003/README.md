# § 2.2-4003 Venue.

<p>In all proceedings under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4019/'>2.2-4019</a> or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4020/'>2.2-4020</a> venue shall be in the city or county where the administrative agency maintains its principal office or as the parties may otherwise agree. In all proceedings under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4026/'>2.2-4026</a>, venue shall be as specified in subdivision 1 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-261/'>8.01-261</a>.</p><p>1975, c. 503, § 9-6.14:5; 1977, c. 624; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2007, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0873'>873</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0916'>916</a>.</p>
