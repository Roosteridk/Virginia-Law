# ยง 2.2-4513.1 Investment of funds in qualified investment pools.

Notwithstanding the provisions of Article 1 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1300/'>15.2-1300</a> et seq.) of Chapter 13 of Title 15.2, in any locality in which the authority to invest moneys belonging to or within the control of the locality has been granted to its elected treasurer, the treasurer may act on behalf of his locality to become a participating political subdivision in qualified investment pools without an ordinance adopted by the locality approving a joint exercise of power agreement.
