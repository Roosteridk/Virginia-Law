# § 2.2-1841 Blanket surety bond plan for moneys under control of court.

<p>The Division shall establish a program of blanket surety bonding to provide surety for the faithful discharge of duty with respect to moneys held pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-582/'>8.01-582</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-600/'>8.01-600</a> by all general receivers and clerks. General receivers and clerks shall participate in the program. The Division's cost of obtaining and administering the blanket surety bond shall be paid from those moneys covered by the bond.</p><p>1988, c. 841, § 2.1-526.9:1; 2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0618'>618</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0632'>632</a>, § 2.1-191.14; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>.</p>
