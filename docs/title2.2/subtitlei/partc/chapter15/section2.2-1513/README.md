# ยง 2.2-1513 Submission of additional information to legislative committees.

<p>A. To enable the House Committee on Appropriations, the House Committee on Finance, and the Senate Committee on Finance and Appropriations to perform their prescribed duties, the Governor shall provide:</p><p>1. Written monthly reports on the status of revenue collections relative to the current fiscal year's estimate; and</p><p>2. Written quarterly assessments of the current economic outlook for the Commonwealth relative to the current fiscal year.</p><p>B.
