<p>A. The Department of Human Resource Management is authorized to contract for and purchase such coverage or use other actuarially sound funding necessary to effectuate this provision.</p><p>B. The Department shall adopt regulations regarding the establishment and the administration of the plan or plans.</p><p>D.
