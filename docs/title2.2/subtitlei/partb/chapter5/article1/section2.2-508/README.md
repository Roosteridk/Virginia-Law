# § 2.2-508 Legal service in certain redistricting proceedings.

<p>Upon notification by a county, city or town of a pending civil action challenging the legality of its election district boundaries as required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-304.5/'>24.2-304.5</a>, the Attorney General shall review the papers in the civil action and may represent the interests of the Commonwealth in developing an appropriate remedy that is consistent with requirements of law, including but not limited to Article VII, Section 5 of the Constitution of Virginia, Chapter 3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-302.2/'>24.2-302.2</a> et seq.) of Title 24.2, or Chapter 39 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-263/'>30-263</a> et seq.) of Title 30.</p><p>1989, c. 112, § 2.1-121.1; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0249'>249</a>; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP1000'>1000</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0001'>1</a>.</p>
