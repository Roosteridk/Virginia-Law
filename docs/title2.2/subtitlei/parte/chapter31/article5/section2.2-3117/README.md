# § 2.2-3117 Disclosure form.

<p>The disclosure form to be used for filings required by subsections A and D of <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3114/'>§ 2.2-3114</a> and subsections A and E of <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3115/'>§ 2.2-3115</a> shall be prescribed by the Council. Except as otherwise provided in <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3115/'>§ 2.2-3115</a>, all completed forms shall be filed electronically with the Council in accordance with the standards approved by it pursuant to <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-356/'>§ 30-356</a>. Any person who knowingly and intentionally makes a false statement of a material fact on the Statement of Economic Interests is guilty of a Class 5 felony.</p><p>1987, Sp.
