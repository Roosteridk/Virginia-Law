# § 2.2-3002 Exemptions from chapter.

<p>The provisions of this chapter shall not apply to:</p><p>1. Appointees of elected groups or individuals except as provided in subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3001/'>2.2-3001</a>;</p><p>2. Agency heads or chief executive officers of government agencies and public institutions of higher education appointed by boards and commissions;</p><p>3. Law-enforcement officers as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-500/'>9.1-500</a> whose grievances are subject to Chapter 5 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-500/'>9.1-500</a> et seq.) and who have elected to resolve such grievances under those provisions; and</p><p>4. Employees in positions designated in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2905/'>2.2-2905</a> as exempt from the Virginia Personnel Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2900/'>2.2-2900</a> et seq.).</p><p>1995, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0770'>770</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0818'>818</a>, § 2.1-116.013; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>.</p>
