# § 2.2-2201 Short title; definitions.

<p>A. This article shall be known and may be cited as the "Virginia Commercial Space Flight Authority Act."</p><p>B. As used in this article, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Authority" means the Virginia Commercial Space Flight Authority.</p><p>"Board" means the board of directors of the Authority.</p><p>"Project" means the construction, improvement, furnishing, maintenance, acquisition or operation of any facility or the provision for or funding of any activity that will further the purposes described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2202/'>2.2-2202</a>.</p><p>1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0758'>758</a>, §§ 9-266.1, 9-266.2; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0779'>779</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0817'>817</a>.</p>
