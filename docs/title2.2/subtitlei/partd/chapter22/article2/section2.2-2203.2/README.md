# ยง 2.2-2203.2 Strategic plan.

<p>Every four years the Executive Director shall present to the Board for its consideration and adoption a strategic plan for the Authority for at least the next six years. Such plan shall include the following:</p><p>1. An analysis of the current operating performance of the Authority and trends in the aerospace industry;</p><p>2. An analysis of the Authority's economic benefit and expected future performance over the term of the plan;</p><p>3. An analysis and identification of opportunities to expand the Authority's market share in sectors of the aerospace industry in which the Authority is active;</p><p>4. An analysis and identification of opportunities to expand the Authority's operations into other sectors of the aerospace industry and other adjacent industries;</p><p>5. An implementation strategy based on the analyses required by subdivisions one through four;</p><p>6. A capital plan to support the implementation strategy; and</p><p>7. The establishment of performance indicators to be used for the Authority covering the term of the plan.</p><p>2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0779'>779</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0817'>817</a>; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0633'>633</a>.</p>
