# ยง 2.2-2631 Powers and duties of Council.

<p>The Council shall have the following powers and duties to:</p><p>1. Determine the studies and research to be conducted by the Council;</p><p>2. Collect and disseminate information regarding the status of women in the Commonwealth and the nation;</p><p>3. Advise the Governor, the General Assembly, and the Governor's Secretaries on matters pertaining to women in the Commonwealth and the nation;</p><p>4. Establish and award scholarships pursuant to regulations and conditions prescribed by the Council; and</p><p>5. Develop programs and projects on matters pertaining to women in the Commonwealth and the nation through public-private partnerships.</p><p>1985, c. 497, ยง 9-116.1; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0502'>502</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0057'>57</a>.</p>
