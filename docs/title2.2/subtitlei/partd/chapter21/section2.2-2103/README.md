# § 2.2-2103 Cooperation of other agencies with authorities, boards, commissions, councils, and other collegial bodies.

<p>Upon request, all agencies and political subdivisions of the Commonwealth shall assist any authority, board, commission, council or other collegial body established in this title in carrying out the respective duties for which each was created.</p><p>1968, c. 567, § 9-84.05; 1973, c. 431, § 9-84.9; 1978, c. 103, § 2.1-335.3; 1991, cc. 44, 594, § 9-145.20; 1992, c. 609, § 9-145.34; 1993, c. 162, § 9-145.49; 1995, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0638'>638</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0815'>815</a>, §§ 2.1-548.42, 9-344; 1996, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0097'>97</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0589'>589</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0599'>599</a>, §§ 2.1-548.52, 9-153; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0976'>976</a>, § 9-304.5; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>.</p>
