# § 44-32 Oath of National Guard officers.

<p>Commissioned officers of the National Guard shall take and subscribe to the following oath of office:</p><p>"I............................… do solemnly swear that I will support and</p><p>defend the Constitution of the United States and the Constitution of the <br/>Commonwealth of Virginia, against all enemies, foreign and domestic; that I <br/>will bear true faith and allegiance to the same; that I will obey the orders <br/>of the President of the United States and of the Governor of the Commonwealth <br/>of Virginia; that I make this obligation freely, without mental reservation or <br/>purpose of evasion, and that I will well and faithfully discharge the duties <br/>of the office of..............................… in the National Guard of the</p><p>United States and of the Commonwealth of Virginia, upon which I am about to </p><p>enter; so help me God."</p><p>1930, p. 954; Michie Code 1942, § 2673(22); 1958, c. 393.</p>
