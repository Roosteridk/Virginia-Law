# ยง 8.8A-402 Assurance that endorsement or instruction is effective.

<p>(a) An issuer may require the following assurance that each necessary endorsement or each instruction is genuine and authorized:</p><p>(1) in all cases, a guaranty of the signature of the person making an endorsement or originating an instruction including, in the case of an instruction, reasonable assurance of identity;</p><p>(2) if the endorsement is made or the instruction is originated by an agent, appropriate assurance of actual authority to sign;</p><p>(3) if the endorsement is made or the instruction is originated by a fiduciary pursuant to ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.8A-107/'>8.8A-107</a> (a) (4) or (a) (5), appropriate evidence of appointment or incumbency;</p><p>(4) if there is more than one fiduciary, reasonable assurance that all who are required to sign have done so; and</p><p>(5) if the endorsement is made or the instruction is originated by a person not covered by another provision of this subsection, assurance appropriate to the case shall correspond as nearly as may be to the provisions of this subsection.</p><p>(b) An issuer may elect to require reasonable assurance beyond that specified in this section.</p><p>(c) In this section:</p><p>(1) "Guaranty of the signature" means a guaranty signed by or on behalf of a person reasonably believed by the issuer to be responsible. An issuer may adopt standards with respect to responsibility if they are not manifestly unreasonable.</p><p>(2) "Appropriate evidence of appointment or incumbency" means:</p><p>(i) in the case of a fiduciary appointed or qualified by a court, a certificate issued by or under the direction or supervision of the court or an officer thereof and dated within sixty days before the date of presentation for transfer; or</p><p>(ii) in any other case, a copy of a document showing the appointment or a certificate issued by or on behalf of a person reasonably believed by an issuer to be responsible or, in the absence of that document or certificate, other evidence the issuer reasonably considered appropriate.</p><p>1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0216'>216</a>.</p>
