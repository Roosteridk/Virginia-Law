# § 46.2-214.4 Discount for online transactions.

<p>The Department may offer a $1 discount for the following transactions if conducted using the Internet: (i) a driver's license renewal pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-330/'>46.2-330</a>, (ii) a driver's license duplicate or reissue pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-343/'>46.2-343</a>, (iii) an identification card renewal pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-345/'>46.2-345</a>, (iv) an identification card duplicate or reissue pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-345/'>46.2-345</a>, or (v) a certificate of title replacement pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-607/'>46.2-607</a>.</p><p>2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0368'>368</a>.</p>
