# ยง 46.2-490.8 Grounds for denying, suspending, or revoking licenses of clinics and clinic instructors.

<p>A clinic or instructor license may be denied, suspended, or revoked on any one or more of the following grounds:</p><p>1. Material misstatement or omission in an application for a driver improvement clinic license or a driver improvement clinic instructor license;</p><p>2. Failure to comply subsequent to receipt of a written warning from the Department for any willful failure to comply with any provision of this chapter or any regulation promulgated by the Commissioner under this chapter; or any criteria established by the Department pursuant to this chapter;</p><p>3. Defrauding any student in a driver improvement clinic, or any other person in the conduct of a driver improvement clinic's business;</p><p>4. Employment of fraudulent devices, methods or practices in connection with compliance with the requirements under the statutes of the Commonwealth;</p><p>5. Having used deceptive acts or practices;</p><p>6. Knowingly advertising by any means any assertion, representation, or statement of fact which is untrue, misleading, or deceptive in any particular relating to the conduct of a clinic;</p><p>7. Having been convicted of any fraudulent act in connection with a driver improvement clinic or driver training school, or any consumer-related fraud;</p><p>8. Having been convicted of any criminal act involving the operation of a driver improvement clinic or driver training school;</p><p>9. Having been convicted of a felony;</p><p>10. Failing or refusing to pay civil penalties imposed by the Department pursuant to ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-490.6/'>46.2-490.6</a>.</p><p>2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0622'>622</a>.</p>
