# ยง 46.2-341.14:7 Notification requirements.

<p>A. Every third party tester shall:</p><p>1. Notify the Department in a format prescribed by the Department within 10 days of any change in:</p><p>a. The third party tester's name or address; or</p><p>b. The third party examiners who are employed by the third party tester.</p><p>2. Notify the Department in a format prescribed by the Department within 10 days of any of the following occurrences:</p><p>a. The third party tester ceases business operations in Virginia;</p><p>b. The third party tester fails to comply with any of the requirements set forth in this article; or</p><p>c. Any third party examiner fails to comply with any of the requirements set forth in this article.</p><p>3. Notify the Department of any proposed change in the skills test route at least 30 days before the third party tester plans to change the route.</p><p>B. Every third party examiner shall notify the Department, within 10 days after leaving the employ of the third party tester, of his change in employment.</p><p>2013, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0165'>165</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0582'>582</a>.</p>
