# § 46.2-341.22 Requirements upon disqualification.

<p>Any person who has been disqualified pursuant to any provision of this article shall be subject to the provisions of §§ <a href='/vacode/46.2-370/'>46.2-370</a> and <a href='/vacode/46.2-414/'>46.2-414</a>, and shall be required to comply with the provisions of §§ <a href='/vacode/46.2-370/'>46.2-370</a> and <a href='/vacode/46.2-411/'>46.2-411</a> as conditions to the reinstatement of his privilege to drive a commercial motor vehicle.</p><p>Any person who has been disqualified pursuant to the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/46.2-341.18/'>46.2-341.18</a> shall be required as further conditions to reinstatement of his privilege to operate a commercial motor vehicle, to (i) apply for such license; (ii) pass the knowledge and skills tests required for the class and type of commercial motor vehicle for which he seeks to be licensed; and (iii) satisfy all other applicable licensing requirements, including the payment of licensing fees, imposed by the laws of the Commonwealth.</p><p>The provisions of this section shall not apply to out-of-service orders issued pursuant to §§ <a href='/vacode/46.2-341.26:2/'>46.2-341.26:2</a> and <a href='/vacode/46.2-341.26:3/'>46.2-341.26:3</a>.</p><p>1989, c. 705, § 46.1-372.21; 1992, c. 830; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0750'>750</a>.</p>
