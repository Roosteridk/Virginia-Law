# ยง 46.2-312 Persons using bioptic telescopic lenses.

<p>A. Persons using bioptic telescopic lenses shall be eligible for driver's licenses if they:</p><p>1. Demonstrate a visual acuity of at least 20/200 in one or both eyes and a field of seventy degrees horizontal vision without or with corrective carrier lenses or a comparable measurement that demonstrates a visual field within this range, or if these persons have vision in one eye only, they demonstrate a field of at least forty degrees temporal and thirty degrees nasal horizontal vision or a comparable measurement that demonstrates a visual field within this range;</p><p>2. Demonstrate a visual acuity of at least 20/70 in one or both eyes with the bioptic telescopic lenses and without the use of field expanders;</p><p>3. Meet all other criteria for licensure;</p><p>4. Accompany the license application with a report of examination by an ophthalmologist or optometrist on a form prescribed by the Department for evaluation by the Medical Advisory Board.</p><p>B. Persons using bioptic telescopic lenses shall be eligible for learner's permits issued under ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-335/'>46.2-335</a> provided they first meet the requirements of subsection A of this section, except for that part of the examination requiring the applicant to drive a motor vehicle.</p><p>C. Persons using bioptic telescopic lenses shall be subject to the following restrictions:</p><p>1. They shall not be eligible for any of the driver's license endorsements provided for in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-328/'>46.2-328</a>;</p><p>2. Their driver's licenses shall permit the operation of motor vehicles only during the period beginning one-half hour after sunrise and ending one-half hour before sunset.</p><p>D. Notwithstanding the provisions of subdivision C 2 of this section, persons using bioptic telescopic lenses may be licensed to drive motor vehicles between one-half hour before sunset and one-half hour after sunrise if they:</p><p>1. Demonstrate a visual acuity of at least 20/40 in one or both eyes with the bioptic telescopic lenses and without the use of field expanders;</p><p>2. Have been licensed under subsection C of this section for at least one year; and</p><p>3. Pass a skills test taken at night.</p><p>1986, c. 115, ยง 46.1-357.3; 1989, cc. 147, 727; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0018'>18</a>.</p>
