# § 46.2-319 Refusal or revocation of license for certain fraudulent acts in obtaining a driver's license.

<p>The Department shall not issue any permit or license under this title to any person who has been convicted, or found not innocent in the case of a juvenile, of violating § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-348/'>46.2-348</a>, when the violation was based on the taking of any examination under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-311/'>46.2-311</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-322/'>46.2-322</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-325/'>46.2-325</a> or the provisions of the Virginia Commercial Driver's License Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.1/'>46.2-341.1</a> et seq.) for another person, or the appearance for another for renewal of a license under this chapter, for a period of ten years from the date of conviction, or finding of not innocent in the case of a juvenile. If the person has a license or permit issued pursuant to this title, the Commissioner shall revoke the license or permit for a period of ten years from the date of the conviction, or finding of not innocent in the case of a juvenile.</p><p>1968, c. 642, § 46.1-365; 1974, c. 453; 1989, cc. 705, 727.</p>
