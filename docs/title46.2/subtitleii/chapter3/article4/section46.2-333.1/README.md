<p>Notwithstanding any contrary provision of this chapter, there are hereby imposed, in addition to other fees imposed by this chapter, the following surcharges in the following amounts:</p><p>1. For the issuance of any driver's license other than a commercial driver's license, or a driver privilege card issued under ยง <a href='/vacode/46.2-328.3/'>46.2-328.3</a>, $1.60 per year of validity of the license;</p><p>2. For the issuance of any commercial driver's license, $1 per year of validity of the license;</p><p>3. For the reissuance or replacement of any driver's license, $5; and</p><p>4.
