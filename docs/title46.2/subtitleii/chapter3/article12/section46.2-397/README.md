# § 46.2-397 Suspension of license for certain violations while transporting explosives, inflammable gas or liquid.

<p>When the driver of any motor vehicle is convicted of any violation of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-816/'>46.2-816</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-820/'>46.2-820</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-823/'>46.2-823</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-825/'>46.2-825</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-826/'>46.2-826</a> or §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-852/'>46.2-852</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-864/'>46.2-864</a>, or of any of the applicable speed limits prescribed in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-870/'>46.2-870</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-878/'>46.2-878</a> and the violation was committed while driving a motor vehicle, tractor truck, trailer, or semitrailer, transporting explosives or any inflammable gas or liquid, in addition to any penalty imposed, the court may suspend the driver's license of the convicted person for a period of ninety days from the date of conviction.</p><p>Code 1950, § 46-197.2; 1954, c. 377; 1958, c. 541, § 46.1-424; 1984, c. 780; 1989, c. 727.</p>
