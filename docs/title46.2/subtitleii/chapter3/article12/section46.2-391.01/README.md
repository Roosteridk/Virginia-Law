# § 46.2-391.01 Administrative enforcement of ignition interlock requirements.

<p>If the court, as a condition of license restoration or as a condition of a restricted license under subsection C or D of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-271.1/'>18.2-271.1</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-391/'>46.2-391</a>, or when required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-270.1/'>18.2-270.1</a>, fails to prohibit an offender from operating a motor vehicle that is not equipped with a functioning, certified ignition interlock system, the Commissioner shall enforce the requirements relating to installation of such systems in accordance with the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-270.1/'>18.2-270.1</a>.</p><p>2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0739'>739</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0811'>811</a>; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0707'>707</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0729'>729</a>.</p>
