# § 46.2-685 Payment of fees into special fund.

<p>Except as otherwise provided, all fees collected by the Commissioner under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-651/'>46.2-651</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-653/'>46.2-653</a> shall be paid into the state treasury and set aside as a special fund to be used to meet the expenses of the Department.</p><p>1987, c. 696, § 46.1-44.2; 1989, c. 727; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0443'>443</a>.</p>
