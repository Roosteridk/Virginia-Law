<p>The fee for the replacement or duplication of license plates, decals, registration cards, or certificates of title shall be as follows:</p><p>1. For a certificate of title, $5;</p><p>3. For license plates or license plates with decals, $10;</p><p>4. For a license plate with decals issued for trailers, $5; and</p><p>5.
