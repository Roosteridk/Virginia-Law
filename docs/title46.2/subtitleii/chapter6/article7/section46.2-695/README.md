# § 46.2-695 Small rented ridesharing vehicles.

<p>The fees required by subdivisions A 8 and A 9 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-694/'>46.2-694</a> to be paid for registration of motor vehicles used for rent or hire shall not be required for the operation of any motor vehicle with a normal seating capacity of not more than fifteen adults including the driver while used (i) not for profit in transporting persons in a ridesharing arrangement, as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1400/'>46.2-1400</a>, or (ii) by a lessee renting or hiring such vehicle for such purpose for a period of twelve months or longer under a written lease or agreement. For the purposes of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-694/'>46.2-694</a>, the fee for the annual registration card and license plates for such vehicle shall be the same as for a private passenger car of the same weight.</p><p>Code 1950, § 46-158; 1952, c. 415; 1958, c. 541, § 46.1-150; 1964, c. 218; 1966, c. 608; 1976, c. 60; 1989, c. 727; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0337'>337</a>.</p>
