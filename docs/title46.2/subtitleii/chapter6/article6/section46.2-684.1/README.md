# § 46.2-684.1 Insurance coverage and proof of insurance for exempted motor vehicles.

<p>A. Any motor vehicle, trailer, or semi-trailer that is exempt from motor vehicle registration requirements pursuant to this article shall be insured under a general liability policy that includes personal injury liability insurance as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-117/'>38.2-117</a> and property damage liability insurance as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-118/'>38.2-118</a>, under a policy of motor vehicle insurance as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-124/'>38.2-124</a>, or under an umbrella or excess insurance policy. Any such umbrella or excess insurance policy shall not be required to comply with the provisions of Chapter 22 (§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-2200/'>38.2-2200</a> et seq.) of Title 38.2 of the Code of Virginia that relate to the ownership, maintenance, or use of the exempt motor vehicle, trailer, or semi-trailer.</p><p>B. Any person who owns a motor vehicle, trailer, or semi-trailer that is exempt from motor vehicle registration requirements pursuant to this article may be required by a law-enforcement officer to furnish proof that such motor vehicle, trailer, or semi-trailer is insured as required in subsection A. Failure to furnish proof of insurance when required by a law-enforcement officer as provided in this section within 30 days shall constitute a traffic infraction punishable by a $600 fine that shall be paid into the Uninsured Motorists Fund created pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/38.2-3000/'>38.2-3000</a>.</p><p>2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0445'>445</a>; 2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0736'>736</a>.</p>
