# ยง 46.2-678 Forklift trucks.

<p>A. No person shall be required to obtain the registration certificate, license plates, or decals for or pay a registration fee for any forklift truck provided it is:</p><p>1. Operated by a person holding a valid Virginia driver's license;</p><p>2. Operated along or across highways only in traveling from one plant, factory, or job site to another by the most direct route;</p><p>3. Not carrying or transporting any object or person, other than the driver;</p><p>4. Displaying a slow-moving vehicle emblem in conformity with ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1081/'>46.2-1081</a>;</p><p>5. In compliance with requirements of the federal Occupational Safety and Health Administration;</p><p>6. Not operated on or along any limited access highway; and</p><p>7. Not operated for a distance of more than ten miles.</p><p>B. For the purposes of this section, "forklift truck" means a self-propelled machine used for hoisting and transporting heavy objects by means of steel fingers inserted under the load.</p><p>1973, c. 194, ยง 46.1-45.2; 1980, c. 37; 1986, c. 220; 1987, cc. 151, 342, 388; 1989, c. 727; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0142'>142</a>.</p>
