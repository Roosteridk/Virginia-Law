# § 46.2-608 When application for registration or certificate of title rejected.

<p>The Department may reject an application for the registration of a motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer or certificate of title when:</p><p>1. The applicant for registration is not entitled to it under the provisions of this title or Title 43;</p><p>2. The applicant has neglected or refused to furnish the Department with the information required on the appropriate official form or other information required by the Department;</p><p>3. The required fees have not been paid;</p><p>4. The vehicle is not equipped with equipment required by this title or the vehicle is equipped with equipment prohibited by this title;</p><p>5. The applicant, if not a resident of the Commonwealth, has not filed with the Commissioner a power of attorney appointing him the applicant's authorized agent or attorney-in-fact upon whom process or notice may be served as required in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-601/'>46.2-601</a>;</p><p>6. There is reason to believe that the application or accompanying documents have been altered or contain any false statement;</p><p>7. The vehicle is a commercial motor vehicle and is being operated by a motor carrier that has been prohibited to operate by a federal agency;</p><p>8. The vehicle is a commercial motor vehicle and the vehicle has been assigned for safety to a motor carrier that has been prohibited from operating by a federal agency or a motor carrier whose business is operated, managed, or otherwise controlled or affiliated with a person who is ineligible for registration, including the owner or a relative, family member, corporate officer, or shareholder; or</p><p>9. The vehicle is a commercial motor vehicle and the applicant has applied on behalf of or for the benefit of the real party in interest who has been issued a federal out of service order or if the applicant's business is operated, managed, or otherwise controlled or affiliated with a person who is ineligible for registration, including the applicant or an entity, relative, family member, corporate officer, or shareholder.</p><p>For purposes of this section, the terms "commercial motor vehicle" and "motor carrier" shall be as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/52-8.4/'>52-8.4</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 46-54; 1958, c. 541, § 46.1-56; 1968, c. 605; 1986, c. 490; 1989, c. 727; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0061'>61</a>.</p>
