# ยง 46.2-2099.41 Certification requirements.

<p>A. A person may apply to the Department for certification as an operator of an excursion train. The Department shall certify an applicant if the Department determines that the applicant will operate a passenger train that:</p><p>1. Is primarily used for tourism or public service; and</p><p>2. Leads to the promotion of the tourist industry in the Commonwealth.</p><p>B. An application for certification shall include:</p><p>1. The name and address of each person who owns an interest of at least 10 percent of the excursion train operation;</p><p>2. An address in the Commonwealth where the excursion train is based;</p><p>3. An operations plan, including the route to be used and a schedule of operations and stops along the route; and</p><p>4. Evidence of insurance that meets the requirements of subsection C.</p><p>C. The Department shall not certify to a person under subsection A unless the person files with the Department evidence of insurance providing coverage of liability resulting from injury to persons or damages to property in the amount of at least $10 million for the operation of the train.</p><p>D. The Department shall not certify an applicant under subsection A if the applicant or any other person owning interest in the excursion train also owns or operates a regularly scheduled passenger train service with interstate connection.</p><p>2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0596'>596</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0286'>286</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0813'>813</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0431'>431</a>.</p>
