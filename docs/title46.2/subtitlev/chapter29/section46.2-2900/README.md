# ยง 46.2-2900 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, the following words and terms shall have the following meaning unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:</p><p>"Certified escort vehicle driver" means a person 18 years of age or older who holds a valid driver's license and a valid escort vehicle driver certificate issued (i) by the Commonwealth or (ii) by a state whose escort vehicle driver certification program has been determined to be substantially similar to the Commonwealth's and to which the Commonwealth has extended reciprocity.</p><p>"Escort vehicle driver certificate" means a credential issued under the laws of the Commonwealth or other state authorizing the holder to escort a permitted vehicle or vehicles.</p><p>"Permitted vehicle or vehicles" means any vehicle being operated under the provisions of a valid highway hauling permit issued pursuant to ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1139/'>46.2-1139</a> that requires that the permitted vehicle or vehicles be accompanied by a certified escort vehicle driver or drivers.</p><p>2013, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0312'>312</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0477'>477</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0258'>258</a>.</p>
