# ยง 46.2-1310 Authority to deputize persons to direct traffic in certain circumstances.

<p>The chief of police of any county, city, or town, or the sheriff of any county which does not have a chief of police, may deputize persons over the age of eighteen years for the limited purpose of directing traffic in accordance with ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1309/'>46.2-1309</a> during periods of heavy traffic or congestion.
