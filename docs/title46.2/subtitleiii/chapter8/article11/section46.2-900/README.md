# § 46.2-900 Penalty for violation of §§ 46.2-895 through 46.2-897.

<p>Any person convicted of violating the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-895/'>46.2-895</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-897/'>46.2-897</a> shall, if such accident results in injury to or the death of any person, be guilty of a Class 6 felony. If such accident results only in damage to property, the person so convicted shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor; however, if the vehicle or other property struck is unattended and such damage is less than $250, such person shall be guilty of a Class 4 misdemeanor. A motor vehicle operator convicted of a Class 4 misdemeanor under this section shall be assigned three demerit points by the Commissioner of the Department of Motor Vehicles.</p><p>Code 1950, § 46-190; 1958, c. 541, § 46.1-177; 1962, c. 302; 1973, c. 8; 1979, c. 653; 1989, c. 727; 1992, c. 279; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0808'>808</a>.</p>
