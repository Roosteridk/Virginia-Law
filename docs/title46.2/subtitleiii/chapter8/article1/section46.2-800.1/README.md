# § 46.2-800.1 Riding animals on highways after sunset.

<p>A. No person riding upon any animal on a highway between sunset and sunrise shall ride the animal on the roadway unless the rider:</p><p>1. Wears a hat made of or coated with reflectorized material; or</p><p>2. Wears upper body clothing made of or coated with reflectorized material visible from 360°; or</p><p>3. Displays at least 100 square inches of solid reflectorized material at shoulder level visible from 360°; or</p><p>4. Carries a light visible in clear weather from a distance of 500 feet.</p><p>B. The requirements of subsection A of this section shall only apply to the riders of the first and last animals in a group riding one behind the other.</p><p>C. A violation of this section shall not be construed as negligence per se in any civil action.</p><p>1989, c. 295, § 46.1-171.01.</p>
