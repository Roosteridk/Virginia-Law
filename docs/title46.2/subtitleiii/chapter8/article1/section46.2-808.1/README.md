# ยง 46.2-808.1 Use of crossovers on controlled access highways; penalty.

<p>It is unlawful for the driver of any vehicle other than an authorized vehicle to use or attempt to use any crossover posted for authorized vehicles only on any controlled access highway.</p><p>For the purposes of this section, "authorized vehicle" means (i) Department of Transportation vehicles; (ii) law-enforcement vehicles; (iii) emergency vehicles as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-920/'>46.2-920</a>; (iv) towing and recovery vehicles operating under the direction of a law-enforcement agency or the Department of Transportation; (v) vehicles for which permits authorizing use of such crossovers have been issued by the Department of Transportation; (vi) vehicles operated pursuant to a Department of Transportation safety service patrol program as defined in subsection B of ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-920.1/'>46.2-920.1</a> when engaged in providing services under such program; (vii) vehicles operated pursuant to a contract with the Department of Transportation for, or that includes, traffic incident management services as defined in subsection B of ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-920.1/'>46.2-920.1</a> when providing such traffic incident management services; and (viii) other vehicles operating in medical emergency situations.</p><p>Violation of any provision of this section shall constitute a traffic infraction punishable by a fine of no more than $250.</p><p>1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0881'>881</a>; 2008, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0470'>470</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0647'>647</a>; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0350'>350</a>.</p>
