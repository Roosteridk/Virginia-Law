# § 46.2-917.2 School buses operating under State Corporation Commission or Department certificate.

<p>Notwithstanding § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-917/'>46.2-917</a>, any person holding a special or charter party certificate issued by the State Corporation Commission or the Department pursuant to Chapter 23 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-2300/'>46.2-2300</a> et seq.) of this title may transport special or charter parties in school buses provided all lettering required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1089/'>46.2-1089</a> and warning devices required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1090/'>46.2-1090</a> are covered with some opaque detachable material.</p><p>1989, c. 727; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0283'>283</a>.</p>
