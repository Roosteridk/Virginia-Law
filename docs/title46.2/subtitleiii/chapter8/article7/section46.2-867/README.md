# § 46.2-867 Racing; seizure of motor vehicle.

<p>If the owner of a motor vehicle (i) is convicted of racing such vehicle in a prearranged, organized, and planned speed competition in violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-865/'>46.2-865</a>, (ii) is present in the vehicle which is being operated by another in violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-865/'>46.2-865</a>, and knowingly consents to the racing, or (iii) is convicted of a violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-865.1/'>46.2-865.1</a>, the vehicle shall be seized and shall be forfeited to the Commonwealth, and upon being condemned as forfeited in proceedings under Chapter 22.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-386.1/'>19.2-386.1</a> et seq.) of Title 19.2, the proceeds of sale shall be disposed of according to law. Such sections shall apply mutatis mutandis.</p><p>The penalties imposed by these sections are in addition to any other penalty imposed by law.</p><p>1972, c. 702, § 46.1-191.2; 1989, c. 727; 1993, c. 866; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0859'>859</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0283'>283</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0756'>756</a>.</p>
