# § 46.2-868.1 Aggressive driving; penalties.

<p>A. A person is guilty of aggressive driving if (i) the person violates one or more of the following: § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-802/'>46.2-802</a> (Drive on right side of highways), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-804/'>46.2-804</a> (Failure to observe lanes marked for traffic), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-816/'>46.2-816</a> (Following too closely), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-821/'>46.2-821</a> (Vehicles before entering certain highways shall stop or yield right-of-way), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-833.1/'>46.2-833.1</a> (Evasion of traffic control devices), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-838/'>46.2-838</a> (Passing when overtaking a vehicle), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-841/'>46.2-841</a> (When overtaking vehicle may pass on right), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-842/'>46.2-842</a> (Driver to give way to overtaking vehicle), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-842.1/'>46.2-842.1</a> (Driver to give way to certain overtaking vehicles on divided highway), § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-843/'>46.2-843</a> (Limitations on overtaking and passing), any provision of Article 8 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-870/'>46.2-870</a> et seq.) of Chapter 8 of Title 46.2 (Speed), or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-888/'>46.2-888</a> (Stopping on highways); and (ii) that person is a hazard to another person or commits an offense in clause (i) with the intent to harass, intimidate, injure or obstruct another person.</p><p>B. Aggressive driving shall be punished as a Class 2 misdemeanor. However, aggressive driving with the intent to injure another person shall be punished as a Class 1 misdemeanor. In addition to the penalties described in this subsection, the court may require successful completion of an aggressive driving program.</p><p>2002, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0752'>752</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0782'>782</a>.</p>
