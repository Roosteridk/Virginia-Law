# ยง 46.2-931 Localities may prohibit or regulate distribution of handbills, etc., solicitation of contributions, and sale of merchandise or services on highways within their boundaries or on public roadways and medians.

<p>A. Any county, city, or town is hereby authorized to adopt an ordinance prohibiting or regulating:</p><p>1. The distribution of handbills, leaflets, bulletins, literature, advertisements, or similar material to the occupants of motor vehicles on highways located within its boundaries or on public roadways and medians;</p><p>2. The solicitation of contributions of any nature from the occupants of motor vehicles on highways located within its boundaries or on public roadways and medians; and</p><p>3. The sale of merchandise or services or the attempted sale of merchandise or services to the occupants of motor vehicles on highways located within its boundaries or on public roadways and medians.</p><p>B. Ordinances adopted pursuant to this section may provide that any person violating the provisions of such ordinances shall be guilty of a traffic infraction.</p><p>C. The Virginia Department of Transportation may regulate activities within such streets and highways under its jurisdiction, subject to regulations promulgated by the Commonwealth Transportation Board. Nothing in this section shall be construed to allow any locality to permit activities within any highway under the maintenance and operational jurisdiction of the Virginia Department of Transportation.</p><p>1980, c. 113; 1989, c. 727; 2005, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0488'>488</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0541'>541</a>; 2008, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0120'>120</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0235'>235</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0310'>310</a>; 2009, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0422'>422</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0656'>656</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0722'>722</a>; 2010, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0378'>378</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0589'>589</a>.</p>
