# § 46.2-1233.3 Improper towing; penalty.

<p>A. This section shall apply only to tow truck drivers and towing and recovery operators removing a vehicle without the consent of its owner from a location in Planning District 8.</p><p>B. In addition to any action brought pursuant to subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-119/'>46.2-119</a>, any tow truck driver who violates subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-118/'>46.2-118</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1217/'>46.2-1217</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1231/'>46.2-1231</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1233.1/'>46.2-1233.1</a>, or any ordinance adopted therefrom, or any ordinance adopted pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1233/'>46.2-1233</a>, or any towing or recovery operator who violates subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-118/'>46.2-118</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1217/'>46.2-1217</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1231/'>46.2-1231</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1233.1/'>46.2-1233.1</a>, or any ordinance adopted therefrom, or any ordinance adopted pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1233/'>46.2-1233</a>, is subject to a civil penalty of $150 per violation. Such penalty shall be collected by the Office of the Attorney General, and the proceeds shall be deposited into the Literary Fund.</p><p>2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0825'>825</a>.</p>
