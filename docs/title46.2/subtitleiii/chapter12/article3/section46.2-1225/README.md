# ยง 46.2-1225 Enforcement provisions in city or county parking ordinances.

<p>Any city or county ordinance regulating parking under this article shall require:</p><p>1. That uncontested payment of parking citation penalties be collected and accounted for by a local administrative official or officials who shall be compensated by the locality or by a private management company under contract with the locality;</p><p>2. That contest by any person of any parking citation shall be certified on an appropriate form, to the appropriate district court, by such official or officials; and</p><p>3. That the local administrative official or officials shall cause complaints, summons, or warrants to be issued for delinquent parking citations.</p><p>Every action to collect unpaid parking citation penalties imposed for violation of a city or county ordinance regulating parking under this article shall be commenced within three years of the date upon which such penalty became delinquent.</p><p>1974, c. 403, ยง 46.1-254.1; 1975, c. 440; 1977, c. 671; 1980, c. 355; 1989, c. 727; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0459'>459</a>; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0563'>563</a>.</p>
