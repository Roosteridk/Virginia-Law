# ยง 46.2-1037 Lights on parked vehicles.

<p>Any vehicle parked or stopped on a highway, whether attended or unattended, between sunset and sunrise shall display at least one light projecting a white or amber light visible in clear weather from a distance of 500 feet to the front of such vehicle and projecting a red light visible under like conditions from a distance of 500 feet to the rear. No lights, however, need be displayed upon any such vehicle when legally parked.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 46-282; 1958, c. 541, ยง 46.1-276; 1959, Ex. Sess., c. 86; 1989, c. 727.</p>
