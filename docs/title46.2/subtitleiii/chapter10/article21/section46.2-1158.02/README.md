# § 46.2-1158.02 Penalty for failure to have motor vehicle inspection.

<p>A. Notwithstanding the penalty provisions of § <a href='/vacode/46.2-1171/'>46.2-1171</a>, a violation of § <a href='/vacode/46.2-1158/'>46.2-1158</a> constitutes a traffic infraction. The court may, in its discretion, dismiss a summons issued under § <a href='/vacode/46.2-1158/'>46.2-1158</a> where correction of vehicle or safety equipment defects or proof of compliance with § <a href='/vacode/46.2-1158/'>46.2-1158</a> is provided to the court subsequent to the issuance of the summons.</p><p>B. The operator of a motor vehicle who is cited for a violation of § <a href='/vacode/46.2-1158/'>46.2-1158</a> shall not be cited during the same occurrence for a violation of vehicle equipment requirements set forth in Article 3 (§ <a href='/vacode/46.2-1010/'>46.2-1010</a> et seq.) through Article 9 (§ <a href='/vacode/46.2-1066/'>46.2-1066</a> et seq.) for such vehicle, nor shall the operator of the motor vehicle that is subject to the citation be cited for a violation of such vehicle equipment requirements for such vehicle for a period of 15 calendar days.</p><p>2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0283'>283</a>; 2020, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1230'>1230</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1275'>1275</a>.</p>
