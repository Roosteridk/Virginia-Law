# § 46.2-1051 Local ordinances; vehicle exhaust.

<p>A. The governing body of any county, city, or town may, by ordinance, regulate noise from a vehicle operated on a highway that is not equipped with a muffler and exhaust system conforming to §§ <a href='/vacode/46.2-1047/'>46.2-1047</a> and <a href='/vacode/46.2-1049/'>46.2-1049</a>.</p><p>B. The provisions of subsection E of § <a href='/vacode/46.2-1300/'>46.2-1300</a> shall not apply to ordinances adopted pursuant to this section.</p><p>1983, c. 211, § 46.1-302.4; 1989, c. 727; 2004, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0947'>947</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0973'>973</a>; 2006, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0830'>830</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0896'>896</a>; 2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0490'>490</a>.</p>
