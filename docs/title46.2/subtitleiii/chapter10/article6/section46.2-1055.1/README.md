# ยง 46.2-1055.1 Windshield defroster or defogger.

<p>Every Virginia-registered motor vehicle manufactured for the 1969 or subsequent model years and required to be equipped with a windshield shall be equipped with a windshield defroster or defogger. The defroster or defogger shall be in good working order at all times when the vehicle is operated on the highways.</p><p>1990, c. 955.</p>
