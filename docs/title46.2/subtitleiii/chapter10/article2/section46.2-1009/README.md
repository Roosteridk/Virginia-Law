# § 46.2-1009 Exemptions for certain electrically powered vehicles; standards and permits for such vehicles.

<p>The provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1002/'>46.2-1002</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1008/'>46.2-1008</a> shall not apply to vehicles which are powered solely by electricity, capable of speeds of no more than fifteen miles per hour. The Superintendent may establish standards for safety equipment to be used on such vehicles. Upon the establishment of such standards, permits to use such vehicles may be issued to persons owning vehicles meeting such standards by the officer in charge of the division of the Department of State Police having jurisdiction in the county, city, or town in which such person resides.</p><p>1973, c. 455, § 46.1-314.1; 1989, c. 727.</p>
