<p>Every semitrailer, trailer, or separate vehicle attached by a drawbar, chain, or coupling to a towing vehicle other than a farm tractor or a vehicle not required to obtain a registration certificate and having an actual gross weight of 3,000 pounds or more, shall be equipped with brakes controlled or operated by the driver of the towing vehicle, which shall conform to the specifications set forth in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1067/'>46.2-1067</a> and shall be of a type approved by the Superintendent.
