# ยง 46.2-1401 Motor carrier laws do not apply.

<p>The following laws and regulations of the Commonwealth shall not apply to any ridesharing arrangement using a motor vehicle with a seating capacity for not more than fifteen persons, including the driver:</p><p>1. Laws and regulations containing insurance requirements that are specifically applicable to motor carriers or commercial vehicles;</p><p>2. Laws imposing a greater standard of care on motor carriers or commercial vehicles than that imposed on other drivers or owners of motor vehicles;</p><p>3. Laws and regulations with equipment requirements and special accident reporting requirements that are specifically applicable to motor carriers or commercial vehicles; and</p><p>4. Laws imposing a tax on fuel purchased in another state by a motor carrier or road user taxes on commercial buses.</p><p>1981, c. 218, ยง 46.1-557; 1989, c. 727; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0337'>337</a>.</p>
