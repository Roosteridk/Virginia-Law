# § 46.2-1255 Confiscation of disabled parking placards.

<p>A. Any law-enforcement officer or private security guard acting pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1243/'>46.2-1243</a> who issues a summons to or arrests an individual for any violation of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1247/'>46.2-1247</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1249/'>46.2-1249</a> and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1251/'>46.2-1251</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1253/'>46.2-1253</a> may confiscate the defendant's permanent, temporary, or organizational removable windshield placard and shall notify, by mail or facsimile, the Department of Motor Vehicles of such confiscation and the number of the placard involved.</p><p>B. After receiving notice specified in subsection A of this section, the Department may prohibit the issuance of any form of disabled parking license plate or placard to the defendant until the defendant's charge under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1247/'>46.2-1247</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1249/'>46.2-1249</a> and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1251/'>46.2-1251</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1253/'>46.2-1253</a> reaches final disposition, including appeals.</p><p>C. Upon the defendant's acquittal for any violation of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1247/'>46.2-1247</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1249/'>46.2-1249</a> and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1251/'>46.2-1251</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1253/'>46.2-1253</a>, the law-enforcement officer or private security guard shall return the confiscated placard to the defendant and the court shall notify the Department of such acquittal by electronic or other means. Upon the defendant's conviction for any violation of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1247/'>46.2-1247</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1249/'>46.2-1249</a> and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1251/'>46.2-1251</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1253/'>46.2-1253</a>, the law-enforcement officer or private security guard shall send the confiscated placard to the Department and the court shall notify the Department pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1256/'>46.2-1256</a>.</p><p>1997, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0783'>783</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0904'>904</a>.</p>
