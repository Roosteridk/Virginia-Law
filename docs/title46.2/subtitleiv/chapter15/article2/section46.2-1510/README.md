# ยง 46.2-1510 Dealers required to have established place of business.

<p>No license shall be issued to any motor vehicle dealer unless he has an established place of business, owned or leased by him, where a substantial portion of the sales activity of the business is routinely conducted and which:</p><p>1. Satisfies all local zoning regulations;</p><p>2. Has sales, service, and office space devoted exclusively to the dealership of at least 250 square feet in a permanent, enclosed building not used as a residence;</p><p>3. Houses all records the dealer is required to maintain by ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1529/'>46.2-1529</a>;</p><p>4. Is equipped with a desk, chairs, filing space, a working telephone listed in the name of the dealership, working utilities including electricity and provisions for space heating, and an Internet connection and email address;</p><p>5. Displays a sign and business hours as required by this chapter; and</p><p>6. Has contiguous space designated for the exclusive use of the dealer adequate to permit the display of at least 10 vehicles.</p><p>Any dealer licensed on or before July 1, 1995, shall be considered in compliance with subdivisions 2 and <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0006'>6</a> of this section for that licensee.</p><p>1988, c. 865, ยง 46.1-525.01; 1989, c. 727; 1995, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0767'>767</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0816'>816</a>; 1998, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0418'>418</a>; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0791'>791</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0615'>615</a>.</p>
