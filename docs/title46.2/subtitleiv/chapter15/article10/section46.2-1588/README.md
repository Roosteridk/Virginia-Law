# ยง 46.2-1588 Course provider responsibilities.

<p>Approved course providers shall:</p><p>1. Provide to each participant who successfully completes the required recertification course a certificate providing, at a minimum, (i) the name of the provider, (ii) name of the participant, and (iii) the date of completion;</p><p>2. Maintain all records on courses and participants for a period of five years and make those records available to the Board upon request;</p><p>3. Enter the names of participants completing the course into a database as directed by the Board within five days of the participant's completion of the course; and</p><p>4. Collect the recertification application fee from applicants and transmit such fee to the Board as directed by the Board within 15 days of receiving the fee from the applicant.</p><p>2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0574'>574</a>.</p>
