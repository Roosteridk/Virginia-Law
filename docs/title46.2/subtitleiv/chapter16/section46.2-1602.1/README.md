# § 46.2-1602.1 Duties of insurance companies upon acquiring certain vehicles.

<p>Every insurance company which acquires, as a result of the claims process, any late model vehicle titled in the Commonwealth or any recovered stolen vehicle whose estimated cost of repair exceeds seventy-five percent of its actual cash value shall apply to and obtain from the Department either (i) a salvage certificate or certificate of title as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1603/'>46.2-1603</a> or (ii) a nonrepairable certificate as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1603.2/'>46.2-1603.2</a> for each such vehicle. An insurance company may apply to and obtain from the Department either a salvage certificate as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1603/'>46.2-1603</a> or a nonrepairable certificate as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1603.2/'>46.2-1603.2</a> for any other vehicle which is determined to be either a salvage vehicle or a nonrepairable vehicle.</p><p>1992, c. 148; 1993, c. 376; 2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0235'>235</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0257'>257</a>.</p>
