<p>The Commissioner shall cancel the commercial feed license and product registrations of any person who fails to comply with the chapter by:</p><p>1. Failing to file the tonnage report;</p><p>2. Failing to accurately report any of the information required to be submitted under this chapter;</p><p>7. Failing to keep records for a period of three years; or</p><p>8.
