# § 3.2-1800 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Approved seed potatoes" means disease-free potatoes and parts thereof that conform to the standards established by the Potato Board.</p><p>"Board" means the Potato Board.</p><p>"Handler" means any person who is a processor, dealer, shipper, or exporter who purchases potatoes from a grower, or who acts as the grower's agent, or any person who holds a produce dealer or commission merchant license from the Department. The term also means any producer who packs, processes, or otherwise performs the functions of a handler pursuant to the provisions of this chapter.</p><p>"Jurisdiction" means any locality where potatoes are produced according to the records of the Department.</p><p>"Potatoes" means all varieties of potatoes except sweet potatoes.</p><p>"Producer" means any person who, in a calendar year, grows a minimum of 40,000 pounds of potatoes in the Commonwealth.</p><p>"Seed potatoes" means potatoes and parts thereof intended for the propagation or production of commercial potatoes.</p><p>1982, c. 126, §§ 3.1-684.21, 3.1-684.39; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0803'>803</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0835'>835</a>.</p>
