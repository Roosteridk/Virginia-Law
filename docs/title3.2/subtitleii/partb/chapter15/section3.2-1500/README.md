# ยง 3.2-1500 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Assessment" means moneys to be collected as authorized by this chapter.</p><p>"Bale" means a closely pressed package of ginned cotton that weighs approximately 480 pounds.</p><p>"Cotton" means the field crop of the genus Gossypium grown to be further processed into consumable goods.</p><p>"Farming unit" means any sole proprietorship, corporation, or partnership and includes land owned and leased by any such business entity.</p><p>"Gin" means to remove seed and foreign matter from cotton and make it into a bale.</p><p>"Handler" means a commercial enterprise that gins cotton.</p><p>"Producer" means any person who grows, harvests, and sells cotton in Virginia.</p><p>1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0873'>873</a>, ยง 3.1-1081; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0751'>751</a>; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>.</p>
