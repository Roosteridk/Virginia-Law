# ยง 3.2-2800 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Beekeeper" means any person who keeps and manages bees for profit, and shall include those growers who keep bees for pollinating crops.</p><p>"Cross pollination" means the transfer of pollen from the anthers of blossoms to the stigmas of other blossoms of the same crops or a variety of the same crop.</p><p>"Pollination contractor" means any person who contracts to supply a means of cross pollinating the blossoms of any specified plant.</p><p>"Producer" means any person engaged in the business of raising crops that benefit from cross pollination by honeybees or other pollinating insects.</p><p>1977, c. 452, ยง 3.1-610.23; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>; 2011, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0594'>594</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0681'>681</a>.</p>
