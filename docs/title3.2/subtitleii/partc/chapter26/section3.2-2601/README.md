# ยง 3.2-2601 Powers and duties of Commissioner.

<p>The Commissioner shall have the following powers and duties:</p><p>1. To provide information and assistance in obtaining permits relating to aquacultural activities;</p><p>2. To promote aquaculture including encouraging investment in aquaculture facilities to expand production, processing capacity, and marketing;</p><p>3. To work with appropriate state and federal agencies to review, develop, and implement policies and procedures to facilitate aquacultural development;</p><p>4. To consult with and assist aquaculture industry groups, aquaculture associations, and academic institutions to develop, maintain, and expand the aquaculture industry; and</p><p>5. To develop, support, and implement policies beneficial to Virginia aquaculture.</p><p>1992, c. 643, ยง 3.1-73.7; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0320'>320</a>; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>.</p>
