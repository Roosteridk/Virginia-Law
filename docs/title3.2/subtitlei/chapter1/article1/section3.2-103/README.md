# ยง 3.2-103 Records to be held in confidence.

<p>The Commissioner shall hold the following records of the Department in confidence unless otherwise directed by the Governor or the Board:</p><p>1. Schedules of work for regulatory inspection;</p><p>2. Trade secrets and commercial or financial information supplied by individuals or business entities to the Department;</p><p>3. Reports of criminal violations made to the Department by persons outside the Department;</p><p>4. Records of active investigations until the investigations are closed;</p><p>5. Financial records of applicants for assistance from the Virginia Farm Loan Revolving Account except those records that are otherwise a matter of public record; and</p><p>6.
