# § 3.2-5143 Enforcement against companies.

<p>When construing and enforcing the provisions of this chapter and Chapters 52 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-5200/'>3.2-5200</a> et seq.) and 54 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-5400/'>3.2-5400</a> et seq.), the act, omission, or failure of any officer, agent, or other individual acting for or employed by any partnership, corporation, company, society, or association within the scope of his employment or office, shall in every case be also deemed the act, omission, or failure of such partnership, corporation, company, society, or association, as well as that of the individual.</p><p>Code 1950, § 3-340; 1966, c. 702, § 3.1-419; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0185'>185</a>; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>.</p>
