# ยง 3.2-5703 Registration of service agency.

<p>A. A service agency shall not operate in the Commonwealth without first obtaining registration issued annually by the Commissioner. The application for registration as a service agency or renewal of application shall be made in writing on a form approved by the Commissioner.</p><p>B. Each application for registration or renewal shall contain:</p><p>1. The name of the service agency, including any fictitious names under which it intends to operate;</p><p>2. The principal business address of the service agency;</p><p>3. The name of every person functioning as a service technician in the Commonwealth who is in the employ of the service agency;</p><p>4. Documentation of verification pursuant to ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-5706/'>3.2-5706</a> of the weights or measures standards and calibrating equipment used or to be used by the service agency;</p><p>5. The fee required by ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-5704/'>3.2-5704</a>; and</p><p>6. Proof of a uniquely identifiable security seal for the service agency.</p><p>C. The Commissioner may deny, suspend, or revoke any registration or renewal if the application is incomplete, false, or fraudulent. It shall be a violation of this chapter for a person to submit to the Commissioner an application for registration or renewal that he knows to be false or fraudulent.</p><p>1992, c. 242, ยง 3.1-969.4; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0168'>168</a>.</p>
