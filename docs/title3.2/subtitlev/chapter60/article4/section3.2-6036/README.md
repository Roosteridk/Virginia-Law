# § 3.2-6036 Department may issue, deny, and revoke licenses.

<p>A. The Department shall issue a license for shooting enclosures only to those enclosures that were in operation on or before January 1, 1995. These enclosures shall hold only those animals described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-6040/'>3.2-6040</a> and as specified in regulations. The Department shall issue a license to a shooting enclosure only if it meets the requirements of this article and regulations adopted hereunder. No person shall operate an enclosure unless he has obtained a license from the Department.</p><p>B. The Department may deny, suspend, or revoke a license if the applicant for a license or a licensee, violates, or is otherwise not in compliance with this article or the regulations adopted pursuant thereto.</p><p>C. Before a shooting enclosure is licensed and throughout the duration of the license, the Department shall inspect the shooting enclosure to ensure compliance with this article or the regulations adopted pursuant thereto.</p><p>D. The State Veterinarian is authorized to seize and dispose of any livestock, as described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-6040/'>3.2-6040</a>, found in shooting enclosures that are not licensed under this article.</p><p>E. Any person convicted of operating an unlicensed shooting enclosure shall pay all reasonable costs incurred by the Department in the seizure and disposal of any confiscated livestock.</p><p>F. It is unlawful for any person to knowingly provide livestock, as described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-6040/'>3.2-6040</a>, to an unlicensed shooting enclosure.</p><p>1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0822'>822</a>, § 3.1-763.5:2; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0298'>298</a>; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>.</p>
