# ยง 3.2-6588 Intentional interference with a guide or leader dog; penalty.

<p>A. It is unlawful for a person to, without just cause, willfully impede or interfere with the duties performed by a dog if the person knows or has reason to believe the dog is a guide or leader dog. A violation of this subsection is a Class 3 misdemeanor.</p><p>B. It is unlawful for a person to, without just cause, willfully injure a dog if the person knows or has reason to believe the dog is a guide or leader dog. A violation of this subsection is a Class 1 misdemeanor.</p><p>"Guide or leader dog" means a dog that: (i) serves as a dog guide for a blind person as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.5-60/'>51.5-60</a> or for a person with a visual disability; (ii) serves as a listener for a deaf or hard-of-hearing person as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.5-111/'>51.5-111</a>; or (iii) provides support or assistance for a physically disabled or handicapped person.</p><p>1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0209'>209</a>, ยง 3.1-796.128:1; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>.</p>
