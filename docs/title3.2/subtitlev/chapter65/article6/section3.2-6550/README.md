# ยง 3.2-6550 Requirements for foster homes; penalty.

<p>In addition to any other requirements of this chapter, foster homes shall be subject to the following:</p><p>1. No foster home shall be operated in violation of any local zoning ordinance; and</p><p>2. No private residential dwelling and its surrounding grounds that serves as a foster home shall keep more than 50 companion animals on site at one time.</p><p>Any foster home found in violation of this section may be subject to a civil penalty not to exceed $250.</p><p>2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1007'>1007</a>, ยง 3.1-796.96:6; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0148'>148</a>.</p>
