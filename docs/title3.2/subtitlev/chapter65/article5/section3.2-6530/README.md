# § 3.2-6530 When license tax payable.

<p>A. The license tax as prescribed in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-6528/'>3.2-6528</a> is due not later than 30 days after a dog or cat has reached the age of four months, or not later than 30 days after an owner acquires a dog or cat four months of age or older, and each year thereafter.</p><p>Licensing periods for individual dogs and cats may be equal to and may run concurrently with the rabies vaccination effective period.</p><p>B. The governing body of a county or city may by ordinance provide for a lifetime dog or cat license. Such a license shall be valid only as long as the animal's owner resides in the issuing locality and the animal's rabies vaccination is kept current.</p><p>C. Any kennel license tax prescribed pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-6528/'>3.2-6528</a> shall be due on January 1 and not later than January 31 of each year.</p><p>1984, cc. 248, 492, § 29-213.58; 1986, c. 169; 1987, c. 488, § 3.1-796.88; 1990, c. 365; 1993, c. 817; 2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0836'>836</a>; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>; 2017, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0559'>559</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0567'>567</a>.</p>
