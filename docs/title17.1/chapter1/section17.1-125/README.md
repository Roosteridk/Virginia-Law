# § 17.1-125 Civil order book.

<p>The clerk shall record (i) trust fund orders pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-123/'>17.1-123</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-124/'>17.1-124</a> and (ii) the annual trust fund report required pursuant to subsection G of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-600/'>8.01-600</a> in a book known as the civil order book, in which shall be recorded all reports, orders, and decrees concerning moneys received or to be received by general receivers pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-582/'>8.01-582</a> and by clerks pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-600/'>8.01-600</a>.</p><p>1988, c. 553, § 17-28.1; 1998, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0872'>872</a>; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0035'>35</a>.</p>
