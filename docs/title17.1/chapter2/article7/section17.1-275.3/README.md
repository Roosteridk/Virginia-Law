# ยง 17.1-275.3 Fixed felony revocation fee.

<p>Upon the partial or full revocation of suspension of sentence or probation of a convicted felon pursuant to ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-306/'>19.2-306</a>, other than a revocation for failure to pay previously assessed court costs, there shall be assessed as court costs a fee of $158 to be known as the fixed felony revocation fee.
