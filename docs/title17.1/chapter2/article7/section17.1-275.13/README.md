# § 17.1-275.13 Additional fee for offenses related to sex trafficking.

<p>In addition to the fees provided for by §§ <a href='/vacode/17.1-275.1/'>17.1-275.1</a>, <a href='/vacode/17.1-275.2/'>17.1-275.2</a>, <a href='/vacode/17.1-275.7/'>17.1-275.7</a>, <a href='/vacode/17.1-275.10/'>17.1-275.10</a>, and <a href='/vacode/17.1-275.12/'>17.1-275.12</a>, any person convicted of a misdemeanor violation of § <a href='/vacode/18.2-346.01/'>18.2-346.01</a> or of § <a href='/vacode/18.2-348/'>18.2-348</a> or <a href='/vacode/18.2-349/'>18.2-349</a> shall be ordered to pay a $100 fee, and any person convicted of a violation of clause (ii), (iii), or (iv) of § <a href='/vacode/18.2-48/'>18.2-48</a>, or of § <a href='/vacode/18.2-368/'>18.2-368</a>, or any felony violation of the laws pertaining to commercial sex trafficking or prostitution offenses pursuant to Article 3 (§ <a href='/vacode/18.2-346/'>18.2-346</a> et seq.) of Chapter 8, with the exception of § <a href='/vacode/18.2-361/'>18.2-361</a>, shall be ordered to pay a $500 fee. All fees collected pursuant to this section shall be deposited into the Virginia Prevention of Sex Trafficking Fund to be used in accordance with § <a href='/vacode/9.1-116.4/'>9.1-116.4</a>.</p><p>2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0728'>728</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0122'>122</a>; 2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0188'>188</a>.</p>
