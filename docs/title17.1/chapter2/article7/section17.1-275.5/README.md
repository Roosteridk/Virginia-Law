# § 17.1-275.5 Amounts to be added; judgment in favor of the Commonwealth.

<p>A. The clerk shall assess, in addition to the fees provided for by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/'>17.1-275.1</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/'>17.1-275.2</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.3/'>17.1-275.3</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.4/'>17.1-275.4</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.7/'>17.1-275.7</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/'>17.1-275.8</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.9/'>17.1-275.9</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.10/'>17.1-275.10</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11/'>17.1-275.11</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11:1/'>17.1-275.11:1</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.12/'>17.1-275.12</a>, the following costs:</p><p>1. Any amount paid by the Commonwealth for legal representation of the defendant;</p><p>2. Any amount paid for trial transcripts;</p><p>3. Extradition costs;</p><p>4. Costs of psychiatric evaluation;</p><p>5. Costs taxed against the defendant as appellant under Rule 5A:30 of the Rules of the Supreme Court;</p><p>6. Any fee for a returned check or disallowed credit card charge assessed pursuant to subdivision A 28 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275/'>17.1-275</a>;</p><p>7. Any jury costs;</p><p>8. Any assessment made pursuant to subdivision A 10 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275/'>17.1-275</a>;</p><p>9. Any fees prescribed in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-268.8/'>18.2-268.8</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.26:8/'>46.2-341.26:8</a>;</p><p>10. Any court costs related to an ignition interlock device;</p><p>11. Any fee for testing for HIV;</p><p>12. Any fee for processing an individual admitted to jail as prescribed in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1613.1/'>15.2-1613.1</a>;</p><p>13. Any fee for courthouse security personnel as prescribed in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-120/'>53.1-120</a>;</p><p>14. Any fee for a DNA sample as prescribed in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-310.2/'>19.2-310.2</a>;</p><p>15. Reimbursement to the Commonwealth of medical fees as prescribed in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-165.1/'>19.2-165.1</a>;</p><p>16. Any fee for a local criminal justice training academy as prescribed in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-106/'>9.1-106</a>;</p><p>17. Any fee prescribed by §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/'>16.1-69.48:1</a>.01 and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11/'>17.1-275.11</a>;</p><p>18. Any expenses charged pursuant to subsection B or F of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-187.1/'>19.2-187.1</a>; and</p><p>19. Any fee for an electronic summons system as prescribed in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-279.1/'>17.1-279.1</a>.</p><p>B. The total amount of assessments described in subsection A, including (i) the fees provided for by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/'>17.1-275.1</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/'>17.1-275.2</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.3/'>17.1-275.3</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.4/'>17.1-275.4</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.7/'>17.1-275.7</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/'>17.1-275.8</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.9/'>17.1-275.9</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.10/'>17.1-275.10</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11/'>17.1-275.11</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11:1/'>17.1-275.11:1</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.12/'>17.1-275.12</a> and (ii) all other fines and costs, shall be docketed by the clerk as a judgment against the defendant in favor of the Commonwealth in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-446/'>8.01-446</a>.</p><p>1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0009'>9</a>;
