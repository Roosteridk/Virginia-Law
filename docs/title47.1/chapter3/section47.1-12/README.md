# ยง 47.1-12 Powers.

<p>Each notary shall be empowered to perform the following notarial acts: (i) take acknowledgments, (ii) administer oaths and affirmations, (iii) certify that a copy of any document, other than a document in the custody of a court, is a true copy thereof, (iv) certify affidavits or depositions of witnesses, (v) perform verification of fact, and (vi) perform such other acts as may be specifically permitted by law.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 47-2.1; 1978, c. 621; 1980, c. 580; 2007, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0269'>269</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0590'>590</a>; 2011, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0731'>731</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0834'>834</a>.</p>
