# ยง 47.1-13 Jurisdiction; Virginia notarial powers outside the Commonwealth.

<p>A. The powers of any notary commissioned pursuant to this title may be exercised anywhere within the Commonwealth of Virginia.</p><p>B. Any notary commissioned pursuant to this title may likewise perform notarial acts outside the Commonwealth, where such notarial acts are performed in accordance with this chapter.</p><p>C. An employee of the federal government authorized to perform notarial acts may perform notarial acts in accordance with this chapter.</p><p>D.
