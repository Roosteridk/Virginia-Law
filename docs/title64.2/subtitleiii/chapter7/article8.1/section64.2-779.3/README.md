# ยง 64.2-779.3 Application; governing law.

<p>This article applies to a trust created before, on, or after July 1, 2017, that:</p><p>1. Has its principal place of administration in the Commonwealth, including a trust whose principal place of administration has been changed to the Commonwealth; or</p><p>2. Provides by its trust instrument that it is governed by the law of the Commonwealth or is governed by the law of the Commonwealth for the purpose of:</p><p>a. Administration, including administration of a trust whose governing law for purposes of administration has been changed to the law of the Commonwealth;</p><p>b. Construction of terms of the trust; or</p><p>c. Determining the meaning or effect of terms of the trust.</p><p>2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0592'>592</a>.</p>
