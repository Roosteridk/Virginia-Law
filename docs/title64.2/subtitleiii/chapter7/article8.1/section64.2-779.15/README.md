# ยง 64.2-779.15 Removal or replacement of authorized fiduciary.

<p>An authorized fiduciary may not exercise the decanting power to modify a provision in a first-trust instrument granting another person power to remove or replace the fiduciary unless:</p><p>1. The person holding the power consents to the modification in a signed record and the modification applies only to the person;</p><p>2. The person holding the power and the qualified beneficiaries of the second trust consent to the modification in a signed record and the modification grants a substantially similar power to another person; or</p><p>3. The court approves the modification and the modification grants a substantially similar power to another person.</p><p>2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0592'>592</a>.</p>
