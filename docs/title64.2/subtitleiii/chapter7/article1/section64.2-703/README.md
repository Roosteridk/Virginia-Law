# § 64.2-703 Default and mandatory rules.

<p>A. Except as otherwise provided in the terms of the trust, this chapter governs the duties and powers of a trustee, relations among trustees, and the rights and interests of a beneficiary.</p><p>B. The terms of a trust prevail over any provision of this chapter except:</p><p>1. The requirements for creating a trust;</p><p>2. Subject to subsection I of § <a href='/vacode/64.2-756/'>64.2-756</a> and §§ <a href='/vacode/64.2-779.32/'>64.2-779.32</a> and <a href='/vacode/64.2-779.34/'>64.2-779.34</a>, the duty of a trustee to act in good faith and in accordance with the terms and purposes of the trust and the interests of the beneficiaries;</p><p>3. The requirement that a trust and its terms be for the benefit of its beneficiaries, and that the trust have a purpose that is lawful, not contrary to public policy, and possible to achieve;</p><p>4. The power of the court to modify or terminate a trust under §§ <a href='/vacode/64.2-728/'>64.2-728</a> through <a href='/vacode/64.2-734/'>64.2-734</a>;</p><p>5. The effect of a spendthrift provision and the rights of certain creditors and assignees to reach a trust as provided in Article 5 (§ <a href='/vacode/64.2-742/'>64.2-742</a> et seq.);</p><p>6. The power of the court under § <a href='/vacode/64.2-755/'>64.2-755</a> to require, dispense with, or modify or terminate a bond;</p><p>7. The power of the court under subsection B of § <a href='/vacode/64.2-761/'>64.2-761</a> to adjust a trustee's compensation specified in the terms of the trust that is unreasonably low or high;</p><p>8. The effect of an exculpatory term under § <a href='/vacode/64.2-799/'>64.2-799</a>;</p><p>9. The rights under §§ <a href='/vacode/64.2-801/'>64.2-801</a> through <a href='/vacode/64.2-804/'>64.2-804</a> of a person other than a trustee or beneficiary;</p><p>10. Periods of limitation for commencing a judicial proceeding; and</p><p>11. The power of the court to take such action and exercise such jurisdiction as may be necessary in the interests of justice.</p><p>2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0935'>935</a>, § 55-541.05; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0216'>216</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0768'>768</a>.</p>
