# ยง 64.2-779.34 No duty to monitor, inform, or advise.

<p>A. Unless the terms of a trust provide otherwise:</p><p>1. A trustee does not have a duty to:</p><p>a. Monitor a trust director; or</p><p>b. Inform or give advice to a settlor, beneficiary, trustee, or trust director concerning an instance in which the trustee might have acted differently than the trust director; and</p><p>2. By taking an action described in subdivision 1, a trustee does not assume the duty excluded by subdivision 1.</p><p>B. Unless the terms of a trust provide otherwise:</p><p>1. A trust director does not have a duty to:</p><p>a. Monitor a trustee or another trust director; or</p><p>b. Inform or give advice to a settlor, beneficiary, trustee, or another trust director concerning an instance in which the trust director might have acted differently than a trustee or another trust director; and</p><p>2. By taking an action described in subdivision 1, a trust director does not assume the duty excluded by subdivision 1.</p><p>2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0768'>768</a>.</p>
