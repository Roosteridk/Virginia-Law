# ยง 64.2-761 Compensation of trustee.

<p>A. If the terms of a trust do not specify the trustee's compensation, a trustee is entitled to compensation that is reasonable under the circumstances.</p><p>B. If the terms of a trust specify the trustee's compensation, the trustee is entitled to be compensated as specified, but the court may allow more or less compensation if:</p><p>1. The duties of the trustee are substantially different from those contemplated when the trust was created; or</p><p>2. The compensation specified by the terms of the trust would be unreasonably low or high.</p><p>2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0935'>935</a>, ยง 55-547.08; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
