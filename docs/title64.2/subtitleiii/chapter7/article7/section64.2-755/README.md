# ยง 64.2-755 Trustee's bond.

<p>A. Except as otherwise provided in Part A (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1200/'>64.2-1200</a> et seq.) of Subtitle IV, a trustee shall give bond, or bond with surety or other security, to secure performance of the trustee's duties only if the court finds that a bond is needed to protect the interests of the beneficiaries or is required by the terms of the trust and the court has not dispensed with the requirement.</p><p>B.
