<p>A. A violation by a trustee of a duty the trustee owes to a beneficiary is a breach of trust.</p><p>B. To remedy a breach of trust that has occurred or may occur, the court may:</p><p>1. Compel the trustee to perform the trustee's duties;</p><p>2. Enjoin the trustee from committing a breach of trust;</p><p>3. Compel the trustee to redress a breach of trust by paying money, restoring property, or other means;</p><p>4. Order a trustee to account;</p><p>5. Appoint a special fiduciary to take possession of the trust property and administer the trust;</p><p>6. Suspend the trustee;</p><p>7.
