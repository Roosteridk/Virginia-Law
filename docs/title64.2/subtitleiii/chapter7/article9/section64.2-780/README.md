# § 64.2-780 Definition of terms.

<p>As used in this article:</p><p>"Controlling document" means the will, agreement, power of attorney, court order, or other instrument creating the fiduciary powers.</p><p>"Trust" includes the assets under the control or management of the trustee.</p><p>"Trustee" includes any fiduciary as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-2/'>8.01-2</a>, an attorney-in-fact or agent acting for a principal under a written power of attorney, a custodian under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1911/'>64.2-1911</a>, and a custodial trustee under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-906/'>64.2-906</a>.</p><p>1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0772'>772</a>, § 26-45.13; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0517'>517</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
