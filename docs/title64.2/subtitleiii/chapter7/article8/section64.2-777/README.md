# ยง 64.2-777 General powers of trustee.

<p>A. A trustee, without authorization by the court, may exercise:</p><p>1. Powers conferred by the terms of the trust; and</p><p>2. Except as limited by the terms of the trust:</p><p>a. All powers over the trust property that an unmarried competent owner has over individually owned property;</p><p>b. Any other powers appropriate to achieve the proper investment, management, and distribution of the trust property; and</p><p>c. Any other powers conferred by this chapter.</p><p>B. The exercise of a power is subject to the fiduciary duties prescribed by this article.</p><p>C. Any reference in a trust instrument incorporating the powers authorized under ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-105/'>64.2-105</a> shall not be construed to limit powers a trustee may exercise pursuant to this section, unless the settlor expressly states in the trust instrument that such reference should be so construed.</p><p>2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0935'>935</a>, ยง 55-548.15; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
