# § 64.2-1043 Unitrust policy.

<p>A. In administering a unitrust under this article, a fiduciary shall follow a unitrust policy adopted under subdivision A 1 or 2 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a> or amended or replaced under subdivision A 2 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a>.</p><p>B. A unitrust policy shall provide:</p><p>1. The unitrust rate or the method for determining the unitrust rate under § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/'>64.2-1044</a>;</p><p>2. The method for determining the applicable value under § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/'>64.2-1045</a>; and</p><p>3. The rules described in §§ <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/'>64.2-1044</a> through <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1047/'>64.2-1047</a> that apply in the administration of the unitrust, whether the rules are:</p><p>a. Mandatory, as provided in subsection A of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/'>64.2-1045</a> and subsection A of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1046/'>64.2-1046</a>; or</p><p>b. Optional, as provided in §<a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/'> 64.2-1044</a>, subsection B of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/'>64.2-1045</a>, subsection B of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1046/'>64.2-1046</a>, and subsection A of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1047/'>64.2-1047</a>, to the extent the fiduciary elects to adopt those rules.</p><p>2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0354'>354</a>.</p>
