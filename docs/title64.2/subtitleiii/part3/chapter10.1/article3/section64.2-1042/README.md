# § 64.2-1042 Notice.

<p>A. A notice required by subdivision B 2 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a> shall be sent in a manner authorized under § <a href='/vacode/64.2-707/'>64.2-707</a> to:</p><p>1. The qualified beneficiaries determined under § <a href='/vacode/64.2-701/'>64.2-701</a>, other than the Attorney General;</p><p>2. Each person acting as trust director of the trust under the Uniform Directed Trust Act (§ <a href='/vacode/64.2-779.26/'>64.2-779.26</a> et seq.); and</p><p>3. Each person that is granted a power by the terms of the trust to appoint or remove a trustee or person described in subdivision 2, to the extent the power is exercisable when the person that exercises the power is not then serving as a trustee or person described in subdivision 2.</p><p>B. The representation provisions of Article 3 (§ <a href='/vacode/64.2-714/'>64.2-714</a> et seq.) of Chapter 7 apply to notice under this section.</p><p>C. A person may consent in a record at any time to action proposed under subdivision B 2 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a>. A notice required by subdivision B 2 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a> need not be sent to a person that consents under this subsection.</p><p>D. A notice required by subdivision B 2 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a> shall include:</p><p>1. The action proposed under subdivision B 2 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a>;</p><p>2. For a conversion of an income trust to a unitrust, a copy of the unitrust policy adopted under subdivision A 1 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a>;</p><p>3. For a change in the percentage or method used to calculate the unitrust amount, a copy of the unitrust policy or amendment or replacement of the unitrust policy adopted under subdivision A 2 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/'>64.2-1041</a>;</p><p>4. A statement that the person to which the notice is sent may object to the proposed action by stating in a record the basis for the objection and sending or delivering the record to the fiduciary;</p><p>5. The date by which an objection under subdivision 4 must be received by the fiduciary, which must be at least 30 days after the date the notice is sent;</p><p>6. The date on which the action is proposed to be taken and the date on which the action is proposed to take effect;</p><p>7. The name and contact information of the fiduciary; and</p><p>8. The name and contact information of a person that may be contacted for additional information.</p><p>2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0354'>354</a>.</p>
