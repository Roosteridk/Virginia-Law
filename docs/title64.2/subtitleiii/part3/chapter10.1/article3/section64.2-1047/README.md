# § 64.2-1047 Special tax benefits; other rules.

<p>A. A unitrust policy may:</p><p>1. Provide methods and standards for:</p><p>a. Determining the timing of distributions;</p><p>b. Making distributions in cash or in kind or partly in cash and partly in kind; or</p><p>c. Correcting an underpayment or overpayment to a beneficiary based on the unitrust amount if there is an error in calculating the unitrust amount;</p><p>2. Specify sources and the order of sources, including categories of income for federal income tax purposes, from which distributions of a unitrust amount are paid; or</p><p>3. Provide other standards and rules the fiduciary determines serve the interests of the beneficiaries.</p><p>B. If a trust qualifies for a special tax benefit or a fiduciary is not an independent person:</p><p>1. The unitrust rate established under § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/'>64.2-1044</a> may not be less than three percent or more than five percent;</p><p>2. The only provisions of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/'>64.2-1045</a> that apply are subsection A and subdivisions B 1, B 4, B 5 a, and B 9 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/'>64.2-1045</a>;</p><p>3. The only period that may be used under § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1046/'>64.2-1046</a> is a calendar year under subdivision A 1 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1046/'>64.2-1046</a>; and</p><p>4. The only other provisions of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1046/'>64.2-1046</a> that apply are subdivisions B 2 a and B 3 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1046/'>64.2-1046</a>.</p><p>2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0354'>354</a>.</p>
