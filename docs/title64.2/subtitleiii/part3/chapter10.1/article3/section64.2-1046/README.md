# § 64.2-1046 Period.

<p>A. A unitrust policy must provide the period used under §§ <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/'>64.2-1044</a> and <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/'>64.2-1045</a>. Except as otherwise provided in subdivision B 3 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1047/'>64.2-1047</a>, the period may be:</p><p>1. A calendar year;</p><p>2. A 12-month period other than a calendar year;</p><p>3. A calendar quarter;</p><p>4. A three-month period other than a calendar quarter; or</p><p>5. Another period.</p><p>B. Except as otherwise provided in subsection B of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1047/'>64.2-1047</a>, a unitrust policy may provide standards for:</p><p>1. Using fewer preceding periods under subdivision A 2 b, B 3, or B 4 of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/'>64.2-1044</a> if:</p><p>a. The trust was not in existence in a preceding period; or</p><p>b. Market indices or other published data are not available for a preceding period;</p><p>2. Using fewer preceding periods under subdivision B 5 a, B 5 b, B 6 b, or B 7 b of § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/'>64.2-1045</a> if:</p><p>a. The trust was not in existence in a preceding period; or</p><p>b. Fair market values are not available for a preceding period; and</p><p>3. Prorating the unitrust amount on a daily basis for a part of a period in which the trust or the administration of the trust as a unitrust or the interest of any beneficiary commences or terminates.</p><p>2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0354'>354</a>.</p>
