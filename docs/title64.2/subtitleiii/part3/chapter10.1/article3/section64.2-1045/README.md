<p>A. A unitrust policy must provide the method for determining the fair market value of an asset for the purpose of determining the unitrust amount, including:</p><p>1. The frequency of valuing the asset, which need not require a valuation in every period; and</p><p>2. The date for valuing the asset in each period in which the asset is valued.</p><p>B. Except as otherwise provided in subdivision B 2 of ยง <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1047/'>64.2-1047</a>, a unitrust policy may provide methods for determining the amount of the net fair market value of the trust to take into account in determining the applicable value, including:</p><p>1. Obtaining an appraisal of an asset for which fair market value is not readily available;</p><p>2. Exclusion of specific assets or groups or types of assets;</p><p>3. Other exceptions or modifications of the treatment of specific assets or groups or types of assets;</p><p>4. Identification and treatment of cash or property held for distribution;</p><p>5. Use of:</p><p>a. An average of fair market values over a stated number of preceding periods; or</p><p>b. Another mathematical blend of fair market values over a stated number of preceding periods;</p><p>6. A limit on how much the applicable value of all assets, groups of assets, or individual assets may increase over:</p><p>a. The corresponding applicable value for the preceding period; or</p><p>b. A mathematical blend of applicable values over a stated number of preceding periods;</p><p>7. A limit on how much the applicable value of all assets, groups of assets, or individual assets may decrease below:</p><p>a. The corresponding applicable value for the preceding period; or</p><p>b. A mathematical blend of applicable values over a stated number of preceding periods;</p><p>8. The treatment of accrued income and other features of an asset that affect value; and</p><p>9. Determining the liabilities of the trust, including treatment of liabilities to conform with the treatment of assets under subdivisions 1 through 8.</p><p>2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0354'>354</a>.</p>
