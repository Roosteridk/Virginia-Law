# ยง 64.2-913 Expenses, compensation, and bond of custodial trustee.

<p>Except as otherwise provided in the instrument creating the custodial trust, in an agreement with the beneficiary, or by court order, a custodial trustee:</p><p>1. Is entitled to reimbursement from custodial trust property for reasonable expenses incurred in the performance of fiduciary services;</p><p>2. Has a noncumulative election, to be made no later than six months after the end of each calendar year, to charge a reasonable compensation for fiduciary services performed during that year; and</p><p>3. Need not furnish a bond or other security for the faithful performance of fiduciary duties.</p><p>1990, c. 264, ยง 55-34.14; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
