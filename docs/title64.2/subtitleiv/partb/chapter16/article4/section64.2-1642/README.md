# ยง 64.2-1642 Effect on existing powers of attorney.

<p>Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, on July 1, 2010:</p><p>1. This chapter applies to a power of attorney created before, on, or after July 1, 2010;</p><p>2. This chapter applies to a judicial proceeding concerning a power of attorney commenced on or after July 1, 2010;</p><p>3. This chapter applies to a judicial proceeding concerning a power of attorney commenced before July 1, 2010, unless the court finds that application of a provision of this chapter would substantially interfere with the effective conduct of the judicial proceeding or prejudice the rights of a party, in which case that provision does not apply and the superseded law applies; and</p><p>4. Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, an act done before July 1, 2010, is not affected by this chapter.</p><p>2010, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0455'>455</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0632'>632</a>, ยง 26-116; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
