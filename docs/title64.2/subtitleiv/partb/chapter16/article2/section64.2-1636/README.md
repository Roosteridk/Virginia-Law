# § 64.2-1636 Retirement plans.

<p>A. In this section, "retirement plan" means a plan or account created by an employer, the principal, or another individual to provide retirement benefits or deferred compensation of which the principal is a participant, beneficiary, or owner, including a plan or account under the following sections of the Internal Revenue Code:</p><p>1. An individual retirement account under Internal Revenue Code 26 U.S.C. § 408, as amended;</p><p>2. A Roth individual retirement account under Internal Revenue Code 26 U.S.C. § 408A, as amended;</p><p>3. A deemed individual retirement account under Internal Revenue Code 26 U.S.C. § 408 (q), as amended;</p><p>4. An annuity or mutual fund custodial account under Internal Revenue Code 26 U.S.C. § 403 (b), as amended;</p><p>5. A pension, profit-sharing, stock bonus, or other retirement plan qualified under Internal Revenue Code 26 U.S.C. § 401 (a), as amended;</p><p>6. A plan under Internal Revenue Code 26 U.S.C. § 457 (b), as amended; and</p><p>7. A nonqualified deferred compensation plan under Internal Revenue Code 26 U.S.C. § 409A, as amended.</p><p>B. Unless the power of attorney otherwise provides, language in a power of attorney granting general authority with respect to retirement plans authorizes the agent to:</p><p>1. Select the form and timing of payments under a retirement plan and withdraw benefits from a plan;</p><p>2. Make a rollover, including a direct trustee-to-trustee rollover, of benefits from one retirement plan to another;</p><p>3. Establish a retirement plan in the principal's name;</p><p>4. Make contributions to a retirement plan;</p><p>5. Exercise investment powers available under a retirement plan; and</p><p>6. Borrow from, sell assets to, or purchase assets from a retirement plan.</p><p>2010, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0455'>455</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0632'>632</a>, § 26-109; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
