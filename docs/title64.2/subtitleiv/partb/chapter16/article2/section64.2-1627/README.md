# ยง 64.2-1627 Stocks and bonds.

<p>Unless the power of attorney otherwise provides, language in a power of attorney granting general authority with respect to stocks and bonds authorizes the agent to:</p><p>1. Buy, sell, and exchange stocks and bonds;</p><p>2. Establish, continue, modify, or terminate an account with respect to stocks and bonds;</p><p>3. Pledge stocks and bonds as security to borrow, pay, renew, or extend the time of payment of a debt of the principal;</p><p>4. Receive certificates and other evidences of ownership with respect to stocks and bonds; and</p><p>5. Exercise voting rights with respect to stocks and bonds in person or by proxy, enter into voting trusts, and consent to limitations on the right to vote.</p><p>2010, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0455'>455</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0632'>632</a>, ยง 26-100; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
