# § 64.2-1431 Sale of property and payment of proceeds to nonresident trustee.

<p>If, in any proceeding under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1427/'>64.2-1427</a> or in case of an interest in property acquired by a will that does not restrict the transfer of property out of the Commonwealth upon petition under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1427/'>64.2-1427</a>, the circuit court may order the property, or any part of it, to be sold, and the proceeds to be paid to the foreign guardian, conservator, committee, or nonresident trustee.</p><p>Code 1919, § 5355; Code 1950, § 26-66; 1968, c. 399; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0801'>801</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0935'>935</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
