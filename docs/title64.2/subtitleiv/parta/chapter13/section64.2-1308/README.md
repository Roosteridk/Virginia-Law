# § 64.2-1308 Forms for inventories and accounts.

<p>The Office of the Executive Secretary of the Supreme Court shall provide to each circuit court clerk forms and instructions for the inventories required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1300/'>64.2-1300</a> and forms and instructions for accounts. The clerk shall provide the appropriate forms to every fiduciary who qualifies in the clerk's office. An inventory filed pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1300/'>64.2-1300</a> or an account filed pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1206/'>64.2-1206</a> may be made on the form provided to the fiduciary by the clerk of the court, on a computer-generated facsimile of the appropriate form, or in any other clear format.</p><p>1966, c. 336, §§ 26-12.1, 26-17.3; 1972, c. 411; 1993, cc. 581, 689, § 26-17.3; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0842'>842</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
