# § 64.2-2027 Use of estate of incapacitated person in a state facility not limited by provisions relating to expenses.

<p>Nothing in Article 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-715/'>37.2-715</a> et seq.) of Chapter 7 of Title 37.2 shall be construed to relieve the fiduciary of any individual receiving services in a state facility from paying to the state facility a sum for extra comforts or to make it unlawful for the fiduciary to make voluntary gifts that the fiduciary may deem conducive to the happiness and comfort of the individual.</p><p>Code 1950, § 37-153; 1950, p. 924; 1968, c. 477, § 37.1-146; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0716'>716</a>, § 37.2-1028; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0476'>476</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0507'>507</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
