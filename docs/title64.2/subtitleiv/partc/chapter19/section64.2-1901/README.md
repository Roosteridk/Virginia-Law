<p>A. This chapter applies to any transfer that refers to the Uniform Transfers to Minors Act or this chapter in the designation under subsection A of ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1908/'>64.2-1908</a> by which the transfer is made if, at the time of the transfer, the transferor, the minor, or the custodian is a resident of the Commonwealth or the custodial property is located in the Commonwealth.
