# ยง 64.2-2734 Remedy for breach of contract to appoint or not to appoint.

<p>The remedy for a powerholder's breach of a contract to appoint or not to appoint appointive property is limited to damages payable out of the appointive property or, if appropriate, specific performance of the contract.</p><p>2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0266'>266</a>.</p>
