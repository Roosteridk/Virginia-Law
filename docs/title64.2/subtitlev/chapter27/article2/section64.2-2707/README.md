# ยง 64.2-2707 Creation of power of appointment.

<p>A. A power of appointment is created only if:</p><p>1. The instrument creating the power:</p><p>a. Is valid under applicable law; and</p><p>b. Except as otherwise provided in subsection B, transfers the appointive property; and</p><p>2. The terms of the instrument creating the power manifest the donor's intent to create in a powerholder a power of appointment over the appointive property exercisable in favor of a permissible appointee.</p><p>B. Subdivision A 1 b does not apply to the creation of a power of appointment by the exercise of a power of appointment.</p><p>C. A power of appointment may not be created in a deceased individual.</p><p>D. Subject to an applicable rule against perpetuities, a power of appointment may be created in an unborn or unascertained powerholder.</p><p>2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0266'>266</a>.</p>
