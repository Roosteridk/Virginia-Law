# ยง 64.2-2719 Impermissible appointment.

<p>A. An exercise of a power of appointment in favor of an impermissible appointee is ineffective.</p><p>B. An exercise of a power of appointment in favor of a permissible appointee is ineffective to the extent that the appointment is a fraud on the power.</p><p>2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0266'>266</a>.</p>
