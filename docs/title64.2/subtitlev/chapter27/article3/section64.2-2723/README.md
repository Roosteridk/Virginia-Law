# ยง 64.2-2723 Disposition of unappointed property under released or unexercised nongeneral power.

<p>To the extent that a powerholder releases, ineffectively exercises, or fails to exercise a nongeneral power of appointment:</p><p>1. The gift-in-default clause controls the disposition of the unappointed property; or</p><p>2. If there is no gift-in-default clause or to the extent that the clause is ineffective, the unappointed property:</p><p>a. Passes to the permissible appointees if:</p><p>(1) The permissible appointees are defined and limited; and</p><p>(2) The terms of the instrument creating the power do not manifest a contrary intent; or</p><p>b. If there is no taker under subdivision 2 a, passes under a reversionary interest to the donor or the donor's transferee or successor in interest.</p><p>2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0266'>266</a>.</p>
