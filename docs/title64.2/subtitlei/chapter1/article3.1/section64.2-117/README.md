# ยง 64.2-117 Applicability.

<p>A. This article applies to:</p><p>1. A fiduciary acting under a will or power of attorney executed before, on, or after July 1, 2017;</p><p>2. A personal representative acting for a decedent who died before, on, or after July 1, 2017;</p><p>3. A conservatorship proceeding commenced before, on, or after July 1, 2017;</p><p>4. A guardianship proceeding commenced before, on, or after July 1, 2017; and</p><p>5. A trustee acting under a trust created before, on, or after July 1, 2017.</p><p>B. This article applies to a custodian if the user resides in the Commonwealth or resided in the Commonwealth at the time of the user's death.</p><p>C. This article does not apply to a digital asset of an employer used by an employee in the ordinary course of the employer's business.</p><p>2017, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0033'>33</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0080'>80</a>.</p>
