# § 64.2-405 Interested persons as competent witnesses.

<p>No person is incompetent to testify for or against a will solely by reason of any interest he possesses in the will or the estate of the testator.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 64-53, 64-54; 1962, c. 338; 1968, c. 656, § 64.1-51; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614'>614</a>.</p>
