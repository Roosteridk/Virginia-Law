<p>Any citizen, resident of this Commonwealth, who has reason to believe that there are waste and unappropriated lands in this Commonwealth (not being excluded under ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/41.1-3/'>41.1-3</a> from grant), shall have the right to file a proceeding in the name of the county or city seeking the sale and disposition of such land. Each landowner adjoining the tract in question shall be made a party to the proceedings.</p><p>He shall file with the motion a copy of a plat prepared by a licensed land surveyor giving the metes and bounds of the land alleged to be waste and unappropriated.
