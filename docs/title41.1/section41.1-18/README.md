# ยง 41.1-18 Same; subsequent proceedings; disposition of proceeds of sale.

<p>Thereafter the proceedings shall conform, mutatis mutandis, to the provisions of Article 4 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3965/'>58.1-3965</a> et seq.) of Chapter 39 of Title 58.1 but on the motion of any party the sale of such land shall be public.
