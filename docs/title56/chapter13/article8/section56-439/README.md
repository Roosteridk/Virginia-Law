# § 56-439 Application of §§ 56-436 through 56-438.

<p>The provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-436/'>56-436</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-438/'>56-438</a> shall not apply to any railroad company which has its line of road enclosed with fences and cattle guards, as required by law.</p><p>Code 1919, § 3997.</p>
