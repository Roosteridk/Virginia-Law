# § 56-231.48 Applicability of other laws.

<p>All of the provisions of the Virginia Stock Corporation Act, Chapter 9 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-601/'>13.1-601</a> et seq.) of Title 13.1, and the Virginia Nonstock Corporation Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-801/'>13.1-801</a> et seq.), insofar as not inconsistent with this article, are hereby made applicable to stock and nonstock cooperatives, respectively, when the articles of incorporation are filed in the office of the Commission; provided, however, that subsections D through G of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-620/'>13.1-620</a> and subdivision 1 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-825/'>13.1-825</a> shall not apply to any affiliate or subsidiary of a cooperative. A cooperative must have a registered office and a registered agent, pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-634/'>13.1-634</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-833/'>13.1-833</a>, as appropriate.</p><p>1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0874'>874</a>.</p>
