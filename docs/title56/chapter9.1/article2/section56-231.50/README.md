# § 56-231.50 Regulation by State Corporation Commission.

<p>The regulated utility services and operations of any cooperative organized under this article shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the Commission in the same manner and to the same extent as are all other providers of such regulated utility services under the laws of this Commonwealth; but with regard to sales of energy at wholesale, no cooperative shall be subject to the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-234/'>56-234</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-245/'>56-245</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-247/'>56-247</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-249/'>56-249</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-249.6/'>56-249.6</a>. With regard to business activities which are not regulated utility services, no cooperative shall be subject to the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-234/'>56-234</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-245/'>56-245</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-249/'>56-249</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-249.6/'>56-249.6</a>. All other business activities of a cooperative and its affiliates shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the Commission to the extent provided by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-231.50:1/'>56-231.50:1</a> and any other applicable laws of this Commonwealth.</p><p>1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0874'>874</a>.</p>
