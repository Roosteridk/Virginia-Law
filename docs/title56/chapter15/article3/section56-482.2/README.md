# ยง 56-482.2 Penalties.

<p>Any interexchange company which willfully and knowingly fails to provide on time a report required by ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-482.1/'>56-482.1</a> or willfully and knowingly understates the volume or type of use of service or facilities in such report shall be liable to the local exchange telephone company covered by such report.
