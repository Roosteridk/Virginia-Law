# ยง 56-555.1 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Administration" means the U.S. Department of Transportation's Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Administration.</p><p>"Federal Act" means the federal Natural Gas Pipeline Safety Act of 1968, P.L. 90-481, as amended, including natural gas pipeline safety regulations set forth in 49 CFR Parts 191, 192, 193, and 199 and federal rules adopted pursuant thereto.</p><p>"Gas" means natural gas, flammable gas, or toxic or corrosive gas.</p><p>"Gas pipeline facility" includes a pipeline, a right of way, a facility, a building, or equipment used in transporting gas or treating gas during its transportation.</p><p>"Interstate gas pipeline facility" means a gas pipeline facility that is used to transport gas and is subject to jurisdiction of the Secretary under the federal Natural Gas Act, 15 U.S.C. ยง 717 et seq.</p><p>"Secretary" means the U.S. Secretary of Transportation.</p><p>2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0261'>261</a>.</p>
