# § 56-533 Regulation by State Corporation Commission.

<p>Every small investor-owned telephone utility shall be subject to Chapters 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-1/'>56-1</a> et seq.), 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-49/'>56-49</a> et seq.), 10 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-232/'>56-232</a> et seq.), 10.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-265.1/'>56-265.1</a> et seq.), 10.3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-265.14/'>56-265.14</a> et seq.) and 15 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-458/'>56-458</a> et seq.) of this title, except that the obligations of small investor-owned telephone utilities as to tariff filings shall be defined by this chapter. Small investor-owned telephone utilities shall also be subject to Chapters 3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-55/'>56-55</a> et seq.) and 4 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-76/'>56-76</a> et seq.) of this title when they have affiliated interests as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-76/'>56-76</a>.</p><p>1986, c. 337.</p>
