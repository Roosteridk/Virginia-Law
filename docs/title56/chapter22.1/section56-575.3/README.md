# § 56-575.3 Prerequisite for operation of a qualifying project.

<p>A. Any private entity seeking authorization under this chapter to develop or operate a qualifying project shall first obtain approval of the responsible public entity under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-575.4/'>56-575.4</a>. Such private entity may initiate the approval process by requesting approval pursuant to subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-575.4/'>56-575.4</a> or the responsible public entity may request proposals or invite bids pursuant to subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-575.4/'>56-575.4</a>.</p><p>B. Any facility, building, infrastructure or improvement included in a proposal as a part of a qualifying project shall be identified specifically or conceptually.</p><p>C. Upon receipt by the responsible public entity of a proposal submitted by a private entity initiating the approval process pursuant to subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-575.4/'>56-575.4</a>, the responsible public entity shall determine whether to accept such proposal for consideration in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-575.16/'>56-575.16</a>. If the responsible public entity determines not to accept for consideration the proposal submitted by the private entity pursuant to subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-575.4/'>56-575.4</a>, it shall return the proposal, together with all fees and accompanying documentation, to the private entity.</p><p>D. The responsible public entity may reject any proposal initiated by a private entity pursuant to subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-575.4/'>56-575.4</a> at any time. If the responsible public entity rejects a proposal initiated by a private entity that purports to develop specific cost savings, the public entity shall specify the basis for the rejection.</p><p>2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0571'>571</a>; 2003, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0292'>292</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1034'>1034</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0865'>865</a>; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0308'>308</a>.</p>
