# § 56-265.13:7 Regulation by State Corporation Commission.

<p>A. Every small water or sewer utility subject to this chapter shall be subject only to the following provisions: §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-234.4/'>56-234.4</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-236/'>56-236</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-239/'>56-239</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-245/'>56-245</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-245.1/'>56-245.1</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-246/'>56-246</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-247.1/'>56-247.1</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-248/'>56-248</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-249/'>56-249</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-249.2/'>56-249.2</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-250/'>56-250</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-254/'>56-254</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-256/'>56-256</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-265/'>56-265</a>, and Chapters 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-1/'>56-1</a> et seq.), 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-49/'>56-49</a> et seq.), 5 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-88/'>56-88</a> et seq.) and 10.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-265.1/'>56-265.1</a> et seq.) of Title 56. Small water or sewer utilities shall not be subject to Chapters 3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-55/'>56-55</a> et seq.) and 4 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-76/'>56-76</a> et seq.) of Title 56.</p><p>B. The Commission is authorized to promulgate any rules necessary to implement this chapter.</p><p>1986, c. 323; 1992, c. 376; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0313'>313</a>; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0048'>48</a>.</p>
