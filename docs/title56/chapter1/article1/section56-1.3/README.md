# ยง 56-1.3 Regulation of Voice-over-Internet protocol service.

<p>Notwithstanding any provision of law, except ยง <a href='/vacode/58.1-1730/'>58.1-1730</a>, to the contrary:</p><p>1. "Telecommunications service" and "telephone service" shall not include the provision of Voice-over-Internet protocol service for purposes of regulation by the Commission.</p><p>2. The Commission shall not have jurisdiction with respect to the regulation of Voice-over-Internet protocol service, including but not limited to the imposition of regulatory fees, certification requirements, and the filing or approval of tariffs.</p><p>3. Nothing herein shall be construed to either mandate or prohibit the payment of switched network access rates or other intercarrier compensation, if any, related to Voice-over-Internet protocol service, as may be determined by the Commission.</p><p>2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0691'>691</a>; 2018, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0532'>532</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0533'>533</a>.</p>
