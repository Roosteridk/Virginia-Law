# § 18.2-246.13 Civil penalties; penalties.

<p>A. In addition to any criminal penalties for violations of this article and except for civil penalties otherwise provided in this article, a first violation of any provision of this article shall be punishable by a civil penalty of no more than $1,000. A second or subsequent violation of any provision of this article shall be punishable by a civil penalty of no more than $10,000.</p><p>B. Any prospective consumer who knowingly submits a false certification under subdivision A 1 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.8/'>18.2-246.8</a> shall be subject to a civil penalty of no more than $5,000 for each such offense.</p><p>C. Any person failing to collect or remit to the Board or the Department of Taxation any tax required in connection with a delivery sale shall be assessed, in addition to any other applicable penalty, a civil penalty of no more than five times the retail value of the cigarettes involved.</p><p>D. Any civil penalty collected under this article shall be paid to the general fund.</p><p>E. Any person who fails to file the statement required by subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.11/'>18.2-246.11</a> and thereafter makes a delivery sale is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor and for any second or subsequent offense is guilty of a violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-498.3/'>18.2-498.3</a>.</p><p>F. Any person who knowingly and with the intent to defraud, mislead, or deceive makes a statement filed as required by subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.11/'>18.2-246.11</a> which is false is guilty of a violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-498.3/'>18.2-498.3</a>. Each such filed statement containing one or more false statements shall constitute a separate offense.</p><p>G. Any person who fails to make the report required by subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.11/'>18.2-246.11</a> is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor and for any second or subsequent offense is guilty of a violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-498.3/'>18.2-498.3</a>.</p><p>H. Any person who knowingly and with the intent to defraud, mislead, or deceive makes a materially false statement in any report required by subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.11/'>18.2-246.11</a> is guilty of a violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-498.3/'>18.2-498.3</a>. Each such report containing one or more false statements constitutes a separate offense.</p><p>2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1010'>1010</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0995'>995</a>; 2009, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0847'>847</a>; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0625'>625</a>.</p>
