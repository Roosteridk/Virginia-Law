# § 18.2-246.9 Disclosure requirements.

<p>The notice required under subdivision A 3 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.8/'>18.2-246.8</a> shall include:</p><p>1. A prominent and clearly legible statement that cigarette sales to consumers below the legal minimum purchase age are illegal;</p><p>2. A prominent and clearly legible statement that consists of one of the warnings set forth in section 4(a)(1) of the Federal Cigarette Labeling and Advertising Act (15 U.S.C. § 1333 (a)(1)) rotated on a quarterly basis;</p><p>3. A prominent and clearly legible statement that sales of cigarettes are restricted to those consumers who provide verifiable proof of age in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.8/'>18.2-246.8</a>; and</p><p>4. A prominent and clearly legible statement that cigarette sales are subject to tax under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1001/'>58.1-1001</a>, and an explanation of how such tax has been, or is to be, paid with respect to such delivery sale.</p><p>2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1010'>1010</a>.</p>
