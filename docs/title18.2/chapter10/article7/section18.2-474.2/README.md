# § 18.2-474.2 Bribery in correctional facilities; penalty.

<p>A. Any person who receives any pecuniary benefit or other consideration to act in violation of § <a href='/vacode/18.2-474/'>18.2-474</a> or <a href='/vacode/18.2-474.1/'>18.2-474.1</a> is guilty of bribery, punishable as a Class 4 felony.</p><p>B. Any law-enforcement officer as defined in § <a href='/vacode/9.1-101/'>9.1-101</a>, jail officer as defined in § <a href='/vacode/53.1-1/'>53.1-1</a>, or correctional officer as defined in § <a href='/vacode/53.1-1/'>53.1-1</a> who violates this section shall be decertified in accordance with § <a href='/vacode/15.2-1707/'>15.2-1707</a>, if applicable, and shall be forever ineligible for reemployment as a law-enforcement officer, jail officer, or correctional officer in the Commonwealth.</p><p>2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0289'>289</a>.</p>
