# § 18.2-46.3:3 Enhanced punishment for gang activity taking place in a gang-free zone; penalties.

<p>Any person who violates § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.2/'>18.2-46.2</a> (i) upon the property, including buildings and grounds, of any public or private elementary, secondary, or postsecondary school or institution of higher education; (ii) upon public property or any property open to public use within 1,000 feet of such school property; (iii) on any school bus as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-100/'>46.2-100</a>; or (iv) upon the property, including buildings and grounds, of any publicly owned or operated community center or any publicly owned or operated recreation center is guilty of a felony punishable as specified in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.2/'>18.2-46.2</a>, and shall be sentenced to a mandatory minimum term of imprisonment of two years to be served consecutively with any other sentence. A person who violates subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3/'>18.2-46.3</a> upon any property listed in this section is guilty of a Class 6 felony, except that any person 18 years of age or older who violates subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3/'>18.2-46.3</a> upon any property listed in this section, when such offense is committed against a juvenile, is guilty of a Class 5 felony. Any person who violates subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3/'>18.2-46.3</a> upon any property listed in this section is guilty of a Class 5 felony. It is a violation of this section if the person violated § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.2/'>18.2-46.2</a> or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3/'>18.2-46.3</a> on the property described in clauses (i) through (iii) regardless of where the person intended to commit such violation.</p><p>2005, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0764'>764</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0813'>813</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0364'>364</a>; 2013, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0761'>761</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0774'>774</a>.</p>
