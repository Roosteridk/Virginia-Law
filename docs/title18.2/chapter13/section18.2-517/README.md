# ยง 18.2-517 Venue for prosecution.

<p>For the purposes of venue, any violation of this chapter shall be considered to have been committed in any county or city:</p><p>1. In which any act was performed in furtherance of any course of conduct that violates this chapter;</p><p>2. That is the principal place of the enterprise in the Commonwealth;</p><p>3. In which any offender had control or possession of any proceeds of a violation of this chapter, or of any records, or any other material or objects, which were used in furtherance of a violation;</p><p>4. In which any offender resides; or</p><p>5. Any place of venue under Article 2 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-244/'>19.2-244</a> et seq.) of Chapter 15 of Title 19.2.</p><p>2004, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0883'>883</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0996'>996</a>.</p>
