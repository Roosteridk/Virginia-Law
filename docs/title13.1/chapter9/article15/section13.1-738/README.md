# § 13.1-738 After-acquired shares.

<p>A. A corporation may elect to withhold payment required by § <a href='/vacode/13.1-737/'>13.1-737</a> from any shareholder who was required to, but did not certify that beneficial ownership of all of the shareholder's shares for which appraisal rights are asserted was acquired before the date set forth in the appraisal notice sent pursuant to subdivision B 1 of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-734/'>13.1-734</a>.</p><p>B. If the corporation elected to withhold payment under subsection A, it shall, within 30 days after the form required by subdivision B 2 b of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-734/'>13.1-734</a> is due, notify all shareholders who are described in subsection A:</p><p>1. Of the information required by subdivision B 1 of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-737/'>13.1-737</a>;</p><p>2. Of the corporation's estimate of fair value pursuant to subdivision B 2 of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-737/'>13.1-737</a> and its offer to pay such value plus interest;</p><p>3. That they may accept the corporation's estimate of fair value plus interest in full satisfaction of their demands or demand for appraisal under § <a href='/vacode/13.1-739/'>13.1-739</a>;</p><p>4. That those shareholders who wish to accept such offer must so notify the corporation's secretary of their acceptance of the corporation's offer within 30 days after receiving the offer; and</p><p>5. That those shareholders who do not satisfy the requirements for demanding appraisal under § <a href='/vacode/13.1-739/'>13.1-739</a> shall be deemed to have accepted the corporation's offer.</p><p>C. Within 10 days after receiving a shareholder's acceptance pursuant to subsection B, the corporation shall pay in cash the amount it offered under subdivision B 2, plus interest, to each shareholder who agreed to accept the corporation's offer in full satisfaction of the shareholder's demand.</p><p>D. Within 40 days after delivering the notice described in subsection B, the corporation shall pay in cash the amount it offered to pay under subdivision B 2, plus interest, to each shareholder described in subdivision B 5.</p><p>1985, c. 522; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0765'>765</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0165'>165</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0734'>734</a>.</p>
