# § 13.1-733 Notice of intent to demand payment.

<p>A. If a corporate action specified in subsection A of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-730/'>13.1-730</a> is submitted to a vote at a shareholders' meeting, a shareholder who wishes to assert appraisal rights with respect to any class or series of shares:</p><p>1. Must deliver to the corporation's secretary before the vote is taken written notice of the shareholder's intent to demand payment if the proposed action is effectuated; and</p><p>2. Must not vote, or cause or permit to be voted, any shares of such class or series in favor of the proposed action.</p><p>B. If a corporate action specified in subsection A of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-730/'>13.1-730</a> is to be approved by shareholders by written consent, a shareholder who wishes to assert appraisal rights with respect to any class or series of shares:</p><p>1. Shall deliver to the corporation's secretary before the proposed action becomes effective written notice of the shareholder's intent to demand payment if the proposed action is effectuated, except that such written notice is not required if the notice required by subsection C of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-732/'>13.1-732</a> is given less than 25 days prior to the date such proposed action is effectuated; and</p><p>2. Shall not sign a consent in favor of the proposed action with respect to that class or series of shares.</p><p>C. If a corporate action specified in subsection A of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-730/'>13.1-730</a> does not require shareholder approval pursuant to subsection G of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-718/'>13.1-718</a>, a shareholder who wishes to assert appraisal rights with respect to any class or series of shares (i) shall deliver to the secretary of the corporation before the shares are purchased pursuant to the offer written notice of the shareholder's intent to demand payment if the proposed action is effectuated; and (ii) shall not tender, or cause or permit to be tendered, any shares of such class or series in response to such offer.</p><p>D. A shareholder who fails to satisfy the requirements of subsection A, B, or C is not entitled to payment under this article.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 13-85, 13.1-75, 13.1-78; 1956, c. 428; 1968, c. 733; 1972, c. 425; 1975, c. 500; 1984, c. 613; 1985, c. 522; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0765'>765</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0165'>165</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0782'>782</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0611'>611</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0734'>734</a>.</p>
