# § 13.1-616 Fees for filing documents or issuing certificates.

<p>The Commission shall charge and collect the following fees, except as provided in § <a href='/vacode/12.1-21.2/'>12.1-21.2</a>:</p><p>1. For filing of articles of conversion to convert a corporation to an eligible entity, the fee shall be $100.</p><p>2. For filing any one of the following, the fee shall be $25:</p><p>a. Articles of incorporation or domestication.</p><p>b. Articles of conversion to convert an eligible entity to a corporation.</p><p>c. Articles of amendment or restatement.</p><p>d. Articles of merger or share exchange.</p><p>e. Articles of correction.</p><p>f. Articles of ratification.</p><p>g. An application of a foreign corporation for a certificate of authority to transact business in the Commonwealth.</p><p>h. An application of a foreign corporation for an amended certificate of authority to transact business in the Commonwealth.</p><p>i. A copy of an amendment of the articles of incorporation of a foreign corporation holding a certificate of authority to transact business in the Commonwealth.</p><p>j. A copy of articles of merger of a foreign corporation holding a certificate of authority to transact business in the Commonwealth.</p><p>k. A copy of an instrument of conversion of a foreign corporation holding a certificate of authority to transact business in the Commonwealth.</p><p>l. An application to register or to renew the registration of a corporate name.</p><p>3. For filing any one of the following, the fee shall be $10:</p><p>a. An application to reserve or to renew the reservation of a corporate name.</p><p>b. A notice of transfer of a reserved corporate name.</p><p>c. An application for use of an indistinguishable name.</p><p>d. Articles of dissolution.</p><p>e. Articles of revocation of dissolution.</p><p>f. Articles of termination of corporate existence.</p><p>g. An application for a certificate of withdrawal of a foreign corporation.</p><p>h. A notice of release of a registered name.</p><p>4. For issuing a certificate pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/13.1-781/'>13.1-781</a>, the fee shall be $6.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 13-18, 13.1-123, 13.1-124.1; 1956, c. 428; 1958, c. 564; 1964, c. 551; 1972, c. 579; 1975, c. 500; 1981, c. 522; 1982, c. 460; 1984, c. 294; 1985, c. 522; 1988, c. 405; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0368'>368</a>; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0545'>545</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0001'>1</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0274'>274</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0765'>765</a>; 2007, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0771'>771</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0810'>810</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0130'>130</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0734'>734</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1226'>1226</a>.</p>
