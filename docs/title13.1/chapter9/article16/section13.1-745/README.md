# § 13.1-745 Effect of dissolution.

<p>A. A dissolved corporation continues its corporate existence but may not carry on any business except that appropriate to wind up and liquidate its business and affairs, including:</p><p>1. Collecting its assets;</p><p>2. Disposing of its properties that will not be distributed in kind to its shareholders;</p><p>3. Discharging or making provision for discharging its liabilities;</p><p>4. Making distributions of its remaining assets among its shareholders according to their interests; and</p><p>5. Doing every other act necessary to wind up and liquidate its business and affairs.</p><p>B. Dissolution of a corporation does not:</p><p>1. Transfer title to the corporation's property;</p><p>2. Prevent transfer of its shares or securities, although the authorization to dissolve may provide for closing the corporation's share transfer records;</p><p>3. Subject its directors to standards of conduct different from those prescribed in Article 9 (§ <a href='/vacode/13.1-673/'>13.1-673</a> et seq.);</p><p>4. Change (i) quorum or voting requirements for its board of directors or shareholders; (ii) provisions for selection, resignation, or removal of its directors or officers; or (iii) provisions for amending its bylaws;</p><p>5. Prevent commencement of a proceeding by or against the corporation in its corporate name;</p><p>6. Abate or suspend a proceeding pending by or against the corporation on the effective date of dissolution; or</p><p>7. Terminate the authority of the registered agent of the corporation.</p><p>C. A distribution in liquidation under this section may only be made by a dissolved corporation. For purposes of determining the shareholders entitled to receive a distribution in liquidation, the board of directors may fix a future date as a record date. If the board of directors does not fix a record date for the determination, the record date is the date the board of directors authorizes the distribution.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 13.1-83, 13.1-84; 1956, c. 428; 1985, c. 522; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0734'>734</a>.</p>
