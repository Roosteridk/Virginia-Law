# ยง 13.1-810.1 Number of members.

<p>A. For purposes of this Act, the following identified as a member in a corporation's current record of members constitutes one member:</p><p>1. Two or more persons who together have a single membership interest in the corporation;</p><p>2. A corporation, limited liability company, partnership, limited partnership, business trust, trust, estate, or other entity; or</p><p>3. The trustees, guardians, custodians, or other fiduciaries of a single trust, estate, or account.</p><p>B. For purposes of this Act, membership interests registered in substantially similar names constitute one member if it is reasonable to believe that the names represent the same person.</p><p>2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0925'>925</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0623'>623</a>.</p>
