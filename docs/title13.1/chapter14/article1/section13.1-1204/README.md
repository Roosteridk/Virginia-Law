# § 13.1-1204 Fees for filing documents and issuing certificates.

<p>The Commission shall charge and collect the following fees, except as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/12.1-21.2/'>12.1-21.2</a>: </p><p>1. For filing any one of the following, the fee shall be $100:</p><p>a. Articles of trust.</p><p>b. An application for registration as a foreign business trust.</p><p>c. Articles of domestication.</p><p>d. Articles of entity conversion.</p><p>2. For filing any one of the following, the fee shall be $25:</p><p>a. Articles of amendment.</p><p>b. Articles of restatement.</p><p>c. Articles of cancellation.</p><p>d. Articles of correction referred to in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1213/'>13.1-1213</a>, a copy of an amendment or a correction referred to in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1245/'>13.1-1245</a>, or an amended application for registration referred to in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1245/'>13.1-1245</a>, provided that an amended application shall not require a separate fee when it is filed with a copy of an amendment or a correction referred to in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1245/'>13.1-1245</a>.</p><p>e. A copy of an instrument of merger of a foreign business trust referred to in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1250/'>13.1-1250</a>.</p><p>f. Articles of merger.</p><p>g. Articles of trust surrender.</p><p>h. A copy of an instrument of entity conversion of a foreign business trust holding a certificate of registration to transact business in the Commonwealth.</p><p>i. An application for a certificate of cancellation of a foreign business trust.</p><p>3. For filing any one of the following, the fee shall be $10:</p><p>a. An application to reserve or to renew the reservation of a name for use by a domestic or foreign business trust.</p><p>b. A notice of the transfer of a name reserved for use by a domestic or foreign business trust.</p><p>4. For issuing a certificate pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1285/'>13.1-1285</a>, the fee shall be $6.</p><p>2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0621'>621</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0373'>373</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0274'>274</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0771'>771</a>; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0101'>101</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0130'>130</a>; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0025'>25</a>.</p>
