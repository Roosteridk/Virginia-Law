# ยง 13.1-1272 Entity conversion.

<p>A. A domestic business trust may become a domestic limited liability company pursuant to a plan of entity conversion that is approved by the domestic business trust in accordance with the provisions of this article.</p><p>B. A domestic limited liability company may become a domestic business trust pursuant to a plan of entity conversion that is approved by the limited liability company in accordance with the provisions of Article 15 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1081/'>13.1-1081</a> et seq.) of Chapter 12.</p><p>C. Unless otherwise provided for in Chapter 2.2 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.79/'>50-73.79</a> et seq.) of Title 50, a domestic partnership that has filed either a statement of partnership authority or a statement of registration as a registered limited liability partnership with the Commission that is not canceled may become a domestic business trust pursuant to a plan of entity conversion that is approved by the domestic partnership in accordance with the provisions of this article.</p><p>D. Unless otherwise provided for in Chapter 2.1 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.1/'>50-73.1</a> et seq.) of Title 50, a domestic limited partnership that has filed a certificate of limited partnership with the Commission that is not canceled may become a domestic business trust pursuant to a plan of entity conversion that is approved by the domestic limited partnership in accordance with the provisions of this article.</p><p>E. An other entity may become a domestic business trust pursuant to a plan of entity conversion that is approved by the other entity in accordance with the provisions of its governing instrument for amendments to the governing instrument.</p><p>2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0621'>621</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0288'>288</a>.</p>
