# ยง 13.1-1218 Existence.

<p>A. Except to the extent otherwise provided in this chapter, in the articles of trust or in the governing instrument of the business trust, a business trust:</p><p>1. Shall have perpetual existence; and</p><p>2. May not be terminated or revoked by a beneficial owner or other person except in accordance with the terms of the articles of trust or the governing instrument of the business trust.</p><p>B. Except to the extent otherwise provided in the articles of trust or in the governing instrument of a business trust, the death, incapacity, dissolution, termination, or bankruptcy of a beneficial owner shall not result in the termination or dissolution of a business trust.</p><p>C. In the event that a business trust does not have perpetual existence, a business trust is dissolved and its affairs shall be wound up in accordance with Article 8 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1234/'>13.1-1234</a> et seq.) of this chapter at the time or on the happening of events specified in the articles of trust or the governing instrument.</p><p>2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0621'>621</a>.</p>
