# ยง 13.1-1217 Restatement of articles of trust.

<p>A. Except to the extent otherwise provided in this chapter, in the articles of trust or in the governing instrument of the business trust, the sole trustee or a majority of the trustees may restate the articles of trust of a business trust at any time.</p><p>B. The restatement may include one or more amendments to the articles, including an amendment to delete the name and address of the initial registered agent or registered office, if a statement of change described in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1221/'>13.1-1221</a> is on file with the Commission.</p><p>C. A business trust restating its articles of trust shall file with the Commission articles of restatement setting forth:</p><p>1. The name of the business trust immediately prior to restatement;</p><p>2. Whether the restatement contains an amendment to the articles of trust;</p><p>3. The text of the restated articles of trust or amended and restated articles of trust;</p><p>4. The date of adoption of the articles of restatement; and</p><p>5. A statement that the restatement was adopted in accordance with the articles of trust and the governing instrument of the business trust.</p><p>D. If the Commission finds that the articles of restatement comply with the requirements of law and that all required fees have been paid, it shall issue a certificate of restatement. When the certificate of restatement is effective, the restated articles of trust or amended and restated articles of trust supersede the original articles of trust and all amendments to the original articles of trust.</p><p>E. The Commission may certify restated articles of trust or amended and restated articles of trust as the articles of trust currently in effect.</p><p>2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0621'>621</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0373'>373</a>; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0101'>101</a>.</p>
