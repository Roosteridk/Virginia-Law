# ยง 13.1-1099.19 Effect of merger.

<p>When a merger under ยง <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.16/'>13.1-1099.16</a> becomes effective, in addition to the effects stated in ยง <a href='/vacode/13.1-1073/'>13.1-1073</a>:</p><p>1. As provided in the plan of merger, each protected series of each merging company which was established before the merger:</p><p>a. Is a relocated protected series or continuing protected series; or</p><p>b. Is dissolved, wound up, and canceled;</p><p>2. Any protected series to be established as a result of the merger is established;</p><p>3. Any relocated protected series or continuing protected series is the same person without interruption as it was before the merger;</p><p>4. All property of a relocated protected series or continuing protected series continues to be vested in the protected series without transfer, reversion, or impairment;</p><p>5. All debts, obligations, and other liabilities of a relocated protected series or continuing protected series continue as debts, obligations, and other liabilities of the protected series;</p><p>6. Except as otherwise provided by law or the plan of merger, all the rights, privileges, immunities, powers, and purposes of a relocated protected series or continuing protected series remain in the protected series;</p><p>7. The new name of a relocated protected series may be substituted for the former name of the protected series in any pending action or proceeding;</p><p>8. If provided in the plan of merger:</p><p>a. A person becomes an associated member or protected series assignee of a relocated protected series or continuing protected series;</p><p>b. A person becomes an associated member of a protected series established by the surviving company as a result of the merger;</p><p>c. Any change in the rights or obligations of a person in the person's capacity as an associated member or protected series assignee of a relocated protected series or continuing protected series takes effect; and</p><p>d. Any consideration to be paid to a person that before the merger was an associated member or protected series assignee of a relocated protected series or continuing protected series is due; and</p><p>9. Any person that is a member of a relocated protected series becomes a member of the surviving company, if not already a member.</p><p>2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0636'>636</a>.</p>
