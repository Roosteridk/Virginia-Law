# § 13.1-1099.20 Application of § 13.1-1099.10 after merger.

<p>A. A creditor's right that existed under § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.10/'>13.1-1099.10</a> immediately before a merger under § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.16/'>13.1-1099.16</a> may be enforced after the merger in accordance with the following rules:</p><p>1. A creditor's right that existed immediately before the merger against the surviving company, a continuing protected series, or a relocated protected series continues without change after the merger.</p><p>2. A creditor's right that existed immediately before the merger against a non-surviving company:</p><p>a. May be asserted against an asset of the non-surviving company that vested in the surviving company as a result of the merger; and</p><p>b. Does not otherwise change.</p><p>3. Subject to subsection B, the following rules apply:</p><p>a. In addition to the remedy stated in subdivision 1, a creditor with a right under § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.10/'>13.1-1099.10</a> that existed immediately before the merger against a non-surviving company or a relocated protected series may assert the right against:</p><p>(1) An asset of the surviving company, other than an asset of the non-surviving company that vested in the surviving company as a result of the merger;</p><p>(2) An asset of a continuing protected series;</p><p>(3) An asset of a protected series established by the surviving company as a result of the merger;</p><p>(4) If the creditor's right was against an asset of the non-surviving company, an asset of a relocated protected series; or</p><p>(5) If the creditor's right was against an asset of a relocated protected series, an asset of a relocated protected series.</p><p>b. In addition to the remedy stated in subdivision 2, a creditor with a right that existed immediately before the merger against the surviving company or a continuing protected series may assert the right against:</p><p>(1) An asset of a relocated protected series; or</p><p>(2) An asset of a non-surviving company that vested in the surviving company as a result of the merger.</p><p>B. For the purposes of subdivision A 3 and subdivisions B 1a, B 2a, and B 3a of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.10/'>13.1-1099.10</a>, the incurrence date is deemed to be the date on which the merger becomes effective.</p><p>C. A merger under § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.16/'>13.1-1099.16</a> does not affect the manner in which § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.10/'>13.1-1099.10</a> applies to a liability incurred after the merger.</p><p>2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0636'>636</a>.</p>
