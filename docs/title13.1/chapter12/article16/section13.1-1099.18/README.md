# § 13.1-1099.18 Articles of merger.

<p>In a merger under § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.16/'>13.1-1099.16</a>, the articles of merger shall:</p><p>1. Comply with § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1072/'>13.1-1072</a> and include a plan of merger that complies with the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1099.17/'>13.1-1099.17</a>;</p><p>2. Be accompanied by the following records, each to become effective when the merger becomes effective:</p><p>a. For a protected series of a merging company being canceled as a result of the merger, a statement of designation cancellation;</p><p>b. For a protected series of a non-surviving company which after the merger will be a relocated protected series:</p><p>(1) A statement of relocation that contains the name of the non-surviving company and the name of the protected series before and after the merger; and</p><p>(2) A statement of protected series designation; and</p><p>c. For a protected series being established by the surviving company as a result of the merger, a statement of protected series designation; and</p><p>3. A statement presented with articles of merger pursuant to this section may be filed with the Commission without payment of the fee specified in § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1005/'>13.1-1005</a>.</p><p>2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0636'>636</a>.</p>
