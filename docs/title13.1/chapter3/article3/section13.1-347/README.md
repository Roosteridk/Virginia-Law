# § 13.1-347 Formation of worker cooperative; purpose.

<p>A. Any corporation incorporated under Article 3 (§ <a href='/vacode/13.1-618/'>13.1-618</a> et seq.) of Chapter 9 may elect to be governed as a worker cooperative in accordance with the provisions of this article by so stating in its articles of incorporation or articles of amendment filed in accordance with § <a href='/vacode/13.1-710/'>13.1-710</a>. The offering of an employee stock ownership plan governed by 26 U.S.C. § 401 by a corporation incorporated under Article 3 (§ <a href='/vacode/13.1-618/'>13.1-618</a> et seq.) of Chapter 9 to its employees shall not be considered an election to be governed as a worker cooperative.</p><p>B. A worker cooperative may be formed for any lawful purpose, provided that it shall be organized and shall conduct its business primarily for the mutual benefit of its members.</p><p>2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0673'>673</a>.</p>
