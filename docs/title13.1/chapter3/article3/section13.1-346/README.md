# ยง 13.1-346 Definitions.

<p>As used in this article:</p><p>"Collective reserve account" means an account on the corporate books representing the worker cooperative's entire net book value minus balances in any other equity accounts.</p><p>"Member" means an individual who has been accepted for membership in, and owns a membership share issued by, a worker cooperative.</p><p>"Membership fee" means an initial payment, if required by the articles of incorporation or bylaws of the worker cooperative, made by a worker to a worker cooperative as a condition of becoming a member.</p><p>"Patronage" means the amount of work performed for a worker cooperative, measured in accordance with criteria set forth in the articles of incorporation or bylaws of the worker cooperative.</p><p>"Worker" means an individual employed by a worker cooperative.</p><p>"Worker cooperative" means a corporation incorporated under the provisions of Article 3 (ยง <a href='/vacode/13.1-618/'>13.1-618</a> et seq.) of Chapter 9 that has elected to be governed by this article.</p><p>2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0673'>673</a>.</p>
