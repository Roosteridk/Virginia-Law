# ยง 20-170 Collaborative law participation agreement; requirements.

<p>A. A collaborative law participation agreement shall:</p><p>1. Be in a record;</p><p>2. Be signed by the parties;</p><p>3. State the parties' intention to resolve a collaborative matter through a collaborative law process under this chapter;</p><p>4. Describe the nature and scope of the matter;</p><p>5. Identify the collaborative lawyer who represents each party in the process; and</p><p>6. Contain a statement by each collaborative lawyer confirming the lawyer's representation of a party in the collaborative law process, which may be contained in a separate writing.</p><p>B. Parties may agree to include in a collaborative law participation agreement additional provisions not inconsistent with this chapter.</p><p>2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0346'>346</a>.</p>
