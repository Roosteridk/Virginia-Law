# § 27-4 Contract of county, city, or town to furnish fire protection.

<p>Any county, city, or town that operates firefighting equipment as provided for in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/27-15.2/'>27-15.2</a> and any county, city, or town mentioned in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/27-6.02/'>27-6.02</a> may contract with counties, cities, or towns in, adjacent to, or near such county, city, or town, including the District of Columbia, for fire protection in the manner provided for in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/27-2/'>27-2</a>.</p><p>1946, p. 609; Michie Suppl. 1946, § 2743m; 1948, p. 160; 1970, c. 187; 1991, c. 54; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0461'>461</a>; 2015, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0502'>502</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0503'>503</a>.</p>
