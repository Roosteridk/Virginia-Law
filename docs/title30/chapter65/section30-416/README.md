# ยง 30-416 (Expires July 1, 2024) Quorum; meetings; voting on recommendations.

<p>A majority of the members shall constitute a quorum. The meetings of the Commission shall be held at the call of the chairman or whenever the majority of the members so request.</p><p>No recommendation of the Commission shall be adopted if a majority of the Senate members or a majority of the House members appointed to the Commission (i) vote against the recommendation and (ii) vote for the recommendation to fail notwithstanding the majority vote of the Commission.</p><p>No recommendation of the Commission shall be adopted if the majority of the members representing Tribal Nations vote against the recommendations.</p><p>2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0788'>788</a>.</p>
