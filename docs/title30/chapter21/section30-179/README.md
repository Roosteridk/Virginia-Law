# § 30-179 (Effective until September 1, 2022) Powers and duties of the Council.

<p>The Council shall:</p><p>1. Furnish, upon request, advisory opinions or guidelines, and other appropriate information regarding the Freedom of Information Act (§ <a href='/vacode/2.2-3700/'>2.2-3700</a> et seq.) to any person or public body, in an expeditious manner;</p><p>2. Conduct training seminars and educational programs for the members and staff of public bodies and other interested persons on the requirements of the Freedom of Information Act (§ <a href='/vacode/2.2-3700/'>2.2-3700</a> et seq.);</p><p>3. Publish such educational materials as it deems appropriate on the provisions of the Freedom of Information Act (§ <a href='/vacode/2.2-3700/'>2.2-3700</a> et seq.);</p><p>4. Request from any public body such assistance, services, and information as will enable the Council to effectively carry out its responsibilities. Information provided to the Council by a public body shall not be released to any other party unless authorized by such public body;</p><p>5. Assist in the development and implementation of the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/2.2-3704.1/'>2.2-3704.1</a>;</p><p>6. Develop the public comment form for use by designated public bodies in accordance with subdivision D 6 of § <a href='/vacode/2.2-3708.2/'>2.2-3708.2</a>;</p><p>7. Develop an online public comment form to be posted on the Council's official public government website, as defined in § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3701/'>2.2-3701</a>, to enable any requester to comment on the quality of assistance provided to the requester by a public body; and</p><p>8. Report annually on or before December 1 of each year on its activities and findings regarding the Freedom of Information Act (§ <a href='/vacode/2.2-3700/'>2.2-3700</a> et seq.), including recommendations for changes in the law, to the General Assembly and the Governor. The annual report shall be published as a state document.</p><p>2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0917'>917</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0987'>987</a>, § 2.1-346.3; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2004, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0730'>730</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP1000'>1000</a>; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0694'>694</a>; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0645'>645</a>; 2018, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0055'>55</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0056'>56</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0354'>354</a>, 2022, c. <a href='https://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0325&221+ful+CHAP0325'>325</a>.</p>
