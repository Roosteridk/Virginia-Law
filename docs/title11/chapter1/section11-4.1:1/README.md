# ยง 11-4.1:1 Waiver of payment bond claims and contract claims; construction contracts.

<p>A subcontractor as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/43-1/'>43-1</a>, lower-tier subcontractor, or material supplier may not waive or diminish his right to assert payment bond claims or his right to assert claims for demonstrated additional costs in a contract in advance of furnishing any labor, services, or materials.
