# ยง 24.2-947.6 Filing schedule for candidates for office; November elections.

<p>A. Any candidate for any office to be filled at a November general election shall file the prescribed campaign finance reports as follows:</p><p>1. Not later than July 15 in a nonelection year for the period January 1 through June 30;</p><p>2. Not later than January 15 following a nonelection year for the period July 1 through December 31;</p><p>3. In an election year, not later than April 15 for the period January 1 through March 31 and pursuant to subdivisions 4 through 9 of this section;</p><p>4. Not later than the eighth day before the primary date complete through the twelfth day before the primary date;</p><p>5. Not later than July 15 complete through June 30;</p><p>6. Not later than September 15 complete through August 31;</p><p>7. Not later than October 15 complete through September 30;</p><p>8. Not later than the eighth day before the November election date complete through the twelfth day before the election date;</p><p>9. Not later than the thirtieth day after the November election date complete through the twenty-third day after the election date; and</p><p>10. Not later than January 15 following an election year complete through December 31, and then in accordance with subdivisions A 1 and A 2 or subdivisions A 3 through A 9, as appropriate, of this subsection until a final report is filed.</p><p>B. Any candidate, who was subject to the election year filing schedule set out in subdivisions A 3 through A 9 and who has not filed a final report, shall file reports in any subsequent election year for the same office in accordance with the election year filing schedule set out in subdivisions A 3 through A 9.</p><p>C.
