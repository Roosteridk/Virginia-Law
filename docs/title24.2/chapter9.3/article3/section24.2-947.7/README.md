# ยง 24.2-947.7 Filing schedule for candidates for office; May elections.

<p>A. Any candidate for election to a local office to be filled at a May general election shall file the prescribed campaign finance reports as follows:</p><p>1. Not later than July 15 in a nonelection year for the period January 1 through June 30;</p><p>2. Not later than January 15 following a nonelection year for the period July 1 through December 31;</p><p>3. For municipal primary candidates only, not later than the eighth day before the primary date complete through the twelfth day before the primary;</p><p>4. Not later than April 15 of the election year complete through March 31;</p><p>5. Not later than the eighth day before the election date complete through the twelfth day before the election date;</p><p>6. Not later than June 15 of the election year complete through June 10;</p><p>7. Not later than July 15 of the election year complete through June 30; and</p><p>8. Not later than the following January 15 complete through December 31, and then in accordance with subdivisions A 1 and A 2 or subdivisions A 3 through A 7, as appropriate, of this subsection until a final report is filed.</p><p>B. Any candidate, who was subject to the election year filing schedule set out in subdivisions A 3 through A 8 and who has not filed a final report, shall file reports in any subsequent election year for the same office in accordance with the election year filing schedule set out in subdivisions A 3 through A 8.</p><p>C.
