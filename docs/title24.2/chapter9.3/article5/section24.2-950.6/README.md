# ยง 24.2-950.6 Filing schedule for political party committees.

<p>A. Political party committees shall file the prescribed campaign finance reports in accordance with the applicable provisions of this section. The first filed report shall be complete for the entire period from the time the committee was organized or contributions were received.</p><p>B. The reporting requirements shall continue in effect for each committee until a final report is filed.</p><p>C. Political party committees shall file the prescribed campaign finance reports as follows:</p><p>1. Not later than April 15 complete from the preceding report through March 31;</p><p>2. Not later than July 15 complete from the preceding report through June 30;</p><p>3. Not later than October 15 complete from the preceding report through September 30; and</p><p>4.
