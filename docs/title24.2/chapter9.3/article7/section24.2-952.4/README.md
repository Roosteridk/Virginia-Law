# § 24.2-952.4 Filing schedule for inaugural committees.

<p>An inaugural committee shall file the prescribed reports of contributions and expenditures as follows:</p><p>1. Not later than March 15 immediately following the inauguration for all contributions and expenditures made prior to the preceding March 1;</p><p>2. Not later than July 15 of the inauguration year complete through June 30;</p><p>3. Not later than the following January 15 complete through December 31; and</p><p>4. Not later than January 15 complete through December 31 and annually thereafter until a final report is filed.</p><p>1991, cc. 474, 709, §§ 24.1-254.3, 24.1-924; 1993, c. 641, §§ 24.2-913, 24.2-924; 2006, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0787'>787</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0892'>892</a>.</p>
