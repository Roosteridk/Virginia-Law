# § 24.2-420.1 (Effective until October 1, 2022) Extended time for certain persons to register in person.

<p>A. Notwithstanding the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/24.2-416/'>24.2-416</a>, the following persons shall be entitled to register in person up to and including the day of the election:</p><p>1. Any member of a uniformed service of the United States, as defined in § <a href='/vacode/24.2-452/'>24.2-452</a>, who is on active duty;</p><p>2. Any person who resides temporarily outside of the United States; and</p><p>3. Any spouse or dependent residing with a person listed in subdivision 1 or 2 of this subsection.</p><p>The provisions of this subsection shall apply only to those persons who are otherwise qualified to register and who, by reason of such active duty or temporary overseas residency, either (i) are normally absent from the city or county in which they reside or (ii) have been absent from such city or county and returned to reside there during the twenty-eight days immediately preceding the election.</p><p>B. Notwithstanding the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/24.2-416/'>24.2-416</a>, any person who was on active duty as a member of a uniformed service as defined in § <a href='/vacode/24.2-452/'>24.2-452</a> and discharged from the uniformed service during the sixty days immediately preceding the election, and his spouse or dependent, shall be entitled to register, if otherwise qualified, in person up to and including the day of the election.</p><p>C. The Department shall prescribe procedures for the addition of persons registered under this section to the lists of registered voters.</p><p>1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0296'>296</a>; 2002, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0785'>785</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0819'>819</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0740'>740</a>.</p>
