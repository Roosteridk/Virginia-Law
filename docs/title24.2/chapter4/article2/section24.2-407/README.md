# § 24.2-407 Statement for persons receiving lists of persons registered or voting; penalties.

<p>Any person receiving lists pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-405/'>24.2-405</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-406/'>24.2-406</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-706/'>24.2-706</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-710/'>24.2-710</a> shall sign the following statement:</p><p>"I understand that the lists requested are the property of the State Board of Elections of the Commonwealth of Virginia, and I hereby state or agree, subject to felony penalties for making false statements pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-1016/'>24.2-1016</a>, that (i) I am a person authorized by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-405/'>24.2-405</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-406/'>24.2-406</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-706/'>24.2-706</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-710/'>24.2-710</a> of the Code of Virginia to receive a copy of the lists described; (ii) the lists will be used only for the purposes prescribed and for no other use; and (iii) I will not permit the use or copying of the lists by persons not authorized by the Code of Virginia to obtain them.</p><p>Signature of Purchaser ______________________________."</p><p>1970, c. 462, § 24.1-23; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 119; 1972, c. 620; 1973, c. 30; 1974, cc. 369, 428; 1975, c. 515; 1976, c. 616; 1978, c. 778; 1983, c. 348; 1993, c. 641; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0812'>812</a>.</p>
