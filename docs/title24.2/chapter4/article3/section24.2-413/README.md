# § 24.2-413 Accessible registration locations.

<p>The office of the general registrar, and each agency, business, and establishment set for registration pursuant to §§ <a href='/vacode/24.2-411.2/'>24.2-411.2</a> and <a href='/vacode/24.2-411.3/'>24.2-411.3</a> and subsection B of § <a href='/vacode/24.2-412/'>24.2-412</a> shall be accessible as required by the provisions of the Virginians with Disabilities Act (§ <a href='/vacode/51.5-1/'>51.5-1</a> et seq.), the Voting Accessibility for the Elderly and Handicapped Act (52 U.S.C. § 20101 et seq.), and the Americans with Disabilities Act relating to public services (42 U.S.C. § 12131 et seq.). The Department shall provide instructions to the Department of Motor Vehicles, state-designated voter registration agencies, local electoral boards, and general registrars to assist them in complying with the requirements of the Acts.</p><p>In the selection of additional registration sites as provided in § <a href='/vacode/24.2-412/'>24.2-412</a>, consideration shall be given to accessibility so that a reasonable number of accessible sites are provided and the requirements of the above cited Acts are met.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 24-52, 24-52.1, 24-55, 24-61, 24-65, 24-66, 24-118.1; 1954, c. 691; 1962, c. 475; 1964, c. 608; 1968, cc. 97, 141; 1970, c. 462, § 24.1-43; 1973, c. 30; 1974, c. 428; 1975, c. 515; 1976, c. 12; 1978, c. 778; 1981, c. 425; 1982, c. 290; 1983, c. 511; 1984, c. 480; 1985, c. 197; 1986, c. 558; 1988, c. 528; 1993, c. 641; 1996, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0072'>72</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0073'>73</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0740'>740</a>; 2020, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0908'>908</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0909'>909</a>.</p>
