# § 24.2-646.1 Permitted use of paper ballots.

<p>The official paper ballot shall be used by a voter to cast his vote only in one of the following circumstances:</p><p>1. The official paper ballot is the only ballot in use in the precinct.</p><p>2. The official paper ballot is used by voters voting outside of the polling place pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/24.2-649.1/'>24.2-649.1</a>.</p><p>3. The voter is casting a provisional ballot.</p><p>4. The voter is provided an official paper ballot or copy thereof pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/24.2-642/'>24.2-642</a> when voting equipment is inoperable or otherwise unavailable.</p><p>5. The official absentee paper ballot voted in accordance with (§ <a href='/vacode/24.2-700/'>24.2-700</a> et seq.).</p><p>6. The voter is provided an official paper ballot for a presidential election pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/24.2-402/'>24.2-402</a> or for federal elections pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/24.2-453/'>24.2-453</a>.</p><p>2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0492'>492</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0313'>313</a>; 2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0163'>163</a>.</p>
