# § 24.2-602 Exemption for ballots and election materials from certain purchasing and procurement requirements.

<p>The provisions of Article 3, Division of Purchases and Supply, (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1109/'>2.2-1109</a> et seq.) of Chapter 11 of Title 2.2 and of Articles 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4300/'>2.2-4300</a> et seq.), 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4303/'>2.2-4303</a> et seq.), 3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4343/'>2.2-4343</a> et seq.), and 5 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4357/'>2.2-4357</a> et seq.) of Chapter 43, Virginia Public Procurement Act, of Title 2.2 shall not apply to contracts for equipment, software, services, the printing of ballots or statements of results, or other materials essential to the conduct of the election. The provisions of Articles 4 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4347/'>2.2-4347</a> et seq.) and 6 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4367/'>2.2-4367</a> et seq.) of Chapter 43, Virginia Public Procurement Act, of Title 2.2 shall apply to such contracts.</p><p>1980, c. 639, § 24.1-113.1; 1981, c. 425; 1982, c. 647; 1992, c. 105; 1993, c. 641; 2004, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0993'>993</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP1010'>1010</a>.</p>
