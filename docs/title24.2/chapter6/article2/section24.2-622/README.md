# § 24.2-622 Unofficial sample ballots.

<p>Sample ballots not authorized by electoral boards and provided by electoral boards or general registrars to precincts pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/24.2-641/'>24.2-641</a> are permitted to be printed and circulated, which includes publication in newspapers or on the Internet.</p><p>Such sample ballots shall not be printed on white paper and shall include on their face the words "sample ballot" in a font size no smaller than 24 point.</p><p>All sample ballots, excepting those official sample ballots authorized by electoral boards and provided by electoral boards or general registrars to precincts pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/24.2-641/'>24.2-641</a>, are advertisements for purposes of Chapter 9.5 (§ <a href='/vacode/24.2-955/'>24.2-955</a> et seq.). Voters may take sample ballots into the voting booth or enclosure, but shall not give, tender, or exhibit such sample ballot to any person, other than an assistant designated under § <a href='/vacode/24.2-649/'>24.2-649</a>, while inside the polling place or within the prohibited area designated by § <a href='/vacode/24.2-604/'>24.2-604</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 24-240; 1970, c. 462, § 24.1-122; 1974, c. 428; 1979, c. 265; 1993, c. 641; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0487'>487</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1015'>1015</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0370'>370</a>; 2006, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0787'>787</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0892'>892</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0283'>283</a>.</p>
