# § 19.2-368.2 Definitions.

<p>For the purpose of this chapter:</p><p>"Claimant" means the person filing a claim pursuant to this chapter.</p><p>"Commission" means the Virginia Workers' Compensation Commission.</p><p>"Crime" means an act committed by any person in the Commonwealth of Virginia which would constitute a crime as defined by the Code of Virginia or at common law. However, no act involving the operation of a motor vehicle which results in injury shall constitute a crime for the purpose of this chapter unless the injuries (i) were intentionally inflicted through the use of such vehicle or (ii) resulted from a violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-51.4/'>18.2-51.4</a> or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-266/'>18.2-266</a> or from a felony violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-894/'>46.2-894</a>.</p><p>"Family," when used with reference to a person, means (i) any person related to such person within the third degree of consanguinity or affinity, (ii) any person residing in the same household with such person, or (iii) a spouse.</p><p>"Sexual abuse" means sexual abuse as defined in subdivision 6 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-67.10/'>18.2-67.10</a> and acts constituting rape, sodomy, object sexual penetration or sexual battery as defined in Article 7 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-61/'>18.2-61</a> et seq.) of Chapter 4 of Title 18.2.</p><p>"Victim" means a person who suffers personal physical injury or death as a direct result of a crime including a person who is injured or killed as a result of foreign terrorism or who suffers personal emotional injury as a direct result of being the subject of a violent felony offense as defined in subsection C of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-805/'>17.1-805</a>, or stalking as described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-60.3/'>18.2-60.3</a>, or attempted robbery or abduction.</p><p>1976, c. 605;
