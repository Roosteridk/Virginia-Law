# § 19.2-386.15 Seizure of property used in connection with or derived from terrorism.

<p>A. The following property shall be subject to lawful seizure by any law-enforcement officer charged with enforcing the provisions of Article 2.2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.4/'>18.2-46.4</a> et seq.) of Chapter 4 of Title 18.2: all moneys or other property, real or personal, together with any interest or profits derived from the investment of such money and used in substantial connection with an act of terrorism as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.4/'>18.2-46.4</a>.</p><p>B. All seizures and forfeitures under this section shall be governed by the procedures contained in Chapter 22.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-386.1/'>19.2-386.1</a> et seq.) of this title.</p><p>2002, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0588'>588</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0623'>623</a>, § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.9/'>18.2-46.9</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0995'>995</a>.</p>
