# § 19.2-386.26 Seizure and forfeiture of drug paraphernalia.

<p>All drug paraphernalia as defined in Article 1.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-247/'>18.2-247</a> et seq.) of Chapter 7 of Title 18.2 shall be forfeited to the Commonwealth and may be seized and disposed of in the same manner as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-386.23/'>19.2-386.23</a>, subject to the rights of an innocent lienor, to be recognized as under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-386.8/'>19.2-386.8</a>.</p><p>1981, c. 598, § 18.2-265.4; 1993, c. 866; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0995'>995</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0283'>283</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0756'>756</a>.</p>
