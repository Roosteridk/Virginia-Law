# § 19.2-386.33 Forfeiture of money, etc., derived from violation of §§ 2.2-3103 through 2.2-3112.

<p>In addition to any other fine or penalty provided by law, any money or other thing of value derived by an officer or employee from a violation of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3103/'>2.2-3103</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3112/'>2.2-3112</a> shall be forfeited, in accordance with the procedures contained in Chapter 22.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-386.1/'>19.2-386.1</a> et seq.). If the thing of value received by the officer or employee in violation of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3103/'>2.2-3103</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3112/'>2.2-3112</a> increases in value between the time of the violation and the time of discovery of the violation, the greater value shall determine the amount of forfeiture.</p><p>2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0283'>283</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0756'>756</a>.</p>
