# § 19.2-80.2 Duty of arresting officer; providing magistrate or court with criminal history information.

<p>In any case in which an officer proceeds under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-76/'>19.2-76</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-80/'>19.2-80</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-82/'>19.2-82</a>, such officer shall, to the extent possible, obtain and provide the magistrate or court with the arrested person's criminal history information prior to any proceeding under Article 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-119/'>19.2-119</a> et seq.) of Chapter 9 of this title. A pretrial services agency established pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-152.2/'>19.2-152.2</a> may, in lieu of the arresting officer, provide the criminal history to the magistrate or court.</p><p>1999, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0829'>829</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0846'>846</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0133'>133</a>.</p>
