# § 19.2-46.2 Full-time magistrates; certification for retirement coverage.

<p>The Committee on District Courts shall certify to the director of the Virginia Retirement System the names of those magistrates serving on a regular full-time basis. Certification by the Committee shall qualify a magistrate as a state employee, for purposes of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-124.3/'>51.1-124.3</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-152/'>51.1-152</a> of the Virginia Retirement System (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-124.1/'>51.1-124.1</a> et seq.), effective on the date given in the certificate as the date on which such magistrate first served on a regular full-time basis on or after January 1, 1974.</p><p>1974, c. 353, § 14.1-44.2:1; 1998, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0872'>872</a>.</p>
