# § 19.2-392.10 (For contingent effective date see Acts 2021, Sp. Sess. I, cc. 524 and 542) Process for automatic sealing of offenses resulting in acquittal, nolle prosequi, or dismissal.

<p>A. On at least a monthly basis, the Executive Secretary of the Supreme Court and any circuit court clerk who maintains a case management system that interfaces with the Department of State Police under subsection B1 of § <a href='/vacode/17.1-502/'>17.1-502</a> shall provide an electronic list of all offenses in such case management system to the Department of State Police that were ordered to be automatically sealed pursuant to §§ <a href='/vacode/19.2-392.8/'>19.2-392.8</a> and <a href='/vacode/19.2-392.9/'>19.2-392.9</a>.</p><p>B. Upon receipt of the electronic lists under subsection A, the Department of State Police shall proceed as set forth in § <a href='/vacode/19.2-392.13/'>19.2-392.13</a>.</p><p>2021, Sp. Sess. I, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0524'>524</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0542'>542</a>.</p>
