# ยง 66-12 Definitions.

<p>Unless a different meaning clearly appears from the context, as used in this title:</p><p>"Board" or "State Board" means the Board of Juvenile Justice;</p><p>"Child" means any natural person under eighteen years of age;</p><p>"Department" means the Department of Juvenile Justice;</p><p>"Director" means the Director of Juvenile Justice.</p><p>1989, c. 733.</p>
