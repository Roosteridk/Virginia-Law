# ยง 66-25.6 Board shall promulgate regulations; local school board exemption.

<p>A. The Board shall make, adopt and promulgate regulations governing the following aspects of private management and operation of juvenile correctional facilities:</p><p>1. Contingency plans for state operation of a contractor-operated facility in the event of a termination of the contract;</p><p>2. Use of physical force and mechanical restraint by the contractors' security personnel;</p><p>3. Methods of monitoring a contractor-operated facility by the Department or the Board;</p><p>4. Public access to a contractor-operated facility; and</p><p>5. Such other regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.</p><p>B. Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to require local school boards to provide educational services to juveniles while committed to a state juvenile correctional facility.</p><p>1996, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0795'>795</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0942'>942</a>.</p>
