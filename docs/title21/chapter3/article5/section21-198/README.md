# ยง 21-198 Purchase by commission.

<p>The commission is authorized, out of any funds available therefor, to purchase any bonds of the commission at a price not more than the redemption price thereof on the next succeeding redemption date or, if such bonds be not redeemable, the principal amount thereof, together with, in either case, interest accrued to the date of purchase.</p><p>1938, p. 521; 1942, p. 606; Michie Code 1942, ยง 1560oo.</p>
