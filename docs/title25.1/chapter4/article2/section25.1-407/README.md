# § 25.1-407 Optional moving expense allowance for persons displaced from dwelling.

<p>Any displaced person eligible for payments under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-406/'>25.1-406</a> who is displaced from a dwelling may elect to accept the payments authorized by this section in lieu of the payments authorized by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-406/'>25.1-406</a>, which displaced person so electing shall receive a moving expense allowance of an amount determined according to a schedule established by the state agency. The acceptance of the payment authorized by this section shall be in lieu of any payment under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-406/'>25.1-406</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 33-75.04, 33.1-132.4; 1970, c. 40, § 25-239; 1972, c. 738; 1989, c. 714; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0851'>851</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0940'>940</a>.</p>
