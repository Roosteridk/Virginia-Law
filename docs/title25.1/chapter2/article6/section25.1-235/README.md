# § 25.1-235 Compensation of commissioners or jurors.

<p>The commissioners or jurors summoned shall, for every day or portion thereof they may be employed in the performance of their duties, receive an allowance in the amount prescribed in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-618/'>17.1-618</a> as compensation for their attendance, travel and other costs, to be paid by the petitioner.</p><p>Code 1919, § 4366; 1928, p. 335; 1948, p. 169; Code 1950, § 25-15; 1962, c. 426, § 25-46.22; 1973, c. 26; 1974, c. 346; 1976, c. 308; 1982, c. 566; 1993, c. 137; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1029'>1029</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0940'>940</a>; 2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0586'>586</a>; 2010, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0177'>177</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0835'>835</a>.</p>
