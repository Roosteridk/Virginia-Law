# ยง 22.1-1 (For Expiration Date, see 2022 Acts cc. 549, 550, cl. 2) Definitions.

<p>As used in this title, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Board" or "State Board" means the Board of Education.</p><p>"Department" means the Department of Education.</p><p>"Division superintendent" means the division superintendent of schools of a school division.</p><p>"Elementary" includes kindergarten.</p><p>"Elementary and secondary" and "elementary or secondary" include elementary, middle, and high school grades.</p><p>"Governing body" or "local governing body" means the board of supervisors of a county, council of a city, or council of a town, responsible for appropriating funds for such locality, as the context may require.</p><p>"Middle school" means separate schools for early adolescents and the middle school grades that might be housed at elementary or high schools.</p><p>"Parent" or "parents" means any parent, guardian, legal custodian, or other person having control or charge of a child.</p><p>"Person of school age" means a person who will have reached his fifth birthday on or before September 30 of the school year and who has not reached twenty years of age on or before August 1 of the school year.</p><p>"School board" means the school board that governs a school division.</p><p>"Superintendent" means the Superintendent of Public Instruction.</p><p>1980, c. 559; 1985, c. 407; 1991, c. 178; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0852'>852</a>; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0629'>629</a>; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0828'>828</a>; 2020, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0860'>860</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0861'>861</a>.</p>
