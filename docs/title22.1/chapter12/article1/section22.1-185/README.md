<p>The governing body of any county, city or town may expend funds for the construction and maintenance at points on school bus routes of such shelters, platforms or other structures as it may deem necessary or convenient for the protection and comfort of children of school age who go to such points to meet school buses.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 22-282; 1980, c. 559.</p>
