# § 22.1-263 Violation constitutes misdemeanor.

<p>Any person violating the provisions of either § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/'>22.1-254</a>, except for clause (ii) of subsection A, §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-255/'>22.1-255</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-258/'>22.1-258</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-267/'>22.1-267</a>, or the parental responsibility provisions relating to compulsory school attendance included in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-279.3/'>22.1-279.3</a>, shall be guilty of a Class 3 misdemeanor. Upon a finding that a person knowingly and willfully violated any provision of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/'>22.1-254</a>, except for clause (ii) of subsection A, or any provision of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-255/'>22.1-255</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-258/'>22.1-258</a>, or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-267/'>22.1-267</a> and that such person has been convicted previously of a violation of any provision of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/'>22.1-254</a>, except for clause (ii) of subsection A, or any provision of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-255/'>22.1-255</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-258/'>22.1-258</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-267/'>22.1-267</a>, such person shall be guilty of a Class 2 misdemeanor.</p><p>Code 1950, § 22-275.5; 1959, Ex. Sess., c. 72; 1976, c. 283; 1980, c. 559; 1990, c. 797; 1991, c. 295; 1996, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0891'>891</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0964'>964</a>; 1999, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0488'>488</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0526'>526</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0552'>552</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0573'>573</a>.</p>
