# § 22.1-254.2 Testing for high school equivalency; eligibility; guidelines.

<p>A. The Board of Education shall establish a program of testing for high school equivalency through which a person may pass a high school equivalency examination approved by the Board of Education through which persons may earn a high school equivalency certificate or may earn a diploma as provided in subsection F of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-253.13:4/'>22.1-253.13:4</a>. The following persons may participate in the testing program:</p><p>1. Persons who are at least 18 years of age and not enrolled in public school or not otherwise meeting the school attendance requirements set forth in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/'>22.1-254</a>;</p><p>2. Persons 16 years of age or older who have been instructed by their parents in their home pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254.1/'>22.1-254.1</a> and who have completed such home school instruction;</p><p>3. Persons who have been excused from school attendance pursuant to subsections B and D of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/'>22.1-254</a>;</p><p>4. Persons for whom an individual student alternative education plan has been granted pursuant to subsection E of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/'>22.1-254</a>;</p><p>5. Persons 16 through 18 years of age who are housed in adult correctional facilities and who are actively pursuing a passing score on a high school equivalency examination approved by the Board of Education but who are not enrolled in an individual student alternative education plan pursuant to subsection E of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/'>22.1-254</a>;</p><p>6. Persons 16 years of age or older who have been expelled from school pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-277.06/'>22.1-277.06</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-277.08/'>22.1-277.08</a>; and</p><p>7. Persons required by court order to participate in the testing program.</p><p>Under no circumstances shall persons under the age of 16 be eligible for the testing program.</p><p>B. From such funds as may be appropriated for this purpose, local school boards shall implement programs of preparation and testing for high school equivalency consistent with guidelines to be developed by the Board of Education. Such guidelines shall include a provision that allows preparatory and testing programs to be offered jointly by two or more school boards.</p><p>1989, c. 225; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0458'>458</a>; 1999, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0488'>488</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0552'>552</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0688'>688</a>; 2004, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0251'>251</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0939'>939</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0955'>955</a>; 2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0335'>335</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0605'>605</a>; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0084'>84</a>.</p>
