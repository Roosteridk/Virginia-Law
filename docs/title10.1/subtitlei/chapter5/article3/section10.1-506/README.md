# ยง 10.1-506 Power to create new districts and to relocate or define district boundaries; composition of districts.

<p>A. The Board shall have the power to (i) create a new district from territory not previously within an existing district, (ii) merge or divide existing districts, (iii) transfer territory from an existing district to another district, (iv) modify or create a district by a combination of the above and (v) relocate or define the boundaries of soil and water conservation districts in the manner hereinafter prescribed.</p><p>B. An incorporated town within any county having a soil and water conservation district shall be a part of that district. If a town lies within the boundaries of more than one county, it shall be considered to be wholly within the county in which the larger portion of the town lies.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 21-2; 1956, c. 654; 1970, c. 480, ยง 21-12.1; 1988, c. 891.</p>
