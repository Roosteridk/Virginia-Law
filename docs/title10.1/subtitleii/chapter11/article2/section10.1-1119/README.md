# ยง 10.1-1119 Preservation of evidence as to conserving forest supply; reports to General Assembly; publications.

<p>The State Forester shall preserve all evidence taken by him with reference to conserving the forests of the Commonwealth and the methods best adapted to accomplish such object. He shall report his actions, conclusions and recommendations to each session of the General Assembly and from time to time publish for public distribution, in bulletin or other form, such conclusions and recommendations as may be of immediate public interest.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 10-41; 1984, c. 750; 1986, c. 567; 1988, c. 891.</p>
