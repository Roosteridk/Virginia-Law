# § 17.1-275.6 Fees collected from court reporter fund.

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, in any court in which electronic devices are used for recording testimony, a sum not to exceed twenty dollars for each day or part of a day of the trial shall be paid by the clerk, from the court reporter fund as set forth in §§ [17.1-275.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/), [17.1-275.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/) and [17.1-275.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.3/), or a sum not to exceed five dollars for each day or part of a day of the trial of a case wherein costs are assessed pursuant to § [17.1-275.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/) shall be paid by the clerk, from the court reporter fund as set forth in § [17.1-275.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/), into a special fund to be used for the purpose of repairing, replacing or supplementing such electronic devices or, if a sufficient amount is available, to pay the purchase price of such devices in whole or in part. For the purpose of this section, repairing shall include maintenance and service contracts. Fees collected under this article shall be retained locally and shall not be subject to the provisions of § [17.1-286](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-286/).
