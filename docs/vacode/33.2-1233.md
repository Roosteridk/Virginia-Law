# § 33.2-1233 Penalty for violation of § 33.2-1232.

Any person who violates any of the provisions of § [33.2-1232](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-1232/) shall be subject to a fine not to exceed $10 for each offense, and it shall be deemed a separate offense for the same person to erect, or permit to be erected, a similar sign at each of two or more places.

Code 1950, § 33-325; 1970, c. 322, § 33.1-380; 2014, c. [805](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805).
