# § 36-132.1 Commission on Local Government.

The Department shall include the Commission on Local Government, which shall exercise the powers and duties described in §§ [15.2-1301](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1301/) and [15.2-2303.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2303.2/) and Chapters 29 (§ [15.2-2900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2900/) et seq.), 32 (§ [15.2-3200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-3200/) et seq.), 33 (§ [15.2-3300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-3300/) et seq.), 34 (§ [15.2-3400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-3400/) et seq.), 35 (§ [15.2-3500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-3500/) et seq.), 36 (§ [15.2-3600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-3600/) et seq.), 38 (§ [15.2-3800](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-3800/) et seq.), 39 (§ [15.2-3900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-3900/) et seq.), 40 (§ [15.2-4000](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-4000/) et seq.), and 41 (§ [15.2-4100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-4100/) et seq.) of Title 15.2 and § [30-19.03](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-19.03/).
