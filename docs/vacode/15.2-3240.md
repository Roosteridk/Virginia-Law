# § 15.2-3240 Transfer of registration records.

Upon entry of the court order under § [15.2-3238](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-3238/), the registration records of voters residing within the territory shall be transferred, and the appropriate notice given, in accordance with § [24.2-114](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-114/).

Code 1950, § 15-157; 1962, c. 623, § 15.1-1063; 1997, c. 587.
