# ยง 46.2-2150 Required certificates of fitness.

No household goods carrier, unless otherwise exempted, shall engage in intrastate operations on any highway within the Commonwealth without first having obtained from the Department a certificate of fitness authorizing such operation.

2001, c. [596](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0596); 2011, cc. [881](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0881), [889](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0889).
