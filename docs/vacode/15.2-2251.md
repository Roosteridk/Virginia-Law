# § 15.2-2251 Local planning commission shall prepare and recommend ordinance; notice and hearing on ordinance.

In every locality the local planning commission shall prepare and recommend the subdivision ordinance and transmit it to the governing body. The governing body of every locality shall approve and adopt a subdivision ordinance only after notice has been published, and a public hearing held, in accordance with § [15.2-2204](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2204/).

Code 1950, §§ 15-782, 15-967.5; 1962, c. 407, § 15.1-470; 1975, c. 641; 1997, c. 587.
