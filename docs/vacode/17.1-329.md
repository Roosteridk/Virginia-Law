# ยง 17.1-329 Disposition of fees of Clerk of Supreme Court.

The Clerk of the Supreme Court shall keep an accurate account of all fees and costs collected by him and shall make monthly deposits thereof in a depository, or depositories, approved by the State Treasurer, to the credit of the Commonwealth of Virginia. A report of each deposit shall be promptly submitted to the State Treasurer, and detailed reports thereof shall be made monthly to the State Comptroller. Except as provided in ยง [17.1-328](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-328/), all such fees and costs shall be credited by the Comptroller to the general fund of the state treasury.
