# § 19.2-22 Same; arrest without a warrant.

A person arrested without a warrant by any conservator of the peace or other law-enforcement officer for any of the acts set forth in § [19.2-19](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-19/) committed in the presence of such conservator of the peace or law-enforcement officer, shall be brought forthwith before a magistrate or judge, and proceedings shall be had in accordance with §§ [19.2-20](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-20/) and [19.2-21](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-21/).

1975, c. 495.
