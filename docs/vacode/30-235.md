# § 30-235 (Contingent expiration date -- see notes) Compensation and expenses.

Legislative members of the Commission shall receive such compensation as provided in § [30-19.12](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-19.12/), and nonlegislative citizen members shall receive such compensation as provided in § [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/) for the performance of their duties. All members shall be reimbursed for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2825/). However, all such compensation and expenses shall be paid from existing appropriations to the Commission.

2004, cc. [992](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0992), [1015](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP1015).
