B. The Commission shall mail to each domestic and foreign business trust that fails to pay its annual registration fee within the time prescribed in ยง [13.1-1252](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1252/) a notice of assessment of the penalty imposed herein and of the impending cancellation of its existence or certificate of registration, as the case may be.
