# § 64.2-1408 Circuit court may exercise same powers in suit to enforce or administer trust.

A circuit court may exercise all the powers conferred by §§ [64.2-1405](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1405/), [64.2-1406](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1406/), [64.2-1407](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1407/), and [64.2-1412](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1412/) in a suit pending to enforce or administer the trust.

Code 1919, § 6302; Code 1950, § 26-52; 2012, c. [614](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614).
