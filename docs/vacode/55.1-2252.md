# § 55.1-2252 Penalties.

A. Any person who willfully violates any of the provisions of § [55.1-2217](/vacode/55.1-2217/), [55.1-2218](/vacode/55.1-2218/), [55.1-2219](/vacode/55.1-2219/), [55.1-2220](/vacode/55.1-2220/), [55.1-2221](/vacode/55.1-2221/), [55.1-2229](/vacode/55.1-2229/), [55.1-2233](/vacode/55.1-2233/), or [55.1-2238](/vacode/55.1-2238/), or any order issued pursuant to §§ [55.1-2247](/vacode/55.1-2247/) through [55.1-2250](/vacode/55.1-2250/) is guilty of a Class 5 felony.

Any person who willfully violates any of the provisions of § [55.1-2226](/vacode/55.1-2226/), [55.1-2228](/vacode/55.1-2228/), or [55.1-2244](/vacode/55.1-2244/) or any order issued pursuant to §§ [55.1-2247](/vacode/55.1-2247/) through [55.1-2250](/vacode/55.1-2250/) regarding a violation of § [55.1-2226](/vacode/55.1-2226/), [55.1-2228](/vacode/55.1-2228/), or [55.1-2244](/vacode/55.1-2244/) is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.

Each violation shall be deemed a separate offense.

B. Any developer, member, agent or affiliate of any developer of time-shares registered pursuant to § [55.1-2241](/vacode/55.1-2241/), or any reseller, who violates any provision of this chapter or regulations promulgated pursuant to this chapter, and who is not criminally prosecuted, may be subject to a civil penalty. If it has been determined by the Board upon or after a hearing that a respondent has violated this chapter or the Board's rules and regulations, the Board shall proceed to determine the amount of the civil penalty for such violation, which shall not exceed $2,000 for each violation. Such penalty may be sued for and recovered in the name of the Commonwealth.
