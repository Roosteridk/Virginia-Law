# ยง 44-146.40 Joint emergency planning committee; certain localities.

Any joint emergency planning committee serving Fairfax County and the City of Fairfax shall have the authority to require any facility within its emergency planning district to submit the information required and participate in the emergency planning provided for in Subtitle A of Title 3 of Public Law 99-499. For the purposes of this section, "facility" shall include any development or installation having an aggregate storage capacity of at least one million gallons of oil as defined in ยง [62.1-44.34:10](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.34:10/), or the potential for a sudden release of 10,000 pounds or more of any other flammable liquid or gas not exempt from the provisions of ยง 327 of Title 3 of Public Law 99-499. This requirement shall not occur until after public notice and the opportunity to comment. The committee shall notify the facility owner or operator of any requirement to comply with this section.
