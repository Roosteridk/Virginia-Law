# ยง 32.1-229.2 Costs of inspection conducted by Health Department; fees to be used to support program.

In order to minimize competition with the private sector, the fee schedule developed by the Board for routine inspections of X-ray machines by Department of Health inspectors shall include all reasonable costs of such inspections.

1987, c. 666; 2008, cc. [41](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0041), [466](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0466).
