# § 65.2-519 When limitations inapplicable to injuries arising out of pneumoconiosis.

The limitations as to the maximum periods and maximum total amounts listed in §§ [65.2-500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-500/), [65.2-512](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-512/), and [65.2-518](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-518/) shall not apply to injuries arising out of pneumoconiosis.

1972, c. 615, § 65.1-71.1; 1991, c. 355.
