# § 6.2-1018 Minimum capital; state of incorporation; form of entity.

A certificate shall not be issued under § [6.2-1017](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1017/) to an applicant:

1. Unless it meets the minimum capital requirement for a trust company prescribed by § [6.2-1017](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1017/); and

2. That is not a corporation organized under the laws of the Commonwealth.

1993, c. 432, §§ 6.1-32.15, 6.1-32.16; 1994, c. [7](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0007); 2010, c. [794](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794).
