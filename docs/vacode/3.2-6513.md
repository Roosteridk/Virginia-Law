# § 3.2-6513 Inclusion of false or misleading statements in certificate violation of Consumer Protection Act.

It shall be a violation of the Virginia Consumer Protection Act (§ [59.1-196](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-196/) et seq.) for a pet dealer to include in the pet dealer's animal history certificate provided for in § [3.2-6512](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-6512/) any false or misleading statement regarding the information to be contained therein.

1984, c. 492, § 29-213.49; 1987, c. 488, § 3.1-796.79; 2008, c. [860](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860).
