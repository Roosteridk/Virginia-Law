# § 64.2-1043 Unitrust policy.

A. In administering a unitrust under this article, a fiduciary shall follow a unitrust policy adopted under subdivision A 1 or 2 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/) or amended or replaced under subdivision A 2 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/).

B. A unitrust policy shall provide:

1. The unitrust rate or the method for determining the unitrust rate under § [64.2-1044](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/);

2. The method for determining the applicable value under § [64.2-1045](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/); and

3. The rules described in §§ [64.2-1044](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/) through [64.2-1047](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1047/) that apply in the administration of the unitrust, whether the rules are:

a. Mandatory, as provided in subsection A of § [64.2-1045](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/) and subsection A of § [64.2-1046](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1046/); or

b. Optional, as provided in § [64.2-1044](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1044/), subsection B of § [64.2-1045](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1045/), subsection B of § [64.2-1046](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1046/), and subsection A of § [64.2-1047](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1047/), to the extent the fiduciary elects to adopt those rules.
