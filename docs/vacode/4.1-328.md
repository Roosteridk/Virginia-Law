# § 4.1-328 Prohibited trade practices; penalty.

A. No person subject to the jurisdiction of the Board shall violate, attempt to violate, solicit another person to violate or consent to any violation of § [4.1-216](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-216/) or [4.1-216.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-216.1/), or regulations adopted pursuant to subdivision B 3 of § [4.1-111](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-111/).

B. Any person found by the Board to have committed a violation of this section shall be subject to a civil penalty as provided in § [4.1-227](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-227/).

1989, c. 528, § 4-79.1; 1992, c. 349; 1993, c. 866; 2007, c. [494](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0494).
