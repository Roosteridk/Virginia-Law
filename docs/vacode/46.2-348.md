However, where the license is used, or the fact concealed, or fraud is done, with the intent to purchase a firearm or use as proof of residency under ยง [9.1-903](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-903/), a violation of this section shall be punishable as a Class 4 felony.
