# § 16.1-69.46 How salaries payable.

All salaries determined according to the provisions of §§ [16.1-69.44](/vacode/16.1-69.44/) and [16.1-69.45](/vacode/16.1-69.45/) and any salary payment required by § [16.1-69.37](/vacode/16.1-69.37/) shall be payable by the Commonwealth, except any supplements paid to district court employees. All annual salaries shall be paid in semimonthly installments within the limits fixed by the Committee.
