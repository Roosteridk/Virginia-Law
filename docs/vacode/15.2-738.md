# § 15.2-738 Modification of grievance procedure.

Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ [2.2-1202.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1202.1/), [15.2-1506](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1506/), and [15.2-1507](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1507/) to the contrary, in any county which has the county manager plan of government provided for in this chapter, a grievance procedure may be established which permits an Equal Employment Opportunity officer, except the Director of the Department of Human Resource Management appointed pursuant to § [2.2-1200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1200/) and any employees thereof, to be present at any step of a grievance procedure established under § [15.2-1506](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1506/). Such officer shall not be an advocate or representative on behalf of either the grievant or management.
