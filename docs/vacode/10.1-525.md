# § 10.1-525 Canvassing returns.

The result of the election shall be canvassed and certified by the electoral board for the county or city in which the candidate resides pursuant to §§ [24.2-671](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-671/) through [24.2-678](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-678/). The State Board of Elections shall, promptly after the meeting required by § [24.2-679](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-679/), certify to the Director of the Department of Conservation and Recreation a list of the candidates elected and certified as Directors of Soil and Water Conservation Districts, as reported pursuant to § [24.2-675](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-675/).

Code 1950, § 21-38; 1960, c. 208; 1964, c. 512; 1988, c. 891; 2001, c. [53](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0053); 2002, cc. [143](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0143), [236](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0236).
