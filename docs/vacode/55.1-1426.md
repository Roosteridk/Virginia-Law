# § 55.1-1426 Fee of clerk.

The clerk shall be paid for recording, granting certificate, and noting publication, as required by § [55.1-1425](/vacode/55.1-1425/), the fee prescribed in subdivision A 2 of § [17.1-275](/vacode/17.1-275/) and shall collect and account for the same tax upon every such act of reentry offered for record as is levied by law upon deeds of conveyance.

Code 1919, § 5537; Code 1950, § 55-246; 1994, c. [432](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0432); 2019, c. [712](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712).
