# ยง 10.1-411.2 Russell Fork State Scenic River.

The Russell Fork River from the Splashdam railroad crossing to the Kentucky state line, a distance of nine miles in Dickenson County, is hereby designated as the Russell Fork State Scenic River, a component of the Virginia Scenic Rivers System.

2010, c. [290](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0290); 2014, c. [823](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0823); 2018, c. [273](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0273).
