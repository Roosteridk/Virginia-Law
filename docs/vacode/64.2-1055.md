# § 64.2-1055 Insubstantial allocation not required.

A. If a fiduciary determines that an allocation between income and principal required by § [64.2-1056](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1056/), [64.2-1057](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1057/), [64.2-1058](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1058/), [64.2-1059](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1059/), or [64.2-1062](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1062/) is insubstantial, the fiduciary may allocate the entire amount to principal, unless subsection E of § [64.2-1038](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1038/) applies to the allocation.

B. A fiduciary may presume an allocation is insubstantial under subsection A if:

1. The amount of the allocation would increase or decrease net income in an accounting period, as determined before the allocation, by less than 10 percent; and

2. The asset producing the receipt to be allocated has a fair market value less than 10 percent of the total fair market value of the assets owned or held by the fiduciary at the beginning of the accounting period.

C. The power to make a determination under subsection A may be:

1. Exercised by a co-fiduciary in the manner described in subsection F of § [64.2-1038](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1038/); or

2. Released or delegated for a reason described in subsection G of § [64.2-1038](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1038/) and in the manner described in subsection H of § [64.2-1038](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1038/).
