# § 58.1-3732.1 Limitation on gross receipts; pari-mutuel wagering.

Gross receipts for license tax purposes under Chapter 37 (§ [58.1-3700](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3700/) et seq.) shall not include the license and admission taxes established under §§ [59.1-392](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-392/) and [59.1-393](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-393/), respectively, nor shall it include pari-mutuel wagering pools as established under Article 1.1 (§ [18.2-340.15](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-340.15/) et seq.) of Chapter 8 of Title 18.2 or § [59.1-392](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-392/).

1992, c. 820; 2013, cc. [36](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0036), [350](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0350).
