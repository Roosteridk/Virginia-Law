# § 18.2-403.1 Offenses involving animals — Class 1 misdemeanors.

The following unlawful acts and offenses against animals shall constitute and be punished as a Class 1 misdemeanor:

1. Violation of subsection A of § [3.2-6570](/vacode/3.2-6570/) pertaining to cruelty to animals, except as provided for second or subsequent violations in that section.

2. Violation of § [3.2-6508](/vacode/3.2-6508/) pertaining to transporting animals under certain conditions.

3. Making a false claim or receiving money on a false claim under § [3.2-6553](/vacode/3.2-6553/) pertaining to compensation for livestock and poultry killed by dogs.

4. Violation of § [3.2-6518](/vacode/3.2-6518/) pertaining to boarding establishments and groomers as defined in § [3.2-6500](/vacode/3.2-6500/).

5. Violation of § [3.2-6504](/vacode/3.2-6504/) pertaining to the abandonment of animals.

6. Violation of subdivision B 3 of § [3.2-6587](/vacode/3.2-6587/) pertaining to an animal confinement agreement or plan set forth in § [3.2-6562.1](/vacode/3.2-6562.1/).
