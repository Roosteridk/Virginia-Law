# § 40.1-33 Certain assignments not affected.

The two preceding sections (§§ [40.1-31](/vacode/40.1-31/), [40.1-32](/vacode/40.1-32/)) shall not be construed to apply to assignments of salaries, wages and income for the benefit of creditors as provided for in Article 2 (§ [8.01-525.6](/vacode/8.01-525.6/) et seq.) of Chapter 18.1 of Title 8.01.

Code 1950, § 40-32; 1970, c. 321.
