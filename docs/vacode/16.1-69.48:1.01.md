# § 16.1-69.48:1.01 Additional fee assessed for conviction of certain offenses.

Beginning May 1, 2003, the clerk shall assess a person, in addition to the fees provided for by § [16.1-69.48:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/), a fee of $100 upon conviction of any and each charge of a violation of §§ [18.2-36.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-36.1/), [18.2-51.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-51.4/), [18.2-266](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-266/), [18.2-266.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-266.1/), [18.2-268.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-268.3/), [46.2-341.24](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.24/) or § [46.2-341.26:3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.26:3/), or any similar local ordinance.

2003, c. [1042](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1042), cl. 9.
