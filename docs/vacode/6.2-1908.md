# ยง 6.2-1908 Notice of proposed revocation.

The Commission may not revoke a license issued under this chapter upon any of the grounds set forth in ยง [6.2-1907](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1907/) until it has given the licensee 21 days' notice in writing of the reasons for the proposed revocation and has given the licensee an opportunity to introduce evidence and be heard. The notice shall be sent by certified mail to the principal place of business of such licensee and shall state with particularity the grounds for the contemplated action. Within 14 days of mailing the notice, the person or persons named therein may file with the clerk of the Commission a written request for a hearing. If a hearing is requested, the Commission shall not revoke the license except based upon findings made at such hearing.
