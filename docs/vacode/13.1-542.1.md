# § 13.1-542.1 Practice of certain professions by corporations.

Unless otherwise prohibited by law or regulation, the professional services defined in subsection A of § [13.1-543](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-543/) may be rendered in this Commonwealth by:

1. A corporation organized as a professional corporation pursuant to the provisions of this chapter;

2. A foreign corporation that has obtained a certificate of authority pursuant to the provisions of this chapter;

3. A corporation organized pursuant to the provisions of Chapter 9 (§ [13.1-601](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-601/) et seq.) or Chapter 10 (§ [13.1-801](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-801/) et seq.) of this title; or

4. A foreign corporation that has obtained a certificate of authority pursuant to the provisions of Chapter 9 (§ [13.1-601](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-601/) et seq.) or Chapter 10 (§ [13.1-801](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-801/) et seq.) of this title.
