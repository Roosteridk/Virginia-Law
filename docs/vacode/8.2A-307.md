# § 8.2A-307 Priority of liens arising by attachment or levy on, security interests in, and other claims to goods.

(1) Except as otherwise provided in § [8.2A-306](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-306/), a creditor of a lessee takes subject to the lease contract.

(2) Except as otherwise provided in subsection (3) of this section and in §§ [8.2A-306](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-306/) and [8.2A-308](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-308/), a creditor of a lessor takes subject to the lease contract unless the creditor holds a lien that attached to the goods before the lease contract became enforceable.

(3) Except as otherwise provided in §§ [8.9A-317](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-317/), [8.9A-321](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-321/), and [8.9A-323](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-323/), a lessee takes a leasehold interest subject to a security interest held by a creditor of the lessor.

1991, c. 536; 2000, c. [1007](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007).
