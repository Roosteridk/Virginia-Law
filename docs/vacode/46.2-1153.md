# § 46.2-1153 Permissible lengths of combination vehicles being towed in emergencies.

In an emergency as provided in § [46.2-1149](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1149/), the towing of disabled vehicles which cannot be separated for safety, physical, or mechanical reasons and which exceed length limits established in Article 16 (§ [46.2-1112](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1112/) et seq.) of this chapter, shall be permissible for the purpose of towing any such vehicle to the nearest facility which can make the necessary repairs but not more than fifty miles from the point such vehicle was disabled.
