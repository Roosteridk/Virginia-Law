# § 19.2-81.1 Arrest without warrant by correctional officers in certain cases.

Any correctional officer, as defined in § [53.1-1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-1/), may arrest, in the same manner as provided in § [19.2-81](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-81/), persons for crimes involving:

(a) The escape of an inmate from a correctional institution, as defined in § [53.1-1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-1/);

(b) Assisting an inmate to escape from a correctional institution, as defined in § [53.1-1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-1/);

(c) The delivery of contraband to an inmate in violation of § [18.2-474](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-474/) or § [18.2-474.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-474.1/); and

(d) Any other criminal offense which may contribute to the disruption of the safety, welfare, or security of the population of a correctional institution.

1976, c. 752.
