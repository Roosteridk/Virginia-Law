# § 5.1-131 Other remedies not extinguished.

Nothing in the preceding nine sections (§§ [5.1-122](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-122/) through [5.1-130](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-130/)) shall be held to extinguish any remedy or right of action not inconsistent therewith.

Code 1950, § 56-186; 1970, c. 708.
