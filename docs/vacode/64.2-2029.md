# § 64.2-2029 Application to guardians and conservators appointed pursuant to § 64.2-2115.

Except as otherwise provided in an order entered pursuant to § [64.2-2115](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-2115/), a guardian or conservator appointed pursuant to § [64.2-2115](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-2115/) shall be subject to the provisions of §§ [64.2-2011](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-2011/) and [64.2-2012](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-2012/) and this article.

2011, c. [518](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0518), § 37.2-1030; 2012, c. [614](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614).
