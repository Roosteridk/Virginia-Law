# ยง 58.1-3508.3 Separate classification of machinery and tools used directly in precision investment castings.

Machinery and tools used directly in the manufacture of precision investment castings shall constitute a classification for local taxation separate from other classifications of machinery and tools, as defined in ยง [58.1-3507](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3507/). The governing body of any county, city, or town may levy a tax on such classification of property at a different rate from the tax levied on other machinery and tools. The rate of tax and the rate of assessment shall not exceed that applicable generally to machinery and tools.
