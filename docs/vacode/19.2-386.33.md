# § 19.2-386.33 Forfeiture of money, etc., derived from violation of §§ 2.2-3103 through 2.2-3112.

In addition to any other fine or penalty provided by law, any money or other thing of value derived by an officer or employee from a violation of §§ [2.2-3103](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3103/) through [2.2-3112](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3112/) shall be forfeited, in accordance with the procedures contained in Chapter 22.1 (§ [19.2-386.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-386.1/) et seq.). If the thing of value received by the officer or employee in violation of §§ [2.2-3103](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3103/) through [2.2-3112](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3112/) increases in value between the time of the violation and the time of discovery of the violation, the greater value shall determine the amount of forfeiture.
