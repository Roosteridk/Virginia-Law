# § 64.2-1308 Forms for inventories and accounts.

The Office of the Executive Secretary of the Supreme Court shall provide to each circuit court clerk forms and instructions for the inventories required by § [64.2-1300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1300/) and forms and instructions for accounts. The clerk shall provide the appropriate forms to every fiduciary who qualifies in the clerk's office. An inventory filed pursuant to § [64.2-1300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1300/) or an account filed pursuant to § [64.2-1206](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1206/) may be made on the form provided to the fiduciary by the clerk of the court, on a computer-generated facsimile of the appropriate form, or in any other clear format.

1966, c. 336, §§ 26-12.1, 26-17.3; 1972, c. 411; 1993, cc. 581, 689, § 26-17.3; 1997, c. [842](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0842); 2012, c. [614](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614).
