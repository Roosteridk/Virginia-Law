# § 46.2-1233.3 Improper towing; penalty.

A. This section shall apply only to tow truck drivers and towing and recovery operators removing a vehicle without the consent of its owner from a location in Planning District 8.

B. In addition to any action brought pursuant to subsection B of § [46.2-119](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-119/), any tow truck driver who violates subsection A of § [46.2-118](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-118/) or § [46.2-1217](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1217/), [46.2-1231](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1231/), or [46.2-1233.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1233.1/), or any ordinance adopted therefrom, or any ordinance adopted pursuant to § [46.2-1233](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1233/), or any towing or recovery operator who violates subsection B of § [46.2-118](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-118/) or § [46.2-1217](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1217/), [46.2-1231](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1231/), or [46.2-1233.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1233.1/), or any ordinance adopted therefrom, or any ordinance adopted pursuant to § [46.2-1233](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1233/), is subject to a civil penalty of $150 per violation. Such penalty shall be collected by the Office of the Attorney General, and the proceeds shall be deposited into the Literary Fund.

2017, c. [825](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0825).
