# § 54.1-2318 Application of Part A (§ 64.2-1200 et seq.) of Subtitle IV of Title 64.2.

Trustees appointed pursuant to this chapter shall be governed in their investment of trust funds by § [2.2-4519](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4519/) and §§ [64.2-1502](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1502/) through [64.2-1506](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1506/), except as otherwise provided in this chapter.

1998, cc. [708](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0708), [721](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0721).
