# § 29.1-101 Game Protection Fund.

The amount received by the State Treasurer from the collection of admittance, parking, or other use fees, the sale of hunting, trapping and fishing licenses, revenue generated from the sales and use tax pursuant to subsection E of § [58.1-638](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-638/), and such other items as may accrue to the Board shall be set aside and shall constitute the Game Protection Fund. The income and principal of this Fund, including any unexpended balance, shall be a separate fund in the state treasury and shall only be used for the payment of the salaries, allowances, wages, and expenses incident to carrying out the provisions of the hunting, trapping and inland fish laws and for no other purpose, except as provided in §§ [29.1-101.01](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-101.01/), [29.1-701](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-701/), subdivision B 1 of [58.1-344.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-344.3/), and [58.1-1410](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1410/).
