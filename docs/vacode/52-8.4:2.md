# § 52-8.4:2 Certain offenses to be considered traffic infractions.

Notwithstanding subsection C of § [52-8.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/52-8.4/), any violation of any of the following provisions of the regulations adopted pursuant to § [52-8.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/52-8.4/) shall constitute traffic infractions as defined in § [46.2-100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-100/) and shall be eligible for designation as traffic infractions for which a pretrial waiver of appearance, plea of guilty, and fine payment may be accepted pursuant to § [16.1-69.40:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.40:1/).
