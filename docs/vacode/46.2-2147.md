# ยง 46.2-2147 Certain household goods carriers exempted from article.

Household goods carriers transporting solely household goods under a certificate of fitness issued pursuant to this chapter are exempt from the provisions of this article.

2001, c. [596](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0596); 2011, cc. [881](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0881), [889](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0889).
