# § 54.1-531 Suspension, revocation, or refusal to renew registration.

A. The Director may limit, suspend, revoke, or refuse to renew a registration of an individual registered under subsection A of § [54.1-530](/vacode/54.1-530/) for conduct that would have justified refusal to issue a certificate of registration under subsection B of § [54.1-530](/vacode/54.1-530/).

B. The Director may suspend or revoke the registration of an individual registered under subsection C of § [54.1-529](/vacode/54.1-529/) or renewed under subsection E of § [54.1-530](/vacode/54.1-530/) for any reason for which the Director could have refused to grant or renew registration or for conduct that would justify refusal to issue a certificate of registration under subsection B of § [54.1-530](/vacode/54.1-530/).

2020, c. [481](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0481), § 54.1-524.
