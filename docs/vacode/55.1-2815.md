# § 55.1-2815 Effect of such law on certain fences.

A declaration made by ordinance adopted pursuant to § [55.1-2814](/vacode/55.1-2814/) shall not apply to relieve the adjoining landowners from making and maintaining their division fences, as defined by § [55.1-2804](/vacode/55.1-2804/); however, Article 6 (§ [55.1-2821](/vacode/55.1-2821/) et seq.) shall apply to such division fences.

Code 1950, § 8-881; 1977, c. 624, § 55-311; 2019, c. [712](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712).
