Whenever the size of the Fund decreases to less than $2 million, the Director shall suspend the return of payments pursuant to ยง [45.2-1237](/vacode/45.2-1237/) and shall assess all members an equal amount for each affected acre, for a total amount sufficient to raise the Fund to $2 million.
