# § 45.2-771  Fire prevention and fire control.

The provisions of Article 5 (§ [45.2-912](/vacode/45.2-912/) et seq.) of Chapter 9 shall apply with respect to any requirement for firefighting equipment, duties in the event of a fire, or fire precautions at any surface area of an underground coal mine.
