# § 63.2-1939 Commissioner may make demand, file and serve liens, when payments appear in jeopardy.

If the Commissioner finds that the collection of any support debt based upon subrogation to or authorization to enforce the amount of support ordered by any court order or decree of divorce is in jeopardy, he may make demand under § [63.2-1916](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1916/) for immediate payment of the support debt. Upon failure or refusal immediately to pay such support debt, he may file and serve liens pursuant to §§ [63.2-1927](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1927/) and [63.2-1928](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1928/), without regard to the ten-day period provided for in § [63.2-1916](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1916/). However, no further action under §§ [63.2-1929](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1929/), [63.2-1933](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1933/) and [63.2-1934](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1934/) may be taken until the notice requirements of § [63.2-1916](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1916/) are met.

1974, c. 413, § 63.1-266; 1976, c. 357; 1988, c. 906; 2002, c. [747](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0747).
