# § 15.2-2273 Fee for processing application under § 15.2-2271 or § 15.2-2272.

Any locality may prescribe and charge a reasonable fee not exceeding $150 for processing an application pursuant to § [15.2-2271](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2271/) or § [15.2-2272](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2272/) for the vacating of any plat.

1970, c. 161, § 15.1-482.1; 1975, c. 641; 1978, c. 554; 1984, c. 285; 1997, c. 587.
