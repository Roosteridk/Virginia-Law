# § 16.1-278.11 Mental illness and intellectual disability.

In cases involving a person who is involuntarily admitted because of a mental illness or is judicially certified as eligible for admission to a training center for persons with intellectual disability, disposition shall be in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 8 (§ [37.2-800](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-800/) et seq.) of Title 37.2. A child shall not be committed pursuant to §§ [16.1-278.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-278.2/) through [16.1-278.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-278.8/) or the provisions of Title 37.2 to a maximum security unit within any state hospital where adults determined to be criminally insane reside.
