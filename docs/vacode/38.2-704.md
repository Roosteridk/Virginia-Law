# § 38.2-704 Penalties.

A. Any person who violates a cease and desist order entered under § [38.2-703](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-703/) shall be subject to the provisions of § [38.2-218](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-218/).

B. Any person convicted of violating this chapter may, in addition, be punished under the provisions of Chapter 1.1 (§ [59.1-9.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-9.1/) et seq.) of Title 59.1.

1952, c. 317, § 38.1-62; 1986, c. 562.
