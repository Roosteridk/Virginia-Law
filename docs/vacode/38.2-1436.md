# § 38.2-1436 Mortgage participations.

Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ [13.1-627](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-627/) and [13.1-826](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-826/), a domestic insurer may acquire or sell participation interests in any loans secured by a mortgage or deed of trust qualifying under § [38.2-1434](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1434/) if the insurer has all or substantially all the rights of a first mortgagee.

1983, c. 457, § 38.1-217.39; 1986, c. 562.
