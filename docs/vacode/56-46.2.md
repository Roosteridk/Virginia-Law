# ยง 56-46.2 Construction of electrical transmission lines.

The construction of all overhead electrical transmission lines shall adhere to the standards set forth in the National Electrical Safety Code. The Commission shall, upon receipt of a written complaint concerning the lack of compliance with these standards in the construction of a particular transmission line, investigate the situation and, if appropriate, exercise its powers granted under ยง [12.1-12](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/12.1-12/) to enforce adherence to the standards.
