# ยง 54.1-2320 Additional deposit not required upon subsequent sale of same grave, crypt or niche.

If ten percent of the sales price of a grave or above-ground crypt or niche has been deposited in a perpetual care trust fund, no deposit shall be required on subsequent sales of the same grave, crypt or niche.

1998, cc. [708](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0708), [721](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0721).
