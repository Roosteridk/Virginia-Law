# § 38.2-1027 Admission of foreign and alien insurers.

Before transacting any insurance business in this Commonwealth, each foreign or alien insurer or reciprocal shall obtain a certificate of authority and shall comply with the applicable provisions of Article 17 (§ [13.1-757](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-757/) et seq.) of Chapter 9 of Title 13.1 in the case of a stock insurer, of Article 14 (§ [13.1-919](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-919/) et seq.) of Chapter 10 of Title 13.1 in the case of a mutual insurer, and of Article 1 (§ [38.2-1200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1200/) et seq.) of Chapter 12 in the case of a reciprocal. The certificate shall be in addition to the license to transact the business of insurance required by § [38.2-1024](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1024/).

Code 1950, §§ 38-32, 38-34; 1952, c. 317, § 38.1-83; 1956, c. 431; 1986, c. 562; 2017, c. [655](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0655).
