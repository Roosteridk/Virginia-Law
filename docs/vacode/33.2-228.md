# § 33.2-228 Agreements between Commissioner of Highways and certain localities.

Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ [33.2-209](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-209/), [33.2-214](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-214/), and [33.2-221](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-221/), the Commissioner of Highways, pursuant to a resolution adopted by the Board and following receipt of a resolution adopted by the governing body of a locality, may enter into an agreement with any such locality pursuant to which the locality assumes responsibility for the design, right-of-way acquisition, and construction of highways or portions thereof in such locality, using funds allocated pursuant to § [33.2-371](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-371/).

2004, c. [623](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0623), § 33.1-12.1; 2014, c. [805](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805); 2015, c. [684](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0684).
