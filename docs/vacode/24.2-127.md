# ยง 24.2-127 Impairment of voting rights of registered voters.

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to deny, impair, or otherwise adversely affect the right to vote of any registered voter.

2021, Sp. Sess. I, cc. [528](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0528), [533](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0533).
