# § 25.1-106 Condemnation of lands within agricultural and forestal districts.

No property that is within an agricultural and forestal district as provided by Chapter 43 (§ [15.2-4300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-4300/) et seq.) of Title 15.2 shall be condemned except in accordance with §§ [15.2-4312](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-4312/) and [15.2-4313](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-4313/).

1977, c. 681, § 25-46.2:2; 2003, c. [940](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0940).
