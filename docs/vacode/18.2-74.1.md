# § 18.2-74.1 Abortion, etc., when necessary to save life of woman.

In the event it is necessary for a licensed physician to terminate a human pregnancy or assist in the termination of a human pregnancy by performing an abortion or causing a miscarriage on any woman in order to save her life, in the opinion of the physician so performing the abortion or causing the miscarriage, §§ [18.2-71](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-71/), [18.2-73](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-73/) and [18.2-74](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-74/) shall not be applicable.

Code 1950, § 18.1-62.3; 1970, c. 508; 1975, cc. 14, 15.
