B. The Commission shall mail to each domestic and foreign limited partnership that fails to pay the annual registration fee within the time prescribed in ยง [50-73.67](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.67/) notice of assessment of the penalty imposed herein and of the impending cancellation of its existence or certificate of registration, as the case may be.
