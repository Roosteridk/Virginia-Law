# § 59.1-123 Exemptions from article.

The provisions of this article shall not apply to:

1. The sale of secondhand material mentioned in § [59.1-117](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-117/) taken from premises occupied by the owner, when sold by such owner on the premises, or the sale of such articles when purchased from a public utility corporation at its place of business or a governmental agency;

2. Scrap metal purchasers as provided in Article 4 (§ [59.1-136.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-136.1/) et seq.);

3. Authorized scrap sellers;

4. Public utilities;

5. Public transportation companies;

6. Peddlers permitted under § [59.1-118](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-118/);

7. Industrial and manufacturing companies;

8. Marine, automobile, and aircraft salvage and wrecking companies;

9. Governmental entities; or

10. The donation of secondhand material mentioned in § [59.1-117](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-117/) by the material's owner or the owner's contractor or subcontractor to a nonprofit corporation as defined in § 501(c)(3) of the U.S. Internal Revenue Code or the sale of such donated material by such a nonprofit corporation.
