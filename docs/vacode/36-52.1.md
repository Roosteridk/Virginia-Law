# ยง 36-52.1 Authority for localities to create conservation or redevelopment areas.

A locality has no authority to create conservation or redevelopment areas, except through a redevelopment and housing authority and only in accordance with this chapter.

1964, c. 378; 1966, c. 81; 2006, c. [784](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0784).
