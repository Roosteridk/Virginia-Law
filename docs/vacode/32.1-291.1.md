# § 32.1-291.1 Revised Uniform Anatomical Gift Act; short title.

This Act consists of §§ [32.1-291.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/32.1-291.1/) through [32.1-291.25](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/32.1-291.25/) and may be cited as the Revised Uniform Anatomical Gift Act.

2007, cc. [92](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0092), [907](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0907).
