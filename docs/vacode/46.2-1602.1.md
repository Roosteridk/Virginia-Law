# § 46.2-1602.1 Duties of insurance companies upon acquiring certain vehicles.

Every insurance company which acquires, as a result of the claims process, any late model vehicle titled in the Commonwealth or any recovered stolen vehicle whose estimated cost of repair exceeds seventy-five percent of its actual cash value shall apply to and obtain from the Department either (i) a salvage certificate or certificate of title as provided in § [46.2-1603](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1603/) or (ii) a nonrepairable certificate as provided in § [46.2-1603.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1603.2/) for each such vehicle. An insurance company may apply to and obtain from the Department either a salvage certificate as provided in § [46.2-1603](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1603/) or a nonrepairable certificate as provided in § [46.2-1603.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1603.2/) for any other vehicle which is determined to be either a salvage vehicle or a nonrepairable vehicle.
