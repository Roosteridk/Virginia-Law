# § 22.1-31 Oath.

Before entering upon the duties of office, each person appointed to a school board shall take and subscribe the oath prescribed for an officer of this Commonwealth as provided in Chapter 1 (§ [49-1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/49-1/) et seq.) of Title 49.

Code 1950, §§ 22-66, 22-91; 1980, c. 559.
