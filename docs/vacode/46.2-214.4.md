# § 46.2-214.4 Discount for online transactions.

The Department may offer a $1 discount for the following transactions if conducted using the Internet: (i) a driver's license renewal pursuant to § [46.2-330](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-330/), (ii) a driver's license duplicate or reissue pursuant to § [46.2-343](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-343/), (iii) an identification card renewal pursuant to § [46.2-345](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-345/), (iv) an identification card duplicate or reissue pursuant to § [46.2-345](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-345/), or (v) a certificate of title replacement pursuant to § [46.2-607](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-607/).

2016, c. [368](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0368).
