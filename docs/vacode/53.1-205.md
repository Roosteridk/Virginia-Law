# § 53.1-205 Jurisdiction for trial of prisoners; nature of proceedings.

Subject to the provisions of §§ [53.1-203](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-203/) and [53.1-204](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-204/), the jurisdiction, proceedings, trial and judgment in a criminal proceeding against a person confined in a state correctional facility shall be as is provided for in other cases of criminal prosecution.

Code 1950, §§ 53-295, 53-300; 1966, c. 430; 1970, c. 593; 1973, c. 59; 1982, c. 636.
