# § 54.1-3507.3 Use of titles.

No person shall claim to be, or use the title of, a substance abuse treatment practitioner, a substance abuse counselor, or a substance abuse counseling assistant unless he has been licensed or certified as such pursuant to §§ [54.1-3507](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3507/), [54.1-3507.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3507.1/) or § [54.1-3507.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3507.2/).

2001, c. [460](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0460).
