# ยง 54.1-2957.02 When nurse practitioner signature accepted.

Whenever any law or regulation requires a signature, certification, stamp, verification, affidavit or endorsement by a physician, it shall be deemed to include a signature, certification, stamp, verification, affidavit or endorsement by a nurse practitioner.

2004, c. [855](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0855).
