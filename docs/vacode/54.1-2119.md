# ยง 54.1-2119 Revocation of license of regulant upon payment from fund.

Upon payment by the Director to a claimant from the fund as provided in ยง [54.1-2118](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2118/), the Board shall immediately revoke the license of the regulant whose improper or dishonest conduct, as defined in the act, resulted in payment from the fund.
