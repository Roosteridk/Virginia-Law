# ยง 46.2-679.2 Off-road motorcycles.

No person shall be required to obtain the registration certificate, license plate, or decals for or pay a registration fee for any off-road motorcycle.

2006, c. [896](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0896); 2016, c. [142](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0142).
