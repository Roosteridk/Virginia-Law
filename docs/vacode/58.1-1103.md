# § 58.1-1103 Exempt professions and businesses; how property used therein taxable.

Section [58.1-1100](/vacode/58.1-1100/), except subdivision A 10 of § [58.1-1101](/vacode/58.1-1101/), shall not be construed to apply to (i) any profession that the Commonwealth regulates by law, (ii) industrial development corporations organized pursuant to the terms of §§ [13.1-981](/vacode/13.1-981/) through [13.1-998](/vacode/13.1-998/), or (iii) the business of farming, which includes propagating, growing, selling, and planting, as an incident to the sale, of evergreens, shade trees, shrubs, and all other nursery products, ornamental and otherwise, grown by the seller. Property used or employed in such exempt activities shall be taxable in the actual form in which it exists and not as intangible personal property.

Code 1950, § 58-413; 1956, c. 241; 1962, c. 131; 1982, c. 633; 1984, c. 675; 2019, c. [255](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0255).
