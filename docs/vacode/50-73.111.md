# § 50-73.111 Effect of partner's dissociation.

A. If a partner's dissociation results in a dissolution and winding up of the partnership business, Article 8 applies; otherwise, Article 7 applies.

B. Upon a partner's dissociation:

1. The partner's right to participate in the management and conduct of the partnership business terminates, except as otherwise provided in § [50-73.119](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.119/);

2. The partner's duty of loyalty under subdivision B 3 of § [50-73.102](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.102/) terminates; and

3. The partner's duty of loyalty under subdivisions B 1 and B 2 of § [50-73.102](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.102/) and duty of care under subsection C of § [50-73.102](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.102/) continue only with regard to matters arising and events occurring before the partner's dissociation, unless the partner participates in winding up the partnership's business pursuant to § [50-73.119](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.119/).
