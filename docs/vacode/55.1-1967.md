# ยง 55.1-1967 Notice of sale under deed of trust.

In accordance with the provisions of ยง [15.2-979](/vacode/15.2-979/), the unit owners' association shall be given notice whenever a condominium unit becomes subject to a sale under a deed of trust. Upon receipt of such notice, the executive board, on behalf of the unit owners' association, shall exercise whatever due diligence it deems necessary with respect to the unit subject to a sale under a deed of trust to protect the interests of the unit owners' association.
