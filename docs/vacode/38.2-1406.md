# ยง 38.2-1406 Investment conversions.

Investments converted to a new form and resulting in a different investment classification under ยง [38.2-1402](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1402/), at the election of the insurer, shall retain their previous investment classification for a period not exceeding three years unless the Commission prescribes in writing that a longer period is reasonable. The investment conversions shall include those resulting (i) from investments acquired in satisfaction of or on account of loans, mortgages, liens, judgments, or other debts previously owing to the insurer in the course of its business, or (ii) from investments acquired through lawful distributions of assets, lawful plans of reorganization, or lawful and bona fide agreements of bulk reinsurance or of consolidation.
