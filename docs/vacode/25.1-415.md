# § 25.1-415 Adjustments to certain benefit limits.

The monetary limits provided for in §§ [25.1-406](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-406/), [25.1-408](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-408/), [25.1-409](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-409/), and [25.1-410](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-410/) shall be adjusted to conform to future revisions of corresponding monetary benefits under the federal Uniform Relocation Assistance and Real Property Acquisition Policies Act of 1970 (Public Law 91-646).

1981, c. 149, § 25-247.1; 2003, c. [940](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0940).
