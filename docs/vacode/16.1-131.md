# ยง 16.1-131 Subpoenas duces tecum and recognizances of witnesses; applicable provisions.

The provisions of ยง [16.1-90](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-90/) with respect to recognizances for witnesses upon the continuation of any case, shall be applicable to proceedings of a criminal nature as well as to civil actions.
