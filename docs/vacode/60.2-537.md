# § 60.2-537 Reduced tax rate permissible under federal amendment.

Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ [60.2-500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-500/) through [60.2-536](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-536/), if § 3303 of the Internal Revenue Code is amended so as to allow an additional credit under § 3302 (b) of the Internal Revenue Code to employers not otherwise eligible for a reduced rate by reason of the lapse of insufficient time since first becoming subject to this title, the Commission, by a regulation promulgated under § [60.2-111](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-111/), shall fix and determine the tax rate of all such employers at such reduced rate as shall then be permissible under such federal amendment.

Code 1950, § 60-76.1; 1956, c. 440; 1968, c. 738, § 60.1-90; 1986, c. 480.
