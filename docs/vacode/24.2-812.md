# § 24.2-812 Proceedings when court decides no valid election has been held.

If the court decides that there has been no valid election of any person, it shall declare the election void and the vacancy shall be filled in conformity with §§ [24.2-226](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-226/) and [24.2-227](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-227/).

Code 1950, § 24-438; 1970, c. 462, § 24.1-245; 1993, c. 641.
