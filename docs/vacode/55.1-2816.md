# § 55.1-2816 Application to railroad companies.

No action taken under the provisions of § [55.1-2814](/vacode/55.1-2814/) shall relieve any railroad company of any duty or obligation imposed on every such company by § [56-429](/vacode/56-429/), or imposed by any other statute now in force, in reference to fencing their lines of railway and rights-of-way.

Code 1950, § 8-882; 1977, c. 624, § 55-312; 2019, c. [712](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712).
