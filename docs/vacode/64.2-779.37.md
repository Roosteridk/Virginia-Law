# ยง 64.2-779.37 Jurisdiction over trust director.

A. By accepting appointment as a trust director of a trust subject to this article, the trust director submits to personal jurisdiction of the courts of the Commonwealth regarding any matter related to a power or duty of the trust director.

B. This section does not preclude other methods of obtaining jurisdiction over a trust director.

2020, c. [768](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0768).
