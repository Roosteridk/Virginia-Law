# ยง 58.1-632 Memorandum of lien.

The Tax Commissioner is empowered, when any tax becomes delinquent under this chapter, to issue a memorandum of lien for the collection of the tax, penalty and interest from each delinquent taxpayer. Section [58.1-1805](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1805/) shall apply to such memorandum, except that the same may be issued as soon as the tax becomes delinquent.
