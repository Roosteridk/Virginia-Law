# § 37.2-905.1 Substantial compliance.

The provisions of §§ [37.2-903](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-903/), [37.2-904](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-904/), and [37.2-905](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-905/) are procedural and not substantive or jurisdictional. Absent a showing of failure to follow these provisions as a result of gross negligence or willful misconduct, it shall be presumed that there has been substantial compliance with these provisions.

2007, c. [876](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0876); 2009, c. [740](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0740).
