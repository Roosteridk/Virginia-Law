# § 3.2-3603 Publications.

The Commissioner may publish in such forms and with such frequency as he may deem proper: (i) information concerning the distribution of fertilizers; and (ii) results of analysis based on official samples of fertilizer distributed within the Commonwealth, as compared with analysis guaranteed under §§ [3.2-3600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-3600/) and [3.2-3611](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-3611/); and commercial value of nutrients as determined under § [3.2-3614](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-3614/).

1994, c. [740](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0740), § 3.1-106.19; 2008, c. [860](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860).
