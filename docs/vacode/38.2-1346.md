# § 38.2-1346 Licensure.

A. No person shall act in this Commonwealth as a producer, and no resident of this Commonwealth shall act as a producer, unless such person or resident is licensed as an insurance agent pursuant to the provisions of Chapter 18 (§ [38.2-1800](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1800/) et seq.) of this title, or as a reinsurance intermediary or managing general agent pursuant to the provisions of this chapter.

B. As used in this section, the terms "resident" and "insurance agent" have the meanings prescribed in § [38.2-1800](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1800/), and the terms "managing general agent," and "reinsurance intermediary" have the meanings set forth in §§ [38.2-1347](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1347/) and [38.2-1358](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1358/).
