# § 64.2-2101 International application of chapter.

A court of the Commonwealth may treat a foreign country as if it were a state for the purpose of applying this article and Articles 2 (§ [64.2-2105](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-2105/) et seq.), 3 (§ [64.2-2114](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-2114/) et seq.), and 5 (§ [64.2-2119](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-2119/) et seq.).

2011, c. [518](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0518), § 37.2-1033; 2012, c. [614](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614).
