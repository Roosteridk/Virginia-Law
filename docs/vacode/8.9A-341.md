# ยง 8.9A-341 Bank's rights and duties with respect to deposit account.

Except as otherwise provided in ยง [8.9A-340](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-340/) (c), and unless the bank otherwise agrees in an authenticated record, a bank's rights and duties with respect to a deposit account maintained with the bank are not terminated, suspended, or modified by:

(1) the creation, attachment, or perfection of a security interest in the deposit account;

(2) the bank's knowledge of the security interest; or

(3) the bank's receipt of instructions from the secured party.
