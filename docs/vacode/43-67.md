# § 43-67 Release of mechanic's lien upon payment or satisfaction.

When payment or satisfaction has been made of a debt secured by a mechanic's lien it shall be released in the manner provided in §§ [55.1-339](/vacode/55.1-339/) and [55.1-341](/vacode/55.1-341/), insofar as appropriate.

Code 1919, § 6456; 1926, p. 80; 1930, p. 69; 1932, p. 120; 1944, p. 198.
