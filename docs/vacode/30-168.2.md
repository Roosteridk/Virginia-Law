# § 30-168.2 Compensation; expenses.

Members of the Commission shall receive such compensation as provided in § [30-19.12](/vacode/30-19.12/). All members shall be reimbursed for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](/vacode/2.2-2825/). Funding for the costs of compensation and expenses of the members shall be provided by the Joint Commission on Health Care.

2003, c. [633](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0633).
