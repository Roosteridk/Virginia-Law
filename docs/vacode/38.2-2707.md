# § 38.2-2707 When Commission may order implementation of §§ 38.2-2708 and 38.2-2709.

If the Commission finds, after a reasonable period of time, that the residual market facility established by § [38.2-2702](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2702/) is not creating a market that meets the purposes of this chapter, the Commission may order the implementation of §§ [38.2-2708](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2708/) and [38.2-2709](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2709/).

1968, c. 559, § 38.1-749; 1973, c. 451; 1986, c. 562.
