# § 23.1-2427 Exemptions.

The provisions of the Virginia Personnel Act (§ [2.2-2900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2900/) et seq.), the Workforce Transition Act (§ [2.2-3200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3200/) et seq.), the Administrative Process Act (§ [2.2-4000](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4000/) et seq.), and the Virginia Public Procurement Act (§ [2.2-4300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4300/) et seq.) do not apply to the Authority in its exercise of any power conferred to it under this chapter.
