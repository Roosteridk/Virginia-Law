# § 20-105.1 Alternative procedures.

The provisions of Title 8.01 for orders of publication shall be construed as alternatives to the procedures set forth in §§ [20-104](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-104/) through [20-105](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-105/) and not in conflict therewith.

1978, c. 46.
