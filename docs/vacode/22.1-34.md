# § 22.1-34 Application of article.

The school board in each county constituting a school division, except a county to which the provisions of §§ [15.2-410](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-410/), [15.2-531](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-531/), [15.2-627](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-627/), [15.2-837](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-837/) or § [22.1-44](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-44/) are applicable, shall be selected as provided in this article.

1980, c. 559.
