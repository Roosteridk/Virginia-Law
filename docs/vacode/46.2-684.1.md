# § 46.2-684.1 Insurance coverage and proof of insurance for exempted motor vehicles.

A. Any motor vehicle, trailer, or semi-trailer that is exempt from motor vehicle registration requirements pursuant to this article shall be insured under a general liability policy that includes personal injury liability insurance as defined in § [38.2-117](/vacode/38.2-117/) and property damage liability insurance as defined in § [38.2-118](/vacode/38.2-118/), under a policy of motor vehicle insurance as defined in § [38.2-124](/vacode/38.2-124/), or under an umbrella or excess insurance policy. Any such umbrella or excess insurance policy shall not be required to comply with the provisions of Chapter 22 (§ [38.2-2200](/vacode/38.2-2200/) et seq.) of Title 38.2 of the Code of Virginia that relate to the ownership, maintenance, or use of the exempt motor vehicle, trailer, or semi-trailer.

B. Any person who owns a motor vehicle, trailer, or semi-trailer that is exempt from motor vehicle registration requirements pursuant to this article may be required by a law-enforcement officer to furnish proof that such motor vehicle, trailer, or semi-trailer is insured as required in subsection A. Failure to furnish proof of insurance when required by a law-enforcement officer as provided in this section within 30 days shall constitute a traffic infraction punishable by a $600 fine that shall be paid into the Uninsured Motorists Fund created pursuant to § [38.2-3000](/vacode/38.2-3000/).
