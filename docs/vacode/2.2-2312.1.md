# ยง 2.2-2312.1 Risk-based review of outstanding loans; report.

The Authority shall conduct a risk-based review of all outstanding loans at least annually and report the results of such review to the Board.

2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. [147](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0147).
