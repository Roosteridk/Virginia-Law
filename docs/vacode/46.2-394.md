# § 46.2-394 Revocation of license for fourth conviction of certain offenses.

If any person is convicted four times of a violation of §§ [46.2-865](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-865/), [46.2-894](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-894/), or § [46.2-895](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-895/), or any substantially similar ordinance or law of any other jurisdiction, the court shall revoke his driver's license for five years.

1962, c. 424, § 46.1-423.2; 1984, c. 780; 1989, c. 727; 1997, c. [691](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0691); 2000, cc. [956](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0956), [982](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0982).
