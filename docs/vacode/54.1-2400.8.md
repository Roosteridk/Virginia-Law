# § 54.1-2400.8 Immunity for reporting.

In addition to the immunity for reporting as provided by §§ [54.1-2400.6](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2400.6/) and [54.1-2400.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2400.7/), any person (i) making a report regarding the conduct or competency of a health care practitioner as required by law or regulation, (ii) making a voluntary report to the appropriate regulatory board or to the Department of Health Professions regarding the unprofessional conduct or competency of any practitioner licensed, certified, or registered by a health regulatory board, or (iii) providing information pursuant to an investigation or testifying in a judicial or administrative proceeding as a result of such reports shall be immune from any civil liability resulting therefrom unless such person acted in bad faith or with malicious intent.

2004, c. [64](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0064); 2005, c. [932](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0932).
