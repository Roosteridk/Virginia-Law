# § 30-404 (For contingent expiration date, see Acts 2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. 313, cl. 2) Compensation; expenses.

Members of the Commission shall receive such compensation as provided in § [30-19.12](/vacode/30-19.12/). All members shall be reimbursed for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](/vacode/2.2-2825/). Compensation to members of the General Assembly for attendance at official meetings of the Commission shall be paid by the offices of the Clerk of the House of Delegates or Clerk of the Senate, as applicable. All other compensation and expenses shall be paid from existing appropriations to the Commission or, if unfunded, shall be approved by the Joint Rules Committee.
