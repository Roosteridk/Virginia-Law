# § 30-350 Compensation; expenses.

Legislative members of the Commission shall receive such compensation as provided in § [30-19.12](/vacode/30-19.12/), and nonlegislative citizen members shall receive such compensation for the performance of their duties as provided in § [2.2-2813](/vacode/2.2-2813/). All members shall be reimbursed for all reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](/vacode/2.2-2825/).

2014, c. [562](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0562).
