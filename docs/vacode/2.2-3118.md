# § 2.2-3118 Disclosure form; certain citizen members.

The financial disclosure form to be used for filings required pursuant to subsection B of [§ 2.2-3114](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3114/) and subsection B of [§ 2.2-3115](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3115/) shall be filed in accordance with the provisions of [§ 30-356](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-356/). The financial disclosure form shall be prescribed by the Council. Except as otherwise provided in [§ 2.2-3115](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3115/), all completed forms shall be filed electronically with the Council in accordance with the standards approved by it pursuant to [§ 30-356](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-356/).
