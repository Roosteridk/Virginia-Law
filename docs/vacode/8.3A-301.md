# § 8.3A-301 Person entitled to enforce instrument.

"Person entitled to enforce" an instrument means (i) the holder of the instrument, (ii) a nonholder in possession of the instrument who has the rights of a holder, or (iii) a person not in possession of the instrument who is entitled to enforce the instrument pursuant to § [8.3A-309](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.3A-309/) or § [8.3A-418](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.3A-418/) (d). A person may be a person entitled to enforce the instrument even though the person is not the owner of the instrument or is in wrongful possession of the instrument.

Code 1950, § 6-403; 1964, c. 219, § 8.3-301; 1992, c. 693.
