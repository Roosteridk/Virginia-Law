# § 2.2-4007.07 State Air Pollution Control Board; variances.

The provisions of §§ [2.2-4007](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4007/) through [2.2-4007.06](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4007.06/) shall not apply to the issuance by the State Air Pollution Control Board of variances to its regulations.

2007, cc. [873](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0873), [916](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0916).
