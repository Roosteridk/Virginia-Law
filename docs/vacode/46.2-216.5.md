# ยง 46.2-216.5 Partnership of Department and The Library of Virginia to promote use of public library Internet access terminals to complete on-line transactions with the Department.

The Department shall enter into a partnership with The Library of Virginia to promote the use of public library Internet access terminals to complete on-line transactions with the Department.

2003, c. [336](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0336).
