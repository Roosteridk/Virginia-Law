# § 3.2-112 Regulations governing the conduct of referenda.

The Board shall adopt regulations governing the ballots to be used in any referendum, the conduct of any referendum, canvassing the results thereof, and declaring the results of any referendum provided for in this title. The Board shall fix the date, hours, and voting places with respect to the holding of any referendum and may provide for voting by mail. No requirement of this section shall be governed by Article 2 (§ [2.2-4006](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4006/) et seq.) of the Administrative Process Act.
