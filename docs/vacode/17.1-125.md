# § 17.1-125 Civil order book.

The clerk shall record (i) trust fund orders pursuant to §§ [17.1-123](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-123/) and [17.1-124](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-124/) and (ii) the annual trust fund report required pursuant to subsection G of § [8.01-600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-600/) in a book known as the civil order book, in which shall be recorded all reports, orders, and decrees concerning moneys received or to be received by general receivers pursuant to § [8.01-582](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-582/) and by clerks pursuant to § [8.01-600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-600/).

1988, c. 553, § 17-28.1; 1998, c. [872](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0872); 2017, c. [35](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0035).
