# ยง 38.2-815 Other legal service not limited.

Nothing in this article shall limit the right to serve any process or notice upon any unlicensed public adjuster in any other manner permitted by law.

2012, cc. [734](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0734), [735](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0735).
