# § 24.2-229 Appointees to qualify and give bond in thirty days.

All officers appointed to fill vacancies shall qualify and give bond, if bond is required, within thirty days after their appointment in like manner as provided in §§ [15.2-1522](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1522/) and [15.2-1523](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1523/) for the qualification of such officers when elected by the people.

Code 1950, § 24-146; 1970, c. 462, § 24.1-77; 1975, c. 515; 1993, c. 641.
