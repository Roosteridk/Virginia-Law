# § 6.2-922 When publication once in two newspapers sufficient.

If there are two or more newspapers having general circulation in the locality where a suit or petition described in § [6.2-920](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-920/) is pending, the court, in its discretion, in lieu of the publication provided for therein or in § [6.2-921](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-921/), may direct that the list of creditors and the notice, be published once in at least two of the newspapers having general circulation in the locality.

Code 1950, § 6-60; 1966, c. 584, § 6.1-108; 2010, c. [794](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794).
