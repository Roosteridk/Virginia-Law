# § 58.1-1202 Bank capital assessable.

Every bank or trust company shall pay an annual franchise tax measured by its net capital as defined in § [58.1-1205](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1205/). Such tax shall be in lieu of all other taxes whatsoever for state, county or local purposes except the real estate and tangible personal property taxes enumerated in § [58.1-1203](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1203/), retail sales and use taxes under Chapter 6 (§ [58.1-600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-600/) et seq.) of this title, recordation taxes under § [58.1-800](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-800/) et seq., motor vehicle sales and use taxes under Chapter 24 (§ [58.1-2400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2400/) et seq.) of this title, watercraft sales and use taxes under Chapter 14 (§ [58.1-1400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1400/) et seq.) of this title, aircraft sales and use taxes under Chapter 15 (§ [58.1-1500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1500/) et seq.) of this title, taxes properly assessable upon users of utility services, and local license taxes in connection with the sale of tangible personal property sold by banks in connection with promotions or otherwise.

Code 1950, § 58-485.04; 1980, c. 578; 1981, c. 432; 1984, c. 675.
