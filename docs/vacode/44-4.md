# § 44-4 Composition of unorganized militia.

The unorganized militia shall consist of all able-bodied persons as set out in § [44-1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/44-1/), except such as may be included in §§ [44-2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/44-2/) and [44-54.6](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/44-54.6/) and except such as may be exempted as hereinafter provided.

1930, p. 949; Michie Code 1942, § 2673(4); 1970, c. 662; 1973, c. 401; 1984, c. 765; 2015, c. [221](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0221).
