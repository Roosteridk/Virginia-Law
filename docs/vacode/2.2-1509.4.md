# § 2.2-1509.4 Budget bill to include an appropriation for land preservation.

Each year the Governor shall include in "The Budget Bill" submitted pursuant to subsection A of § [2.2-1509](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1509/) or in his amendments to the general appropriation act in effect submitted pursuant to subsection E of § [2.2-1509](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1509/) a recommended appropriation from the general fund pursuant to subdivision D 4 c of § [58.1-512](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-512/) to be allocated as follows: 80 percent of such amount to the Virginia Land Conservation Fund to be used in accordance with § [10.1-1020](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1020/), with no less than 50 percent of such appropriation to be used for fee simple acquisitions with public access or acquisitions of easements with public access; 10 percent of such amount to the Virginia Battlefield Preservation Fund to be used in accordance with § [10.1-2202.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-2202.4/); and 10 percent of such amount to the Virginia Farmland Preservation Fund to be used in accordance with § [3.2-201](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-201/).

2013, c. [798](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0798); 2015, c. [467](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0467).
