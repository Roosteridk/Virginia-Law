# § 64.2-1042 Notice.

A. A notice required by subdivision B 2 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/) shall be sent in a manner authorized under § [64.2-707](/vacode/64.2-707/) to:

1. The qualified beneficiaries determined under § [64.2-701](/vacode/64.2-701/), other than the Attorney General;

2. Each person acting as trust director of the trust under the Uniform Directed Trust Act (§ [64.2-779.26](/vacode/64.2-779.26/) et seq.); and

3. Each person that is granted a power by the terms of the trust to appoint or remove a trustee or person described in subdivision 2, to the extent the power is exercisable when the person that exercises the power is not then serving as a trustee or person described in subdivision 2.

B. The representation provisions of Article 3 (§ [64.2-714](/vacode/64.2-714/) et seq.) of Chapter 7 apply to notice under this section.

C. A person may consent in a record at any time to action proposed under subdivision B 2 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/). A notice required by subdivision B 2 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/) need not be sent to a person that consents under this subsection.

D. A notice required by subdivision B 2 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/) shall include:

1. The action proposed under subdivision B 2 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/);

2. For a conversion of an income trust to a unitrust, a copy of the unitrust policy adopted under subdivision A 1 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/);

3. For a change in the percentage or method used to calculate the unitrust amount, a copy of the unitrust policy or amendment or replacement of the unitrust policy adopted under subdivision A 2 of § [64.2-1041](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title64.2/chapter10.1/section64.2-1041/);

4. A statement that the person to which the notice is sent may object to the proposed action by stating in a record the basis for the objection and sending or delivering the record to the fiduciary;

5. The date by which an objection under subdivision 4 must be received by the fiduciary, which must be at least 30 days after the date the notice is sent;

6. The date on which the action is proposed to be taken and the date on which the action is proposed to take effect;

7. The name and contact information of the fiduciary; and

8. The name and contact information of a person that may be contacted for additional information.
