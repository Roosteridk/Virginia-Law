# § 59.1-284.01 Expiration of chapter; exceptions.

A. All provisions of this chapter except §§ [59.1-279](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-279/), [59.1-280](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-280/), [59.1-280.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-280.1/), [59.1-282.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-282.1/), [59.1-282.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-282.2/) and this section shall expire on July 1, 2005, unless extended by an act of the General Assembly.

B. All enterprise zones designated pursuant to §§ [59.1-274](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-274/), [59.1-274.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-274.1/), and [59.1-274.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-274.2/) as those were in effect prior to July 1, 2005 shall continue in effect until the end of their 20-year designation period. Such zones shall be governed by the provisions of Chapter 49 (§ [59.1-538](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-538/) et seq.).

1995, c. [792](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0792); 2005, cc. [863](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0863), [884](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0884).
