# ยง 55.1-1600 Form of a lease.

A lease may be made in a form substantially similar to the following: "This lease, made the \_\_\_\_ day of \_\_\_\_\_\_, in the year \_\_\_\_, between (herein insert the names of parties), witnesseth: that the said \_\_\_\_\_\_ does (or do) demise unto the said \_\_\_\_\_\_, his personal representative and assigns, all (here describe the property) from the \_\_\_\_ day of \_\_\_\_\_\_, for the term of \_\_\_\_\_\_, thence ensuing, yielding therefor during the said term the rent of (here state the rent and mode of payment). Witness the following signature (or signatures)."
