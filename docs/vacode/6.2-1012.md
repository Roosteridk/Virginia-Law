# § 6.2-1012 Suspension or prohibition of trust institutions.

The Commission may prohibit or suspend from engaging in trust business any (i) trust company that fails to comply with any of the provisions of § [6.2-1005](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1005/), [6.2-1006](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1006/), or [6.2-1008](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1008/); (ii) bank doing a trust business that fails to comply with any of the provisions of § [6.2-821](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-821/), [6.2-1005](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1005/), [6.2-1006](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1006/), or [6.2-1008](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1008/); or (iii) trust subsidiary that fails to comply with the provisions of § [6.2-1006](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1006/) or [6.2-1008](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1008/).

Code 1950, § 6-103; 1966, c. 584, § 6.1-32; 1974, c. 665; 2010, c. [794](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794).
