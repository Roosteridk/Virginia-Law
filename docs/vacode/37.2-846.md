# § 37.2-846 Procedure when person not confined in facility or other institution.

A. In all cases, other than those provided for in § [37.2-845](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-845/), the person may file his petition in the circuit court of the county or the city in which he resides or in which he was found to have a mental illness or in which an order was entered authorizing his continued involuntary inpatient treatment, pursuant to Article 5 (§ [37.2-814](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-814/) et seq.) of Chapter 8 of this title.

B. Any proceeding to challenge the continued secure inpatient treatment of any person held in custody as a sexually violent predator under Chapter 9 (§ [37.2-900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-900/) et seq.) of this title shall be conducted in the circuit court wherein the person was last convicted of a sexually violent offense or wherein the defendant was deemed unrestorably incompetent and referred for commitment pursuant to § [19.2-169.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-169.3/).
