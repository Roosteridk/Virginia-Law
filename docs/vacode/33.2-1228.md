# § 33.2-1228 Disposition of fees.

All moneys received by the Commissioner of Highways under the provisions of this article shall be paid by him into the state treasury, except as provided in §§ [33.2-1224](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-1224/) and [33.2-1229](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-1229/), and allocated to the Board for use in the regulation and control of outdoor advertising and landscaping of highways.

Code 1950, § 33-322; 1964, c. 619; 1970, c. 322, § 33.1-376; 2014, c. [805](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805).
