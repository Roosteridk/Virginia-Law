# ยง 54.1-2408.3 Practice pending appeal.

Any practitioner or entity whose license, certificate, registration, or permit is suspended or revoked by a health regulatory board of the Department of Health Professions shall not engage in practice in the Commonwealth pending appeal of the board's order.

2013, c. [115](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0115).
