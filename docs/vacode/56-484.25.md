# § 56-484.25 Exemption for certain counties.

Notwithstanding any provision of this article to the contrary, the provisions of §§ [56-484.22](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-484.22/) and [56-484.23](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-484.23/) shall not apply with respect to any multiline telephone system located in a county that is not served by an enhanced 9-1-1 service system, until the later to occur of (i) 120 days after the date an enhanced 9-1-1 service system for the county commences operating or (ii) July 1, 2009.

2007, c. [427](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0427).
