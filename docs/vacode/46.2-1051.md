# § 46.2-1051 Local ordinances; vehicle exhaust.

A. The governing body of any county, city, or town may, by ordinance, regulate noise from a vehicle operated on a highway that is not equipped with a muffler and exhaust system conforming to §§ [46.2-1047](/vacode/46.2-1047/) and [46.2-1049](/vacode/46.2-1049/).

B. The provisions of subsection E of § [46.2-1300](/vacode/46.2-1300/) shall not apply to ordinances adopted pursuant to this section.
