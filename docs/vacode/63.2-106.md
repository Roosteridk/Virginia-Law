# § 63.2-106 Failure to obey subpoena or charging illegal fees; penalty.

If any person fails or refuses to obey any subpoena issued under the provisions of § [63.2-220](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-220/) or § [63.2-322](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-322/), or charges or receives any fee contrary to the provisions of § [63.2-508](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-508/), he shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.

Code 1950, §§ 63-139, 63-160, 63-203, 63-219; 1968, c. 578, § 63.1-125; 2002, c. [747](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0747).
