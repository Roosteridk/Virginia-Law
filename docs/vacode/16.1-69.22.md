# § 16.1-69.22 Removal of judges and substitute judges.

Any judge or substitute judge of a district court may be removed from office in the manner and for any of the causes prescribed in Chapter 9 (§ [17.1-900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-900/) et seq.) of Title 17.1; provided, that substitute judges may be removed from office under the provisions of §§ [24.2-230](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-230/) through [24.2-238](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-238/).

1972, c. 708; 1973, c. 546.
