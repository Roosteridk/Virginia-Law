# § 15.2-2255 Administration and enforcement of regulations.

The administration and enforcement of subdivision regulations insofar as they pertain to public improvements as authorized in §§ [15.2-2241](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2241/) through [15.2-2245](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2245/) shall be vested in the governing body of the locality in which the improvements are or will be located.

Except as provided above, the governing body shall be responsible for administering and enforcing the provisions of the subdivision regulations through its local planning commission or otherwise.

Code 1950, §§ 15-788.1, 15-967.9; 1962, c. 407, § 15.1-474; 1975, c. 641; 1997, c. 587.
