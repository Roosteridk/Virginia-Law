# ยง 20-121.01 Decree of divorce from bonds of matrimony without decree from bed and board.

In any case where willful desertion or cruelty is the ground for divorce and the bill of complaint prays for a divorce from bed and board the court may enter a decree of divorce from the bonds of matrimony without the entry of a decree from bed and board if the statutory period, as set out in ยง [20-121](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-121/), has elapsed prior to the entry of said decree and if the court shall be of the opinion that no reconciliation has taken place, or is probable.
