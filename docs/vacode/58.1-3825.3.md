# ยง 58.1-3825.3 (Effective September 1, 2021) Additional transient occupancy tax in Arlington County.

In addition to the transient occupancy tax authorized by ยง [58.1-3819](/vacode/58.1-3819/), Arlington County may impose an additional transient occupancy tax not to exceed one-fourth of one percent of the total price paid by the customer for the use or possession of any room or space occupied in a retail sale.
