B. Any person who attempts to commit any act prohibited by this article is guilty of a criminal offense and shall be punished as provided in ยง [18.2-26](/vacode/18.2-26/), [18.2-27](/vacode/18.2-27/), or [18.2-28](/vacode/18.2-28/), as appropriate.
