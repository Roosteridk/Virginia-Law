# § 20-88.34:1 Application of chapter to resident of foreign country and foreign support proceeding.

A. A tribunal of the Commonwealth shall apply Articles 1 through 9 (§ [20-88.32](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.32/) et seq.) and, as applicable, Article 13 (§ [20-88.83](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.83/) et seq.) to a support proceeding involving a foreign support order, a foreign tribunal, or an obligee, obligor, or child residing in a foreign country.

B. A tribunal of the Commonwealth that is requested to recognize and enforce a support order on the basis of comity may apply the procedural and substantive provisions of Articles 1 through 9 (§ [20-88.32](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.32/) et seq.).

C. Article 13 (§ [20-88.83](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.83/) et seq.) applies only to a support proceeding under the Convention. In such a proceeding, if a provision of Article 13 is inconsistent with Articles 1 through 9 (§ [20-88.32](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.32/) et seq.), Article 13 controls.
