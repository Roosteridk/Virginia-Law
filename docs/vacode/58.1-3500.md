# § 58.1-3500 Defined and segregated for local taxation.

Tangible personal property shall consist of all personal property not otherwise classified by (i) § [58.1-1100](/vacode/58.1-1100/) as intangible personal property, (ii) § [58.1-3510](/vacode/58.1-3510/) as merchants' capital, or (iii) § [58.1-3510.4](/vacode/58.1-3510.4/) as short-term rental property. "Tangible personal property" does not include fixtures, as defined in § [58.1-3295.3](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title58.1/chapter32/section58.1-3295.3/), if such fixtures are taxed in accordance with § [58.1-3295.3](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title58.1/chapter32/section58.1-3295.3/). Such tangible personal property is hereby segregated for and made subject to local taxation only pursuant to Article X, § 4 of the Constitution of Virginia.
