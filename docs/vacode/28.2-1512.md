# § 28.2-1512 Effect of disability.

The provisions of § [8.01-237](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-237/) shall apply with respect to the effect of disabilities on the preservation of a right to bring an action to establish ownership of land pursuant to §§ [28.2-1509](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-1509/) and [28.2-1511](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-1511/).

1995, c. [850](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0850).
