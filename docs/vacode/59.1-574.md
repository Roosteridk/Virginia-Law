# ยง 59.1-574 Local regulation prohibited unless identical.

No locality may establish or continue any regulation relating to cosmetic animal testing that is not identical to the provisions set forth in this chapter.

2021, Sp. Sess. I, cc. [113](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0113), [114](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0114).
