C. The corporation may comply with a shareholder's demand to inspect the record of shareholders under subdivision C 3 of ยง [13.1-771](/vacode/13.1-771/) by providing the shareholder with a list of its shareholders that was compiled no earlier than the date of delivery of the shareholder's demand.
