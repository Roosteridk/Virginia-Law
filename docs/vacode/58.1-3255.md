# § 58.1-3255 General reassessment every four years not required in certain counties.

The governing body of any county which established a department of real estate assessments and provided for annual assessment and reassessment and equalization of assessments of real estate as provided in §§ [15.2-716](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-716/) and [15.2-716.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-716.1/) shall not be required to undertake general reassessments of real estate every four years as otherwise provided in this article.

Code 1950, § 58-784.5; 1973, c. 152; 1976, c. 717; 1984, c. 675; 2010, cc. [154](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0154), [199](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0199).
