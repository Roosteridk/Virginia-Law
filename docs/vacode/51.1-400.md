# § 51.1-400 Definitions.

The definitions listed in §§ [51.1-124.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-124.3/), [51.1-201](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-201/), and [51.1-301](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-301/) shall apply to this chapter except as otherwise provided.

1990, c. 832.
