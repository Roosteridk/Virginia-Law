# § 46.2-1099 Further exemptions.

This article shall not apply to:

The transporting of any child in a vehicle having an interior design which makes the use of such device impractical; or

The transporting of children by public transportation, bus, school bus, or farm vehicle.

For the purposes of this section, "farm vehicle" means a vehicle which is either (i) exempt from registration pursuant to §§ [46.2-664](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-664/), [46.2-665](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-665/), [46.2-666](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-666/), [46.2-667](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-667/), [46.2-670](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-670/), or § [46.2-672](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-672/), (ii) registered as a farm vehicle pursuant to § [46.2-698](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-698/), or (iii) owned by a resident of another state under whose laws the vehicle is either registered as a farm vehicle or exempt from registration by virtue of its use as a farm vehicle.

1982, c. 634, § 46.1-314.6; 1989, c. 727; 1992, cc. 119, 405; 1993, c. 181.
