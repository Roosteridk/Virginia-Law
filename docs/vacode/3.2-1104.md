# § 3.2-1104 Preemption by federal law.

No provision of Chapter 12 (§ [3.2-1200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-1200/) et seq.), Chapter 13 (§ [3.2-1300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-1300/) et seq.), Chapters 15 (§ [3.2-1500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-1500/) et seq.) through 21 (§ [3.2-2100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-2100/) et seq.), Chapter 23 (§ [3.2-2300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-2300/) et seq.), or Chapter 24 (§ [3.2-2400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-2400/) et seq.) that is expressly preempted by a federal act or agreement governing commodity assessments shall be enforced or imposed until the termination of such act or agreement.

2016, c. [565](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0565).
