# ยง 64.2-1034 Scope.

Except as otherwise provided in the terms of the trust or this chapter, this chapter applies to:

1. A trust or estate; and

2. A life estate or other term interest in which the interest of one or more persons will be succeeded by the interest of one or more other persons.

2022, c. [354](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0354).
