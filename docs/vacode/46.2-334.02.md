# § 46.2-334.02 Licenses issued to persons less than twenty years old subject to certain restrictions.

Notwithstanding the provisions of § [46.2-498](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-498/), whenever the driving record of a person who is at least eighteen years old but less than twenty years old shows that he has been convicted of (i) an offense for which demerit points have been assessed or are assessable under Article 19 (§ [46.2-489](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-489/) et seq.) of this chapter or (ii) a violation of any provision of Article 12 (§ [46.2-1091](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1091/) et seq.) or Article 13 (§ [46.2-1095](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1095/) et seq.) of Chapter 10 of this title, the Commissioner shall direct such person to attend a driver improvement clinic.

2001, cc. [659](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0659), [665](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0665).
