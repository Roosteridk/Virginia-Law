# § 38.2-4123 Exemptions.

Except as herein provided, societies shall be governed by this chapter and §§ [38.2-100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-100/) through [38.2-134](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-134/), Chapters 2 (§ [38.2-200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-200/) et seq.) through 9 (§ [38.2-900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-900/) et seq.), §§ [38.2-1300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1300/) through [38.2-1315](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1315/), [38.2-1315.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1315.1/), [38.2-1317](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1317/) through [38.2-1340](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1340/), and [38.2-1367](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1367/), Chapters 14 (§ [38.2-1400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1400/) et seq.), 15 (§ [38.2-1500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1500/) et seq.), and 18 (§ [38.2-1800](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1800/) et seq.), §§ [38.2-3100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3100/) through [38.2-3125](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3125/) and [38.2-3300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3300/) through [38.2-3317](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3317/), Chapter 34 (§ [38.2-3400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3400/) et seq.), §§ [38.2-3500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3500/) through [38.2-3520](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3520/), Chapter 36 (§ [38.2-3600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3600/) et seq.), Chapter 52 (§ [38.2-5200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5200/) et seq.), and Chapter 55 (§ [38.2-5500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5500/) et seq.), and shall be exempt from all other provisions of this title unless expressly designated therein, or unless they are specifically made applicable by this chapter.
