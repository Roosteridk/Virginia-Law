# § 33.2-114 Virginia Aviation Board and Virginia Port Authority powers.

The powers of the Virginia Aviation Board set out in Chapter 1 (§ [5.1-1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-1/) et seq.) of Title 5.1 and the Virginia Port Authority set out in Chapter 10 (§ [62.1-128](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-128/) et seq.) of Title 62.1 are in no way diminished by the provisions of this title.
