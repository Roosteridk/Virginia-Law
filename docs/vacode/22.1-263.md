# § 22.1-263 Violation constitutes misdemeanor.

Any person violating the provisions of either § [22.1-254](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/), except for clause (ii) of subsection A, §§ [22.1-255](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-255/), [22.1-258](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-258/), [22.1-267](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-267/), or the parental responsibility provisions relating to compulsory school attendance included in § [22.1-279.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-279.3/), shall be guilty of a Class 3 misdemeanor. Upon a finding that a person knowingly and willfully violated any provision of § [22.1-254](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/), except for clause (ii) of subsection A, or any provision of §§ [22.1-255](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-255/), [22.1-258](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-258/), or § [22.1-267](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-267/) and that such person has been convicted previously of a violation of any provision of § [22.1-254](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-254/), except for clause (ii) of subsection A, or any provision of §§ [22.1-255](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-255/), [22.1-258](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-258/) or § [22.1-267](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-267/), such person shall be guilty of a Class 2 misdemeanor.
