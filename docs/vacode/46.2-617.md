# § 46.2-617 Sale of vehicle without certificate of title.

Except as provided in §§ [46.2-644.03](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-644.03/) and [58.1-3942](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3942/), any person who sells, trades, exchanges, or barters a motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer in the Commonwealth without first having secured a certificate of title for it or without legally having in his possession a certificate of title for the vehicle issued to its owner, except as otherwise provided in this title, shall be guilty of a Class 3 misdemeanor.

Code 1950, § 46-7; 1958, c. 541, § 46.1-88; 1968, c. 605; 1978, c. 605; 1988, c. 363; 1989, c. 727; 2009, c. [664](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0664); 2012, c. [623](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0623).
