# § 59.1-127 Violation of § 59.1-125 or § 59.1-126 a misdemeanor; revocation of dealer's license.

Any person violating any of the provisions of § [59.1-125](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-125/) or § [59.1-126](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-126/) shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Code 1950, § 59-155; 1958, c. 614; 1968, c. 439; 1972, c. 598; 1988, c. 765.
