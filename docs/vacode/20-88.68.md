# ยง 20-88.68 Effect of registration for enforcement.

A. A support order or income-withholding order issued in another state or a foreign support order is registered when the order is filed in the registering tribunal of the Commonwealth.

B. A registered support order issued in another state or in a foreign country is enforceable in the same manner and is subject to the same procedures as an order issued by a tribunal of the Commonwealth.

C. Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, a tribunal of the Commonwealth shall recognize and enforce, but may not modify, a registered order if the issuing tribunal had jurisdiction.

1994, c. [673](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0673); 2015, c. [727](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0727).
