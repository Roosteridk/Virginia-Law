# ยง 64.2-629 Notice, delivery, acceptance, consideration not required.

A transfer on death deed is effective without:

1. Notice or delivery to or acceptance by the designated beneficiary during the transferor's life; or

2. Consideration.

2013, c. [390](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0390).
