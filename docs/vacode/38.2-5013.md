# ยง 38.2-5013 Limitation on claims.

Any claim under this chapter that is filed more than ten years after the birth of an infant alleged to have a birth-related neurological injury is barred.

1987, c. 540; 1999, c. [806](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0806); 2006, c. [919](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0919).
