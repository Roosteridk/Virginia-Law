# § 18.2-380 Punishment for first offense.

Any person, firm, association or corporation convicted for the first time of an offense under §§ [18.2-374](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-374/), [18.2-375](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-375/), [18.2-376](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-376/), [18.2-377](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-377/), [18.2-378](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-378/) or § [18.2-379](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-379/), shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.

Code 1950, § 18.1-235.1; 1968, c. 662; 1975, cc. 14, 15; 1983, c. 412; 1985, c. 279.
