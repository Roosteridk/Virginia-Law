# § 55.1-2817 No authority to adopt more stringent fence laws.

Nothing in § [55.1-2814](/vacode/55.1-2814/) shall authorize or require the boards of supervisors or other governing bodies of counties to declare a more stringent fence as a lawful fence for any county, magisterial district, or selected portion of any county than as prescribed by § [55.1-2804](/vacode/55.1-2804/).

Code 1950, § 8-883; 1977, c. 624, § 55-313; 2019, c. [712](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712).
