# ยง 18.2-190.4 Manufacture of an unlawful electronic communication device; penalty.

A person who knowingly manufactures an unlawful electronic communication device shall be guilty of a Class 6 felony.

1993, c. 439; 1998, c. [518](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0518); 2002, c. [671](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0671); 2003, c. [354](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0354).
