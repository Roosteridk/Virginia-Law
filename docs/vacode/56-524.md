# § 56-524 Penalties.

Any person violating any of the provisions of §§ [56-512](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-512/), [56-515](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-515/) or § [56-517](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-517/) shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and upon conviction of same shall be fined not less than $10 nor more than $1,000, and may be imprisoned in jail for not more than 12 months.

1952, c. 696.
