# § 52-28.1 Reporting of gang-related criminal information; inclusion in annual Crime in Virginia report.

The Department of State Police shall include arrest statistics for violations of §§ [18.2-46.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.2/), [18.2-46.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3/), [18.2-46.3:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3:1/), [18.2-46.3:3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3:3/), and [18.2-55.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-55.1/) in the annual Crime in Virginia report.

2008, c. [746](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0746).
