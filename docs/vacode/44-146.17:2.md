# ยง 44-146.17:2 Annual statewide drill.

The Governor shall conduct an annual statewide drill on response to a large-scale disaster, including electrical power outages. Such drill shall include the participation of local governments, affected state agencies, public utilities, law-enforcement agencies, and other entities as determined by the Governor.

2004, c. [430](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0430); 2019, c. [615](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0615).
