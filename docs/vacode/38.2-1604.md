# § 38.2-1604 Association created; members; divided into three accounts.

The nonprofit unincorporated legal entity known as the Virginia Property and Casualty Insurance Guaranty Association, created by former § 38.1-761, shall continue in existence. All insurers defined as "member insurers" under § [38.2-1603](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1603/) shall be and remain members of the Association as a condition of their license to transact the business of insurance in this Commonwealth. The Association shall perform its functions under a plan of operation established and approved under § [38.2-1607](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1607/) and shall exercise its powers through a board of directors established under § [38.2-1605](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1605/). For purposes of administration and assessment, the Association shall have three separate accounts: (i) the workers' compensation insurance account; (ii) the automobile insurance account; and (iii) the account for all other insurance to which this chapter applies. These accounts shall be in addition to and separate from the safety fund authorized by § [38.2-1619](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1619/).

1970, c. 766, § 38.1-761; 1986, c. 562; 1998, c. [230](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0230).
