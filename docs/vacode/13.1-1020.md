# ยง 13.1-1020 Parties to actions.

A member of a limited liability company, solely by reason of being a member, is not a proper party to a proceeding by or against a limited liability company, except where (i) the object is to enforce a member's right against or liability to the limited liability company or (ii) as provided in Article 8 (ยง [13.1-1042](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1042/) et seq.) of this chapter.
