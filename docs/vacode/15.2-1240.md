# § 15.2-1240 Violation of § 15.2-1238 or § 15.2-1239 a misdemeanor.

Any violation of § [15.2-1238](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1238/) or § [15.2-1239](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1239/) shall be a misdemeanor and shall be punishable as provided by § [18.2-12](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-12/).

Code 1950, § 15-548; 1960, c. 360; 1962, c. 623, § 15.1-112; 1997, c. 587.
