# § 58.1-1718 City or county probate tax.

In addition to the state tax and fee imposed by §§ [58.1-1712](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1712/) and [58.1-1717.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1717.1/), the governing body of any county and the council of any city may, as provided in § [58.1-3805](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3805/), (i) impose a county or city tax in an amount equal to one-third of the amount of the state tax on the probate of a will or grant of administration on the probate of every such will or grant of administration and (ii) charge a $25 fee for the recordation of a list of heirs pursuant to § [64.2-509](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-509/) or an affidavit pursuant to § [64.2-510](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-510/), as provided in § [58.1-1717.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1717.1/).

Code 1950, § 58-67.1; 1960, c. 60; 1984, c. 675; 2010, c. [266](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0266).
