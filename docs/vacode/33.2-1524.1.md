# § 33.2-1524.1 Transportation Trust Fund.

There is hereby created in the Department of Treasury a special nonreverting fund to be known as the Transportation Trust Fund, consisting of funds distributed from the Commonwealth Transportation Fund pursuant to § [33.2-1524](/vacode/33.2-1524/). The revenues deposited pursuant to subdivision B 1 of § [33.2-1524](/vacode/33.2-1524/) shall be distributed during the year to result in the following:

1. For construction programs pursuant to § [33.2-358](/vacode/33.2-358/), 53 percent;

2. To the Commonwealth Mass Transit Fund established pursuant to § [33.2-1526](/vacode/33.2-1526/), 23 percent;

3. To the Commonwealth Rail Fund established pursuant to § [33.2-1526.4](/vacode/33.2-1526.4/), 7.5 percent;

4. To the Commonwealth Port Fund established pursuant to § [33.2-1526.5](/vacode/33.2-1526.5/), 2.5 percent;

5. To the Commonwealth Aviation Fund established pursuant to § [33.2-1526.6](/vacode/33.2-1526.6/), 1.5 percent;

6. To the Commonwealth Space Flight Fund established pursuant to § [33.2-1526.7](/vacode/33.2-1526.7/), one percent;

7. To the Priority Transportation Fund established pursuant to § [33.2-1527](/vacode/33.2-1527/), 10.5 percent; and

8. To a special fund within the Commonwealth Transportation Fund in the state treasury, one percent to be used to meet the necessary expenses of the Department of Motor Vehicles.
