# § 18.2-474.2 Bribery in correctional facilities; penalty.

A. Any person who receives any pecuniary benefit or other consideration to act in violation of § [18.2-474](/vacode/18.2-474/) or [18.2-474.1](/vacode/18.2-474.1/) is guilty of bribery, punishable as a Class 4 felony.

B. Any law-enforcement officer as defined in § [9.1-101](/vacode/9.1-101/), jail officer as defined in § [53.1-1](/vacode/53.1-1/), or correctional officer as defined in § [53.1-1](/vacode/53.1-1/) who violates this section shall be decertified in accordance with § [15.2-1707](/vacode/15.2-1707/), if applicable, and shall be forever ineligible for reemployment as a law-enforcement officer, jail officer, or correctional officer in the Commonwealth.

2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. [289](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0289).
