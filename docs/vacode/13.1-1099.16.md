# § 13.1-1099.16 Merger authorized; parties restricted.

A series limited liability company may be party to a merger in accordance with Article 13 (§ [13.1-1069.1](/vacode/13.1-1069.1/) et seq.), this section, and §§ [13.1-1099.17](/vacode/13.1-1099.17/) through [13.1-1099.20](/vacode/13.1-1099.20/) only if:

1. Each party to the merger is a limited liability company; and

2. The surviving company is not created in the merger.

2019, c. [636](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0636).
