# § 32.1-175.01 Issuance of special orders.

Notwithstanding any other provision of law and to the extent consistent with federal requirements, following a proceeding as provided in § [2.2-4019](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4019/), the Board may issue a special order that may include a civil penalty against an owner who violates this article or any order or regulation adopted thereto by the Board. The issuance of a special order shall be considered a case decision as defined in § [2.2-4001](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4001/). Civil penalties collected pursuant to this section shall be paid into the state treasury and credited to the Water Supply Assistance Grant Fund created pursuant to § [32.1-171.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/32.1-171.2/).

1997, c. [342](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0342), § 32.1-176.1:1; 1999, c. [786](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0786).
