# § 18.2-136.1 Enforcement of §§ 18.2-131 through 18.2-135.

Conservation police officers, sheriffs and all other law-enforcement officers shall enforce the provisions of §§ [18.2-131](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-131/), [18.2-132](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-132/), [18.2-133](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-133/), [18.2-134](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-134/) and [18.2-135](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-135/).

1975, cc. 14, 15.
