# § 38.2-3919 Agents' licenses required.

A. Except as provided in subsection B, each individual who is a resident of this Commonwealth who desires to obtain a license to sell, solicit, or negotiate any of the classes of insurance specified in § [38.2-3902](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3902/) shall obtain that license only when that individual has passed a written examination prescribed by the Commission.

B. Any individual who is licensed prior to July 1, 1990, and whose license is restricted to the classes of insurance specified in Category A of § [38.2-3902](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3902/) shall be exempted from the written examination provision noted above.

C. Business entities, as defined in § [38.2-1800](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1800/), whether resident or nonresident, as well as nonresident individuals who desire to obtain a license to sell, solicit, or negotiate any of the classes of insurance specified in § [38.2-3902](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3902/) shall be eligible for licensing upon satisfaction of the requirements set forth in § [38.2-1836](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1836/).

1985, c. 400, § 38.1-549.20; 1986, c. 562; 2001, c. [706](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0706).
