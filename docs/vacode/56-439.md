# § 56-439 Application of §§ 56-436 through 56-438.

The provisions of §§ [56-436](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-436/) through [56-438](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-438/) shall not apply to any railroad company which has its line of road enclosed with fences and cattle guards, as required by law.

Code 1919, § 3997.
