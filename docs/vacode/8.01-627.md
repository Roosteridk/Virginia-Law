# § 8.01-627 To what clerk order for injunction directed.

Every order, awarding an injunction made under § [8.01-620](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-620/) or § [8.01-626](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-626/), shall be directed to the clerk of the court which has venue under § [8.01-261](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-261/) and the proceedings thereupon shall be as if the order has been made by such court.

Code 1950, § 8-619; 1977, c. 617.
