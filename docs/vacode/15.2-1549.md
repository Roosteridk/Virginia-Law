# § 15.2-1549 Investment of assets of trusts or equivalent arrangement for postemployment benefits other than pensions.

All funds appropriated to a trust or equivalent arrangement for postemployment benefits other than pensions, as defined in § [15.2-1545](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1545/), and all funds accrued from the investment of any such funds that are on hand at any time and are not necessary for immediate payment of benefits shall be invested by the finance board, by any person or entity serving as an alternative thereto pursuant to § [15.2-1547](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1547/), or by an investment manager who is serving as a trustee of the funds. All such funds shall be invested in accordance with the prudent person standard established by § [51.1-803](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-803/) and such investments shall not be limited by Chapter 45 (§ [2.2-4500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4500/) et seq.) of Title 2.2.
