# § 8.2A-104 Leases subject to other law.

(1) A lease, although subject to this title, is also subject to any applicable:

(a) Certificate of title statute of this Commonwealth such as Article 2 (§ [46.2-616](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-616/) et seq.) of Chapter 6 of Title 46.2 and Article 2.1 (§ [29.1-733.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-733.2/) et seq.) of Chapter 7 of Title 29.1;

(b) Certificate of title statute of another jurisdiction (§ [8.2A-105](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-105/)); or

(c) Consumer protection statute of this Commonwealth, such as Chapter 17 (§ [59.1-196](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-196/) et seq.) of Title 59.1, or final consumer protection decision of a court of this Commonwealth existing on the effective date of this title.

(2) In case of conflict between this title, other than §§ [8.2A-105](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-105/), [8.2A-304](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-304/) (3) and [8.2A-305](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-305/) (3), and a statute or decision referred to in subsection (1) of this section, the statute or decision controls.

(3) Failure to comply with an applicable law has only the effect specified therein.

1991, c. 536; 2013, c. [787](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0787).
