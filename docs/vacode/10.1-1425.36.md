# ยง 10.1-1425.36 Fees not authorized.

This article does not authorize the Department to impose a fee, including a recycling fee or registration fee, on a consumer, manufacturer, retailer, or person who recycles or reuses computer equipment.

2008, c. [541](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0541).
