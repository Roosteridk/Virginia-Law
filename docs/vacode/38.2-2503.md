# § 38.2-2503 Classes of insurance that may be written by mutual assessment property and casualty insurers; minimum surplus to policyholders required.

A. Any mutual assessment property and casualty insurer with surplus to policyholders of at least $25,000 may write the following classes:

1. Fire insurance as defined in § [38.2-110](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-110/);

2. Miscellaneous property damage insurance as defined in § [38.2-111](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-111/); and

3. Animal insurance as defined in § [38.2-116](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-116/).

B. Any mutual assessment property and casualty insurer with surplus to policyholders of at least $100,000 may write the following classes of insurance, in addition to those classes enumerated in subsection A of this section:

1. Water damage insurance as defined in § [38.2-112](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-112/);

2. Burglary and theft insurance as defined in § [38.2-113](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-113/);

3. Glass insurance as defined in § [38.2-114](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-114/);

4. Boiler and machinery insurance as defined in § [38.2-115](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-115/);

5. Personal injury liability insurance as defined in § [38.2-117](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-117/);

6. Property damage liability insurance as defined in § [38.2-118](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-118/);

7. Marine insurance as defined in § [38.2-126](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-126/);

8. Home protection insurance as defined in § [38.2-129](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-129/);

9. Homeowners insurance as defined in § [38.2-130](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-130/);

10. Farmowners insurance as defined in § [38.2-131](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-131/);

11. Commercial multi-peril insurance as defined in § [38.2-132](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-132/); and

12. Contingent and consequential losses insurance as defined in § [38.2-133](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-133/).

The liability coverages specified in this subsection may be written only by insurers having a surplus to policyholders of at least $300,000 unless the coverages are fully reinsured.

C. Any mutual assessment property and casualty insurer with surplus to policyholders of at least $800,000 may write the following classes of insurance, in addition to those classes enumerated in subsections A and B of this section:

1. Workers' compensation and employers' liability insurance as defined in § [38.2-119](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-119/);

2. Fidelity insurance as defined in § [38.2-120](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-120/);

3. Surety insurance as defined in § [38.2-121](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-121/);

4. Credit insurance as defined in § [38.2-122](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-122/);

5. Motor vehicle insurance as defined in § [38.2-124](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-124/);

6. Aircraft insurance as defined in § [38.2-125](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-125/);

7. Legal services insurance as defined in § [38.2-127](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-127/); and

8. Mortgage guaranty insurance as defined in § [38.2-128](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-128/).

Code 1950, §§ 38-523, 38-526, 38-529; 1952, c. 317, § 38.1-659; 1954, c. 161; 1960, c. 292; 1962, c. 172; 1974, c. 244; 1986, c. 562.
