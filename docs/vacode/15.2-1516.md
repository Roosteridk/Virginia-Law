# § 15.2-1516 Exceptions.

The provisions of §§ [15.2-1513](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1513/) through [15.2-1515](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1515/) shall not be applicable to constitutional officers or their employees or other officers elected by the voters.

1991, c. 234, § 15.1-20.6; 1997, c. 587.
