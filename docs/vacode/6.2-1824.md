# § 6.2-1824 Civil penalties.

In addition to the authority conferred under §§ [6.2-1821](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1821/) and [6.2-1822](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1822/), the Commission may impose a civil penalty not exceeding $1,000 upon any person who it determines, in proceedings commenced in accordance with the Commission's Rules, has violated any of the provisions of this chapter, the regulations adopted by the Commission pursuant thereto, or any other law or regulation applicable to the conduct of the lender's business. For the purposes of this section, each separate violation shall be subject to the civil penalty herein prescribed, and in the case of a violation of § [6.2-1801](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1801/), each loan made or arranged shall constitute a separate violation.
