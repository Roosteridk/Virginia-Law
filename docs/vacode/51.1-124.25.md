# ยง 51.1-124.25 Existing advisory committees of the Virginia Retirement System abolished.

As of March 25, 1994, the existing advisory committees of the Virginia Retirement System are abolished and the members are discharged from any further duties.

1994, cc. [4](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0004), [85](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0085).
