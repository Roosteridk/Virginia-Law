# § 54.1-3442.5 Definitions.

As used in this article:

"Botanical cannabis,""cannabis oil,""cannabis product," and "usable cannabis" have the same meanings as specified in § [54.1-3408.3](/vacode/54.1-3408.3/).

"Cannabis dispensing facility" means a facility that (i) has obtained a permit from the Board pursuant to § [54.1-3442.6](/vacode/54.1-3442.6/); (ii) is owned, at least in part, by a pharmaceutical processor; and (iii) dispenses cannabis products produced by a pharmaceutical processor to a patient, his registered agent, or, if such patient is a minor or a vulnerable adult as defined in § [18.2-369](/vacode/18.2-369/), such patient's parent or legal guardian.

"Designated caregiver facility" has the same meaning as defined in § [54.1-3408.3](/vacode/54.1-3408.3/).

"Pharmaceutical processor" means a facility that (i) has obtained a permit from the Board pursuant to § [54.1-3408.3](/vacode/54.1-3408.3/) and (ii) cultivates Cannabis plants intended only for the production of cannabis oil, botanical cannabis, and usable cannabis, produces cannabis products, and dispenses cannabis products to a patient pursuant to a written certification, his registered agent, or, if such patient is a minor or a vulnerable adult as defined in § [18.2-369](/vacode/18.2-369/), such patient's parent or legal guardian.

"Practitioner" has the same meaning as specified in § [54.1-3408.3](/vacode/54.1-3408.3/).

"Registered agent" has the same meaning as specified in § [54.1-3408.3](/vacode/54.1-3408.3/).
