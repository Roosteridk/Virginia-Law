# ยง 38.2-4506 Terms of participation.

Each dentist or optometrist participating in any plan shall do so in accordance with the terms and conditions imposed on other participating providers under similar circumstances. Participating providers shall have the right to engage in other practice. A nonstock corporation shall not engage in the practice of dentistry or optometry.
