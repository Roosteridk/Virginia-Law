# ยง 19.2-329 Allowance to witnesses.

Sections [17.1-612](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-612/) to [17.1-616](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-616/), inclusive, shall apply to a person attending as a witness, under a recognizance or summons in a criminal case, as well as to a person attending under a summons in a civil case, except that a person residing out of this Commonwealth, who attends a court therein as a witness, shall be allowed by the court a proper compensation for attendance and travel to and from the place of his abode, the amount of the same to be fixed by the court.
