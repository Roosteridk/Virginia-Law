# ยง 8.01-581.014 Court; jurisdiction.

The term "court" means a circuit court or general district court of the Commonwealth having jurisdiction over the subject matter of the controversy.

1986, c. 614; 1995, c. [342](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0342); 2016, c. [181](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0181).
