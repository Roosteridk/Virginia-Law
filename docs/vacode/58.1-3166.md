# § 58.1-3166 Substitute officer continues in office upon removal of predecessor.

The substitute officer, appointed pursuant to § [58.1-3169](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3169/), shall, upon the ouster of his predecessor, unless sooner removed by the Governor or under the provisions of §§ [24.2-230](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-230/) to [24.2-238](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-238/), continue to serve in such capacity during the remainder of the term of his predecessor and until his successor be elected or appointed and qualified.

Code 1950, § 58-956; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 1; 1984, c. 675.
