# § 64.2-1505 Investments that cease to be eligible may be retained.

Investments made under the provisions of § [64.2-1502](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1502/), if in conformity with the requirements of that section at the time the investments were made, may be retained even though they cease to be eligible for purchase under the provisions of that section, but shall be subject to the provisions of the Uniform Prudent Investor Act (§ [64.2-780](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-780/) et seq.).

Code 1919, § 5431; 1942, p. 662; Code 1950, § 26-44; 1992, c. 810; 1999, c. [772](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0772); 2012, c. [614](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614).
