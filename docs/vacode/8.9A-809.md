# ยง 8.9A-809 Priority.

The 2013 amendments determine the priority of conflicting claims to collateral. However, if the relative priorities of the claims were established before July 1, 2013, this title, as it was in effect on June 30, 2013, determines priority.

2012, c. [155](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0155).
