# ยง 20-88.74 Procedure to register child support order of another state for modification.

A party or support enforcement agency seeking to modify, or to modify and enforce, a child support order issued in another state shall register that order in this Commonwealth in the same manner as provided in Article 8 (ยง [20-88.66](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.66/) et seq.) if the order has not been registered.
