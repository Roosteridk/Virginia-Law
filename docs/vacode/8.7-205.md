# ยง 8.7-205 Title under warehouse receipt defeated in certain cases.

A buyer in the ordinary course of business of fungible goods sold and delivered by a warehouseman who is also in the business of buying and selling such goods takes the goods free of any claim under a warehouse receipt even though the receipt is negotiable and has been duly negotiated.

1964, c. 219; 2004, c. [200](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0200).
