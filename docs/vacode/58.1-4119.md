# ยง 58.1-4119 Application for service permit.

A. Any person desiring to obtain a service permit as required by this chapter shall apply on a form prescribed by the Department. The application shall be accompanied by a fee prescribed by the Department.

B. Any application filed hereunder shall be verified by the oath or affirmation of the applicant.
