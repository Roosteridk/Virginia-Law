# § 46.2-1504 Board's powers with respect to hearings under this chapter.

The Board may, in hearings arising under this chapter, except as provided for in Articles 7 (§ [46.2-1566](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1566/) et seq.), 7.2 (§ [46.2-1573.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1573.2/) et seq.), 7.3 (§ [46.2-1573.13](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1573.13/) et seq.), and 7.4 (§ [46.2-1573.25](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1573.25/) et seq.), determine the place in the Commonwealth where they shall be held; subpoena witnesses; take depositions of witnesses residing outside the Commonwealth in the manner provided for in civil actions in courts of record; pay these witnesses the fees and mileage for their attendance as is provided for witnesses in civil actions in courts of record; and administer oaths.

Code 1950, § 46-505; 1958, c. 541, § 46.1-518; 1989, c. 727; 1995, cc. [767](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0767), [816](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0816); 2015, c. [615](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0615).
