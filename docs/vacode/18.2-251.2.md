# § 18.2-251.2 Possession and distribution of flunitrazepam; enhanced penalty.

Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ [54.1-3446](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3446/) and [54.1-3452](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3452/), the drug flunitrazepam shall be deemed to be listed on Schedule I for the purposes of penalties for violations of the Drug Control Act (§ [54.1-3400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3400/) et seq.). Any person knowingly manufacturing, selling, giving, distributing or possessing the drug flunitrazepam shall be punished under the penalties prescribed for such violations in accordance with §§ [18.2-248](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-248/) and [18.2-250](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-250/).

1997, c. [595](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0595).
