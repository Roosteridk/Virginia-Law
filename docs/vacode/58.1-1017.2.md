# ยง 58.1-1017.2 Sealed pack labeled as cigarettes; prima facie evidence of cigarettes.

In any prosecution for violations of this title, where a sealed pack is labeled as containing cigarettes, such labeling shall be prima facie evidence that the contents of the pack meet the definition of "cigarette" as defined by ยง [58.1-1000](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1000/). Nothing shall preclude the introduction of other relevant evidence to establish the contents of a pack, whether sealed or not.
