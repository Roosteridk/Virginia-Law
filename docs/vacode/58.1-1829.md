# § 58.1-1829 Costs in proceedings under §§ 58.1-1825 through 58.1-1828.

If the final order of the court in any proceeding under §§ [58.1-1825](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1825/) through [58.1-1828](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1828/) grants the relief prayed for, no costs shall be taxed against the applicant; but in no event shall any costs be taxed against the Commonwealth in any proceeding under such sections.

Code 1950, §§ 58-1139, 58-1157; 1984, c. 675.
