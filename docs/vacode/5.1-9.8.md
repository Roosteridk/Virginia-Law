# § 5.1-9.8 Same; effect of failure to give or maintain adequate security.

Failure of any contract carrier holding a registration or permit issued by and under the authority of the Department to comply with any of the provisions of §§ [5.1-9.5](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-9.5/) through [5.1-9.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-9.7/) shall be a Class 1 misdemeanor and punishable as such.

1980, c. 721; 2017, c. [793](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0793).
