# § 2.2-4102 Registrar of Regulations; publications.

The Division of Legislative Services shall employ a professionally experienced or trained Registrar of Regulations. Under the direction of the Commission or the Director of the Division of Legislative Services, the Registrar shall perform the duties required by this chapter or assigned by the Commission or the Director of the Division of Legislative Services in accordance with Chapter 40 (§ [2.2-4000](/vacode/2.2-4000/) et seq.), this chapter, or Chapter 15 (§ [30-145](/vacode/30-145/) et seq.) of Title 30. The Commission shall provide for the compilation and publication of the Virginia Register of Regulations and the Virginia Administrative Code pursuant to §§ [2.2-4031](/vacode/2.2-4031/) and [30-146](/vacode/30-146/).
