# § 19.2-18 Powers and duties generally.

Every conservator of the peace shall have authority to arrest without a warrant in such instances as are set out in §§ [19.2-19](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-19/) and [19.2-81](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-81/). Upon making an arrest without a warrant, the conservator of the peace shall proceed in accordance with the provisions of § [19.2-22](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-22/) or § [19.2-82](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-82/) as the case may be.

Code 1950, § 19.1-20; 1960, c. 366; 1968, c. 639; 1972, c. 549; 1975, c. 495.
