# § 28.2-815 Application for special permit.

Application for the special permit provided for in §§ [28.2-810](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-810/) and [28.2-811](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-811/) shall be made on forms provided by the Commissioner of Marine Resources before the removal, transportation or relaying of shellfish from condemned areas. This permit shall not be transferable.

Code 1950, § 28-162.1; 1960, c. 517; 1962, c. 406, § 28.1-179; 1966, c. 684; 1968, c. 745; 1979, c. 274; 1981, c. 52; 1986, c. 184; 1988, c. 600; 1992, c. 836.
