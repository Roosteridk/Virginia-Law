# § 38.2-5113 Financial responsibility policy form or coverage requirements.

Any risk retention group and any insurer who transacts the business of insurance in this Commonwealth with a purchasing group or its members shall not be exempt from the policy form or coverage requirements of this Commonwealth's motor vehicle financial responsibility insurance law. For the purpose of this section, any risk retention group and any insurer who transacts the business of insurance in this Commonwealth with a purchasing group or its members shall comply with §§ [38.2-2200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2200/) through [38.2-2207](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2207/), §§ [38.2-2209](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2209/), [38.2-2211](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2211/), [38.2-2216](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2216/), and §§ [38.2-2218](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2218/) through [38.2-2225](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2225/) and any other applicable law of this Commonwealth.

1987, c. 585.
