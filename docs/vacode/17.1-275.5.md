# § 17.1-275.5 Amounts to be added; judgment in favor of the Commonwealth.

A. The clerk shall assess, in addition to the fees provided for by § [17.1-275.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/), [17.1-275.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/), [17.1-275.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.3/), [17.1-275.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.4/), [17.1-275.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.7/), [17.1-275.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/), [17.1-275.9](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.9/), [17.1-275.10](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.10/), [17.1-275.11](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11/), [17.1-275.11:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11:1/), or [17.1-275.12](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.12/), the following costs:

1. Any amount paid by the Commonwealth for legal representation of the defendant;

2. Any amount paid for trial transcripts;

3. Extradition costs;

4. Costs of psychiatric evaluation;

5. Costs taxed against the defendant as appellant under Rule 5A:30 of the Rules of the Supreme Court;

6. Any fee for a returned check or disallowed credit card charge assessed pursuant to subdivision A 28 of § [17.1-275](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275/);

7. Any jury costs;

8. Any assessment made pursuant to subdivision A 10 of § [17.1-275](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275/);

9. Any fees prescribed in §§ [18.2-268.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-268.8/) and [46.2-341.26:8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.26:8/);

10. Any court costs related to an ignition interlock device;

11. Any fee for testing for HIV;

12. Any fee for processing an individual admitted to jail as prescribed in § [15.2-1613.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1613.1/);

13. Any fee for courthouse security personnel as prescribed in § [53.1-120](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-120/);

14. Any fee for a DNA sample as prescribed in § [19.2-310.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-310.2/);

15. Reimbursement to the Commonwealth of medical fees as prescribed in § [19.2-165.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-165.1/);

16. Any fee for a local criminal justice training academy as prescribed in § [9.1-106](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-106/);

17. Any fee prescribed by §§ [16.1-69.48:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/).01 and [17.1-275.11](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11/);

18. Any expenses charged pursuant to subsection B or F of § [19.2-187.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-187.1/); and

19. Any fee for an electronic summons system as prescribed in § [17.1-279.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-279.1/).

B. The total amount of assessments described in subsection A, including (i) the fees provided for by § [17.1-275.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/), [17.1-275.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/), [17.1-275.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.3/), [17.1-275.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.4/), [17.1-275.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.7/), [17.1-275.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/), [17.1-275.9](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.9/), [17.1-275.10](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.10/), [17.1-275.11](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11/), [17.1-275.11:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.11:1/), or [17.1-275.12](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.12/) and (ii) all other fines and costs, shall be docketed by the clerk as a judgment against the defendant in favor of the Commonwealth in accordance with § [8.01-446](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-446/).
