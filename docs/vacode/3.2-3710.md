# § 3.2-3710 Disposition of funds.

All fees, penalties, funds (including those transferred as specified in subsection D of § [3.2-3709](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-3709/) and except as provided in subsection C of § [3.2-3709](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-3709/)), and assessments under this chapter that the Commissioner receives shall be paid into the Feed, Lime, Fertilizer, and Animal Remedies Fund, established in § [3.2-3617](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-3617/), to be used in carrying out the purpose and provisions of this chapter, to include inspection, sampling and other expenses; except that the Commissioner shall deposit, to the credit of the Virginia Agricultural Foundation Fund, five cents ($0.05) per ton of liming material sold per reporting year of the inspection fee.

1994, c. [649](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0649), § 3.1-126.12:3; 2008, c. [860](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860).
