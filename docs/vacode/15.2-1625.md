# § 15.2-1625 In what court motions may be made.

Any motion under either § [15.2-1623](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1623/) or § [15.2-1624](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1624/) may be made in the circuit court for the county or city in which the default or misconduct of the deputy occurred or was committed.

Code 1950, § 15-523; 1962, c. 623, § 15.1-88; 1997, c. 587.
