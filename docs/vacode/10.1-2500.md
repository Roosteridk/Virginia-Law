# § 10.1-2500 Virginia Environmental Emergency Response Fund established.

A. There is hereby established the Virginia Environmental Emergency Response Fund, hereafter referred to as the Fund, to be used (i) for the purpose of emergency response to environmental pollution incidents and for the development and implementation of corrective actions for pollution incidents, other than pollution incidents addressed through the Virginia Underground Petroleum Storage Tank Fund, as described in § [62.1-44.34:11](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.34:11/) of the State Water Control Law; (ii) to conduct assessments of potential sources of toxic contamination in accordance with the policy developed pursuant to § [62.1-44.19:10](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.19:10/); and (iii) to assist small businesses for the purposes described in § [10.1-1197.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1197.3/).

B. The Fund shall be a nonlapsing revolving fund consisting of grants, general funds, and other such moneys as appropriated by the General Assembly, and moneys received by the State Treasurer for:

1. Noncompliance penalties assessed pursuant to § [10.1-1311](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1311/), civil penalties assessed pursuant to subsection B of § [10.1-1316](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1316/), and civil charges assessed pursuant to subsection C of § [10.1-1316](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1316/).

2. Civil penalties assessed pursuant to subsection C of § [10.1-1418.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1418.1/), civil penalties assessed pursuant to subsections A and E of § [10.1-1455](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1455/), and civil charges assessed pursuant to subsection F of § [10.1-1455](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1455/).

3. (For contingent expiration date, see Acts 2016, cc. 68 and 758, as amended by Acts 2017, c. 345) Civil charges assessed pursuant to subdivision 8d of § [62.1-44.15](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.15/) and civil penalties assessed pursuant to subsection (a) of § [62.1-44.32](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.32/), excluding assessments made for violations of Article 9 (§ [62.1-44.34:8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.34:8/) et seq.) or 10 (§ [62.1-44.34:10](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.34:10/) et seq.), Chapter 3.1 of Title 62.1, or a regulation, administrative or judicial order, or term or condition of approval relating to or issued under those articles.

3. (For contingent effective date, see Acts 2016, cc. 68 and 758, as amended by Acts 2017, c. 345) Civil charges assessed pursuant to subdivision (8d) of § [62.1-44.15](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.15/) and civil penalties assessed pursuant to subsection (a) of § [62.1-44.32](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.32/), excluding assessments made for violations of Article 2.3 (§ [62.1-44.15:24](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.15:24/) et seq.), 2.4 (§ [62.1-44.15:51](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.15:51/) et seq.), 2.5 (§ [62.1-44.15:67](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.15:67/) et seq.), 9 (§ [62.1-44.34:8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.34:8/) et seq.), or 10 (§ [62.1-44.34:10](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.34:10/) et seq.) of Chapter 3.1 of Title 62.1, or a regulation, administrative or judicial order, or term or condition of approval relating to or issued under those articles.

4. Civil penalties and civil charges assessed pursuant to § [62.1-270](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-270/).

5. Civil penalties assessed pursuant to subsection A of § [62.1-252](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-252/) and civil charges assessed pursuant to subsection B of § [62.1-252](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-252/).

6. Civil penalties assessed in conjunction with special orders by the Director pursuant to § [10.1-1186](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1186/) and by the Waste Management Board pursuant to subsection G of § [10.1-1455](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-1455/).
