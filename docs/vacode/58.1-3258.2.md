The Department shall have the power to require remedial education, suspend, revoke, or deny renewal of the certificate of any supervisor, assessor or appraiser who is found to be in violation of the regulations established by the Department pursuant to ยง [58.1-3258.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3258.1/).
