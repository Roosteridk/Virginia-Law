# ยง 55.1-128 Prospective application.

Sections [55.1-124](/vacode/55.1-124/) through [55.1-129](/vacode/55.1-129/) apply to a nonvested property interest or a power of appointment that is created on or after July 1, 2000. For purposes of this section, a nonvested property interest or a power of appointment created by the exercise of a power of appointment is created when the power is irrevocably exercised or when a revocable exercise becomes irrevocable.
