# § 55.1-2607 Status of property loaned to or deposited with museum prior to July 1, 2002.

Except as otherwise provided in a written agreement between a lender and a museum, property loaned to or deposited with a museum prior to July 1, 2002, may be discarded or transferred to another museum located in Virginia, provided that (i) the notice provisions of §§ [55.1-2604](/vacode/55.1-2604/) and [55.1-2605](/vacode/55.1-2605/) have been complied with and (ii) such property is held by the museum receiving the transfer for at least three years before it sells or disposes of such property.

2005, c. [480](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0480), § 55-210.38; 2019, c. [712](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712).
