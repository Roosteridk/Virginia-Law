# § 54.1-2506.01 Investigation of reported violations.

The Department shall investigate all complaints that are within the jurisdiction of the relevant health regulatory board received from (i) the general public and (ii) all reports received pursuant to §§ [54.1-2400.6](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2400.6/), [54.1-2400.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2400.7/), [54.1-2709.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2709.3/), [54.1-2709.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2709.4/), [54.1-2908](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2908/), or § [54.1-2909](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2909/).
