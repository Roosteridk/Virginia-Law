# § 53.1-150 Contributions by persons on parole, probation, and work release.

A. Any person who has costs assessed against him pursuant to §§ [17.1-275.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/), [17.1-275.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/), [17.1-275.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.7/), or § [17.1-275.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/), or subsection B or C of § [16.1-69.48:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/) shall be required to pay, as specified in those sections, a sentencing/supervision fee to be deposited in the general fund of the state treasury.

All fees assessed pursuant to this section shall be paid to the clerk of the sentencing court.

B. Except when the fee referenced in subsection A has been previously assessed, any person (i) who is granted parole or (ii) who participates in a work release program pursuant to the provisions of §§ [53.1-60](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-60/) and [53.1-131](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-131/) shall be required to pay a fee of fifty dollars as a condition of parole or work release.
