# § 46.2-341.31 Driving commercial motor vehicle with any alcohol in blood.

No person shall drive a commercial motor vehicle while having any amount of alcohol in his blood, as measured by a test administered pursuant to the provisions of §§ [46.2-341.26:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.26:1/) through [46.2-341.26:11](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.26:11/). Any person found to have so driven a commercial motor vehicle shall be guilty of a traffic infraction.

1989, c. 705, § 46.1-372.29:1; 1990, c. 218; 1992, c. 830.
