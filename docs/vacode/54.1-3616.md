# ยง 54.1-3616 Use of title "Doctor.".

No person regulated under this chapter shall use the title "Doctor" or the abbreviation "Dr." in writing or in advertising in connection with his practice unless he simultaneously uses a clarifying title, initials, abbreviation or designation or language that identifies the type of practice for which he is licensed.

1996, cc. [937](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0937), [980](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0980).
