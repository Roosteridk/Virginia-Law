# § 58.1-815.4 (Contingent expiration dates) Distribution of recordation tax to the Commonwealth Transportation Fund.

Of the state recordation taxes imposed pursuant to §§ [58.1-801](/vacode/58.1-801/) and [58.1-803](/vacode/58.1-803/), the revenues collected each fiscal year from $0.03 of the total tax imposed under each section shall be deposited by the Comptroller into the Commonwealth Transportation Fund established pursuant to § [33.2-1524](/vacode/33.2-1524/).
