# § 15.2-2246 Site plans submitted in accordance with zoning ordinance.

Site plans or plans of development which are required to be submitted and approved in accordance with subdivision A 8 of § [15.2-2286](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2286/) shall be subject to the provisions of §§ [15.2-2241](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2241/) through [15.2-2245](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2245/), mutatis mutandis.
