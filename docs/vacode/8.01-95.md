# § 8.01-95 Procedure in such case.

The procedure in such suit and the investment of the proceeds of sale shall be in accordance with §§ [8.01-73](/vacode/8.01-73/), [8.01-75](/vacode/8.01-75/) and [8.01-76](/vacode/8.01-76/), so far as the same can be made applicable, and the court may, in its discretion, commute the life estate according to § [55.1-500](/vacode/55.1-500/). In the case of a lease, however, the rents may be made payable direct to the person or persons entitled thereto, for the time being.

Code 1950, § 8-703.2; 1977, c. 617.
