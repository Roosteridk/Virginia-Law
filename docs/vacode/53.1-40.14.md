# § 53.1-40.14 Reporting requirement.

The warden or other official in charge of a state correctional facility shall compile a monthly summary of all written reports received pursuant to §§ [53.1-25.1](/vacode/53.1-25.1/), [53.1-40.12](/vacode/53.1-40.12/), and [53.1-40.13](/vacode/53.1-40.13/) and shall submit the summary to the Director each month.

2020, c. [526](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0526).
