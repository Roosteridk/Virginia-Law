# § 25.1-235 Compensation of commissioners or jurors.

The commissioners or jurors summoned shall, for every day or portion thereof they may be employed in the performance of their duties, receive an allowance in the amount prescribed in § [17.1-618](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-618/) as compensation for their attendance, travel and other costs, to be paid by the petitioner.
