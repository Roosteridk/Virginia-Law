# § 2.2-2722 Compensation; expenses.

Legislative members of the Board shall receive such compensation as provided in § [30-19.12](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-19.12/), and nonlegislative citizen members shall receive such compensation for the performance of their duties as provided in § [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/). All members shall be reimbursed for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2825/). Funding for the costs of compensation and expenses of the members shall be provided from such funds as may be available to the Center.

2004, cc. [938](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0938), [964](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0964).
