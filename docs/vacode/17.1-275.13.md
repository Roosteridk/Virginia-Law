# § 17.1-275.13 Additional fee for offenses related to sex trafficking.

In addition to the fees provided for by §§ [17.1-275.1](/vacode/17.1-275.1/), [17.1-275.2](/vacode/17.1-275.2/), [17.1-275.7](/vacode/17.1-275.7/), [17.1-275.10](/vacode/17.1-275.10/), and [17.1-275.12](/vacode/17.1-275.12/), any person convicted of a misdemeanor violation of § [18.2-346.01](/vacode/18.2-346.01/) or of § [18.2-348](/vacode/18.2-348/) or [18.2-349](/vacode/18.2-349/) shall be ordered to pay a $100 fee, and any person convicted of a violation of clause (ii), (iii), or (iv) of § [18.2-48](/vacode/18.2-48/), or of § [18.2-368](/vacode/18.2-368/), or any felony violation of the laws pertaining to commercial sex trafficking or prostitution offenses pursuant to Article 3 (§ [18.2-346](/vacode/18.2-346/) et seq.) of Chapter 8, with the exception of § [18.2-361](/vacode/18.2-361/), shall be ordered to pay a $500 fee. All fees collected pursuant to this section shall be deposited into the Virginia Prevention of Sex Trafficking Fund to be used in accordance with § [9.1-116.4](/vacode/9.1-116.4/).

2019, c. [728](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0728); 2020, c. [122](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0122); 2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. [188](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0188).
