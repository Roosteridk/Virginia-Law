# § 23.1-2425 (Effective September 1, 2022) Confidential and public information.

A. The Authority is subject to the provisions of the Virginia Freedom of Information Act (§ [2.2-3700](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3700/) et seq.), including the exclusions set forth in subdivision 14 of § [2.2-3705.7](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3705.7/) and subdivision A 23 of § [2.2-3711](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3711/).

B. For purposes of the Virginia Freedom of Information Act (§ [2.2-3700](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3700/) et seq.), meetings of the board are not considered meetings of the board of visitors of the University. Meetings of the board may be conducted through electronic communication means as provided in § [2.2-3708.3](https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3708.3/).
