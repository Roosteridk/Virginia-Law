# § 53.1-50 Vouchers, certificates and warrants not to be questioned.

No voucher, certificate or warrant issued on the Comptroller by any such department, institution or agency shall be questioned by him or by the State Treasurer on the ground that §§ [53.1-47](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-47/) through [53.1-49](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-49/) and § [53.1-52](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-52/) have not been complied with by such department, institution or agency.

Code 1950, § 53-71; 1982, c. 636.
