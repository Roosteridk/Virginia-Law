To qualify for an asbestos contractor's license, an applicant shall:

1. Except as provided in ยง [54.1-504](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-504/), ensure that each of his employees or agents who will come into contact with asbestos or who will be responsible for an asbestos project is licensed as an asbestos supervisor or worker; and
