# § 18.2-246.9 Disclosure requirements.

The notice required under subdivision A 3 of § [18.2-246.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.8/) shall include:

1. A prominent and clearly legible statement that cigarette sales to consumers below the legal minimum purchase age are illegal;

2. A prominent and clearly legible statement that consists of one of the warnings set forth in section 4(a)(1) of the Federal Cigarette Labeling and Advertising Act (15 U.S.C. § 1333 (a)(1)) rotated on a quarterly basis;

3. A prominent and clearly legible statement that sales of cigarettes are restricted to those consumers who provide verifiable proof of age in accordance with § [18.2-246.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-246.8/); and

4. A prominent and clearly legible statement that cigarette sales are subject to tax under § [58.1-1001](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1001/), and an explanation of how such tax has been, or is to be, paid with respect to such delivery sale.
