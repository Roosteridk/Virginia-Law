# ยง 19.2-386.13 Writ of error and supersedeas.

For the purpose of review on a writ of error or supersedeas, a final judgment or order in the cause shall be deemed a final judgment and may be appealed to the Court of Appeals.

1989, c. 690; 2005, c. [681](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0681); 2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. [489](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0489).
