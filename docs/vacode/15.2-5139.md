# § 15.2-5139 Lien for charges.

An authority may place a lien upon the real property of an owner only in the same manner provided by § [15.2-2119](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2119/), and such lien may only be processed, recorded, and released in accordance therewith. An authority may only provide services to lessees or tenants of property owners in accordance with § [15.2-2119.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2119.4/).

An authority may contract with a locality to collect amounts due on properly recorded utility liens in the same manner as unpaid real estate taxes due the locality.
