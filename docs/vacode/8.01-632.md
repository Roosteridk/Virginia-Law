# § 8.01-632 How surety in forthcoming bond may obtain additional security.

Any surety in the forthcoming bond described in §§ [8.01-630](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-630/) and [8.01-631](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-631/), or his personal representative, may move for and obtain an order for other or additional security, in the same manner as the defendant in an injunction.

Code 1950, § 8-624; 1977, c. 617.
