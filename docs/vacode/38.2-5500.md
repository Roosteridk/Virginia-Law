# § 38.2-5500 Applicability.

The provisions of this chapter shall be known as The Risk-Based Capital Act and may be referred to herein as "the Act." The Act shall apply to all persons licensed in the Commonwealth to transact an insurance business pursuant to provisions in Chapter 10 (§ [38.2-1000](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1000/) et seq.), 11 (§ [38.2-1100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1100/) et seq.), 12 (§ [38.2-1200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1200/) et seq.), 25 (§ [38.2-2500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2500/) et seq.), 41 (§ [38.2-4100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-4100/) et seq.), 42 (§ [38.2-4200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-4200/) et seq.), 43 (§ [38.2-4300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-4300/) et seq.), 45 (§ [38.2-4500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-4500/) et seq.), or 61 (§ [38.2-6100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-6100/) et seq.).
