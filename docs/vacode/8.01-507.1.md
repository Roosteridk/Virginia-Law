# § 8.01-507.1 Interrogatories, answers, etc., to be returned to court.

The commissioner shall, at the request of either of the parties, return the interrogatories and answers filed with him, and a report of the proceedings under §§ [8.01-506](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-506/) and [8.01-507](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-507/), to the court in which the judgment is rendered.

Code 1950, § 8-437; 1954, c. 624; 1977, c. 617.
