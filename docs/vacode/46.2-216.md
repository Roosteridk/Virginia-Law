# § 46.2-216 Destruction of records.

In accordance with the provisions of Chapter 7 (§ [42.1-76](/vacode/42.1-76/) et seq.) of Title 42.1, the Commissioner may establish standards for the disposal of any paper or record which need not be preserved as a permanent record. However, the Department shall not dispose of any vehicle information, as defined in § [46.2-208](/vacode/46.2-208/), for any manufactured home.

Code 1950, § 46-37; 1958, c. 541, § 46.1-36; 1960, c. 121; 1989, c. 727; 2022, c. [479](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0479).
