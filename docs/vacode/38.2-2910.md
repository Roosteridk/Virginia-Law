# § 38.2-2910 Annual examination into affairs of Association.

The Commission shall examine the affairs of the Association at least annually. The examination shall be conducted and the report of the examination filed in the manner prescribed in §§ [38.2-1317](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1317/) through [38.2-1321](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1321/). The expenses of each examination shall be borne and paid by the Association.

1988, cc. 769, 783.
