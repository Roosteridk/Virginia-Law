# § 16.1-69.48:3 Fees charged to drug offenders.

Whenever in a general district court the costs provided for in subsection C of § [16.1-69.48:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/) are assessed for a violation of any provision of Article 1 (§ [18.2-247](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-247/) et seq.) of Chapter 7 of Title 18.2, a portion of the costs, as specified in subsection C of § [16.1-69.48:1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/), shall be included in the taxed costs and paid into the Drug Offender Assessment and Treatment Fund.
