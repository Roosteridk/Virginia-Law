# ยง 20-88.77:4 Procedure to register child support order of foreign country for modification.

A party or support enforcement agency seeking to modify, or to modify and enforce, a foreign child support order not under the Convention may register that order in the Commonwealth under Article 8 (ยง [20-88.66](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.66/) et seq.) if the order has not been registered.
