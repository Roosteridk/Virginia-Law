# § 15.2-926.2 Adoption of ordinances prohibiting obscenity.

The locality may adopt ordinances to prohibit obscenity or conduct paralleling the provisions of Article 5 (§ [18.2-372](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-372/) et seq.) and Article 6 (§ [18.2-390](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-390/) et seq.) of Chapter 8 of Title 18.2 and prohibiting the dissemination to juveniles of, and their access to, materials deemed harmful to juveniles as defined in subsection (6) of § [18.2-390](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-390/) in public at places frequented by juveniles or where juveniles are or may be invited as part of the general public. Exceptions as provided in § [18.2-391.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-391.1/) shall apply thereto. The penalty for violating the provisions of such ordinance shall not be greater than the penalty imposed for a Class 1 misdemeanor.
