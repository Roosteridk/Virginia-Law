# § 8.2-703 Seller's remedies in general.

Where the buyer wrongfully rejects or revokes acceptance of goods or fails to make a payment due on or before delivery or repudiates with respect to a part or the whole, then with respect to any goods directly affected and, if the breach is of the whole contract (§ [8.2-612](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-612/)), then also with respect to the whole undelivered balance, the aggrieved seller may

(a) withhold delivery of such goods;

(b) stop delivery by any bailee as hereafter provided (§ [8.2-705](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-705/));

(c) proceed under the next section [§ [8.2-704](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-704/)] respecting goods still unidentified to the contract;

(d) resell and recover damages as hereafter provided (§ [8.2-706](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-706/));

(e) recover damages for nonacceptance (§ [8.2-708](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-708/)) or in a proper case the price (§ [8.2-709](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-709/));

(f) cancel.

1964, c. 219.
