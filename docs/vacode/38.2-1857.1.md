# § 38.2-1857.1 Property and casualty insurance agents may be licensed as surplus lines brokers for certain insurance from eligible nonadmitted insurers.

The Commission may issue a surplus lines broker's license to any individual or business entity actively licensed as a property and casualty insurance agent for the procuring of insurance of the classes enumerated in §§ [38.2-109](/vacode/38.2-109/) through [38.2-122.2](/vacode/38.2-122.2/) and §§ [38.2-124](/vacode/38.2-124/) through [38.2-134](/vacode/38.2-134/) from eligible nonadmitted insurers in the Commonwealth. However, nothing in this article or in Chapter 48 (§ [38.2-4805.1](/vacode/38.2-4805.1/) et seq.) shall apply to the sale, solicitation, or negotiation of (i) the contracts of insurance cited in subsection C of § [38.2-1802](/vacode/38.2-1802/) or (ii) contracts of insurance for any insured whose home state, as defined in § [38.2-4805.2](/vacode/38.2-4805.2/), is a state other than the Commonwealth.
