# § 63.2-213 Powers, duties, titles and functions of agents and employees.

The functions, duties, powers and titles of the agents and employees provided for in § [63.2-212](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-212/), and their salaries and remuneration, not in excess of the amount provided therefor by law, shall be fixed by the Commissioner, subject to the provisions of Chapter 29 (§ [2.2-2900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2900/) et seq.) of Title 2.2.

Code 1950, § 63-11; 1968, c. 578, § 63.1-11; 1981, c. 21; 2002, c. [747](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0747); 2003, c. [467](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0467).
