# ยง 54.1-1125 Assignment of claimant's rights to Board; payment of claim.

Subject to the provisions of ยง [54.1-1123](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-1123/) upon the claimant's execution and delivery to the Director of an assignment to the Board of his rights against the regulant, to the extent he received satisfaction from the Fund, the Director shall pay the claimant from the Fund the amount ordered by the Board.
