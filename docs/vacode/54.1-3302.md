# § 54.1-3302 Restrictions on practitioners of the healing arts.

A practitioner of the healing arts shall not sell or dispense controlled substances except as provided in §§ [54.1-2914](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2914/) and [54.1-3304.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3304.1/). Such exceptions shall extend only to his own patients unless he is licensed to practice pharmacy.

Code 1950, § 54-481; 1966, c. 171; 1968, c. 582, § 54-524.53; 1970, c. 650; 1972, c. 798; 1988, c. 765; 1989, c. 510.
