# § 38.2-1339 Exemptions.

Nothing in this article shall exempt any domestic insurer from the provisions of Article 5 (§ [38.2-1322](/vacode/38.2-1322/) et seq.), Article 5.1 (§ [38.2-1334.3](/vacode/38.2-1334.3/) et seq.), or Article 5.2 (§ [38.2-1334.11](/vacode/38.2-1334.11/) et seq.).

1977, c. 414, § 38.1-178.15; 1986, c. 562; 2014, c. [248](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0248); 2017, c. [643](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0643).
