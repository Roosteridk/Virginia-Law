# § 33.2-1802 Prerequisite for operation.

A. Any private entity seeking authorization under this chapter to develop and/or operate a transportation facility shall first obtain approval of the responsible public entity under § [33.2-1803](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-1803/). Such private entity may initiate the approval process by requesting approval pursuant to subsection A of § [33.2-1803](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-1803/) or the responsible public entity may request proposals pursuant to subsection B of § [33.2-1803](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-1803/).

B. Any responsible public entity that is an agency or institution of the Commonwealth receiving a detailed proposal from a private entity for a qualifying transportation facility that is a port facility as defined in § [62.1-140](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-140/) shall provide notice of the receipt of such proposal to the Public-Private Partnership Advisory Commission established in § [30-279](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-279/).
