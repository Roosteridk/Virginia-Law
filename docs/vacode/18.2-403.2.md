# § 18.2-403.2 Offenses involving animals — Class 3 misdemeanors.

The following unlawful acts and offenses against animals shall constitute and be punished as a Class 3 misdemeanor:

1. Violation of § [3.2-6511](/vacode/3.2-6511/) pertaining to the failure of a shopkeeper or pet dealer to provide adequate care to animals.

2. Violation of § [3.2-6509](/vacode/3.2-6509/) pertaining to the misrepresentation of an animal's condition by the shopkeeper or pet dealer.

3. Violation of § [3.2-6510](/vacode/3.2-6510/) pertaining to the sale of baby fowl.

4. Violation of clause (iv) of subsection A of § [3.2-6570](/vacode/3.2-6570/) pertaining to soring horses.

5. Violation of § [3.2-6519](/vacode/3.2-6519/) pertaining to notice of consumer remedies required to be supplied by boarding establishments.
