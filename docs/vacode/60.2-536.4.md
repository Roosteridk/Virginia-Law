# § 60.2-536.4 Interpretation.

The provisions of §§ [60.2-536.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-536.1/) through [60.2-536.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-536.3/) shall be interpreted and applied in such a manner as to meet the requirements contained in Public Law 108-295.

2005, cc. [47](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0047), [91](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0091).
