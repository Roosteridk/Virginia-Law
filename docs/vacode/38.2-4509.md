B. The provisions of subsection A of § [38.2-322](/vacode/38.2-322/) shall apply to an optometric services plan. The provisions of subsection C of § [38.2-322](/vacode/38.2-322/) shall apply to a dental services plan.

C. The provisions of Article 1.2 (§ [32.1-137.7](/vacode/32.1-137.7/) et seq.) of Chapter 5 of Title 32.1 shall not apply to either an optometric or dental services plan.

D. The provisions of § [38.2-3407.1](/vacode/38.2-3407.1/) shall apply to claim payments made on or after January 1, 2014. No optometric or dental services plan shall be required to pay interest computed under § [38.2-3407.1](/vacode/38.2-3407.1/) if the total interest is less than $5.
