# § 2.2-3002 Exemptions from chapter.

The provisions of this chapter shall not apply to:

1. Appointees of elected groups or individuals except as provided in subsection B of § [2.2-3001](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3001/);

2. Agency heads or chief executive officers of government agencies and public institutions of higher education appointed by boards and commissions;

3. Law-enforcement officers as defined in § [9.1-500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-500/) whose grievances are subject to Chapter 5 (§ [9.1-500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-500/) et seq.) and who have elected to resolve such grievances under those provisions; and

4. Employees in positions designated in § [2.2-2905](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2905/) as exempt from the Virginia Personnel Act (§ [2.2-2900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2900/) et seq.).
