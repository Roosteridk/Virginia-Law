# § 2.2-4003 Venue.

In all proceedings under § [2.2-4019](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4019/) or [2.2-4020](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4020/) venue shall be in the city or county where the administrative agency maintains its principal office or as the parties may otherwise agree. In all proceedings under § [2.2-4026](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4026/), venue shall be as specified in subdivision 1 of § [8.01-261](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-261/).

1975, c. 503, § 9-6.14:5; 1977, c. 624; 2001, c. [844](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844); 2007, cc. [873](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0873), [916](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0916).
