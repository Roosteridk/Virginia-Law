# ยง 10.1-418.5 South Mayo State Scenic River.

The South Mayo River in Henry County from the Patrick County line to the North Carolina line, a distance of approximately 6.9 miles, is hereby designated as the South Mayo State Scenic River, a component of the Virginia Scenic Rivers System.

2008, cc. [14](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0014), [336](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0336); 2018, c. [273](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0273).
