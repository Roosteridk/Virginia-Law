# § 8.01-599 Warrant or motion for judgment against receiver in general district court, when to be tried.

A warrant or motion for judgment before a general district court under §§ [8.01-597](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-597/) and [8.01-598](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-598/) may be tried not less than ten days after service of process.

Code 1950, § 8-743; 1977, c. 617.
