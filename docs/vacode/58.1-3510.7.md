# § 58.1-3510.7 Exemptions; penalties.

Provisions in §§ [58.1-609.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-609.1/) through [58.1-609.11](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-609.11/) of Chapter 6 relating to exemptions, §§ [58.1-635](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-635/) and [58.1-636](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-636/) relating to penalties, and § [58.1-625](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-625/) relating to the manner of collecting the local retail sales and use tax applicable in Chapter 6 (§ [58.1-600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-600/) et seq.) of this title, shall apply mutatis mutandis to the short-term rental property tax, except that the commissioner of revenue shall assess the tax due, and the treasurer or director of finance shall collect the short-term rental property tax, instead of the Department of Taxation. Any other provision in Chapter 6 shall apply if adopted by local ordinance pursuant to § [58.1-3510.6](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3510.6/).

2009, cc. [480](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0480), [692](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0692).
