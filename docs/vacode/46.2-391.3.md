# ยง 46.2-391.3 Content of notice of suspension.

A notice of suspension issued pursuant to ยง [46.2-391.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-391.2/) shall clearly specify (i) the reason and statutory grounds for the suspension, (ii) the effective date and duration of the suspension, (iii) the right of the offender to request a review of that suspension by the appropriate district court of the jurisdiction in which the arrest was made, and (iv) the procedures for requesting such a review.
