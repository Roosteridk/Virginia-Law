# ยง 15.2-1801 Acquisition of real property near parks or other public property.

A locality may acquire pursuant to ยง [15.2-1800](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1800/) real property adjoining its parks, land on which its monuments are located, or other land used for public purposes; or real property in the vicinity of such parks, land on which its monuments are located or other public real property, which is used in such manner as to impair the beauty, usefulness or efficiency of such parks, land on which its monuments are located or other public real property.
