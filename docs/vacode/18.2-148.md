# § 18.2-148 Bona fide repossession under lien.

The provisions of §§ [18.2-102](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-102/), [18.2-146](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-146/) and [18.2-147](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-147/) shall not apply to a bona fide repossession of a vehicle, aircraft, boat or vessel by the holder of a lien on such vehicle, aircraft, boat or vessel, or by the agents or employees of such lienholder.

Code 1950, § 18.1-168; 1960, c. 358; 1975, cc. 14, 15.
