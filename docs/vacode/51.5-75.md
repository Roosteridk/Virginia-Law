# § 51.5-75 Use of earnings of rehabilitative manufacturing and service industries; record of receipts and expenditures.

In furtherance of the purposes of §§ [51.5-63](/vacode/51.5-63/), [51.5-66](/vacode/51.5-66/), and [51.5-72](/vacode/51.5-72/) through [51.5-76](/vacode/51.5-76/), the Department shall have authority to use any receipts or earnings that accrue from the operation of rehabilitative manufacturing and service industries as provided in such sections, but a detailed statement of receipts or earnings and expenditures shall be carefully kept.
