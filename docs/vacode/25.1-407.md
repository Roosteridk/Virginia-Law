# § 25.1-407 Optional moving expense allowance for persons displaced from dwelling.

Any displaced person eligible for payments under § [25.1-406](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-406/) who is displaced from a dwelling may elect to accept the payments authorized by this section in lieu of the payments authorized by § [25.1-406](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-406/), which displaced person so electing shall receive a moving expense allowance of an amount determined according to a schedule established by the state agency. The acceptance of the payment authorized by this section shall be in lieu of any payment under § [25.1-406](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/25.1-406/).

Code 1950, §§ 33-75.04, 33.1-132.4; 1970, c. 40, § 25-239; 1972, c. 738; 1989, c. 714; 2000, c. [851](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0851); 2003, c. [940](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0940).
