# § 8.01-260 Proper venue; preferred forum in certain actions; permissible forums for other actions.

Except for those actions expressly excluded from the operation of this chapter, and subject to the provisions of §§ [8.01-264](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-264/) and [8.01-265](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-265/), the venue for any action shall be deemed proper only if laid in accordance with the provisions of §§ [8.01-261](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-261/) and [8.01-262](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-262/).

1977, c. 617.
