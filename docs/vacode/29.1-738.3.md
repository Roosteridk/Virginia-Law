# § 29.1-738.3 Presumptions from alcohol or drug content.

In any prosecution for operating a watercraft or motorboat that is underway in violation of clause (ii), (iii), or (iv) of subsection B of § [29.1-738](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-738/), or of a similar ordinance of any county, city or town, the amount of alcohol or drugs in the blood of the accused at the time of the alleged offense as indicated by a chemical analysis of a sample of the accused's blood or breath to determine the alcohol or drug content of his blood (i) in accordance with the provisions of § [29.1-738.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-738.2/) or (ii) performed by the Department of Forensic Science in accordance with the provisions of §§ [18.2-268.5](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-268.5/), [18.2-268.6](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-268.6/), and [18.2-268.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-268.7/) on the suspect's whole blood drawn pursuant to a search warrant shall give rise to the rebuttable presumptions of subdivisions A 1 through 4 of § [18.2-269](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-269/).

1989, c. 726; 1995, c. [130](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0130); 2005, c. [616](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0616); 2017, c. [623](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0623).
