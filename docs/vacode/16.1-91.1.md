# § 16.1-91.1 Costs to be included in judgment on forthcoming bond.

The judge of a general district court, on giving judgment on a forthcoming bond, shall include in the costs of the judgment the clerk's fee as stated in subdivision A 21 of § [17.1-275](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275/); and the clerk shall not receive any fee or reward for any service he is required to perform by the provisions of § [8.01-529](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-529/), except that specified in subdivision A 21 of § [17.1-275](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275/).

Code 1950, § 8-460; 1962, c. 10; 1977, c. 624.
