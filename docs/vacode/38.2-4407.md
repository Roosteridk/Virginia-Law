# § 38.2-4407 Board of directors of plan created by attorneys.

Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ [13.1-675](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-675/), [13.1-677](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-677/) and [13.1-855](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-855/) to the contrary, any legal services organization operating a plan created by a group of attorneys shall have a board of directors consisting of no more than fifteen members of whom a majority may be providers of legal services. This section does not apply to a plan operated under § [38.2-4403](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-4403/).

1978, c. 658, § 38.1-797; 1982, c. 387; 1986, c. 562.
