# § 46.2-384 Law-enforcement officers arresting drivers for certain offenses to request abstracts or transcripts of drivers' conviction records.

Every law-enforcement officer who has arrested any person for (i) driving while under the influence of intoxicants or drugs in violation of § [18.2-51.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-51.4/) or § [18.2-266](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-266/) or a parallel local ordinance, or § [46.2-341.24](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.24/), (ii) reckless driving in violation of §§ [46.2-852](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-852/) through [46.2-865](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-865/) or a parallel local ordinance, (iii) failure to stop at the scene of an accident in violation of §§ [46.2-894](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-894/) through [46.2-899](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-899/) or a parallel local ordinance or (iv) driving without a license or while his license has been suspended or revoked in violation of § [18.2-51.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-51.4/) or § [18.2-272](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-272/), or §§ [46.2-300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-300/) through [46.2-302](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-302/) or a parallel local ordinance or while he is disqualified in violation of § [46.2-341.21](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.21/) of the Commercial Vehicle Driver's License Act (§ [46.2-341.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.1/) et seq.), shall request from the Department an abstract or transcript of the person's driver's conviction record on file at the Department. The Department shall furnish the abstract or transcript to the attorney for the Commonwealth of the jurisdiction in which the case will be heard, to be held available for the court in which the person is to be tried for the violation or charge. However, the failure of the attorney for the Commonwealth to receive the abstract or transcript in any case shall not constitute grounds for the granting of a continuance of such case. In any such prosecution wherein a necessary element of the offense charged is that the defendant was previously convicted of the same or similar offense, a copy, certified as provided in § [46.2-215](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-215/), of (1) the abstract of the relevant prior conviction, certified as provided in § [46.2-386](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-386/), or (2) that portion of the transcript relating to the relevant prior conviction, shall be prima facie evidence of the facts stated therein with respect to the prior offense.
