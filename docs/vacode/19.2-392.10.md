# § 19.2-392.10 (For contingent effective date see Acts 2021, Sp. Sess. I, cc. 524 and 542) Process for automatic sealing of offenses resulting in acquittal, nolle prosequi, or dismissal.

A. On at least a monthly basis, the Executive Secretary of the Supreme Court and any circuit court clerk who maintains a case management system that interfaces with the Department of State Police under subsection B1 of § [17.1-502](/vacode/17.1-502/) shall provide an electronic list of all offenses in such case management system to the Department of State Police that were ordered to be automatically sealed pursuant to §§ [19.2-392.8](/vacode/19.2-392.8/) and [19.2-392.9](/vacode/19.2-392.9/).

B. Upon receipt of the electronic lists under subsection A, the Department of State Police shall proceed as set forth in § [19.2-392.13](/vacode/19.2-392.13/).
