# ยง 46.2-644.3 Acquisition of all-terrain vehicle or off-road motorcycle by dealer.

Any dealer licensed under ยง [46.2-1508](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1508/) who acquires an all-terrain vehicle or off-road motorcycle for resale shall be exempt from the titling requirements of this title.

Any dealer transferring an all-terrain vehicle or off-road motorcycle titled under this title shall assign the title to the new owner or, in the case of a new all-terrain vehicle or off-road motorcycle, assign the certificate of origin.

2006, c. [896](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0896); 2015, c. [615](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0615).
