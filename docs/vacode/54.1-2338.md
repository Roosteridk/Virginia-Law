# ยง 54.1-2338 Use of trustee's name in advertisements.

No person subject to the provisions of this chapter shall use the name of the trustee in any advertisement or other public solicitation without written permission of the trustee.

1998, cc. [708](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0708), [721](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0721).
