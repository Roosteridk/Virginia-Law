# § 22.1-41 Application of article.

The provisions of this article shall be applicable in any county constituting a school division except a county to which the provisions of §§ [15.2-410](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-410/), [15.2-531](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-531/), [15.2-627](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-627/) or § [15.2-837](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-837/) are applicable.

1980, c. 559.
