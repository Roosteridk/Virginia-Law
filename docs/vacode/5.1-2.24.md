# § 5.1-2.24 Applicability of certain sections.

The provisions of §§ [5.1-2.5](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.5/), [5.1-2.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.7/), [5.1-2.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.8/), [5.1-2.9](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.9/), [5.1-2.10](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.10/), [5.1-2.11](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.11/), [5.1-2.12](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.12/), [5.1-2.13](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.13/), and [5.1-2.20](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-2.20/) of this chapter shall be applicable only to any airport owned by the Virginia Airports Authority or its successor agency on January 1, 1980.

1980, c. 750.
