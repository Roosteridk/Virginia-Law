# § 8.9A-309 Security interest perfected upon attachment.

The following security interests are perfected when they attach:

(1) a purchase-money security interest in consumer goods, except as otherwise provided in § [8.9A-311](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-311/) (b) with respect to consumer goods that are subject to a statute or treaty described in § [8.9A-311](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-311/) (a);

(2) an assignment of accounts or payment intangibles which does not by itself or in conjunction with other assignments to the same assignee transfer a significant part of the assignor's outstanding accounts or payment intangibles;

(3) a sale of a payment intangible;

(4) a sale of a promissory note;

(5) a security interest created by the assignment of a health-care-insurance receivable to the provider of the health-care goods or services;

(6) a security interest arising under §§ [8.2-401](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-401/), [8.2-505](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-505/), [8.2-711](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-711/) (3), or § [8.2A-508](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-508/) (5), until the debtor obtains possession of the collateral;

(7) a security interest of a collecting bank arising under § [8.4-210](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.4-210/);

(8) a security interest of an issuer or nominated person arising under § [8.5A-118](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.5A-118/);

(9) a security interest arising in the delivery of a financial asset under § [8.9A-206](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-206/) (c);

(10) a security interest in investment property created by a broker or securities intermediary;

(11) a security interest in a commodity contract or a commodity account created by a commodity intermediary;

(12) an assignment for the benefit of all creditors of the transferor and subsequent transfers by the assignee thereunder; and

(13) a security interest created by an assignment of a beneficial interest in a decedent's estate.

1964, c. 219, § 8.9-302; 1966, c. 569; 1973, c. 509; 1983, cc. 204, 367; 1984, c. 613; 1996, c. [216](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0216), §§ 8.9-115, 8.9-116; 2000, c. [1007](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007).
