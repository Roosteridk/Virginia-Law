# § 60.2-209 Taxes.

"Taxes" means the money imposed by and collectible under this title and includes payments in lieu of taxes collectible under §§ [60.2-501](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-501/) through [60.2-507](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-507/).

Code 1950, § 60-11; 1968, c. 738, § 60.1-11; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 235; 1986, c. 480.
