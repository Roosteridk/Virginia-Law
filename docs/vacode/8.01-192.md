# § 8.01-192 How claims to be prosecuted.

When the Comptroller or other authorized person shall disallow, either in whole or in part, any such claim against the Commonwealth as is provided for by §§ [2.2-814](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-814/), [2.2-815](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-815/) or [8.01-605](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-605/) at which time a right of action under this section shall be deemed to accrue, the person presenting such claim may petition an appropriate circuit court for redress.

Code 1950, § 8-752; 1966, c. 452; 1977, c. 617.
