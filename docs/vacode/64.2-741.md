# § 64.2-741 Powers of courts not impaired by §§ 64.2-736 through 64.2-740.

Nothing in §§ [64.2-736](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-736/) through [64.2-740](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-740/) shall impair the power of a court of competent jurisdiction with respect to any such foundation or trust.

2005, c. [935](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0935), § 55-544.23; 2012, c. [614](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614); 2015, c. [709](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0709).
