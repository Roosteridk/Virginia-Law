# ยง 15.2-7216 Directors and persons executing bonds not liable thereon.

Neither the Board of Directors nor any person executing the bonds shall be liable personally on the Authority's bonds by reasons of the issuance thereof.

2010, cc. [117](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0117), [210](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0210).
