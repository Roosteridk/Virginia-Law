# § 38.2-3229 Effective date.

After July 1, 1979, for contracts subject to subsection B, C, or D of § [38.2-3221](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3221/), the operative date for application of § [38.2-3219](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3219/) shall be the earlier of July 1, 1981, or the date on which the insurer filed with the Commission a written notice of its election to comply with the provisions of §§ [38.2-3219](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3219/) through [38.2-3229](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3229/) after a specified date before July 1, 1981. For contracts subject to subsection F of § [38.2-3221](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3221/), the operative date shall be July 1, 2005, unless the insurer specifies an earlier date by filing with the Commission written notice of its election to apply subdivisions F 1 through F 4 of § [38.2-3221](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3221/) on a contract-form-by-contract-form basis after a specified date, which shall be on or after July 1, 2004, and before July 1, 2005.

1979, c. 437, § 38.1-470.1; 1986, c. 562; 2004, c. [313](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0313).
