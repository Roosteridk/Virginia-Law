# ยง 20-88.66 Registration of order for enforcement.

A foreign support order, or a support order or an income-withholding order issued in another state, may be registered in the Commonwealth for enforcement.

1994, c. [673](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0673); 2015, c. [727](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0727).
