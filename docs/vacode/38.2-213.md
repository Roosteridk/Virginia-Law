# § 38.2-213 Violation of § 38.2-210 or § 38.2-211.

Any company, officer or director violating any provision of § [38.2-210](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-210/) or § [38.2-211](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-211/) shall be guilty upon conviction of a Class 1 misdemeanor. Any funds of any company invested or used in violation of either of § [38.2-210](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-210/) or § [38.2-211](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-211/) may not be reported as an admitted asset in accordance with guidance set forth in the National Association of Insurance Commissioners accounting practices and procedures manuals.

Code 1950, § 38-4.4; 1952, c. 317, § 38.1-36; 1986, c. 562; 2000, c. [46](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0046).
