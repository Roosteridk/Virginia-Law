# § 58.1-406 Allocation and apportionment of income.

Any corporation having income from business activity which is taxable both within and without the Commonwealth shall allocate and apportion its Virginia taxable income as provided in §§ [58.1-407](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-407/) through [58.1-420](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-420/).

Code 1950, § 58-151.035; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 171; 1976, c. 436; 1979, c. 371; 1984, c. 675.
