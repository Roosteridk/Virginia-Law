# § 65.2-518 Limitation upon total compensation.

The total compensation payable under this title shall in no case be greater than 500 weeks nor shall it exceed the result obtained by multiplying the average weekly wage of the Commonwealth as defined in § [65.2-500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-500/) for the applicable year by 500, except in cases of permanent and total incapacity as defined in § [65.2-503](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-503/) C and in cases of permanent disability under subdivision A 4 of § [65.2-504](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-504/) and death from coal worker's pneumoconiosis under § [65.2-513](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-513/).
