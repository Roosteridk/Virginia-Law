# § 13.1-995 Members to have rights of shareholders.

The rights given to shareholders under the provisions of §§ [13.1-614](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-614/), [13.1-624](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-624/), [13.1-629](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-629/), [13.1-742](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-742/) and [13.1-744](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-744/) shall apply to members as well as to shareholders of a corporation created hereunder.

Code 1950, § 13.1-152; 1960, c. 80; 1985, c. 522.
