# § 55.1-125 When nonvested property interest or power of appointment created.

A. Except as provided in subsections B and C and in § [55.1-128](/vacode/55.1-128/), the time of creation of a nonvested property interest or a power of appointment is determined under general principles of property law.

B. For the purposes of §§ [55.1-124](/vacode/55.1-124/) through [55.1-129](/vacode/55.1-129/), if there is a person who alone can exercise a power created by a governing instrument to become the unqualified beneficial owner of (i) a nonvested property interest or (ii) a property interest subject to a power of appointment described in subsection B or C in § [55.1-124](/vacode/55.1-124/), the nonvested property interest or power of appointment is created when the power to become the unqualified beneficial owner terminates.

C. For the purposes of §§ [55.1-124](/vacode/55.1-124/) through [55.1-129](/vacode/55.1-129/), a nonvested property interest or a power of appointment arising from a transfer of property to a previously funded trust or other existing property arrangement is created when the nonvested property interest or power of appointment in the original contribution was created.

2000, c. [714](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0714), § 55-12.2; 2019, c. [712](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712).
