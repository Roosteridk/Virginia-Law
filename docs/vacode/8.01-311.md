# § 8.01-311 Continuance of action where service made on Commissioner or Secretary.

The court, in which an action is pending against a nonresident growing out of an accident or collision as specified in §§ [8.01-308](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-308/) and [8.01-309](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-309/), may order such continuances as necessary to afford such nonresident reasonable opportunity to defend the action.

Code 1950, § 8-67.3; 1954, c. 547; 1977, c. 617.
