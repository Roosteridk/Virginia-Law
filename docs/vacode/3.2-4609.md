# § 3.2-4609 Denial, suspension or revocation of registration.

The Commissioner may deny, suspend or revoke registration provided for in § [3.2-4602](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-4602/) after a hearing, in any case in which he finds that there has been a failure or refusal to comply with the provisions of this chapter or regulations adopted hereunder. All regulations, actions, and hearings for a denial, suspension or revocation of the registration shall be subject to the provisions of Chapter 40 (§ [2.2-4000](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4000/) et seq.) of Title 2.2.

Code 1950, § 3-717; 1964, c. 214; 1966, c. 702, § 3.1-998; 2008, c. [860](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860).
