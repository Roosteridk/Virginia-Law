# § 18.2-376.1 Enhanced penalties for using a computer in certain violations.

Any person who uses a computer in connection with a violation of §§ [18.2-374](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-374/), [18.2-375](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-375/), or § [18.2-376](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-376/) is guilty of a separate and distinct Class 1 misdemeanor, and for a second or subsequent such offense within 10 years of a prior such offense is guilty of a Class 6 felony, the penalties to be imposed in addition to any other punishment otherwise prescribed for a violation of any of those sections.

2003, cc. [987](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0987), [1016](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1016).
