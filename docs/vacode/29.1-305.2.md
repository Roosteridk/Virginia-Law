# § 29.1-305.2 Special fox hunting licenses.

There shall be a special license for hunting foxes on horseback with hounds but without firearms. The special license shall exempt the licensee from the hunter education requirement of § [29.1-300.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-300.1/). The fee for the special license shall be the same as any license required by § [29.1-303](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-303/) and shall be subject to subsequent revision by the Board pursuant to § [29.1-103](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-103/). This special license shall not be required of any person holding a hunting license required by § [29.1-303](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-303/).
