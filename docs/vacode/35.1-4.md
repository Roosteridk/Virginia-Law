# § 35.1-4 Applicability of Administrative Process Act.

The Administrative Process Act (§ [2.2-4000](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4000/) et seq.) shall govern the procedures for rendering all case decisions, as defined in § [2.2-4001](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4001/), and for issuing all orders and regulations promulgated pursuant to the authority of this title.

Code 1950, §§ 35-24, 35-42, 35-52; 1970, c. 273; 1981, c. 468.
