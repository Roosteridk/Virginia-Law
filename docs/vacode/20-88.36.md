# § 20-88.36 Duration of personal jurisdiction.

Personal jurisdiction acquired by a tribunal of the Commonwealth in a proceeding under this chapter or other law of the Commonwealth relating to a support order continues as long as a tribunal of the Commonwealth has continuing, exclusive jurisdiction to modify its order or continuing jurisdiction to enforce its order as provided by §§ [20-88.39](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.39/), [20-88.40](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.40/) and [20-88.43:2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/20-88.43:2/).

1994, c. [673](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0673); 1995, c. [484](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0484); 2005, c. [754](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0754).
