# § 56-265.13:7 Regulation by State Corporation Commission.

A. Every small water or sewer utility subject to this chapter shall be subject only to the following provisions: §§ [56-234.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-234.4/), [56-236](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-236/), [56-239](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-239/), [56-245](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-245/), [56-245.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-245.1/), [56-246](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-246/), [56-247.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-247.1/) through [56-248](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-248/), [56-249](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-249/) through [56-249.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-249.2/), [56-250](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-250/), [56-254](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-254/), [56-256](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-256/) through [56-265](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-265/), and Chapters 1 (§ [56-1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-1/) et seq.), 2 (§ [56-49](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-49/) et seq.), 5 (§ [56-88](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-88/) et seq.) and 10.1 (§ [56-265.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-265.1/) et seq.) of Title 56. Small water or sewer utilities shall not be subject to Chapters 3 (§ [56-55](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-55/) et seq.) and 4 (§ [56-76](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-76/) et seq.) of Title 56.

B. The Commission is authorized to promulgate any rules necessary to implement this chapter.
