# § 29.1-302.02 Special resident and nonresident hunting and fishing licenses for partially disabled veterans.

A. Any nonresident veteran who is rated by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs as having at least a 70 percent service-connected disability, upon certification, shall pay an amount equal to one-half the fee for the state nonresident basic hunting license required by subdivision 3 of § [29.1-303](/vacode/29.1-303/). The license fees established by this section may be revised by the Board pursuant to § [29.1-103](/vacode/29.1-103/).

B. Any nonresident veteran who is rated by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs as having at least a 70 percent service-connected disability, upon certification, shall pay an amount equal to one-half the fee for the state nonresident basic fishing license required by subdivision A 3 of § [29.1-310](/vacode/29.1-310/). The license fees established by this section may be revised by the Board pursuant to § [29.1-103](/vacode/29.1-103/).
