# § 8.01-148 Right of defendant to resort to equity not affected.

Whether the defendant shall or shall not make or attempt a defense under §§ [8.01-146](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-146/) and [8.01-147](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-147/), he shall not be precluded from resorting to equity for any relief to which he would have been entitled if such sections had not been enacted.

Code 1950, § 8-818; 1954, c. 333; 1977, c. 617.
