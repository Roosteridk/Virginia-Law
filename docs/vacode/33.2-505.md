# § 33.2-505 Exclusion of HOT lanes from certain other laws.

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the provisions of §§ [22.1-187](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-187/), [33.2-501](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-501/), [33.2-613](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-613/), [46.2-819](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-819/), and [46.2-819.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-819.1/) shall not apply to HOT lanes.

2004, c. [783](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0783), § 33.1-56.5; 2014, c. [805](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805).
