# § 3.2-1105 Commodity boards; appointment terms; quorum.

The following provisions apply to each commodity board established pursuant to the provisions of Chapter 12 (§ [3.2-1200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-1200/) et seq.), Chapter 13 (§ [3.2-1300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-1300/) et seq.), Chapters 16 (§ [3.2-1600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-1600/) et seq.) through 19 (§ [3.2-1900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-1900/) et seq.), Chapter 21 (§ [3.2-2100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-2100/) et seq.), or Chapter 24 (§ [3.2-2400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-2400/) et seq.):

1. The term for each appointment to a commodity board shall be for four years, with the exception of an appointment to fill a vacancy, which shall be for the unexpired term, unless otherwise authorized in this subtitle; and

2. A majority of the members of a commodity board shall constitute a quorum of that commodity board unless otherwise authorized in this subtitle.
