Where any loan or sale on credit contains a provision that a rebate of unearned interest shall be calculated in accordance with the Rule of 78 rebate method as defined in ยง [6.2-403](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-403/), the note or other instrument evidencing the loan or sale on credit shall contain a notice advising the borrower of the effect of the interest calculation.
