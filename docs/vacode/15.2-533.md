# § 15.2-533 Elected school boards.

Notwithstanding any contrary provisions of §§ [15.2-531](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-531/) and [15.2-532](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-532/), a county which has an elected school board shall comply with the applicable provisions of Article 7 (§ [22.1-57.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/22.1-57.1/) et seq.) of Title 22.1.

1997, c. 587.
