# § 2.2-1150.1 Lease or conveyance of any interest in State Police communication tower.

Proceeds and any in-kind goods or services received from all sales or leases or conveyances of any interest in Department of State Police communication towers received pursuant to §§ [2.2-1150.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1150.2/), [2.2-1151](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1151/), and [2.2-1156](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1156/), above the costs of the transaction, shall be deposited into a special account of the Department of State Police to be used to operate, acquire, construct, maintain, repair, or replace communication towers or sites.
