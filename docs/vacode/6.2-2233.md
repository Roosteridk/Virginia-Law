# ยง 6.2-2233 Additional information.

Nothing in this chapter shall prevent a provider from providing or disclosing additional information on a sales-based financing being offered to a recipient, provided, however, that such additional information shall not be disclosed as part of the disclosure required by this chapter. 

2022, c. [516](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0516).
