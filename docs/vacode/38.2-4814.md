# § 38.2-4814 Penalties.

Any violation of this chapter shall be punished as provided for in §§ [38.2-218](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-218/) and [38.2-1831](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1831/).

Code 1950, § 38.1-314.15; 1960, c. 503; 1979, c. 513, § 38.1-327.59; 1986, c. 562.
