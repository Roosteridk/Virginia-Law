# § 2.2-1167 Commonwealth immune from civil liability.

The Commonwealth and its officers, agents and employees shall be immune from civil liability for actions (i) arising from the establishment and implementation of asbestos inspection standards developed pursuant to § [2.2-1164](/vacode/2.2-1164/) and (ii) undertaken pursuant to the provisions of this article, Chapter 5 (§ [54.1-500](/vacode/54.1-500/) et seq.) of Title 54.1, and §§ [22.1-289.052](/vacode/22.1-289.052/) and [32.1-126.1](/vacode/32.1-126.1/).

1988, c. 723, § 2.1-526.17:1; 2001, c. [844](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844); 2020, cc. [860](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0860), [861](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0861).
