# ยง 53.1-112 Jail or jail farm expenses.

Except as provided in ยง [53.1-114](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-114/), the expenses of operating and maintaining a jail or jail farm and supporting the prisoners working thereon, including board, clothing and medical attention, shall be borne by the participating political subdivisions.
