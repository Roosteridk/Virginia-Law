# ยง 18.2-260.1 Falsifying patient records.

Any person who, with the intent to defraud, falsifies any patient record shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.

1997, c. [619](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0619); 2011, c. [204](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0204).
