# § 55.1-1311 Other provisions of law applicable.

Section [55.1-1202](/vacode/55.1-1202/), subsection A of § [55.1-1204](/vacode/55.1-1204/), §§ [55.1-1207](/vacode/55.1-1207/), [55.1-1208](/vacode/55.1-1208/), [55.1-1216](/vacode/55.1-1216/), [55.1-1224](/vacode/55.1-1224/), [55.1-1226](/vacode/55.1-1226/), [55.1-1228](/vacode/55.1-1228/), [55.1-1234](/vacode/55.1-1234/) through [55.1-1249](/vacode/55.1-1249/), [55.1-1251](/vacode/55.1-1251/), [55.1-1252](/vacode/55.1-1252/), and [55.1-1259](/vacode/55.1-1259/) shall, insofar as they are not inconsistent with this chapter, apply, mutatis mutandis, to the rental and occupancy of a manufactured home lot.
