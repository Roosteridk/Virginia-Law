# § 19.2-76.3 Failure to appear on return date for summons issued under § 19.2-76.2.

A. If any person fails to appear on the date of the return contained in the summons issued in accordance with § [19.2-76.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-76.2/), then a summons shall be delivered to the sheriff of the county, city, or town or to another authorized process server for service on that person as set out in § [8.01-296](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-296/).

B. If such person then fails to appear on the date of return as contained in the summons so issued, a summons shall be executed in the manner set out in § [19.2-76](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-76/).

C. No proceedings for contempt or arrest of any person summoned under the provisions of this section shall be instituted unless such person has been personally served with a summons and has failed to appear on the return date contained therein.
