# § 15.2-6015.3 Compensation; expenses.

Members shall receive such compensation for the performance of their duties as provided in § [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/). All members shall be reimbursed for all reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2825/). Funding for the costs of compensation and expenses of the members shall be provided by the Virginia Coalfields Economic Development Authority.

2017, c. [543](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0543).
