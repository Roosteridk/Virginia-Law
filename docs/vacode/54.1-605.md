# § 54.1-605 Taxation of auctioneer.

An auctioneer may not have a local license tax imposed by any county, city, or town except that in which his office is maintained. If a branch office is maintained elsewhere in Virginia, a local license tax may be imposed by the county, city or town in which the branch office is located, pursuant to §§ [58.1-3707](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3707/) and [58.1-3709](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3709/).

1982, c. 538, § 54-824.15; 1988, c. 765.
