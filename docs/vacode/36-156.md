# ยง 36-156 Reports.

On or before September 30 of each year, each local government recipient shall report to the Department on the status of the properties acquired by the locality with the grant.

1999, c. [1018](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP1018); 2004, c. [577](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0577).
