# § 15.2-1633 Part-time compensated assistants to attorneys for the Commonwealth.

Notwithstanding any contrary provisions of §§ [15.2-1627.1](/vacode/15.2-1627.1/), [15.2-1628](/vacode/15.2-1628/) and [15.2-1630](/vacode/15.2-1630/), the Compensation Board at the request of the attorney for the Commonwealth may provide for one compensated part-time assistant to a full-time attorney for the Commonwealth.

1983, c. 476, § 15.1-50.2; 1994, cc. [780](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0780), [792](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0792); 1997, c. [587](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0587); 2000, c. [913](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0913).
