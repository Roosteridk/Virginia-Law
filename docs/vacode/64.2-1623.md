# § 64.2-1623 Incorporation of authority.

A. An agent has authority described in this article if the power of attorney refers to general authority with respect to the descriptive term for the subjects stated in §§ [64.2-1625](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1625/) through [64.2-1638](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1638/), or cites the section in which the authority is described.

B. A reference in a power of attorney to general authority with respect to the descriptive term for a subject in §§ [64.2-1625](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1625/) through [64.2-1638](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1638/) or a citation to a section of §§ [64.2-1625](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1625/) through [64.2-1638](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1638/) incorporates the entire section as if it were set out in full in the power of attorney.

C. A principal may modify authority incorporated by reference.
