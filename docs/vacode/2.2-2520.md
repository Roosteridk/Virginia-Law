# § 2.2-2520 Compensation; expenses.

Members of the Commission shall serve without compensation. All members shall be reimbursed for all reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2825/). Funding for the expenses of the members shall be provided by the Office of the Secretary of the Commonwealth.
