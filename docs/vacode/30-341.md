# § 30-341 Compensation; expenses.

Legislative members of the Commission shall receive such compensation as provided in § [30-19.12](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/30-19.12/). All members shall be reimbursed for all reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2825/). Funding for the compensation and costs of expenses of members shall be provided by the State Corporation Commission.

2013, c. [709](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0709).
