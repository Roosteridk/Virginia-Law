# ยง 46.2-2011.14 Notice of abandonment of service.

Every motor carrier, TNC broker, broker, or excursion train operator who ceases operation or abandons his rights under a license, certificate, or permit issued shall notify the Department within 30 days of such cessation or abandonment.

2001, c. [596](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0596); 2002, c. [861](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0861); 2017, c. [635](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0635).
