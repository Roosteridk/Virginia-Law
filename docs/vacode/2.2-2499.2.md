# § 2.2-2499.2 Compensation; expenses.

Members shall receive no compensation for their services, but shall be reimbursed for all reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](/vacode/2.2-2825/).

2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. [169](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0169).
