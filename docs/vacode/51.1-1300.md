# § 51.1-1300 Definitions.

As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:

"Board" means the Board of Trustees of the Virginia Retirement System.

"Member" means any member whose benefits under the Virginia Retirement System (§ [51.1-124.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-124.1/) et seq.), the State Police Officers' Retirement System (§ [51.1-200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/) et seq.), the Virginia Law Enforcement Officers' Retirement System (§ [51.1-211](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-211/) et seq.), or the Judicial Retirement System (§ [51.1-300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-300/) et seq.) would otherwise be limited by § 415(b) of the Internal Revenue Code.

"Retirement System" means the Virginia Retirement System.
