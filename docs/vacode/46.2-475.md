# § 46.2-475 Policy must comply with law.

No policy required under this chapter shall be issued or delivered in the Commonwealth unless it complies with §§ [38.2-2218](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2218/) through [38.2-2225](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-2225/), with all other applicable and not inconsistent laws of the Commonwealth, and with the terms and conditions of this chapter.

Code 1950, § 46-495; 1958, c. 541, § 46.1-507; 1989, c. 727.
