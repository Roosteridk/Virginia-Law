# § 60.2-630 Authority to set aside or vacate determinations and decisions.

The Commission may, in its discretion, at any time before a determination or decision becomes final pursuant to §§ [60.2-619](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-619/), [60.2-621](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-621/), or § [60.2-622](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-622/), with good cause set aside or modify any such determination or decision.

1981, c. 74, § 60.1-68.1; 1986, c. 480.
