# § 17.1-275.11 Additional fee assessed for conviction of certain offenses.

Beginning May 1, 2003, the clerk shall assess a person, in addition to the fees provided for by §§ [17.1-275.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/), [17.1-275.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/), [17.1-275.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.3/), [17.1-275.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.4/), [17.1-275.5](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.5/), [17.1-275.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.7/), [17.1-275.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/), and [17.1-275.9](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.9/), a fee of $100 upon conviction of any and each charge of a violation of §§ [18.2-36.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-36.1/), [18.2-51.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-51.4/), [18.2-266](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-266/), [18.2-266.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-266.1/), [18.2-268.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-268.3/), [46.2-341.24](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.24/) or § [46.2-341.26:3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-341.26:3/), or any similar local ordinance.
