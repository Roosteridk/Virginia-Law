# § 58.1-2533 Reimbursement for certain costs.

Any costs related to the insurance premiums tax that are incurred by the Department shall be recovered from the assessment for expenses under §§ [38.2-400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-400/) and [38.2-403](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-403/).

2011, c. [850](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0850).
