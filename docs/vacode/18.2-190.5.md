# § 18.2-190.5 Separate offenses; penalty.

For purposes of imposing criminal penalties for violations of §§ [18.2-190.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-190.3/) and [18.2-190.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-190.4/), the commission of the prohibited activity regarding each unlawful electronic communication device shall be deemed a separate offense.

2002, c. [671](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0671); 2003, c. [354](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0354).
