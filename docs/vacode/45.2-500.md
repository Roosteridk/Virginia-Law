# § 45.2-500  Coal Mine Safety Act.

For purposes of this title, this chapter and Chapters 7 (§ [45.2-700](/vacode/45.2-700/) et seq.), 8 (§ [45.2-800](/vacode/45.2-800/) et seq.), and 9 (§ [45.2-900](/vacode/45.2-900/) et seq.) shall be known as the Coal Mine Safety Act.

1984, c. 590, § 45.1-1.10; 1994, c. [28](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0028), § 45.1-161.7; 1997, c. [390](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0390); 2021, Sp. Sess. I, c. [387](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0387).
