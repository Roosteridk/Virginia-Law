# § 38.2-6108 Plan dentist contracts; preferred providers; assignment of benefits.

A. Each contract with a plan dentist shall contain the following provisions:

1. A hold harmless clause that satisfies the requirements of subdivision C 9 of § [38.2-5805](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5805/).

2. A provision specifying when the contract becomes effective.

3. A provision specifying the date the contract terminates.

4. A provision specifying the renewal terms.

5. Provisions incorporating the rights, remedies and obligations applicable to providers and dental plan organizations set forth in §§ [38.2-3407.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407.1/), [38.2-3407.10](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407.10/), [38.2-3407.15](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407.15/), and [38.2-5805](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5805/).

B. A dental plan organization that offers or administers a plan under which preferred provider policies or contracts are issued shall comply with the requirements of § [38.2-3407](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407/) in contracting with dentists for services in connection with the preferred provider policies or contracts.

C. All dental plan organizations shall comply with the provisions of § [38.2-3407.13](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407.13/) relating to assignments of benefits by subscribers and enrollees.
