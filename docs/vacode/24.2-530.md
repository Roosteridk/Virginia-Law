# § 24.2-530 Who may vote in primary.

All persons qualified to vote, pursuant to §§ [24.2-400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-400/) through [24.2-403](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-403/), may vote at the primary. No person shall vote for the candidates of more than one party.

Code 1950, § 24-367; 1970, c. 462, § 24.1-182; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 205; 1976, c. 616; 1993, c. 641.
