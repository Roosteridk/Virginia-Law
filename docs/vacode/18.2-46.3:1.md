# § 18.2-46.3:1 Third or subsequent conviction of criminal street gang crimes.

Upon a felony conviction of § [18.2-46.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.2/) or § [18.2-46.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3/), where it is alleged in the warrant, information or indictment on which a person is convicted that (i) such person has been previously convicted twice under any combination of § [18.2-46.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.2/) or § [18.2-46.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-46.3/), within 10 years of the third or subsequent offense, and (ii) each such offense occurred on different dates, such person is guilty of a Class 3 felony.

2004, cc. [396](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0396), [435](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0435), [847](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0847).
