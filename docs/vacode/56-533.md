# § 56-533 Regulation by State Corporation Commission.

Every small investor-owned telephone utility shall be subject to Chapters 1 (§ [56-1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-1/) et seq.), 2 (§ [56-49](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-49/) et seq.), 10 (§ [56-232](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-232/) et seq.), 10.1 (§ [56-265.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-265.1/) et seq.), 10.3 (§ [56-265.14](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-265.14/) et seq.) and 15 (§ [56-458](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-458/) et seq.) of this title, except that the obligations of small investor-owned telephone utilities as to tariff filings shall be defined by this chapter. Small investor-owned telephone utilities shall also be subject to Chapters 3 (§ [56-55](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-55/) et seq.) and 4 (§ [56-76](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-76/) et seq.) of this title when they have affiliated interests as defined in § [56-76](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-76/).

1986, c. 337.
