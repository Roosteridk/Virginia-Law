# § 38.2-3501 Policy forms; powers of Commission.

A. Individual accident and sickness insurance policy forms and the rate manuals showing rules and classification of risks applicable to individual accident and sickness insurance policy forms shall be subject to the provisions of § [38.2-316](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-316/). The Commission, subject to § [38.2-316](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-316/), may disapprove or withdraw approval of any such policy form if it finds that the benefits provided in the policy form are or are likely to be unreasonable in relation to the premium charged. If the Commission disapproves a policy form or withdraws approval of a form, an insurer may proceed as indicated in § [38.2-1926](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1926/).

B. The provisions of this section shall not apply in any instance in which the provisions of this section are inconsistent or in conflict with a provision of Article 6 (§ [38.2-3438](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3438/) et seq.) of Chapter 34.

1979, c. 726, § 38.1-362.8; 1986, c. 562; 2013, c. [751](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0751).
