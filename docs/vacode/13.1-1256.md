# § 13.1-1256 Collection by suit and of unpaid bills.

The provisions of §§ [13.1-775.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-775.1/) and [58.1-2814](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2814/), so far as they are applicable, shall apply to the annual registration fees and penalties imposed by this chapter.

2002, c. [621](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0621).
