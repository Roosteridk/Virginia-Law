# ยง 51.1-1162 Applicability of article.

The provisions of this article shall apply only with respect to disability programs providing payment of disability benefits attributed to work-related injuries.

2012, cc. [701](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0701), [823](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0823).
