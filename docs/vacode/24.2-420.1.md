# ยง 24.2-420.1 (Effective October 1, 2022) Extended time for persons to register in person.

A. Notwithstanding the provisions of ยง [24.2-416](/vacode/24.2-416/), any person who is qualified to register to vote shall be entitled to register in person up to and including the day of the election at the office of the general registrar in the locality in which such person resides or at the polling place for the precinct in which such person resides.
