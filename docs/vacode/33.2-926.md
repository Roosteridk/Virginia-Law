# § 33.2-926 Chapter 20 of Title 15.2 not affected.

No provision of Articles 1 (§ [33.2-900](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-900/) et seq.), 2 (§ [33.2-908](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-908/) et seq.), or this article shall affect the provisions of Chapter 20 (§ [15.2-2000](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2000/) et seq.) of Title 15.2.

Code 1950, § 33-76.24; 1950, p. 737; 1970, c. 322, § 33.1-167; 2014, c. [805](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805).
