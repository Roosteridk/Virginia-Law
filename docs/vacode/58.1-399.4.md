# § 58.1-399.4 Effect of election.

A. If a partnership or partner makes an election pursuant to § [58.1-399.1](/vacode/58.1-399.1/) or [58.1-399.3](/vacode/58.1-399.3/), such election shall not be revocable by such partnership or partner. However, the Department may make a discretionary determination that allows such election to be revoked.

B. If properly reported and paid by the audited partnership or tiered partner, the amount determined pursuant to § [58.1-399.1](/vacode/58.1-399.1/) or [58.1-399.3](/vacode/58.1-399.3/) shall be treated as paid in lieu of taxes owed by a direct or indirect partner, to the extent applicable, on the final federal adjustments. A direct partner or indirect partner shall be prohibited from claiming any subtraction, deduction, credit, or refund for such amount. This section shall not prohibit a partner that is a direct partner and a resident partner from (i) claiming a credit against taxes paid to Virginia pursuant to § [58.1-332](/vacode/58.1-332/) or (ii) claiming a credit for any amount paid by the audited partnership or tiered partner on the resident partner's behalf to another jurisdiction in accordance with the provisions of § [58.1-332](/vacode/58.1-332/).
