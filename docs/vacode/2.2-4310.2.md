# § 2.2-4310.2 Executive branch agency's goals for participation by small businesses; requirements.

Any executive branch agency's goals under § [2.2-4310](/vacode/2.2-4310/) for participation by small businesses shall include within the goals a minimum of three percent participation by service disabled veteran-owned businesses as defined in §§ 2.2-2000.1 and [2.2-4310](/vacode/2.2-4310/) when contracting for goods and services.

As used in this section, "executive branch agency" means the same as that term is defined in § [2.2-2006](/vacode/2.2-2006/).
