# § 33.2-212 Sections not applicable to certain engines and tractors.

The provisions of §§ [33.2-210](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-210/) and [33.2-211](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-211/) shall not apply to traction engines and tractors weighing not less than five tons when drawing threshing machines, hay balers, or other farm machinery for local farm use.

Code 1950, § 33-22; 1970, c. 322, § 33.1-23; 2011, cc. [104](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0104), [164](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0164); 2014, c. [805](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805).
