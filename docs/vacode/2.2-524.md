# § 2.2-524 Powers of local commissions.

A local human rights or human relations commission established prior to the effective date of this article or any predecessor statute may exercise any such additional powers as may have been granted heretofore to that commission pursuant to applicable provisions of §§ [15.2-725](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-725/), [15.2-853](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-853/), and [15.2-854](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-854/) or municipal charter provisions.
