(b) A person acquires a financial asset, other than a security, or an interest therein, under this title, if the person acquires a security entitlement to the financial asset.

(c) A person who acquires a security entitlement to a security or other financial asset has the rights specified in Part 5, but is a purchaser of any security, security entitlement, or other financial asset held by the securities intermediary only to the extent provided in ยง [8.8A-503](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.8A-503/).
