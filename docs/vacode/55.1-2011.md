# § 55.1-2011 Merger not to bar subsequent condominium.

The merger provided for in § [55.1-2010](/vacode/55.1-2010/) shall not bar the subsequent constitution of the property into a condominium whenever so desired, provided that the requirements of the Virginia Condominium Act (§ [55.1-1900](/vacode/55.1-1900/) et seq.) are met.

1962, c. 627, § 10, § 55-79.10; 2019, c. [712](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712).
