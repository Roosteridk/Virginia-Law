# § 38.2-3607 Group or individual Medicare supplement policies; minimum standards.

A. The provisions of §§ [38.2-3418.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3418.1/), [38.2-3604](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3604/), [38.2-3605](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3605/), [38.2-3606](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3606/) and [38.2-3516](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3516/) through [38.2-3520](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3520/) shall be applicable to group Medicare supplement policies. The term "policy" as used in this article shall include a certificate issued under a group Medicare supplement policy which has been delivered or issued for delivery in this Commonwealth.

B. The provisions of § [38.2-3418.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3418.1/) shall be applicable to individual Medicare supplement policies.

C. No Medicare supplement policy or certificate in force in this Commonwealth shall contain benefits that duplicate benefits provided by Medicare.

1981, c. 575, § 38.1-362.17; 1986, c. 562; 1989, c. 646; 1996, c. [11](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0011).
