# § 30-88 Advisory committees.

JCOTS may establish advisory committees composed of persons with expertise in the matters under consideration by JCOTS. Such persons shall serve without compensation, but shall be reimbursed from funds appropriated or otherwise available to JCOTS for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2825/), unless they waive reimbursement.

1997, c. [847](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0847); 2005, c. [322](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0322).
