# § 13.1-944.1 Definitions.

In this article:

"Articles of organization" has the same meaning specified in § [13.1-1002](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1002/).

"Converting entity" means the domestic corporation that adopts a plan of entity conversion pursuant to this article.

"Corporation" has the same meaning specified in § [13.1-803](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-803/).

"Limited liability company" has the same meaning specified in § [13.1-1002](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1002/).

"LLC membership interest" has the same meaning as membership interest in § [13.1-1002](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1002/).

"Member" when used with respect to a corporation has the meaning as specified in § [13.1-803](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-803/), and when used with respect to a limited liability company has the same meaning specified in § [13.1-1002](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-1002/).

"Membership interest" has the same meaning specified in § [13.1-803](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-803/).

"Person" has the same meaning specified in § [13.1-803](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-803/).

"Resulting entity" means the limited liability company that is in existence immediately after consummation of an entity conversion pursuant to this article.

2012, c. [706](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0706).
