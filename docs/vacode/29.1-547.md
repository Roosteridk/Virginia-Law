# § 29.1-547 Trapping, selling, purchasing, etc., migratory game birds.

Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ [29.1-546](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-546/) and [29.1-553](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-553/), any person convicted of trapping or attempting to trap any migratory game bird, as defined in § [29.1-100](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-100/), or convicted of possessing any such migratory game bird taken by means of a trap, shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor. Any person convicted of offering for sale, selling, offering to purchase, or purchasing any migratory game bird shall be subject to penalties as provided in § [29.1-553](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-553/).

1960, c. 173, § 29-161.1; 1987, c. 488; 1994, c. [848](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0848).
