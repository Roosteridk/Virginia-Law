# § 54.1-3611 Restriction of practice; use of titles.

No person, including licensees of the Boards of Counseling; Medicine; Nursing; Psychology; or Social Work, shall claim to be a certified sex offender treatment provider unless he has been so certified. No person who is exempt from licensure under subdivision 4 of §§ [54.1-3501](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3501/), [54.1-3601](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3601/) or § [54.1-3701](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3701/) shall hold himself out as a provider of sex offender treatment services unless he is certified as a sex offender treatment provider by the Board of Psychology.

1994, c. [778](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0778); 1999, c. [630](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0630); 2000, c. [473](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0473).
