# § 2.2-3012 Application of state or local grievance procedure; other remedies.

A. Any whistle blower covered by the state grievance procedure (§ [2.2-3000](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3000/) et seq.) or a local grievance procedure established under § [15.2-1506](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1506/) may initiate a grievance alleging retaliation and requesting relief through that procedure.

B. Any whistle blower disclosing information of wrongdoing or abuse under this chapter where the disclosure results in a recovery of at least $5,000 may file a claim for reward under the Fraud and Abuse Whistle Blower Reward Fund established in § [2.2-3014](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3014/).

C. Except for the provisions of subsection F of § [2.2-3011](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3011/), nothing in this chapter shall be construed to limit the remedies provided by the Virginia Fraud Against Taxpayers Act (§ [8.01-216.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-216.1/) et seq.).
