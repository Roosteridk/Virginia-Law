# § 59.1-335 Penalties.

A. Any person violating any of the provisions of §§ [59.1-317](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-317/), [59.1-318](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-318/), [59.1-326](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-326/), [59.1-327](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-327/), [59.1-328](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-328/), and [59.1-329](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-329/) or a cease and desist or temporary order issued pursuant to § [59.1-324](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-324/) shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor and any person violating any of the provisions of § [59.1-332](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-332/) shall be guilty of a Class 3 misdemeanor. Other than with reference to § [59.1-332](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-332/) each violation shall be deemed a separate offense.

B. Any violation of the provisions of this chapter shall constitute a prohibited practice pursuant to the provisions of § [59.1-200](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-200/) and shall be subject to any and all of the enforcement provisions of the Virginia Consumer Protection Act (§ [59.1-196](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-196/) et seq.) of this title.

1985, c. 409; 1992, c. 545.
