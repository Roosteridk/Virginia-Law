# ยง 46.2-2126 Certificate or permit holder not relieved of liability for negligence.

Nothing in this chapter shall relieve any holder of a certificate or permit by and under the authority of the Department from any liability resulting from his negligence, whether or not he has complied with the requirements of this chapter.

2001, c. [596](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0596); 2017, cc. [790](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0790), [815](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0815).
