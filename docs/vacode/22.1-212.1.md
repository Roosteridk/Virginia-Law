# ยง 22.1-212.1 Obligations of school boards.

Pursuant to ยง [1-511](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/1-511/), school boards shall have no obligation to teach the standard curriculum, except courses in foreign languages, in a language other than English. School boards shall endeavor to provide instruction in the English language which shall be designed to promote the education of students for whom English is a second language.
