# § 6.2-1367 Application of provisions to federal credit unions.

The provisions of §§ [6.2-1365](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1365/), [64.2-601](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-601/), and [64.2-602](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-602/) shall apply to federal credit unions operating in the Commonwealth to the extent that the same are not inconsistent with any federal law applicable to such credit unions.
