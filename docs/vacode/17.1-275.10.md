# § 17.1-275.10 Additional fee.

Beginning May 1, 2003, in addition to the fees set forth in §§ [17.1-275.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/), [17.1-275.2](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/), [17.1-275.3](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.3/), [17.1-275.4](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.4/), [17.1-275.7](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.7/), [17.1-275.8](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/), and [17.1-275.9](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.9/), there shall be assessed as court costs, a fee of $2, at the same time fees in such sections are assessed. All fees collected pursuant to this section shall be deposited into the Intensified Drug Enforcement Jurisdiction Fund.

2003, c. [1042](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1042), cl. 9.
