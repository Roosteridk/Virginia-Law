# § 3.2-5117 Penalty for violation; misdemeanor.

Any person violating any of the provisions of §§ [3.2-5106](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-5106/) through [3.2-5116](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/3.2-5116/) is guilty of a Class 3 misdemeanor.

Code 1950, § 3-301.1; 1966, c. 702, § 3.1-381; 2008, c. [860](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0860).
