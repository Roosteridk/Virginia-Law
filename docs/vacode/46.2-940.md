# § 46.2-940 When arresting officer shall take person before issuing authority.

If any person is (i) believed by the arresting officer to have committed a felony or (ii) believed by the arresting officer to be likely to disregard a summons issued under § [46.2-936](/vacode/46.2-936/), the arresting officer shall promptly take him before a magistrate or other issuing authority having jurisdiction and proceed in accordance with the provisions of § [19.2-82](/vacode/19.2-82/). The magistrate or other authority may issue either a summons or warrant as he shall determine proper.
