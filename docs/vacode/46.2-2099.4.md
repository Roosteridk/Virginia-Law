# ยง 46.2-2099.4 Required certificate of fitness.

No sight-seeing carrier, unless otherwise exempted, shall transport passengers on any highway within the Commonwealth on an intrastate basis without first having obtained from the Department a certificate of fitness authorizing such operation.

2001, c. [596](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0596); 2011, cc. [881](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0881), [889](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0889).
