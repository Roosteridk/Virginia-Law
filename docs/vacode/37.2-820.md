# § 37.2-820 Place of hearing.

The hearing provided for pursuant to §§ [37.2-814](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-814/) through [37.2-819](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-819/) may be conducted by the district court judge or a special justice at the convenient facility or other place open to the public provided for in § [37.2-809](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-809/), if he deems it advisable, even though the facility or place is located in a county or city other than his own. In conducting such hearings in a county or city other than his own, the judge or special justice shall have all of the authority and power that he would have in his own county or city. A district court judge or special justice of the county or city in which the facility or place is located may conduct the hearing provided for in §§ [37.2-814](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-814/) through [37.2-819](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-819/).
