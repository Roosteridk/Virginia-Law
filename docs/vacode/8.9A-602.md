# § 8.9A-602 Waiver and variance of rights and duties.

Except as otherwise provided in § [8.9A-624](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-624/), to the extent that they give rights to a debtor or obligor and impose duties on a secured party, the debtor or obligor may not waive or vary the rules stated in the following listed sections:

(1) § [8.9A-207](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-207/) (b) (4) (C), which deals with use and operation of the collateral by the secured party;

(2) § [8.9A-210](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-210/), which deals with requests for an accounting and requests concerning a list of collateral and statement of account;

(3) § [8.9A-607](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-607/) (c), which deals with collection and enforcement of collateral;

(4) §§ [8.9A-608](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-608/) (a) and [8.9A-615](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-615/) (c) to the extent that they deal with application or payment of noncash proceeds of collection, enforcement, or disposition;

(5) §§ [8.9A-608](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-608/) (a) and [8.9A-615](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-615/) (d) to the extent that they require accounting for or payment of surplus proceeds of collateral;

(6) § [8.9A-609](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-609/) to the extent that it imposes upon a secured party that takes possession of collateral without judicial process the duty to do so without breach of the peace;

(7) §§ [8.9A-610](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-610/) (b), [8.9A-611](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-611/), [8.9A-613](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-613/), and [8.9A-614](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-614/), which deal with disposition of collateral;

(8) § [8.9A-615](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-615/) (f), which deals with calculation of a deficiency or surplus when a disposition is made to the secured party, a person related to the secured party, or a secondary obligor;

(9) § [8.9A-616](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-616/), which deals with explanation of the calculation of a surplus or deficiency;

(10) §§ [8.9A-620](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-620/), [8.9A-621](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-621/), and [8.9A-622](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-622/), which deal with acceptance of collateral in satisfaction of obligation;

(11) § [8.9A-623](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-623/), which deals with redemption of collateral;

(12) § [8.9A-624](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-624/), which deals with permissible waivers; and

(13) §§ [8.9A-625](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-625/) and [8.9A-626](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-626/), which deal with the secured party's liability for failure to comply with this title.

1964, c. 219, § 8.9-501; 1973, c. 509; 2000, c. [1007](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007).
