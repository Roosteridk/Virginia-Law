# § 64.2-530 Lien acquired during lifetime of decedent not affected.

The provisions of §§ [64.2-528](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-528/) and [64.2-529](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-529/) shall not affect any lien acquired during the lifetime of the decedent.

Code 1950, § 64-149; 1968, c. 656, § 64.1-159; 2012, c. [614](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614).
