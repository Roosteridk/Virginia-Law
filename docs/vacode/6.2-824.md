Upon the conversion of a national banking association to a state bank as provided in ยง [6.2-823](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-823/), the state bank shall be considered to be the same business and corporate entity as the former national banking association, except that the state bank shall have the rights, powers, and duties as prescribed by state law.
