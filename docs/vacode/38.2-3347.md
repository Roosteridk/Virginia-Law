# § 38.2-3347 Nonforfeiture benefits and cash surrender values.

Each industrial life insurance policy shall contain a provision for nonforfeiture benefits in accordance with the requirements of §§ [38.2-3202](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3202/) and [38.2-3208](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3208/), and a provision for cash surrender values in accordance with the requirements of §§ [38.2-3203](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3203/) and [38.2-3209](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3209/).

Code 1950, § 38-434(5), (6); 1952, c. 317, § 38.1-415; 1986, c. 562.
