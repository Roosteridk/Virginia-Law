# ยง 36-154 Audit.

The Auditor of Public Accounts or his legally authorized representatives shall audit the accounts of the Fund in accordance with generally accepted auditing standards as determined necessary by the Auditor of Public Accounts, and the cost of such audit services shall be borne by the Fund.

1999, c. [1018](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP1018); 2018, cc. [57](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0057), [307](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0307).
