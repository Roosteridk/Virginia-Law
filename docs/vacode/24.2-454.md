# ยง 24.2-454 Elections covered.

The voting procedures in this chapter apply to:

1. A general, special, or primary election for federal office;

2. A general, special, or primary election for statewide or state legislative office or state referendum measure; and

3. A general, special, or primary election for local constitutional or government office or local referendum measure conducted under Chapter 6 (ยง [24.2-600](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/24.2-600/) et seq.) for which absentee voting is available for other voters.
