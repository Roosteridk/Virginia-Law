# § 6.2-431 Certain cards excepted.

Except as set forth in § [6.2-428](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-428/), the provisions of §§ [6.2-424](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-424/) through [6.2-430](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-430/) shall not apply to any credit card issued by any telephone company that is subject to supervision or regulation by the Commission.

1970, c. 324, § 11-34; 1990, c. 587; 2010, c. [794](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794).
