# § 30-204 (Expires July 1, 2024) Compensation; expenses.

Members of the Commission shall receive such compensation as provided in § [30-19.12](/vacode/30-19.12/) and shall be reimbursed for all reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as provided in §§ [2.2-2813](/vacode/2.2-2813/) and [2.2-2825](/vacode/2.2-2825/). However, all such compensation and expenses shall be paid from existing appropriations to the Commission or, if unfunded, shall be approved by the Joint Rules Committee.

2003, c. [885](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0885).
