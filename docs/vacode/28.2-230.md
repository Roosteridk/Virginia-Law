# ยง 28.2-230 Penalty for false statements or altering a fishing license; penalty.

It shall be unlawful for any person to (i) subscribe to a materially false statement in applying to secure a license to fish or (ii) alter or change such license.

A violation of this section shall be a Class 1 misdemeanor.
