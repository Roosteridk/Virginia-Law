# § 38.2-1206 License required of reciprocals; surplus.

A. No reciprocal shall engage in any insurance transaction in this Commonwealth until it has obtained a license to do so in accordance with the applicable provisions of Articles 5 (§ [38.2-1024](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1024/) et seq.) and 7 (§ [38.2-1045](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1045/) et seq.) of Chapter 10 of this title.

B. No domestic or foreign reciprocal shall be licensed to transact the business of insurance in this Commonwealth unless it has a surplus to policyholders of at least $1,600,000, and no alien reciprocal shall be so licensed unless it has a trusteed surplus, as defined in § [38.2-1031](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1031/), of at least $1,600,000.

Code 1950, § 38-549; 1952, c. 317, § 38.1-695; 1977, c. 322; 1986, c. 562; 1991, c. 261.
