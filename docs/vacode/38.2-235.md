# § 38.2-235 Liability insurance; carbon monoxide exclusions.

No policy of insurance furnishing personal injury liability or property damage liability coverage as defined in §§ [38.2-117](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-117/) and [38.2-118](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-118/), including any endorsements thereto, shall be deemed to exclude coverage for the discharge, dispersal, seepage, migration, release, emission, leakage or escape of carbon monoxide from a residential or commercial heating system unless excluded in such policy by explicit reference thereto.

1997, c. [157](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0157).
