# § 15.2-2278 Vacating plat of subdivision.

Any plat of subdivision recorded in any clerk's office, whether or not pursuant to this article, may be vacated in the manner prescribed by § [15.2-2272](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2272/) and the provisions of §§ [15.2-2274](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2274/) and [15.2-2276](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2276/) shall be applicable to such vacation.

1964, c. 564, § 15.1-365; 1997, c. 587.
