# § 38.2-4809.1 Licensees to pay assessments on insurers.

Every licensed surplus lines broker or any person required to be licensed as a surplus lines broker shall be subject to the annual maintenance fund assessment, penalties, and other provisions of §§ [38.2-400](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-400/), [38.2-403](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-403/), and [38.2-406](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-406/). If any person overpays the assessment, the Commission shall process a refund.
