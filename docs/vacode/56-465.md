# § 56-465 Preceding sections subject to repeal or change at pleasure.

The preceding sections of this chapter and also §§ [56-466](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-466/), [56-467](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-467/) and [56-484](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-484/) shall be subject to repeal, alteration, or modification, and the rights and privileges acquired thereunder shall be subject to revocation or modification by the General Assembly at its pleasure.

Code 1919, § 4041.
