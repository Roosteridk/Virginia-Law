# § 1-502 Custody; impressions displayed in The Library of Virginia.

The seals of the Commonwealth described in §§ [1-500](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/1-500/) and [1-501](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/1-501/) shall be kept by the Secretary of the Commonwealth and used as provided by law, and at least three clear impressions of the seals shall be kept and displayed by the Librarian of Virginia in some suitable place in The Library of Virginia, for public inspection.

Code 1950, § 7-28; 1966, c. 102, § 7.1-28; 1994, c. [64](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0064); 1998, c. [427](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0427); 2005, c. [839](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0839).
