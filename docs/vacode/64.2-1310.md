# § 64.2-1310 Recordation of inventories and accounts of sales.

Every inventory and account of sales returned under §§ [64.2-1300](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1300/) and [64.2-1309](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-1309/) shall be recorded by the clerk in the will book and indexed as required by § [17.1-223](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-223/).

Code 1919, § 5405; Code 1950, § 26-16; 2012, c. [614](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0614).
