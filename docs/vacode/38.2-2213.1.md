# § 38.2-2213.1 Certain action prohibited when motor vehicle owner fails to allow access to recorded data from recording device.

No insurer or agent shall reduce coverage, increase the insured's premium, apply a surcharge, refuse to apply a discount other than a discount that is based on data recorded by a recording device as defined in § [46.2-1088.6](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1088.6/), place in a less favorable tier, refuse to place in the company's best tier, or when there are multiple companies available within a group of insurers, fail to place in the most favorably priced company solely because a motor vehicle owner refuses to allow an insurer access to recorded data as defined in § [46.2-1088.6](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1088.6/) from a recording device as defined in § [46.2-1088.6](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-1088.6/). However, nothing in this section shall prohibit an insurer from charging an actuarially sound rate in accordance with subdivision A 3 of § [38.2-1904](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1904/).
