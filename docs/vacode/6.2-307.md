# § 6.2-307 Assertion of defenses or claims by borrowers; effect of assignment.

As to any loan to which the provisions of §§ [6.2-327](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-327/) and [6.2-328](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-328/) are applicable, the borrower may assert any defense or claim he may have under §§ [6.2-304](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-304/) and [6.2-305](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-305/) against any assignee or transferee of the contract of indebtedness.

1987, c. 622, § 6.1-330.59; 2010, c. [794](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794).
