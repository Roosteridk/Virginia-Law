# ยง 59.1-9.11 Penalty for flagrant violations.

In any action or proceeding brought under ยง [59.1-9.15](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-9.15/) (a) the court may assess for the benefit of the Commonwealth a civil penalty of not more than $100,000 for each willful or flagrant violation of this chapter.
