# § 38.2-2811 Examination into affairs of association.

The Commission shall examine the affairs of the association pursuant to § [38.2-1317](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1317/). The examination shall be performed in the manner prescribed in §§ [38.2-1317](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1317/) through [38.2-1321.1](http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1321.1/).

1976, c. 85, § 38.1-785; 1986, c. 562; 1999, c. [61](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0061).
