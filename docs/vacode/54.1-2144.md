# ยง 54.1-2144 Common law abrogated.

The common law of agency relative to brokerage relationships in real estate transactions to the extent inconsistent with this article shall be expressly abrogated.

1995, cc. [741](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0741), [813](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0813).
