# ยง 46.2-749.81 Special license plates; supporters of NASA facilities in Virginia.

On receipt of an application therefor, the Commissioner shall issue to the applicant special license plates for supporters of NASA facilities in Virginia.

2002, c. [893](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0893); 2018, c. [156](http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0156).
