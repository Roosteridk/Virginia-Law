# § 62.1-44.15:66  No limitation on authority of Department of Energy.

The provisions of this article shall not limit the powers or duties of the Department of Energy as they relate to mine reclamation under Chapters 10 (§ [45.2-1000](/vacode/45.2-1000/) et seq.) and 12 (§ [45.2-1200](/vacode/45.2-1200/) et seq.) or oil or gas exploration under the Virginia Gas and Oil Act (§ [45.2-1600](/vacode/45.2-1600/) et seq.).
