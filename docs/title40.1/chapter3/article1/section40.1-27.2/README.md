# ยง 40.1-27.2 Preference for veterans and spouses.

<p>A. As used in this section, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Disabled veteran" means a veteran who has been found by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs or by the retirement board of one of the several branches of the armed forces to have a compensable service-connected permanent and total disability.</p><p>"Veteran" has the same meaning ascribed to such term in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2903/'>2.2-2903</a>.</p><p>B. An employer may grant preference in hiring and promotion to a veteran or the spouse of a disabled veteran.</p><p>C. Granting preference under subsection B does not violate any local or state equal employment opportunity law.</p><p>2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0740'>740</a>.</p>
