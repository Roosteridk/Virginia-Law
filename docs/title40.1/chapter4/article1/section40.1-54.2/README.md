# ยง 40.1-54.2 Strikes and work stoppages at hospitals prohibited; penalty.

<p>No employee of any hospital shall engage in any strike or work stoppage at such hospital which in any way interferes with the operation of such hospital.</p><p>Any person violating any of the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and punished accordingly.</p><p>Notwithstanding the penalties herein provided, any court of general equity jurisdiction may enjoin conduct proscribed by this section.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 40-64.3; 1970, c. 720.</p>
