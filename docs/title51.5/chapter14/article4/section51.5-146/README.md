# § 51.5-146 Adult services.

<p>Adult services provided by the Department together with local departments of social services shall include:</p><p>1. Home-based services, including homemaker, companion, or chore services, which will allow individuals to attain or maintain self-care and are likely to prevent or reduce dependency, to the extent that federal or state matching funds are made available for such purpose;</p><p>2. Adult foster care, including recruitment, approval, and supervision of adult foster care homes;</p><p>3. Participation in nursing home pre-admission screenings of all individuals pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/32.1-330/'>32.1-330</a>;</p><p>4. Provision of assisted living facility assessments of residents and applicants pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-1804/'>63.2-1804</a>;</p><p>5. Participation in long-term care service coordination pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.5-138/'>51.5-138</a>;</p><p>6. Provision of social services or public assistance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/63.2-100/'>63.2-100</a>, as appropriate, to consumers discharged from state hospitals or training centers pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-505/'>37.2-505</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-837/'>37.2-837</a>; and</p><p>7. Participation in such other services or programs as may be required pursuant to state or federal law.</p><p>2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0803'>803</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0835'>835</a>.</p>
