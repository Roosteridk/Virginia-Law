# § 51.5-182 Responsibility for complaints and investigations.

<p>In addition to its responsibilities for complaints regarding services provided by long-term care facilities pursuant to the Older Americans Act, 42 U.S.C. § 3001 et seq., as amended, the Office of the State Long-Term Care Ombudsman shall investigate complaints regarding services provided by (i) licensed adult day care centers as defined in § <a href='/vacode/63.2-100/'>63.2-100</a>, (ii) home care organizations as defined in § <a href='/vacode/32.1-162.7/'>32.1-162.7</a>, (iii) hospice facilities as defined in § <a href='/vacode/32.1-162.1/'>32.1-162.1</a>, (iv) providers as defined in § <a href='/vacode/37.2-403/'>37.2-403</a>, (v) state hospitals operated by the Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services, and (vi) an area agency on aging or any private nonprofit or proprietary agency providing services.</p><p>Nothing in this section shall affect the services provided by local departments of social services pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/63.2-1605/'>63.2-1605</a>.</p><p>2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0728'>728</a>.</p>
