# § 51.5-75 Use of earnings of rehabilitative manufacturing and service industries; record of receipts and expenditures.

<p>In furtherance of the purposes of §§ <a href='/vacode/51.5-63/'>51.5-63</a>, <a href='/vacode/51.5-66/'>51.5-66</a>, and <a href='/vacode/51.5-72/'>51.5-72</a> through <a href='/vacode/51.5-76/'>51.5-76</a>, the Department shall have authority to use any receipts or earnings that accrue from the operation of rehabilitative manufacturing and service industries as provided in such sections, but a detailed statement of receipts or earnings and expenditures shall be carefully kept.</p><p>Code 1950, § 63-168; 1968, c. 578, § 63.1-75; 1980, c. 4; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0747'>747</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0058'>58</a>; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0572'>572</a>; 2018, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0184'>184</a>.</p>
