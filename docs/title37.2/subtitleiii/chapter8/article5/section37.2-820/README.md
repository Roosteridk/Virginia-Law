# § 37.2-820 Place of hearing.

<p>The hearing provided for pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-814/'>37.2-814</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-819/'>37.2-819</a> may be conducted by the district court judge or a special justice at the convenient facility or other place open to the public provided for in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-809/'>37.2-809</a>, if he deems it advisable, even though the facility or place is located in a county or city other than his own. In conducting such hearings in a county or city other than his own, the judge or special justice shall have all of the authority and power that he would have in his own county or city. A district court judge or special justice of the county or city in which the facility or place is located may conduct the hearing provided for in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-814/'>37.2-814</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-819/'>37.2-819</a>.</p><p>1976, c. 671, § 37.1-67.4; 1981, c. 233; 1982, c. 435; 1986, c. 134; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0716'>716</a>.</p>
