# § 37.2-846 Procedure when person not confined in facility or other institution.

<p>A. In all cases, other than those provided for in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-845/'>37.2-845</a>, the person may file his petition in the circuit court of the county or the city in which he resides or in which he was found to have a mental illness or in which an order was entered authorizing his continued involuntary inpatient treatment, pursuant to Article 5 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-814/'>37.2-814</a> et seq.) of Chapter 8 of this title.</p><p>B. Any proceeding to challenge the continued secure inpatient treatment of any person held in custody as a sexually violent predator under Chapter 9 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-900/'>37.2-900</a> et seq.) of this title shall be conducted in the circuit court wherein the person was last convicted of a sexually violent offense or wherein the defendant was deemed unrestorably incompetent and referred for commitment pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-169.3/'>19.2-169.3</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 37-124; 1950, p. 916; 1968, c. 477, § 37.1-104.1; 1976, c. 671; 2003, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0989'>989</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP1018'>1018</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0716'>716</a>.</p>
