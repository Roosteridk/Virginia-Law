# § 37.2-900 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Commissioner" means the Commissioner of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services.</p><p>"Defendant" means any person charged with a sexually violent offense who is deemed to be an unrestorably incompetent defendant pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/19.2-169.3/'>19.2-169.3</a> and is referred for commitment review pursuant to this chapter.</p><p>"Department" means the Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services.</p><p>"Director" means the Director of the Department of Corrections.</p><p>"Mental abnormality" or "personality disorder" means a congenital or acquired condition that affects a person's emotional or volitional capacity and renders the person so likely to commit sexually violent offenses that he constitutes a menace to the health and safety of others.</p><p>"Respondent" means the person who is subject of a petition filed under this chapter.</p><p>"Sexually violent offense" means a felony under (i) former § 18-54, former § 18.1-44, subdivision A 5 of § <a href='/vacode/18.2-31/'>18.2-31</a>, § <a href='/vacode/18.2-61/'>18.2-61</a>, <a href='/vacode/18.2-67.1/'>18.2-67.1</a>, or <a href='/vacode/18.2-67.2/'>18.2-67.2</a>; (ii) § <a href='/vacode/18.2-48/'>18.2-48</a> (ii), <a href='/vacode/18.2-48/'>18.2-48</a> (iii), <a href='/vacode/18.2-63/'>18.2-63</a>, <a href='/vacode/18.2-64.1/'>18.2-64.1</a>, or <a href='/vacode/18.2-67.3/'>18.2-67.3</a>; (iii) subdivision A 1 of § <a href='/vacode/18.2-31/'>18.2-31</a> where the abduction was committed with intent to defile the victim; (iv) § <a href='/vacode/18.2-32/'>18.2-32</a> when the killing was in the commission of, or attempt to commit rape, forcible sodomy, or inanimate or animate object sexual penetration; (v) the laws of the Commonwealth for a forcible sexual offense committed prior to July 1, 1981, where the criminal behavior is set forth in § <a href='/vacode/18.2-67.1/'>18.2-67.1</a> or <a href='/vacode/18.2-67.2/'>18.2-67.2</a>, or is set forth in § <a href='/vacode/18.2-67.3/'>18.2-67.3</a>; or (vi) conspiracy to commit or attempt to commit any of the above offenses.</p><p>"Sexually violent predator" means any person who (i) has been convicted of a sexually violent offense, or has been charged with a sexually violent offense and is unrestorably incompetent to stand trial pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/19.2-169.3/'>19.2-169.3</a>; and (ii) because of a mental abnormality or personality disorder, finds it difficult to control his predatory behavior, which makes him likely to engage in sexually violent acts.</p><p>1999, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0946'>946</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0985'>985</a>, § 37.1-70.1;
