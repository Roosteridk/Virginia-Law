<p>(a) Applicability of section. Except as otherwise provided in subsection (c), this section applies if:</p><p>(1) there is no outstanding secured obligation; and</p><p>(2) the secured party is not committed to make advances, incur obligations, or otherwise give value.</p><p>(b) Duties of secured party after receiving demand from debtor. Within ten days after receiving an authenticated demand by the debtor, a secured party shall send to an account debtor that has received notification of an assignment to the secured party as assignee under ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-406/'>8.9A-406</a> (a) an authenticated record that releases the account debtor from any further obligation to the secured party.</p><p>(c) Inapplicability to sales. This section does not apply to an assignment constituting the sale of an account, chattel paper, or payment intangible.</p><p>2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007'>1007</a>.</p>
