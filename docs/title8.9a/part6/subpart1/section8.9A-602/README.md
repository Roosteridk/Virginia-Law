# § 8.9A-602 Waiver and variance of rights and duties.

<p>Except as otherwise provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-624/'>8.9A-624</a>, to the extent that they give rights to a debtor or obligor and impose duties on a secured party, the debtor or obligor may not waive or vary the rules stated in the following listed sections:</p><p>(1) § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-207/'>8.9A-207</a> (b) (4) (C), which deals with use and operation of the collateral by the secured party;</p><p>(2) § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-210/'>8.9A-210</a>, which deals with requests for an accounting and requests concerning a list of collateral and statement of account;</p><p>(3) § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-607/'>8.9A-607</a> (c), which deals with collection and enforcement of collateral;</p><p>(4) §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-608/'>8.9A-608</a> (a) and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-615/'>8.9A-615</a> (c) to the extent that they deal with application or payment of noncash proceeds of collection, enforcement, or disposition;</p><p>(5) §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-608/'>8.9A-608</a> (a) and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-615/'>8.9A-615</a> (d) to the extent that they require accounting for or payment of surplus proceeds of collateral;</p><p>(6) § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-609/'>8.9A-609</a> to the extent that it imposes upon a secured party that takes possession of collateral without judicial process the duty to do so without breach of the peace;</p><p>(7) §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-610/'>8.9A-610</a> (b), <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-611/'>8.9A-611</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-613/'>8.9A-613</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-614/'>8.9A-614</a>, which deal with disposition of collateral;</p><p>(8) § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-615/'>8.9A-615</a> (f), which deals with calculation of a deficiency or surplus when a disposition is made to the secured party, a person related to the secured party, or a secondary obligor;</p><p>(9) § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-616/'>8.9A-616</a>, which deals with explanation of the calculation of a surplus or deficiency;</p><p>(10) §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-620/'>8.9A-620</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-621/'>8.9A-621</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-622/'>8.9A-622</a>, which deal with acceptance of collateral in satisfaction of obligation;</p><p>(11) § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-623/'>8.9A-623</a>, which deals with redemption of collateral;</p><p>(12) § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-624/'>8.9A-624</a>, which deals with permissible waivers; and</p><p>(13) §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-625/'>8.9A-625</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-626/'>8.9A-626</a>, which deal with the secured party's liability for failure to comply with this title.</p><p>1964, c. 219, § 8.9-501; 1973, c. 509; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007'>1007</a>.</p>
