# ยง 8.9A-622 Effect of acceptance of collateral.

<p>(a) Effect of acceptance. A secured party's acceptance of collateral in full or partial satisfaction of the obligation it secures:</p><p>(1) discharges the obligation to the extent consented to by the debtor;</p><p>(2) transfers to the secured party all of a debtor's rights in the collateral;</p><p>(3) discharges the security interest or agricultural lien that is the subject of the debtor's consent and any subordinate security interest or other subordinate lien; and</p><p>(4) terminates any other subordinate interest.</p><p>(b) Discharge of subordinate interest notwithstanding noncompliance. A subordinate interest is discharged or terminated under subsection (a), even if the secured party fails to comply with this title.</p><p>2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007'>1007</a>.</p>
