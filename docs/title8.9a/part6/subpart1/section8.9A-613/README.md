# ยง 8.9A-613 Contents and form of notification before disposition of collateral; general.

<p>Except in a consumer-goods transaction, the following rules apply:</p><p>(1) The contents of a notification of disposition are sufficient if the notification:</p><p>(A) describes the debtor and the secured party;</p><p>(B) describes the collateral that is the subject of the intended disposition;</p><p>(C) states the method of intended disposition;</p><p>(D) states that the debtor is entitled to an accounting of the unpaid indebtedness and states the charge, if any, for an accounting; and</p><p>(E) states the time and place of a public disposition or the time after which any other disposition is to be made.</p><p>(2) Whether the contents of a notification that lacks any of the information specified in paragraph (1) are nevertheless sufficient is a question of fact.</p><p>(3) The contents of a notification providing substantially the information specified in paragraph (1) are sufficient, even if the notification includes:</p><p>(A) information not specified by that paragraph; or</p><p>(B) minor errors that are not seriously misleading.</p><p>(4) A particular phrasing of the notification is not required.</p><p>(5) The following form of notification and the form appearing in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-614/'>8.9A-614</a> (3), when completed, each provides sufficient information:</p><p>NOTIFICATION OF DISPOSITION OF COLLATERAL</p><p>To: </p><p>Name of debtor, obligor, or other person to which</p><p>the notification is sent</p><p>From: </p><p>Name, address, and telephone number of secured party</p><p>Name of</p><p>Debtor(s): </p><p>Include only if debtor(s) are not an addressee</p><p>For a public disposition:</p><p>We will sell or lease or license, as applicable, the (describe </p><p>collateral) to the highest qualified bidder in public as follows:</p><p>Day and Date: </p><p>Time: </p><p>Place: </p><p>For a private disposition:</p><p>We will sell or lease or license, as applicable, the (describe <br/>collateral) privately sometime after (day and date).</p><p>You are entitled to an accounting of the unpaid indebtedness secured by <br/>the property that we intend to sell or lease or license, as applicable, for a <br/>charge of $.
