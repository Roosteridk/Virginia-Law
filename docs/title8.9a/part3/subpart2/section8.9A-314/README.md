# § 8.9A-314 Perfection by control.

<p>(a) Perfection by control. A security interest in investment property, deposit accounts, letter-of-credit rights, electronic chattel paper, or electronic documents may be perfected by control of the collateral under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.7-106/'>8.7-106</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-104/'>8.9A-104</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-105/'>8.9A-105</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-106/'>8.9A-106</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-107/'>8.9A-107</a>.</p><p>(b) Specified collateral; time of perfection by control; continuation of perfection. A security interest in deposit accounts, electronic chattel paper, letter-of-credit rights, or electronic documents is perfected by control under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.7-106/'>8.7-106</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-104/'>8.9A-104</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-105/'>8.9A-105</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-107/'>8.9A-107</a> when the secured party obtains control and remains perfected by control only while the secured party retains control.</p><p>(c) Investment property; time of perfection by control; continuation of perfection. A security interest in investment property is perfected by control under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-106/'>8.9A-106</a> from the time the secured party obtains control and remains perfected by control until:</p><p>(1) the secured party does not have control; and</p><p>(2) one of the following occurs:</p><p>(A) if the collateral is a certificated security, the debtor has or acquires possession of the security certificate;</p><p>(B) if the collateral is an uncertificated security, the issuer has registered or registers the debtor as the registered owner; or</p><p>(C) if the collateral is a security entitlement, the debtor is or becomes the entitlement holder.</p><p>1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0216'>216</a>, § 8.9-115; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007'>1007</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0200'>200</a>.</p>
