# § 8.9A-309 Security interest perfected upon attachment.

<p>The following security interests are perfected when they attach:</p><p>(1) a purchase-money security interest in consumer goods, except as otherwise provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-311/'>8.9A-311</a> (b) with respect to consumer goods that are subject to a statute or treaty described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-311/'>8.9A-311</a> (a);</p><p>(2) an assignment of accounts or payment intangibles which does not by itself or in conjunction with other assignments to the same assignee transfer a significant part of the assignor's outstanding accounts or payment intangibles;</p><p>(3) a sale of a payment intangible;</p><p>(4) a sale of a promissory note;</p><p>(5) a security interest created by the assignment of a health-care-insurance receivable to the provider of the health-care goods or services;</p><p>(6) a security interest arising under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-401/'>8.2-401</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-505/'>8.2-505</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-711/'>8.2-711</a> (3), or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-508/'>8.2A-508</a> (5), until the debtor obtains possession of the collateral;</p><p>(7) a security interest of a collecting bank arising under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.4-210/'>8.4-210</a>;</p><p>(8) a security interest of an issuer or nominated person arising under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.5A-118/'>8.5A-118</a>;</p><p>(9) a security interest arising in the delivery of a financial asset under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-206/'>8.9A-206</a> (c);</p><p>(10) a security interest in investment property created by a broker or securities intermediary;</p><p>(11) a security interest in a commodity contract or a commodity account created by a commodity intermediary;</p><p>(12) an assignment for the benefit of all creditors of the transferor and subsequent transfers by the assignee thereunder; and</p><p>(13) a security interest created by an assignment of a beneficial interest in a decedent's estate.</p><p>1964, c. 219, § 8.9-302; 1966, c. 569; 1973, c. 509; 1983, cc. 204, 367; 1984, c. 613; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0216'>216</a>, §§ 8.9-115, 8.9-116; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007'>1007</a>.</p>
