# § 8.9A-401 Alienability of debtor's rights.

<p>(a) Other law governs alienability; exceptions. Except as otherwise provided in subsection (b) and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-406/'>8.9A-406</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-409/'>8.9A-409</a>, whether a debtor's rights in collateral may be voluntarily or involuntarily transferred is governed by law other than this title.</p><p>(b) Agreement does not prevent transfer. An agreement between the debtor and secured party which prohibits a transfer of the debtor's rights in collateral or makes the transfer a default does not prevent the transfer from taking effect.</p><p>1964, c. 219, § 8.9-311; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1007'>1007</a>.</p>
