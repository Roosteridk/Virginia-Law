# § 65.2-1103 When awards entered.

<p>The Commission shall enter awards against the Second Injury Fund in favor of an employer or carrier only upon a finding that: (i) the employee has prior loss or loss of use, supported by medical evidence, of not less than twenty percent of one or more of the members set out in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-1102/'>65.2-1102</a>; (ii) the employee has suffered in an industrial accident an additional loss or loss of use of any one of the members set out in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-1102/'>65.2-1102</a> of not less than twenty percent; (iii) the combination of both impairments has rendered the employee totally or partially disabled as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-1102/'>65.2-1102</a>; (iv) the carrier or employer has paid the compensation due under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-500/'>65.2-500</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-502/'>65.2-502</a>, and the permanent partial disability due under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-503/'>65.2-503</a> and the medical treatment under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-603/'>65.2-603</a>; and (v) the employee is entitled to further compensation for disability which has been paid by the employer or carrier.</p><p>1980, c. 599, § 65.1-141.1; 1991, c. 355.</p>
