# ยง 65.2-710 Enforcement, etc., of orders and awards.

<p>Orders or awards of the Commission may be recorded, enforced, and satisfied as orders or decrees of a circuit court upon certification of such order or award by the Commission. The Commission shall certify such order or award upon satisfactory evidence of noncompliance with the same.</p><p>1976, c. 149, ยง 65.1-100.1; 1991, c. 355.</p>
