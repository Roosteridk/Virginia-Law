# § 65.2-303 Recovery from subcontractor; proceedings against owner or contractor.

<p>A. Nothing in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-302/'>65.2-302</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-304/'>65.2-304</a> shall be construed as preventing a worker from recovering compensation under this title from a subcontractor (as described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-302/'>65.2-302</a>) instead of from the principal contractor (as described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-302/'>65.2-302</a>) but he shall not collect from both.</p><p>B. When compensation is claimed from or proceedings are taken against the owner or contractor (as described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-302/'>65.2-302</a>), then, in the application of this title, reference to the owner or contractor shall be substituted for reference to the subcontractor (as described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/65.2-302/'>65.2-302</a>), except that the amount of compensation shall be calculated with reference to the earnings of the worker under the subcontractor by whom he is immediately employed.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 65-29, 65-31; 1968, c. 660, §§ 65.1-32, 65.1-34; 1991, c. 355.</p>
