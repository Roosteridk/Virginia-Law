# § 58.1-2272 Prohibited acts; criminal penalties.

<p>A. Any person who commits any of the following acts shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor:</p><p>1. Failing to obtain a license required by this chapter;</p><p>2. Failing to file a return required by this chapter;</p><p>3. Failing to pay a tax when due under this chapter;</p><p>4. Failing to pay a tax collected on behalf of a destination state to that state when it is due;</p><p>5. Making a false statement in an application, return, ticket, invoice, statement, or any other document required under this chapter;</p><p>6. Making a false statement in an application for a refund;</p><p>7. Failing to keep records as required under this chapter;</p><p>8. Refusing to allow the Commissioner or a representative of the Commissioner to examine the person's books and records concerning fuel;</p><p>9. Failing to make a required disclosure of the correct amount of fuel sold or used in the Commonwealth;</p><p>10. Failing to file a replacement or additional bond or certificate of deposit as required under this chapter;</p><p>11. Failing to show or give a shipping document as required under this chapter;</p><p>12. Refusing to allow a licensed distributor, licensed exporter, or licensed importer to defer payment of tax to the supplier, as required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2231/'>58.1-2231</a>;</p><p>13. Refusing to allow a bulk user of alternative fuel or a retailer of alternative fuel who has posted a bond in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2246/'>58.1-2246</a> to defer payment of tax to the provider of alternative fuel, as required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2252/'>58.1-2252</a>;</p><p>14. Refusing to allow a licensed distributor or a licensed importer to take a deduction or discount allowed by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2233/'>58.1-2233</a> when remitting the tax to the supplier, or to allow a licensed retailer of alternative fuel to take a deduction or discount allowed by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2254/'>58.1-2254</a> when remitting the tax to the provider of alternative fuel;</p><p>15. Using, delivering, or selling any aviation fuel for use or intended for use in highway vehicles or watercraft;</p><p>16. Violating the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2278/'>58.1-2278</a>;</p><p>17. Interfering with or refusing to permit seizures authorized under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2274/'>58.1-2274</a>; or</p><p>18. Delivering fuel from a transport truck or tank wagon to the fuel tank of a highway vehicle, except in an emergency.</p><p>B. A person who knowingly commits any of the following acts shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor:</p><p>1. Dispenses any fuel on which tax levied pursuant to this chapter has not been paid into the supply tank of a highway vehicle, watercraft, or aircraft; or</p><p>2. Allows any fuel on which tax levied pursuant to this chapter has not been paid to be dispensed into the supply tank of a highway vehicle, watercraft, or aircraft.</p><p>2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0729'>729</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0758'>758</a>; 2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0594'>594</a>.</p>
