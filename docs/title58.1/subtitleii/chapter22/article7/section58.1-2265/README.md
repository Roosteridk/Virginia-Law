# ยง 58.1-2265 Improper sale or use of untaxed fuel; civil penalty.

<p>A. Any person committing any of the following acts shall be subject to the civil penalty specified in subsection B:</p><p>1. Selling or storing any dyed diesel fuel for use in a highway vehicle that is licensed or required to be licensed, unless that use is allowed under 26 U.S.C. ยง 4082;</p><p>2. Willfully altering or attempting to alter the strength or composition of any dye or marker in any dyed diesel fuel;</p><p>3. Using dyed diesel fuel in a highway vehicle unless that use is allowed under 26 U.S.C. ยง 4082;</p><p>4. Acquiring, selling or storing any fuel for use in a watercraft, aircraft, or highway vehicle that is licensed or required to be licensed unless the tax levied by this chapter has been paid; or</p><p>5. Using any fuel in a watercraft, aircraft, or highway vehicle that is licensed or required to be licensed unless the tax levied by this chapter has been paid.</p><p>B. The amount of the civil penalty for any act described in subsection A shall be the greater of $1,000 or ten dollars per gallon of fuel, based on the maximum storage capacity of the storage tank, container or storage tank of the highway vehicle, watercraft or aircraft.</p><p>C. The Commissioner is authorized to reduce or waive any civil penalties under this section if the violation is due to a reasonable or good cause shown to the satisfaction of the Commissioner.</p><p>2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0729'>729</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0758'>758</a>.</p>
