# § 58.1-2612 Lien of taxes.

<p>All the taxes and levies provided for in this chapter shall, until paid, be a lien upon the property within the Commonwealth of the corporation owning the same and take precedence over all other liens or encumbrances.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 58-536, 58-599, 58-615; 1984, c. 675.</p>
