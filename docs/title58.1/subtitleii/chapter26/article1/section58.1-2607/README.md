# § 58.1-2607 Local taxation of real and tangible personal property of railroads.

<p>A. Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2604/'>58.1-2604</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2606/'>58.1-2606</a>, and beginning with assessments initially effective January 1, 1980, all assessments of real estate and tangible personal property of railroads shall be made by application of the local assessment ratio prevailing in such taxing district for other real estate as determined or published by the Department, except that land and noncarrier property shall be assessed as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2609/'>58.1-2609</a>.</p><p>B. The real estate and tangible personal property (other than the rolling stock) of every railway company, but not its franchise, shall be assessed on the valuation fixed by the Department and shall be taxed by a county, city, town, and magisterial district at the real estate tax rate applicable in such respective locality.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 58-514.2:2, 58-522; 1972, c. 813; 1978, c. 784; 1979, c. 160; 1983, c. 570; 1984, c. 675.</p>
