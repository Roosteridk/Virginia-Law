# ยง 58.1-2299.9 Prohibited acts; criminal penalties.

<p>A. Any person who commits any of the following acts is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor:</p><p>1. Failing to obtain a certificate of registration required by this chapter;</p><p>2. Failing to file a return required by this chapter;</p><p>3. Failing to pay a tax when due under this chapter;</p><p>4. Making a false statement in an application, return, ticket, invoice, statement, or any other document required under this chapter;</p><p>5. Failing to keep records as required under this chapter; or</p><p>6. Refusing to allow the Commissioner or a representative of the Commissioner to examine the person's books and records concerning transactions taxable under this chapter.</p><p>B. A person who knowingly commits any of the following acts is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor:</p><p>1. Dispenses into the supply tank of a highway vehicle, watercraft, or aircraft any fuel on which a tax required to be levied under this chapter has not been paid; or</p><p>2. Allows to be dispensed into the supply tank of a highway vehicle, watercraft, or aircraft any fuel on which a tax required to be levied under this chapter has not been paid.</p><p>2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0217'>217</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0225'>225</a>.</p>
