# § 58.1-2533 Reimbursement for certain costs.

<p>Any costs related to the insurance premiums tax that are incurred by the Department shall be recovered from the assessment for expenses under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-400/'>38.2-400</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-403/'>38.2-403</a>.</p><p>2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0850'>850</a>.</p>
