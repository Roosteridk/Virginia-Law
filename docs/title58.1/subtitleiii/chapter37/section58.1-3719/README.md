# § 58.1-3719 Limitations on license taxes imposed on peddlers, itinerant merchants and peddlers at wholesale.

<p>A. Any license tax imposed on peddlers or itinerant merchants or on peddlers at wholesale shall not apply to:</p><p>1. A licensed wholesale dealer who sells and, at the time of such sale, delivers merchandise to retail merchants;</p><p>2. A distributor or vendor of motor fuels and petroleum products;</p><p>3. A distributor or vendor of seafood who catches seafood and sells only the seafood caught by him;</p><p>4. A farmer or producer of agricultural products who sells only the farm or agricultural products produced or grown by him;</p><p>5. A farmers' cooperative association;</p><p>6. A manufacturer who is subject to Virginia tax on intangible personal property who peddles at wholesale, only the goods, wares or merchandise manufactured by him at a plant, whose intangible personal property is taxed by this Commonwealth.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 58-266.8, 58-354; 1950, p. 894; 1982, c. 633; 1983, c. 550; 1984, c. 675.</p>
