# § 58.1-3732.1 Limitation on gross receipts; pari-mutuel wagering.

<p>Gross receipts for license tax purposes under Chapter 37 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3700/'>58.1-3700</a> et seq.) shall not include the license and admission taxes established under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-392/'>59.1-392</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-393/'>59.1-393</a>, respectively, nor shall it include pari-mutuel wagering pools as established under Article 1.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-340.15/'>18.2-340.15</a> et seq.) of Chapter 8 of Title 18.2 or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-392/'>59.1-392</a>.</p><p>1992, c. 820; 2013, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0036'>36</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0350'>350</a>.</p>
