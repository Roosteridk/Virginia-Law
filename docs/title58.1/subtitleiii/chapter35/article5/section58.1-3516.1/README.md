# § 58.1-3516.1 Payment of taxes prorated under § 58.1-3516.

<p>Notwithstanding the contrary language of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3515/'>58.1-3515</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3518/'>58.1-3518</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3913/'>58.1-3913</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3915/'>58.1-3915</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3916/'>58.1-3916</a>, or subdivision B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3516/'>58.1-3516</a>, relating to the tax day, or tax filing or payment dates, or dates on which penalty and interest are to be charged or added to delinquent tax returns or payments, or any other general provisions of law relating to such dates, the City of Winchester is authorized to provide, by ordinance, in combination with the adoption of proration of personal property taxes under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3516/'>58.1-3516</a>, the following:</p><p>1. The payment of the personal property tax on any personal property subject to proration shall be due on the last day of the twelfth month after such personal property has acquired situs within the city; however, if the property loses situs in the City of Winchester, or if the taxpayer sells or otherwise transfers title to the property, the tax shall be due on the last day of the month following the month of the loss of situs or of the sale or other transfer of title of the property.</p><p>2. The penalties for failure to pay the tax on such personal property shall begin the day following the due date for the tax on the property.</p><p>The provisions of Subtitle III (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3000/'>58.1-3000</a> et seq.) of Title 58.1 which are not in conflict herewith shall apply, with the respective differences having been considered, to the imposition and collection of personal property taxes by the City of Winchester, and the tax credit and exemption provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3516/'>58.1-3516</a> for the payment of taxes to other jurisdictions shall specifically apply to any ordinance adopted under the authority of this section.</p><p>1993, cc. 187, 324; 1995, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0131'>131</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0469'>469</a>.</p>
