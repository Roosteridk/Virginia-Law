# § 58.1-3500 Defined and segregated for local taxation.

<p>Tangible personal property shall consist of all personal property not otherwise classified by (i) § <a href='/vacode/58.1-1100/'>58.1-1100</a> as intangible personal property, (ii) § <a href='/vacode/58.1-3510/'>58.1-3510</a> as merchants' capital, or (iii) § <a href='/vacode/58.1-3510.4/'>58.1-3510.4</a> as short-term rental property. "Tangible personal property" does not include fixtures, as defined in § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title58.1/chapter32/section58.1-3295.3/'>58.1-3295.3</a>, if such fixtures are taxed in accordance with § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/title58.1/chapter32/section58.1-3295.3/'>58.1-3295.3</a>. Such tangible personal property is hereby segregated for and made subject to local taxation only pursuant to Article X, § 4 of the Constitution of Virginia.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 58-829, 58-830; 1960, c. 418; 1970, c. 325; 1974, c. 445; 1975, cc. 47, 541; 1978, cc. 178, 656, 843; 1979, c. 576; 1980, c. 412; 1982, c. 633; 1984, cc. 675, 689; 2010, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0255'>255</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0295'>295</a>; 2022, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0671'>671</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0672'>672</a>.</p>
