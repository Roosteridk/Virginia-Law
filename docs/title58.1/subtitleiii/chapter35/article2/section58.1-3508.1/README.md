# ยง 58.1-3508.1 Separate classification of machinery and tools used in semiconductor manufacturing.

<p>Machinery and tools used in semiconductor manufacturing shall constitute a classification for local taxation separate from other classifications of machinery and tools as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3507/'>58.1-3507</a>. The governing body of any county, city or town may levy a tax on such classification of property at a different rate from the tax levied on other machinery and tools. The rate of tax and the rate of assessment shall not exceed that applicable generally to machinery and tools.</p><p>1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0971'>971</a>; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0077'>77</a>.</p>
