# § 58.1-3510.7 Exemptions; penalties.

<p>Provisions in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-609.1/'>58.1-609.1</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-609.11/'>58.1-609.11</a> of Chapter 6 relating to exemptions, §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-635/'>58.1-635</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-636/'>58.1-636</a> relating to penalties, and § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-625/'>58.1-625</a> relating to the manner of collecting the local retail sales and use tax applicable in Chapter 6 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-600/'>58.1-600</a> et seq.) of this title, shall apply mutatis mutandis to the short-term rental property tax, except that the commissioner of revenue shall assess the tax due, and the treasurer or director of finance shall collect the short-term rental property tax, instead of the Department of Taxation. Any other provision in Chapter 6 shall apply if adopted by local ordinance pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3510.6/'>58.1-3510.6</a>.</p><p>2009, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0480'>480</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0692'>692</a>.</p>
