# § 58.1-3510 Definition of merchants' capital.

<p>A. Merchants' capital is defined as follows: Inventory of stock on hand; daily rental vehicles as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1735/'>58.1-1735</a>; and all other taxable personal property of any kind whatsoever, except money on hand and on deposit and except tangible personal property not offered for sale as merchandise, which tangible personal property shall be reported and assessed as such.</p><p>B. For purposes of this section, a repair and service operation (i) carried on as an integral part of and in conjunction with a business that is primarily mercantile and (ii) the principal sales of such business are subject to the tax imposed by Article 9 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1734/'>58.1-1734</a> et seq.) of Chapter 17 or to the tax imposed by Chapter 24 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-2400/'>58.1-2400</a> et seq.) of this title shall be deemed a mercantile business, and all capital, as defined herein, including all repair parts, materials and supplies associated with such repair and service operation shall be deemed merchants' capital.</p><p>C. For purposes of valuing lottery tickets as part of a dealer's inventory, cost shall include only the compensation payable to a licensed sales agent as provided by rules or regulations adopted by the Board consistent with the provisions of subdivision 11 of subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-4007/'>58.1-4007</a>. The value of lottery tickets shall not be based on the cost of the tickets to the merchant.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 58-266.1, 58-833; 1950, p. 155; 1956, c. 242; 1964, cc. 424, 472; 1968, c. 619; 1970, cc. 231, 547; 1974, cc. 196, 438; 1975, cc. 23, 621; 1976, cc. 521, 719; 1977, cc. 56, 320; 1978, cc. 772, 799, 817; 1979, cc. 565, 568, 570; 1980, cc. 318, 736; 1981, cc. 145, 419, 636; 1982, cc. 348, 548, 552, 554, 558, 633; 1983, cc. 544, 554; 1984, cc. 247, 675, 695; 1987, cc. 572, 591; 1989, c. 589; 1990, c. 151; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0853'>853</a>; 2009, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0480'>480</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0692'>692</a>; 2011, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0405'>405</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0639'>639</a>.</p>
