# ยง 58.1-3535 Commissioner of the revenue to furnish information to the treasurer.

<p>The commissioner of the revenue shall timely provide to the treasurer such data or information as may be required for the treasurer to comply with the provisions of this chapter.</p><p>1998, Sp. Sess. I, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?982+ful+CHAP0002'>2</a>.</p>
