# ยง 58.1-3004 Duty of clerk of board in case of appeal; how appeal tried.

<p>The clerk of the governing body, upon any appeal being taken, shall immediately give notice thereof to the governing body and make out a brief return of the proceedings in the case before the governing body, with its decision thereon, and file the same, together with the bond and all the papers in the case in his possession with the clerk of the court to which the appeal is taken. Such appeal shall be entered, tried and determined as appeals of right, on such record with any other pleadings and evidence that the court in its discretion may permit.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 58-842; 1984, c. 675.</p>
