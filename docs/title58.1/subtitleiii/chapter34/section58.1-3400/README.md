# § 58.1-3400 Service charge on certain real property.

<p>Notwithstanding the provisions of Chapter 36 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3600/'>58.1-3600</a> et seq.) of this title relating to the exemption of property from taxation, the governing body of any county, city or town is authorized to impose and collect a service charge upon the owners of all real estate situated within its jurisdiction which is exempted from property taxation under subdivision A 1, except property owned by the Commonwealth, and subdivisions A 3, A 4 and A 7 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3606/'>58.1-3606</a>, subdivisions A 2 through A 7 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3607/'>58.1-3607</a> and all sections in Articles 3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3609/'>58.1-3609</a> et seq.), 4 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3650/'>58.1-3650</a> et seq.), and 4.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3651/'>58.1-3651</a>) of Chapter 36 of this title.</p><p>The service charge may be imposed only if the commissioner of revenue or other assessing officer for such locality, prior to imposing the service charge, publishes and lists all exempt real estate in the land books of such locality, in the same manner as is taxable real estate.</p><p>Code 1950, § 58-16.2; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 133; 1972, c. 770; 1973, c. 444; 1975, c. 646; 1976, c. 427; 1981, c. 602; 1982, c. 641; 1984, c. 675; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0557'>557</a>.</p>
