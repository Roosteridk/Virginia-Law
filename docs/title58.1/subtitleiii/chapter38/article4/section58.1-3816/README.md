# ยง 58.1-3816 Certain counties not to levy consumers' utility tax if such counties levy tax on household goods and personal effects.

<p>No county with a population of over 150,000, shall levy a utility consumers' tax as authorized by this article if such county levies a personal property tax on household goods and personal effects. Household goods shall be limited to furniture, furnishings, machinery, tools and appliances used by an owner or a member of his household in and about their place of residence.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 58-851.5; 1966, c. 542; 1984, c. 675.</p>
