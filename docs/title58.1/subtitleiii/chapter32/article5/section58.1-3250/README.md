# ยง 58.1-3250 General reassessment in cities.

<p>In each of the cities of this Commonwealth, there shall be a general reassessment of real estate every two years. Sections <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3258/'>58.1-3258</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3275/'>58.1-3275</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3271/'>58.1-3271</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3276/'>58.1-3276</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3278/'>58.1-3278</a>, and other relevant provisions of law shall be applicable to general reassessments of real estate in cities. Any city which has a total population of 30,000 or less, may elect by majority vote of its council to conduct its general reassessments at four-year intervals.</p><p>No provision of this section shall affect the power of any city to use the annual or biennial assessment method in lieu of general assessments.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 58-776; 1976, c. 717; 1979, c. 577; 1980, c. 569; 1984, c. 675.</p>
