# ยง 58.1-3202 Taxation of certain multi-unit real estate.

<p>Beginning with assessments effective on January 1, 1984, the fair market value of multi-unit real estate leased primarily to residential tenants shall be determined without regard to its potential for conversion to condominium or cooperative ownership.
