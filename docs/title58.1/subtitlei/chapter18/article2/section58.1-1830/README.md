# § 58.1-1830 Effect of order.

<p>An order of exoneration under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1826/'>58.1-1826</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1827/'>58.1-1827</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1828/'>58.1-1828</a>, when delivered to the Tax Commissioner, shall restrain him from collecting so much as is thus erroneously charged. If what was so erroneously charged has been paid, the order of the court under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1826/'>58.1-1826</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1827/'>58.1-1827</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1828/'>58.1-1828</a>, when presented to the appropriate state or local official, shall serve as the only direction necessary to obtain refund of the amount so ordered.</p><p>Code 1950, § 58-1136; 1980, c. 633; 1984, c. 675.</p>
