# § 58.1-360 Imposition of tax.

<p>A tax is hereby annually imposed, at the rates prescribed by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-320/'>58.1-320</a> for individuals, on the Virginia taxable income for each taxable year of every estate and trust.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 58-151.03, 58-151.021; 1971 Ex. Sess., c. 171; 1972, c. 310; 1978, cc. 159, 796; 1981, c. 402; 1984, c. 675.</p>
