# § 58.1-362 Virginia taxable income of a nonresident estate or trust.

<p>The Virginia taxable income of a nonresident estate or trust shall be its share of income, gain, loss and deduction attributable to Virginia sources as determined under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-363/'>58.1-363</a> increased or reduced, as the case may be, by:</p><p>1. The amount derived from or connected with Virginia sources of any income, gain, loss and deduction recognized for federal income tax purposes but excluded from the computation of distributable net income of the estate or trust; and</p><p>2. The net amount of any modifications as provided for in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-322.01/'>58.1-322.01</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-322.02/'>58.1-322.02</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-322.04/'>58.1-322.04</a> with respect to the income or gain referred to in subdivision 1 of this section.</p><p>Code 1950, § 58-151.024; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 171; 1972, c. 827; 1984, c. 675; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0444'>444</a>.</p>
