# § 58.1-320 Imposition of tax.

<p>A tax is hereby annually imposed on the Virginia taxable income for each taxable year of every individual as follows:</p><p>Two percent on income not exceeding $3,000;</p><p>Three percent on income in excess of $3,000, but not in excess of $5,000;</p><p>Five percent on income in excess of $5,000, but not in excess of $12,000 for taxable years beginning before January 1, 1987;</p><p>Five percent on income in excess of $5,000 but not in excess of $14,000 for taxable years beginning January 1, 1987, through December 31, 1987;</p><p>Five percent on income in excess of $5,000 but not in excess of $15,000 for taxable years beginning January 1, 1988, through December 31, 1988;</p><p>Five percent on income in excess of $5,000 but not in excess of $16,000 for taxable years beginning January 1, 1989, through December 31, 1989;</p><p>Five percent on income in excess of $5,000 but not in excess of $17,000 for taxable years beginning January 1, 1990;</p><p>Five and three-quarters percent on income in excess of $12,000 for taxable years beginning before January 1, 1987;</p><p>Five and three-quarters percent on income in excess of $14,000 for taxable years beginning January 1, 1987, through December 31, 1987;</p><p>Five and three-quarters percent on income in excess of $15,000 for taxable years beginning January 1, 1988, through December 31, 1988;</p><p>Five and three-quarters percent on income in excess of $16,000 for taxable years beginning January 1, 1989, through December 31, 1989; and</p><p>Five and three-quarters percent on income in excess of $17,000 for taxable years beginning on and after January 1, 1990.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 58-151.03, 58-151.011; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 171; 1972, cc. 310, 563; 1978, cc. 159, 796; 1981, c. 402; 1984, c. 675; 1987, c. 9.</p>
