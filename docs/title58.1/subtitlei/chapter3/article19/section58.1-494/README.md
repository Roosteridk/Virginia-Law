# ยง 58.1-494 Sheets or forms for recording declarations of estimated tax; recording.

<p>The commissioner of the revenue shall, for recording declarations of estimated tax, make out assessment sheets or forms daily as and when declarations are received, and shall continue so to make out such sheets or forms daily until all declarations so received by him have been entered on such sheets or forms. The commissioner of the revenue shall each day deliver the original and, if the Department so prescribes, one copy of each such sheet or form so made out that day to the treasurer of the county or city.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 58-151.25; 1962, c. 612; 1968, c. 343; 1981, c. 96; 1984, c. 675.</p>
