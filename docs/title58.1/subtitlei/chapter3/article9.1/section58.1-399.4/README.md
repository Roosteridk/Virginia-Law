# § 58.1-399.4 Effect of election.

<p>A. If a partnership or partner makes an election pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/58.1-399.1/'>58.1-399.1</a> or <a href='/vacode/58.1-399.3/'>58.1-399.3</a>, such election shall not be revocable by such partnership or partner. However, the Department may make a discretionary determination that allows such election to be revoked.</p><p>B. If properly reported and paid by the audited partnership or tiered partner, the amount determined pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/58.1-399.1/'>58.1-399.1</a> or <a href='/vacode/58.1-399.3/'>58.1-399.3</a> shall be treated as paid in lieu of taxes owed by a direct or indirect partner, to the extent applicable, on the final federal adjustments. A direct partner or indirect partner shall be prohibited from claiming any subtraction, deduction, credit, or refund for such amount. This section shall not prohibit a partner that is a direct partner and a resident partner from (i) claiming a credit against taxes paid to Virginia pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/58.1-332/'>58.1-332</a> or (ii) claiming a credit for any amount paid by the audited partnership or tiered partner on the resident partner's behalf to another jurisdiction in accordance with the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/58.1-332/'>58.1-332</a>.</p><p>2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1030'>1030</a>.</p>
