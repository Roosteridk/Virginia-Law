# § 58.1-399.2 Tiered partners.

<p>A. The following categories of partners shall be subject to the reporting and payment requirements specified in § <a href='/vacode/58.1-399/'>58.1-399</a>, entitled to make elections as provided in § <a href='/vacode/58.1-399.1/'>58.1-399.1</a>, and entitled to elect an alternative reporting and payment method as provided in § <a href='/vacode/58.1-399.3/'>58.1-399.3</a>:</p><p>1. Any direct tiered partner of an audited partnership;</p><p>2. Any indirect tiered partner of an audited partnership; and</p><p>3. Any partner of a partner specified in subdivision 1 or 2.</p><p>B. A partner subject to the provisions of subsection A shall make required reports and payments no later than 90 days after the time for filing and providing statements to tiered partners and their partners pursuant to the provisions of § 6226 of the Internal Revenue Code and any regulations promulgated thereunder. The Department may establish procedures and deadlines for reports and payments required pursuant to this section.</p><p>2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1030'>1030</a>.</p>
