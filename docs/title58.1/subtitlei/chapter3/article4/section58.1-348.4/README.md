<p>A. No income tax return preparer may provide tax preparation services for Virginia income tax returns unless he provides his PTIN, as defined in ยง <a href='/vacode/58.1-348.3/'>58.1-348.3</a>, when submitting a return and signing as an income tax return preparer.</p><p>B. In addition to all other penalties provided by law, any person who violates subsection A shall pay a civil penalty to the Department in the amount of $50 per offense, but not to exceed $25,000 per calendar year.
