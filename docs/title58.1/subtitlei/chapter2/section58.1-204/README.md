# § 58.1-204 Publication of rulings, decisions, orders and regulations.

<p>A. The Department shall publish the following documents:</p><p>1. Regulations finally adopted by the Tax Commissioner as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-203/'>58.1-203</a>;</p><p>2. Orders of the Tax Commissioner under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1822/'>58.1-1822</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1824/'>58.1-1824</a>;</p><p>3. Final orders entered by a circuit court under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1826/'>58.1-1826</a> or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1827/'>58.1-1827</a>, and any written opinion or memorandum of the court;</p><p>4. Tax bulletins, guidelines, and any written ruling or other interpretation of Virginia law which the Tax Commissioner believes may be of interest to taxpayers and practitioners.</p><p>B. Notwithstanding § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-4/'>58.1-4</a> or any other provision of law, the Tax Commissioner may publish the documents described above with such changes of name, alterations and deletions as he deems necessary to preserve privileged taxpayer information.</p><p>C. For purposes of this section, documents shall be deemed to be published if they are (i) compiled at regular intervals not exceeding three months, (ii) made available for inspection and copying at the Department, and (iii) published on the Department's website as the Tax Commissioner deems necessary to inform taxpayers and practitioners.</p><p>Code 1950, § 58-48.7; 1980, c. 633; 1984, c. 675; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0800'>800</a>.</p>
