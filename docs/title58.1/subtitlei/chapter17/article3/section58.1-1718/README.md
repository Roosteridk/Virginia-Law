# § 58.1-1718 City or county probate tax.

<p>In addition to the state tax and fee imposed by §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1712/'>58.1-1712</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1717.1/'>58.1-1717.1</a>, the governing body of any county and the council of any city may, as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-3805/'>58.1-3805</a>, (i) impose a county or city tax in an amount equal to one-third of the amount of the state tax on the probate of a will or grant of administration on the probate of every such will or grant of administration and (ii) charge a $25 fee for the recordation of a list of heirs pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-509/'>64.2-509</a> or an affidavit pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-510/'>64.2-510</a>, as provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-1717.1/'>58.1-1717.1</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 58-67.1; 1960, c. 60; 1984, c. 675; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0266'>266</a>.</p>
