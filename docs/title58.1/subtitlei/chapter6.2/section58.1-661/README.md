# § 58.1-661 Certain provisions in Chapter 6 of this title to apply, mutatis mutandis.

<p>The provisions in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-630/'>58.1-630</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-637/'>58.1-637</a> of this title shall apply to this chapter, mutatis mutandis, except as herein provided and except that whenever the term "dealer" is used in these sections, the term "communications services provider" shall be substituted. The Tax Commissioner shall promulgate regulations to interpret and clarify the applicability of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-630/'>58.1-630</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/58.1-637/'>58.1-637</a> to this chapter.</p><p>2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0780'>780</a>.</p>
