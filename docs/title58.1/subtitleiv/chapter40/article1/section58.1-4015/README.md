# ยง 58.1-4015 Sale of ticket or share to person under eighteen prohibited; penalty.

<p>No ticket or share shall be sold to or redeemed from any person under the age of eighteen years. Any licensee who knowingly sells or offers to sell or redeem a lottery ticket or share to or from any person under the age of eighteen years is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.</p><p>1987, c. 531; 1989, c. 478.</p>
