# § 51.1-212 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Employee" means any (i) member of the Capitol Police Force as described in § <a href='/vacode/30-34.2:1/'>30-34.2:1</a>, (ii) campus police officer appointed under the provisions of Article 3 (§ <a href='/vacode/23.1-809/'>23.1-809</a> et seq.) of Chapter 8 of Title 23.1, (iii) conservation police officer in the Department of Wildlife Resources appointed under the provisions of Chapter 2 (§ <a href='/vacode/29.1-200/'>29.1-200</a> et seq.) of Title 29.1, (iv) special agent of the Virginia Alcoholic Beverage Control Authority appointed under the provisions of Chapter 1 (§ <a href='/vacode/4.1-100/'>4.1-100</a> et seq.) of Title 4.1, (v) law-enforcement officer employed by the Virginia Marine Resources Commission as described in § <a href='/vacode/9.1-101/'>9.1-101</a>, (vi) correctional officer as the term is defined in § <a href='/vacode/53.1-1/'>53.1-1</a>, and including correctional officers employed at a juvenile correction facility as the term is defined in § <a href='/vacode/66-25.3/'>66-25.3</a>, (vii) any parole officer appointed pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/53.1-143/'>53.1-143</a>, and (viii) any commercial vehicle enforcement officer employed by the Department of State Police.</p><p>"Member" means any person included in the membership of the Retirement System as provided in this chapter.</p><p>"Normal retirement date" means a member's sixtieth birthday.</p><p>"Retirement System" means the Virginia Law Officers' Retirement System.</p><p>1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0585'>585</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0087'>87</a>; 2015, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0038'>38</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0730'>730</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0958'>958</a>.</p>
