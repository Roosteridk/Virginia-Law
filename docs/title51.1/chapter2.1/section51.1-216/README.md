# § 51.1-216 Service retirement generally.

<p>A. Normal retirement.</p><p>1. Any employee commencing employment or reemployment on or after July 1, 2001, and any employee who makes the election provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-221/'>51.1-221</a>, who is a member in service in any retirement program administered by the Virginia Retirement System at his normal retirement date with five or more years of creditable service (i) as a member in the retirement system established by this chapter, (ii) as a member in the retirement system established by Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or (iii) while earning the benefits permitted by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a>, may retire upon written notification to the Board, setting forth the date the retirement is to become effective.</p><p>Effective December 31, 2003, any employee in service on June 30, 2002, and July 1, 2002, who is credited with five or more years of creditable service rendered under Chapter 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-100/'>51.1-100</a> et seq.) of this title, Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or this chapter shall not be subject to the vesting requirements of this section, and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-205/'>51.1-205</a>.</p><p>2. Any other employee who is a member in service at his normal retirement date with five or more years of creditable service (i) as a member in the retirement system established by this chapter, (ii) as a member in the retirement system established by Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or (iii) while earning the benefits permitted by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> may retire upon written notification to the Board, setting forth the date the retirement is to become effective.</p><p>Effective December 31, 2003, any employee in service on June 30, 2002, and July 1, 2002, who is credited with five or more years of creditable service rendered under Chapter 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-100/'>51.1-100</a> et seq.) of this title, Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or this chapter shall not be subject to the vesting requirements of this section, and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-205/'>51.1-205</a>.</p><p>B. Early retirement.</p><p>1. Any employee commencing employment or reemployment on or after July 1, 2001, and any employee who makes the election provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-221/'>51.1-221</a>, who is a member in service in any retirement program administered by the Virginia Retirement System other than the program established by this chapter shall retire pursuant to the early retirement provisions of the retirement program of which he is a member at the time of retirement.</p><p>Effective December 31, 2003, any employee in service on June 30, 2002, and July 1, 2002, who is credited with five or more years of creditable service rendered under Chapter 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-100/'>51.1-100</a> et seq.) of this title, Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or this chapter shall not be subject to the vesting requirements of this section, and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-205/'>51.1-205</a>.</p><p>2. Any other employee who is a member in service and who has attained his fiftieth birthday with five or more years of creditable service (i) as a member in the retirement system established by this chapter, (ii) as a member in the retirement system established by Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or (iii) while earning the benefits permitted by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> may retire upon written notification to the Board setting forth the date the retirement is to become effective.</p><p>Effective December 31, 2003, any employee in service on June 30, 2002, and July 1, 2002, who is credited with five or more years of creditable service rendered under Chapter 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-100/'>51.1-100</a> et seq.) of this title, Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or this chapter shall not be subject to the vesting requirements of this section, and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-205/'>51.1-205</a>.</p><p>C. Deferred retirement for members terminating service.</p><p>1. Any employee commencing employment or reemployment on or after July 1, 2001, and any employee who makes the election provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-221/'>51.1-221</a>, who terminates service from any position with membership in any retirement program administered by the Virginia Retirement System, may retire under the provisions of subdivision A 1 or B 1 if (i) he is otherwise eligible for such benefits, (ii) he has not withdrawn his accumulated contributions prior to the effective date of his retirement, and (iii) he has five or more years of creditable service (a) as a member in the retirement system established by this chapter, (b) as a member in the retirement system established by Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or (c) while earning the benefits permitted by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> for which his employer has paid the contributions and such contributions cannot be withdrawn. For the purposes of this subsection, any requirements as to the member being in service shall not apply. No member shall be entitled to the benefits of this subsection if his employer certifies that his service was terminated because of dishonesty, malfeasance, or misfeasance in office. The certification may be appealed to the Board.</p><p>Effective December 31, 2003, any employee in service on June 30, 2002, and July 1, 2002, who is credited with five or more years of creditable service rendered under Chapter 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-100/'>51.1-100</a> et seq.) of this title, Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or this chapter shall not be subject to the vesting requirements of this section, and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-205/'>51.1-205</a>.</p><p>2. Any other member who terminates service after five or more years of creditable service (i) as a member in the retirement system established by this chapter, (ii) as a member in the retirement system established by Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or (iii) while earning the benefits permitted by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> may retire under the provisions of subdivision A 2 or B 2 if he has not withdrawn his accumulated contributions prior to the effective date of his retirement or if he has five or more years of creditable service (a) as a member in the retirement system established by this chapter, (b) as a member in the retirement system established by Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or (c) while earning the benefits permitted by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> for which his employer has paid the contributions and such contributions cannot be withdrawn. For the purposes of this subsection, any requirements as to the member being in service shall not apply.</p><p>Effective December 31, 2003, any employee in service on June 30, 2002, and July 1, 2002, who is credited with five or more years of creditable service rendered under Chapter 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-100/'>51.1-100</a> et seq.) of this title, Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.) of this title, or this chapter shall not be subject to the vesting requirements of this section, and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-138/'>51.1-138</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-205/'>51.1-205</a>.</p><p>D. Effective date of retirement. -- The effective date of retirement shall be after the last day of service, but shall not be more than 90 days prior to the filing of the notice of retirement.</p><p>E. Notification on behalf of member. -- If the member is physically or mentally unable to submit written notification of his intention to retire, the member's appointing authority may submit notification on his behalf.</p><p>1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0585'>585</a>; 2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0066'>66</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0657'>657</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0911'>911</a>; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0804'>804</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0466'>466</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0083'>83</a>; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0483'>483</a>.</p>
