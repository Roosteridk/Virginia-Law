# § 51.1-600 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Act" means the Government Employees Deferred Compensation Plan Act.</p><p>"Board" means the Board of Trustees of the Virginia Retirement System.</p><p>"Deferred compensation plan" means a plan established pursuant to the provisions of § 457(b) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended, that may provide for elective and non-elective deferrals of compensation by or on behalf of employees and may include a qualified Roth contribution program as described in § 402A of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended.</p><p>"Employee" means, in the case of the plan described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-602/'>51.1-602</a>, all persons employed by a participating employer, including appointed or elected officials. In the case of a plan adopted by a county, municipality, authority or other political subdivision pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-603/'>51.1-603</a>, an employee shall be defined by such county, municipality, authority or other political subdivision, subject to the approval of the Board.</p><p>"Participating employer" means the Commonwealth or any political subdivision that has elected pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-603.1/'>51.1-603.1</a> to participate in the deferred compensation plan established by the Board pursuant to this chapter.</p><p>1974, c. 461, § 51-111.67:15; 1987, c. 619; 1990, c. 832; 1992, c. 276; 1999, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0980'>980</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0984'>984</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0311'>311</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0253'>253</a>; 2014, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0356'>356</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0747'>747</a>.</p>
