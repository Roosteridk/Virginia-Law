# § 51.1-210 Disability benefit.

<p>Any member in service who (i) is credited with at least twenty years of service in a hazardous duty position and (ii) meets the (a) requirements for a disability retirement allowance under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-156/'>51.1-156</a> and (b) minimum age requirements of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-205/'>51.1-205</a> may elect to receive the higher of the disability retirement allowance payable under Article 9 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-152/'>51.1-152</a> et seq.) of Chapter 1 of this title or the service retirement allowance payable under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-206/'>51.1-206</a> less any reduction in allowance required by subsection C of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-157/'>51.1-157</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-158/'>51.1-158</a>.</p><p>1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0889'>889</a>.</p>
