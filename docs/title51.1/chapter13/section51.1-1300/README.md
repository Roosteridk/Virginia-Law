# § 51.1-1300 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Board" means the Board of Trustees of the Virginia Retirement System.</p><p>"Member" means any member whose benefits under the Virginia Retirement System (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-124.1/'>51.1-124.1</a> et seq.), the State Police Officers' Retirement System (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.), the Virginia Law Enforcement Officers' Retirement System (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-211/'>51.1-211</a> et seq.), or the Judicial Retirement System (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-300/'>51.1-300</a> et seq.) would otherwise be limited by § 415(b) of the Internal Revenue Code.</p><p>"Retirement System" means the Virginia Retirement System.</p><p>2001, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0681'>681</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0692'>692</a>.</p>
