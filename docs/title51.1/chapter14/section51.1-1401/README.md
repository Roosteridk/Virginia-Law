# § 51.1-1401 Health insurance credits for retired teachers.

<p>A. A teacher, as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-124.3/'>51.1-124.3</a>, retired under the Virginia Retirement System, including the hybrid retirement program described in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-169/'>51.1-169</a>, and any employee retired under a defined contribution plan pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-126.6/'>51.1-126.6</a>, who rendered at least 15 years of total creditable service under the System or plan shall receive a health insurance credit to his monthly retirement allowance, which shall be applied to reduce the retired member's health insurance premium cost. The amount of each monthly health insurance credit payable under this section shall be $4 for each full year of the retired member's creditable service; however, each former member whose retirement was for disability, any employee participant pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-126.6/'>51.1-126.6</a> receiving long-term disability, a participant receiving long-term disability pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-1157/'>51.1-1157</a> or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-1165/'>51.1-1165</a>, or a member of the hybrid retirement program receiving long-term disability pursuant to coverage under subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-1153/'>51.1-1153</a> shall receive a monthly health insurance credit of $4 multiplied by the smaller of (i) twice the amount of his creditable service or (ii) the amount of creditable service he would have completed at age 60 if he had remained in service to that age. Eligibility for the credit shall be determined in a manner prescribed by the Virginia Retirement System. Any member who elects to defer his retirement pursuant to subsection C of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-153/'>51.1-153</a> shall be entitled to receive the allowable credit provided by this section on the effective date of his retirement.</p><p>B. Those retired employees who purchase an alternative personal health insurance policy from a carrier or organization of their own choosing shall be eligible to receive a credit in the amount specified in subsection D. Eligibility for the credit and payment of the credit shall be determined in a manner prescribed by the Virginia Retirement System.</p><p>C. The credit shall be in (i) the amount provided in subsection A or (ii) the amount of premium paid for the personal health insurance policy, whichever is less.</p><p>D. Any person included in the membership of a retirement system provided by Chapter 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-124.1/'>51.1-124.1</a> et seq.), 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-200/'>51.1-200</a> et seq.), 2.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-211/'>51.1-211</a> et seq.), or 3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-300/'>51.1-300</a> et seq.) who (i) rendered at least 15 years of total creditable service as a teacher as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-124.3/'>51.1-124.3</a> and (ii) after terminating service as a teacher, was employed by a local government that does not elect to provide a health insurance credit under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-1402/'>51.1-1402</a>, shall be eligible for the credit provided by subsection A and subsection B if provided by the school division from which the service described in clause (i) was rendered, provided that the retired employee is participating in a health insurance plan. The Commonwealth and local school division, if appropriate, shall be charged with the credit as provided for in subsection E. In such case, the health insurance credit shall be determined based upon the amount of state service or service as a teacher, whichever is greater.</p><p>E. The Virginia Retirement System shall (i) actuarially determine the amount necessary to fund all credits provided under this section; (ii) reflect the cost of such credits in the applicable employer contribution rate pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-145/'>51.1-145</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-204/'>51.1-204</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/51.1-304/'>51.1-304</a>; and (iii) prescribe such terms and conditions as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this section. The costs associated with the administration of the health insurance program provided for in this section shall be recovered from the health insurance credit trust fund.</p><p>1992, c. 770, § 2.1-20.1:3; 1993, cc. 743, 938; 1995, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0612'>612</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0734'>734</a>; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0764'>764</a>; 1998, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0195'>195</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0676'>676</a>; 1999, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0832'>832</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0980'>980</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0984'>984</a>; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0911'>911</a>; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0084'>84</a>; 2007, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0866'>866</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0921'>921</a>; 2009, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0023'>23</a>; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0722'>722</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0701'>701</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0823'>823</a>.</p>
