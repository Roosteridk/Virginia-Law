# ยง 23.1-2906 Comprehensive community colleges; duties; workforce.

<p>Each comprehensive community college shall:</p><p>1. Maximize noncredit course offerings made available to business and industry at a time and place that meet current and projected workforce needs and minimize the cost of noncredit offerings to business and industry to the extent feasible;</p><p>2. Deal directly with employers in designing and offering courses to meet real, current, and projected workforce training needs; and</p><p>3. Maximize the availability and use of distance learning courses addressing workforce training needs.</p><p>1966, c. 679, ยง 23-215; 1977, c. 413; 1998, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0111'>111</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0396'>396</a>; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0483'>483</a>; 2002, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0586'>586</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0625'>625</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0146'>146</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0588'>588</a>.</p>
