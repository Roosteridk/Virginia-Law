# ยง 23.1-2903 State Board; officers, meetings, and regulations.

<p>A. The State Board shall elect a chairman from its membership and may provide for the election of one of its members as vice-chairman.</p><p>B. The State Board shall meet at least four times annually and on the call of the chairman when in his opinion additional meetings are expedient or necessary.</p><p>C. Eight members of the State Board shall constitute a quorum for all purposes.</p><p>D. The main office of the State Board shall be in the Commonwealth.</p><p>E. The State Board may adopt necessary regulations for carrying out the purposes of this chapter.</p><p>1966, c. 679, ยง 23-217; 1980, c. 728; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0652'>652</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0588'>588</a>; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0314'>314</a>.</p>
