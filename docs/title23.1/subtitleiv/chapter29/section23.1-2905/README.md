# § 23.1-2905 State Board; powers.

<p>In addition to the powers of governing boards of public institutions of higher education set forth in Chapter 13 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/23.1-1300/'>23.1-1300</a> et seq.), the State Board may:</p><p>1. With the approval of the Governor, accept from any government or governmental department or agency or any public or private body or from any other source grants or contributions of money or property that the State Board may use for or in aid of any of its purposes;</p><p>2. Control and expend funds appropriated by law;</p><p>3. Fix tuition, mandatory fees, and other necessary charges;</p><p>4. Establish policies and guidelines providing for reduced tuition rates at comprehensive community colleges for employees of the System; and</p><p>5. Confer diplomas, certificates, and associate degrees.</p><p>1966, c. 679, §§ 23-218, 23-219; 1996, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0134'>134</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0486'>486</a>; 2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0337'>337</a>; 2015, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0063'>63</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0086'>86</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0588'>588</a>.</p>
