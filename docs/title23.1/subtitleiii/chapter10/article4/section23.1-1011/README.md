# § 23.1-1011 Covered institutions; operational authority; campus police.

<p>A. A covered institution may establish or continue to operate a campus police department in accordance with the provisions of Article 3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/23.1-809/'>23.1-809</a> et seq.) of Chapter 8. Campus police shall possess the powers provided in Article 3 of Chapter 8, except that a covered institution's employment of campus police is governed by the provisions of this article rather than by Chapter 28 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2800/'>2.2-2800</a> et seq.) and Chapter 29 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-2900/'>2.2-2900</a> et seq.) of Title 2.2.</p><p>B. Campus police officers of a covered institution are eligible to participate in the same state-sponsored retirement plans on the same terms and conditions as campus police officers of other public institutions of higher education.</p><p>2005, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0933'>933</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0945'>945</a>, § 23-38.102; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0588'>588</a>.</p>
