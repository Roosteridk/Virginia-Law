# ยง 23.1-3101 A.L. Philpott Manufacturing Extension Partnership established; purpose and duties.

<p>A. The A.L. Philpott Manufacturing Extension Partnership (the Extension Partnership), doing business as Genedge Alliance, is established as a political subdivision of the Commonwealth to help create and maintain industrial and manufacturing jobs. The Extension Partnership shall:</p><p>1. Develop, demonstrate, test, and assist in the implementation of advanced manufacturing technologies;</p><p>2. Promote industrial expansion by providing manufacturing technology consulting services to manufacturers in the Commonwealth;</p><p>3. Foster the creation of manufacturing networks and the development of buyer and supplier relationships in the region and throughout the Commonwealth;</p><p>4. Serve as a resource center for industrial training and technology transfer programs for the renewal, enhancement, and expansion of existing manufacturing enterprises and manufacturing modernization outreach;</p><p>5. Be available as a federal demonstration center for the training of displaced workers in any manufacturing area; and</p><p>6. Receive and accept any available grants from any federal, state, or private agency, corporation, association, or person to be expended in fulfilling the duties enumerated in this subsection.</p><p>B. The Extension Partnership is a local or regional industrial or economic development authority or organization for purposes of the Virginia Freedom of Information Act (ยง <a href='/vacode/2.2-3700/'>2.2-3700</a> et seq.).</p><p>1992, cc. 217, 668, ยง 23-231.8; 1994, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0031'>31</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0383'>383</a>; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0336'>336</a>; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0324'>324</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0588'>588</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0398'>398</a>.</p>
