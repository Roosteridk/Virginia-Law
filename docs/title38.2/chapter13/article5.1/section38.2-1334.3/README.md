<p>As used in this article, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Insurance group" means those insurers and affiliates included within an insurance holding company system as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-1322/'>38.2-1322</a>.</p><p>"Insurer" means an insurance company as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-100/'>38.2-100</a>, except that "insurer" shall not include agencies, authorities, or instrumentalities of the United States, its possessions and territories, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, the District of Columbia, or a state or political subdivision of a state.</p><p>"NAIC" means the National Association of Insurance Commissioners.</p><p>"ORSA Guidance Manual" means the current version of the NAIC Own Risk and Solvency Assessment (ORSA) Guidance Manual developed and adopted by the NAIC and as amended from time to time.
