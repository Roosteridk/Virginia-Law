# § 38.2-1902 Scope of chapter.

<p>A. Except as provided in subsection B, this chapter applies to the classes of insurance defined in §§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-110/'>38.2-110</a> through <a href='/vacode/38.2-122/'>38.2-122</a>, <a href='/vacode/38.2-124/'>38.2-124</a> through <a href='/vacode/38.2-128/'>38.2-128</a> and <a href='/vacode/38.2-130/'>38.2-130</a> through <a href='/vacode/38.2-133/'>38.2-133</a>.</p><p>B. This chapter does not apply to:</p><p>1. Insurance written through the Virginia Workers' Compensation Plan pursuant to Chapter 20 (§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-2000/'>38.2-2000</a> et seq.) of this title;</p><p>2. Insurance on a specific risk as provided in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-1920/'>38.2-1920</a>;</p><p>3. Reinsurance, other than joint reinsurance, to the extent stated in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-1915/'>38.2-1915</a>;</p><p>4. Life insurance as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-102/'>38.2-102</a>;</p><p>5. Annuities as defined in §§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-106/'>38.2-106</a> and <a href='/vacode/38.2-107/'>38.2-107</a>;</p><p>6. Accident and sickness insurance as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-109/'>38.2-109</a>;</p><p>7. Title insurance as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-123/'>38.2-123</a>;</p><p>8. Insurance of vessels or craft used primarily in a trade or business, their cargoes, marine builders' risks and marine protection and indemnity;</p><p>9. Insurance against loss of or damage to hulls of aircraft, including their accessories and equipment, or against liability, other than workers' compensation and employers' liability, arising out of the ownership, maintenance or use of aircraft;</p><p>10. Insurance written through the Virginia Automobile Insurance Plan. However, § <a href='/vacode/38.2-1905/'>38.2-1905</a> shall apply to insurance written through the Plan;</p><p>11. Insurance provided pursuant to Chapter 27 (§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-2700/'>38.2-2700</a> et seq.);</p><p>12. Home protection contracts as defined by § <a href='/vacode/38.2-2600/'>38.2-2600</a> and their rates until such time as the Commission determines there is sufficient competition in the industry as provided by § <a href='/vacode/38.2-2608/'>38.2-2608</a>.</p><p>C. This chapter shall not apply to any class of insurance written (i) by any mutual assessment property and casualty insurance company organized and operating under the laws of this Commonwealth and doing business only in this Commonwealth or (ii) by any mutual insurance company or association organized under the laws of this Commonwealth, conducting business only in this Commonwealth, and issuing only policies providing for perpetual insurance.</p><p>1973, c. 504, § 38.1-279.31; 1976, c. 636; 1981, c. 530; 1986, c. 562; 1987, c. 519; 1993, cc. 774, 985; 1995, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0744'>744</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0803'>803</a>; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0526'>526</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0145'>145</a>; 2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0180'>180</a>.</p>
