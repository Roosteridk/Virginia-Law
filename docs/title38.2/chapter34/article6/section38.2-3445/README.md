# ยง 38.2-3445 Patient access to emergency services.

<p>Notwithstanding any provision of ยง <a href='/vacode/38.2-3407.11/'>38.2-3407.11</a>, <a href='/vacode/38.2-4312.3/'>38.2-4312.3</a>, or any other section of this title to the contrary, if a health carrier providing individual or group health insurance coverage provides any benefits with respect to services in an emergency department of a hospital, the health carrier shall provide coverage for emergency services:</p><p>1. Without the need for any prior authorization determination, regardless of whether the emergency services are provided on an in-network or out-of-network basis;</p><p>2. Without regard to the final diagnosis rendered to the covered person or whether the health care provider furnishing the emergency services is a participating health care provider with respect to such services;</p><p>3. If such services are provided out-of-network, without imposing any administrative requirement or limitation on coverage that is more restrictive than the requirements or limitations that apply to such services received from an in-network provider;</p><p>4. If such services are provided out-of-network, the health carrier shall pay the out-of-network provider in accordance with ยง <a href='/vacode/38.2-3445.01/'>38.2-3445.01</a> less any cost-sharing requirement. Any such cost-sharing requirement shall not exceed the cost-sharing requirement that would apply if such services were provided in-network as provided in ยง <a href='/vacode/38.2-3445.01/'>38.2-3445.01</a>; and</p><p>5. Without regard to any term or condition of such coverage other than the exclusion of or coordination of benefits or an affiliation or waiting period.</p><p>2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0882'>882</a>; 2020, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1080'>1080</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1081'>1081</a>.</p>
