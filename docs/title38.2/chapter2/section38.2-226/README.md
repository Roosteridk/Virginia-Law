# ยง 38.2-226 Provisions of title not to apply to certain mutual aid associations.

<p>This title shall not apply to beneficial, relief, or mutual aid societies, or partnerships, plans, associations, or corporations, established prior to 1935 and formed by churches for the purpose of aiding members who sustain property losses by fire, lightning, hail, storm, flood, explosion, power failure, theft, burglary, vandalism, civil commotion, airplane and vehicular damage, and in which the privileges and memberships in these societies, partnerships, plans, associations, or corporations are confined to members of the churches.</p><p>1981, c. 171, ยง 38.1-42.1; 1985, c. 361; 1986, c. 562.</p>
