# § 38.2-6108 Plan dentist contracts; preferred providers; assignment of benefits.

<p>A. Each contract with a plan dentist shall contain the following provisions:</p><p>1. A hold harmless clause that satisfies the requirements of subdivision C 9 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5805/'>38.2-5805</a>.</p><p>2. A provision specifying when the contract becomes effective.</p><p>3. A provision specifying the date the contract terminates.</p><p>4. A provision specifying the renewal terms.</p><p>5. Provisions incorporating the rights, remedies and obligations applicable to providers and dental plan organizations set forth in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407.1/'>38.2-3407.1</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407.10/'>38.2-3407.10</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407.15/'>38.2-3407.15</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5805/'>38.2-5805</a>.</p><p>B. A dental plan organization that offers or administers a plan under which preferred provider policies or contracts are issued shall comply with the requirements of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407/'>38.2-3407</a> in contracting with dentists for services in connection with the preferred provider policies or contracts.</p><p>C. All dental plan organizations shall comply with the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407.13/'>38.2-3407.13</a> relating to assignments of benefits by subscribers and enrollees.</p><p>2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0668'>668</a>.</p>
