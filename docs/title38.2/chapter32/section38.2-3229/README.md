# § 38.2-3229 Effective date.

<p>After July 1, 1979, for contracts subject to subsection B, C, or D of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3221/'>38.2-3221</a>, the operative date for application of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3219/'>38.2-3219</a> shall be the earlier of July 1, 1981, or the date on which the insurer filed with the Commission a written notice of its election to comply with the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3219/'>38.2-3219</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3229/'>38.2-3229</a> after a specified date before July 1, 1981. For contracts subject to subsection F of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3221/'>38.2-3221</a>, the operative date shall be July 1, 2005, unless the insurer specifies an earlier date by filing with the Commission written notice of its election to apply subdivisions F 1 through F 4 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3221/'>38.2-3221</a> on a contract-form-by-contract-form basis after a specified date, which shall be on or after July 1, 2004, and before July 1, 2005.</p><p>1979, c. 437, § 38.1-470.1; 1986, c. 562; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0313'>313</a>.</p>
