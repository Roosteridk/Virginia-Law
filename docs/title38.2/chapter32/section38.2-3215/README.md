# § 38.2-3215 Same; operative date for § 38.2-3207.

<p>After July 1, 1959, any insurer may file with the Commission a written notice of its election to comply with the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3207/'>38.2-3207</a> after a specified date before January 1, 1966. After the filing of the notice, then upon the specified date, which shall be the operative date of this section for the insurer, § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3207/'>38.2-3207</a> shall become operative with respect to the ordinary policies thereafter issued by that insurer. If an insurer makes no such election, the operative date of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3207/'>38.2-3207</a> for the insurer shall be January 1, 1966.</p><p>1959, Ex. Sess., c. 43, § 38.1-468.1; 1986, c. 562.</p>
