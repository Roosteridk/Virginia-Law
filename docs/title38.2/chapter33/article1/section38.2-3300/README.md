# § 38.2-3300 Requirements; exceptions.

<p>A. No individual life insurance policy shall be delivered or issued for delivery in this Commonwealth unless it contains in substance all of the requirements prescribed in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3301/'>38.2-3301</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3315/'>38.2-3315</a> of this article.</p><p>B. As used in this article, "individual life insurance" means any life insurance other than group life insurance, industrial life insurance, annuities, credit life insurance, and pure endowments, with or without return of premiums or of premiums and interest. However, for the purposes of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3308/'>38.2-3308</a>, "policy" includes annuity contracts that provide for policy loans and certificates issued by a fraternal benefit society.</p><p>C. The requirements of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3300/'>38.2-3300</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3315/'>38.2-3315</a> shall not apply to policies of reinsurance or to policies issued or granted in exchange for lapsed or surrendered policies.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 38-371, 38-373; 1952, c. 317, §§ 38.1-390, 38.1-405; 1977, c. 174; 1986, c. 562.</p>
