# ยง 38.2-508.2 Discrimination prohibited.

<p>No person shall refuse to issue or refuse to continue a life insurance policy on the life of any individual solely because of that individual's race, color, sexual orientation, gender identity, religion, national origin, or sex.</p><p>1993, c. 152; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1137'>1137</a>.</p>
