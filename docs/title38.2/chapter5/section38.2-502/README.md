# ยง 38.2-502 Misrepresentations and false advertising of insurance policies.

<p>No person shall make, issue, circulate, cause or knowingly allow to be made, issued or circulated, any estimate, illustration, circular, statement, sales presentation, omission, or comparison that:</p><p>1. Misrepresents the benefits, advantages, conditions or terms of any insurance policy;</p><p>2. Misrepresents the dividends or share of the surplus to be received on any insurance policy;</p><p>3. Makes any false or misleading statements as to the dividends or share of surplus previously paid on any insurance policy;</p><p>4. Misrepresents or is misleading as to the financial condition of any person or the legal reserve system upon which any life insurer operates;</p><p>5. Uses any name or title of any insurance policy or class of insurance policies that misrepresents the true nature of the policy or policies;</p><p>6. Misrepresents any material fact for the purpose of inducing or tending to induce the lapse, forfeiture, exchange, conversion, replacement, or surrender of any insurance policy;</p><p>7. Misrepresents any material fact for the purpose of effecting a pledge, assignment, or loan on any insurance policy; or</p><p>8. Misrepresents any insurance policy as being a share of stock.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 38.1-52; 1952, c. 317, ยง 38.1-52.1; 1977, c. 529; 1978, c. 441; 1979, c. 324; 1980, c. 404; 1986, c. 562; 1990, c. 265.</p>
