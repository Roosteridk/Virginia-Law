# § 38.2-2602 Limited applicability to certain insurers.

<p>A property and casualty insurer may be licensed to transact home protection insurance as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-129/'>38.2-129</a>. An insurer licensed in this Commonwealth to transact the class of insurance defined by § <a href='/vacode/38.2-111/'>38.2-111</a> on July 1, 1986, may also transact home protection insurance without additional authority. No other provision of this chapter, except § <a href='/vacode/38.2-2606/'>38.2-2606</a> and §§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-2608/'>38.2-2608</a> through <a href='/vacode/38.2-2614/'>38.2-2614</a>, shall be applicable to the insurers, their businesses, or their home protection contracts.</p><p>1981, c. 530, §§ 38.1-933, 38.1-945; 1986, c. 562; 2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0634'>634</a>; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0727'>727</a>.</p>
