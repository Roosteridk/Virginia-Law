# § 38.2-3518 Standards for policy provisions.

<p>A. Pursuant to the authority granted in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-223/'>38.2-223</a>, the Commission may issue rules and regulations to establish standards for the sale of individual and group accident and sickness insurance policies. These rules and regulations shall be in addition to and in accordance with applicable laws of the Commonwealth, including Chapter 34 (§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-3400/'>38.2-3400</a> et seq.), Article 1 (§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-3500/'>38.2-3500</a> et seq.), this article, and Article 3 (§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-3521.1/'>38.2-3521.1</a> et seq.).</p><p>B. Pursuant to the authority granted in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-223/'>38.2-223</a>, the Commission may issue rules and regulations that specify prohibited policies or policy provisions not otherwise specifically authorized by statute that in the opinion of the Commission are unjust, unfair, or unfairly discriminatory to the policyowner, beneficiary, or any person insured under the policy.</p><p>1980, c. 204, § 38.1-362.13; 1981, c. 575; 1986, c. 562; 2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0531'>531</a>.</p>
