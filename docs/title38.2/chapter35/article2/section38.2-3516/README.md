# § 38.2-3516 Purpose.

<p>The purpose of this article is to authorize the Commission, pursuant to the authority granted in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-223/'>38.2-223</a>, to issue rules and regulations to:</p><p>1. Establish the minimum standards for filing of policy forms for individual and small group health benefit plans as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-3438/'>38.2-3438</a>;</p><p>2. Establish the minimum standards, terms, and coverages for individual and group accident and sickness policies known as excepted benefits, as defined in § <a href='/vacode/38.2-3431/'>38.2-3431</a>; and</p><p>3. Establish the minimum standards for short-term limited-duration insurance.</p><p>The Commission shall ensure that policy standards are simple and understandable and are not misleading or unreasonably confusing and that the sale of such policies provides for full disclosure.</p><p>1980, c. 204, § 38.1-362.11; 1986, c. 562; 2022, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0531'>531</a>.</p>
