# ยง 38.2-3546 Cancellation of sick benefit portion of policy.

<p>Every policy of industrial sick benefit insurance issued in this Commonwealth after June 18, 1922, shall contain a provision that:</p><p>1. The sick benefit portion of the policy may be cancelled by either the insurer or the insured and the life portion continued by a payment of twenty percent of the original premium;</p><p>2. If the cancellation is by the insurer, it shall be without prejudice to any claim arising on account of disability commencing prior to the date on which the cancellation takes effect; and</p><p>3. Written notice of the cancellation and payment for the unearned portion of the premium shall be delivered to the insured or mailed to him at his last known address.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 38-359; 1952, c. 317, ยง 38.1-485; 1986, c. 562.</p>
