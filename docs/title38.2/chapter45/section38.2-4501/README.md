# ยง 38.2-4501 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter:</p><p>"Contract holder" means a person entering into a subscription contract with a nonstock corporation.</p><p>"Dental services plans" means any arrangement for offering or administering prepaid dental services by a nonstock corporation licensed under this chapter.</p><p>"Nonstock corporation" means a foreign or domestic nonstock corporation which is subject to regulation and licensing under this chapter and which operates a dental services plan or an optometric services plan.</p><p>"Optometric services plan" means any arrangement for offering or administering prepaid optometric services by a nonstock corporation licensed under this chapter.</p><p>"Plan" means any dental services plan or any optometric services plan subject to regulation under this chapter.</p><p>"Subscriber" means any person entitled to benefits under the terms and conditions of a subscription contract.</p><p>"Subscription contract" means a written contract which is issued to a contract holder by a nonstock corporation and which provides dental or optometric services or benefits for dental or optometric services.</p><p>1986, c. 562.</p>
