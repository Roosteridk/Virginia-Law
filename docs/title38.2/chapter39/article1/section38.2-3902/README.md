# § 38.2-3902 Classes of insurance that may be written by mutual assessment life, accident and sickness insurers.

<p>The following classes of insurance can be written by mutual assessment life, accident and sickness insurers:</p><p>Category A</p><p>1. Life insurance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-102/'>38.2-102</a>;</p><p>2. Industrial life insurance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-104/'>38.2-104</a>; and</p><p>3. Accident and sickness insurance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-109/'>38.2-109</a> except Medicare supplement insurance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3600/'>38.2-3600</a>.</p><p>Category B</p><p>1. Credit life insurance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-103/'>38.2-103</a>;</p><p>2. Variable life insurance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-105/'>38.2-105</a>;</p><p>3. Credit accident and sickness insurance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-108/'>38.2-108</a>; and</p><p>4. Medicare supplement insurance as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3600/'>38.2-3600</a>.</p><p>Category C</p><p>1. Annuities as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-106/'>38.2-106</a>; and</p><p>2. Variable annuities as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-107/'>38.2-107</a>.</p><p>1985, c. 400, § 38.1-549.3; 1986, c. 562.</p>
