# § 38.2-3607 Group or individual Medicare supplement policies; minimum standards.

<p>A. The provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3418.1/'>38.2-3418.1</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3604/'>38.2-3604</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3605/'>38.2-3605</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3606/'>38.2-3606</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3516/'>38.2-3516</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3520/'>38.2-3520</a> shall be applicable to group Medicare supplement policies. The term "policy" as used in this article shall include a certificate issued under a group Medicare supplement policy which has been delivered or issued for delivery in this Commonwealth.</p><p>B. The provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3418.1/'>38.2-3418.1</a> shall be applicable to individual Medicare supplement policies.</p><p>C. No Medicare supplement policy or certificate in force in this Commonwealth shall contain benefits that duplicate benefits provided by Medicare.</p><p>1981, c. 575, § 38.1-362.17; 1986, c. 562; 1989, c. 646; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0011'>11</a>.</p>
