<p>The Commission may suspend, revoke or refuse to renew a license of any insurance premium finance company whenever it finds that:</p><p>1. The licensee no longer meets the standards required for the initial issuance of a license.</p><p>1964, c. 147, ยง 38.1-739; 1986, c. 562.</p>
