# § 38.2-5514 Phase-in provision.

<p>For RBC Reports required to be filed by insurers with respect to 1994, the following requirements shall apply in lieu of the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5503/'>38.2-5503</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5504/'>38.2-5504</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5505/'>38.2-5505</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5506/'>38.2-5506</a>:</p><p>1. In the event of a Company Action Level Event with respect to a domestic insurer, the Commission shall take no regulatory action hereunder.</p><p>2. In the event of a Regulatory Action Level Event under subdivision A 1, A 2 or A 3 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5504/'>38.2-5504</a>, the Commission shall take the actions required under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5503/'>38.2-5503</a>.</p><p>3. In the event of a Regulatory Action Level Event under subdivision 4, 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9 of subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5504/'>38.2-5504</a> or an Authorized Control Level Event, the Commission shall take the actions required under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5504/'>38.2-5504</a> with respect to the insurer.</p><p>4. In the event of a Mandatory Control Level Event with respect to an insurer, the Commission shall take the actions required under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5505/'>38.2-5505</a> with respect to the insurer.</p><p>1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0789'>789</a>.</p>
