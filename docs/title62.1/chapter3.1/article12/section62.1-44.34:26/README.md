# ยง 62.1-44.34:26 Responsibilities of the Council.

<p>The Council shall have the following responsibilities:</p><p>1. To foster the exchange of information between the federal, state, and local government;</p><p>2. To enhance Virginia's participation in the United States Environmental Protection Agency's Region III Response Team;</p><p>3. To review and evaluate the response to emergency situations and recommend changes to the Commonwealth of Virginia's Oil and Hazardous Materials Emergency Response Plan;</p><p>4. To provide ongoing analysis of the most recent technical developments for the remediation of discharges; and</p><p>5. To coordinate its activities with the Secure and Resilient Commonwealth Panel.</p><p>1990, c. 598; 2011, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0594'>594</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0681'>681</a>.</p>
