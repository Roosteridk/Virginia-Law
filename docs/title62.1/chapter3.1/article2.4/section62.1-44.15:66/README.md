# § 62.1-44.15:66  No limitation on authority of Department of Energy.

<p>The provisions of this article shall not limit the powers or duties of the Department of Energy as they relate to mine reclamation under Chapters 10 (§ <a href='/vacode/45.2-1000/'>45.2-1000</a> et seq.) and 12 (§ <a href='/vacode/45.2-1200/'>45.2-1200</a> et seq.) or oil or gas exploration under the Virginia Gas and Oil Act (§ <a href='/vacode/45.2-1600/'>45.2-1600</a> et seq.).</p><p>1973, c. 486, § 21-89.13; 1988, c. 891, § 10.1-571; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0688'>688</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0785'>785</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0819'>819</a>; 2013, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0047'>47</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0129'>129</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0756'>756</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0793'>793</a>; 2021, Sp.
