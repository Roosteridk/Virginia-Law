# § 62.1-44.25 (For contingent expiration date, see Acts 2016, cc. 68 and 758, as amended by Acts 2017, c. 345) Right to hearing.

<p>Any owner under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.16/'>62.1-44.16</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.17/'>62.1-44.17</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.19/'>62.1-44.19</a> aggrieved by any action of the Board taken without a formal hearing, or by inaction of the Board, may demand in writing a formal hearing of such owner's grievance, provided a petition requesting such hearing is filed with the Board. In cases involving actions of the Board, such petition must be filed within thirty days after notice of such action is mailed to such owner by certified mail.</p><p>1970, c. 638.</p>
