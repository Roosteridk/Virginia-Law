# ยง 62.1-44.17:3 Toxic substances reduction in state waters; report required.

<p>A. The Board shall (i) conduct ongoing assessments of the amounts of toxics in Virginia's waters and (ii) develop and implement a plan for the reduction of toxics in Virginia's waters.</p><p>B. The status of the Board's efforts to reduce the level of toxic substances in state waters shall be reported biennially, no later than January 1 in each odd-numbered year, to the House Committee on Agriculture, Chesapeake and Natural Resources and the Senate Committee on Agriculture, Conservation and Natural Resources. The report shall include the following information:</p><p>1. Compliance data on permits that have limits for toxics;</p><p>2. The number of new permits or reissued permits that have toxic limits and the location of each permitted facility;</p><p>3. The location and number of monitoring stations and the period of time that monitoring has occurred at each location;</p><p>4. A summary of pollution prevention and pollution control activities for the reduction of toxics in state waters;</p><p>5. The sampling results from the monitoring stations for the previous two years;</p><p>6. The Board's plan for continued reduction of the discharge of toxics, which shall include, but not be limited to, additional monitoring activities, a work plan for the pollution prevention program, and any pilot projects established for the use of innovative technologies to reduce the discharge of toxics;</p><p>7. The identification of any segments for which the Board or the Director of the Department of Environmental Quality has made a decision to conduct additional evaluation or monitoring. Information regarding these segments shall include, at a minimum, the geographic location of the stream segment within a named county or city; and</p><p>8. The identification of any segments that are designated as toxic impaired waters as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/62.1-44.19:4/'>62.1-44.19:4</a> and any plans to address the impairment.</p><p>1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0155'>155</a>; 2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0017'>17</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1043'>1043</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0173'>173</a>.</p>
