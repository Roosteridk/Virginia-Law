# ยง 62.1-69.32 Withdrawal; dissolution.

<p>A. A locality may withdraw from the Commission one year after providing a written notice to the Commission of its intent to do so.</p><p>B. The Commission may dissolve itself upon a two-thirds vote of all members.</p><p>C. The Commission may be dissolved by repeal or expiration of this chapter.</p><p>D. The Commission shall be dissolved if the membership of the Commission falls below two-thirds of those eligible.</p><p>E. Upon the Commission's dissolution, all funds and assets of the Commission shall be divided on a pro rata basis. The Commonwealth's share of the funds and assets shall be transferred to the Office of the Secretary of Natural and Historic Resources for appropriate distribution.</p><p>1998, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0553'>553</a>; 2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0386'>386</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0456'>456</a>; 2021, Sp.
