# ยง 62.1-241.11 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires otherwise:</p><p>"Combined sewer overflow" or "CSO" means the discharge of untreated sanitary wastes, including industrial wastes and other wastes conveyed through a sanitary sewer system, and stormwater from combined stormwater and sanitary sewers.</p><p>1992, c. 363.</p>
