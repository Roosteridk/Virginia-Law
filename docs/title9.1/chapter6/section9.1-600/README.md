# ยง 9.1-600 Civilian protection in cases of police misconduct; minimum standards.

<p>A. State, local, and other public law-enforcement agencies, which have ten or more law-enforcement officers, shall have procedures as established in subsection B, allowing citizen submission of complaints regarding the conduct of the law-enforcement agency, law-enforcement officers in the agency, or employees of the agency.</p><p>B. Law-enforcement agencies shall ensure, at a minimum, that in the case of all written complaints:</p><p>1. The general public has access to the required forms and information concerning the submission of complaints;</p><p>2. The law-enforcement agency assists individuals in filing complaints; and</p><p>3. Adequate records are maintained of the nature and disposition of such cases.</p><p>1993, c. 722, ยง 2.1-116.9:6; 2001, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0153'>153</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>.</p>
