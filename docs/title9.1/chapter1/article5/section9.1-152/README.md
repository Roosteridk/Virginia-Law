# ยง 9.1-152 Local court-appointed special advocate programs; powers and duties.

<p>A. The Department shall provide a portion of any funding appropriated for this purpose to applicants seeking to establish and operate a local court-appointed special advocate program in their respective judicial districts. Only local programs operated in accordance with this article shall be eligible to receive state funds.</p><p>B. Local programs may be established and operated by local boards created for this purpose. Local boards shall ensure conformance to regulations adopted by the Board and may:</p><p>1. Solicit and accept financial support from public and private sources.</p><p>2. Oversee the financial and program management of the local court-appointed special advocate program.</p><p>3. Employ and supervise a director who shall serve as a professional liaison to personnel of the court and agencies serving children.</p><p>4. Employ such staff as is necessary to the operation of the program.</p><p>1990, c. 752, ยง 9-173.7; 1991, c. 421; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>.</p>
