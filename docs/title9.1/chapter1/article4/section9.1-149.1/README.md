# ยง 9.1-149.1 Unlawful advertisement for regulated services; notice; penalty.

<p>A. It shall be unlawful for any person to place before the public through any medium an advertisement for services in the Commonwealth requiring a license, certification, or registration under this article unless the individual who will perform such services possesses the necessary license, certification, or registration at the time of the posting.</p><p>B. Whenever the Board receives information that an advertisement has been placed in violation of this section, the Board shall provide notice to the entity publishing the advertisement to the public.</p><p>C. Any person who is convicted of a violation of subsection A is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.</p><p>2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0396'>396</a>.</p>
