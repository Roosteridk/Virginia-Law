# § 9.1-113 Compliance with minimum training standards by certain officers; exceptions.

<p>The provisions of this chapter shall not be construed to require (i) law-enforcement officers serving under permanent appointment on July 1, 1971, (ii) officers serving under permanent appointment under the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/56-353/'>56-353</a> appointed prior to July 1, 1982, or (iii) officers serving under permanent appointment under the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/10.1-115/'>10.1-115</a> appointed prior to July 1, 2003; to meet the compulsory minimum training standards provided for in subdivision 2 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-102/'>9.1-102</a>. Nor shall failure of any such officer to meet such standards make him ineligible for any promotional examination for which he is otherwise eligible. However, any law-enforcement officer designated under the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-120/'>53.1-120</a> to provide courthouse and courtroom security shall be required to meet the standards provided under subdivision 7 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/9.1-102/'>9.1-102</a>. Any full-time deputy sheriff who is a law-enforcement officer and who is exempted from the compulsory minimum training standards under this section shall be eligible for the minimum salary established pursuant to Article 3 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1609/'>15.2-1609</a> et seq.) of Chapter 16 of Title 15.2.</p><p>1981, c. 632, § 9-179; 1982, c. 419; 1984, c. 779; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0495'>495</a>; 2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0844'>844</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0744'>744</a>.</p>
