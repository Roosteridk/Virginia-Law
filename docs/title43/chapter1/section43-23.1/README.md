# ยง 43-23.1 Forfeiture of lien.

<p>Any person who shall, with intent to mislead, include in his memorandum of lien work not performed upon, or materials not furnished for, the property described in his memorandum shall thereby forfeit any right to a lien under this chapter.</p><p>1968, c. 568; 1976, c. 253.</p>
