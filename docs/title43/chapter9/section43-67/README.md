# § 43-67 Release of mechanic's lien upon payment or satisfaction.

<p>When payment or satisfaction has been made of a debt secured by a mechanic's lien it shall be released in the manner provided in §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-339/'>55.1-339</a> and <a href='/vacode/55.1-341/'>55.1-341</a>, insofar as appropriate.</p><p>Code 1919, § 6456; 1926, p. 80; 1930, p. 69; 1932, p. 120; 1944, p. 198.</p>
