# ยง 67-1500 (Repealed effective October 1, 2021) Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Authority" means the Virginia Solar Energy Development and Energy Storage Authority created pursuant to this chapter.</p><p>"Developer" means any private developer of a solar energy project or an energy storage project.</p><p>"Energy storage project" means an energy storage facility located within the Commonwealth and includes interests in land, improvements, and ancillary facilities.</p><p>"Solar energy project" means an electric generation facility located within the Commonwealth and includes interests in land, improvements, and ancillary facilities.</p><p>2015, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0090'>90</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0398'>398</a>; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0813'>813</a>.</p>
