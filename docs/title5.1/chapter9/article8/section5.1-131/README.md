# § 5.1-131 Other remedies not extinguished.

<p>Nothing in the preceding nine sections (§§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-122/'>5.1-122</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/5.1-130/'>5.1-130</a>) shall be held to extinguish any remedy or right of action not inconsistent therewith.</p><p>Code 1950, § 56-186; 1970, c. 708.</p>
