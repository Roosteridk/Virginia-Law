# ยง 29.1-346 When license for floating blinds issued; distance from stationary blinds.

<p>Licenses for floating blinds permitted by law, in the public waters, may be obtained on and after July 1. Floating blinds shall have a license plate supplied with the license for that season affixed to the blind. Floating blinds, including any accompanying boat or tender, shall anchor or tie out at least 500 yards from any licensed stationary blind for shooting, whether on the shore or in the water, unless agreed otherwise between the parties.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 29-87; 1970, c. 579; 1987, cc. 94, 488.</p>
