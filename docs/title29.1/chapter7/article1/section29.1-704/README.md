# § 29.1-704 Recording previously awarded numbers.

<p>The owner of any motorboat already covered by a number in effect which has been awarded to it pursuant to then operative federal law or pursuant to a federally approved numbering system of another state shall record the number prior to operating the motorboat on the waters of this Commonwealth in excess of the ninety-day reciprocity period provided for in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-710/'>29.1-710</a>. Such recordation shall be in the manner and pursuant to the procedure required for the award of a number under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-702/'>29.1-702</a>, except that no additional or substitute number shall be issued.</p><p>1960, c. 500, § 62-174.5; 1962, c. 626; 1964, c. 654; 1968, c. 659, § 62.1-170; 1970, c. 240; 1972, c. 412; 1980, c. 256; 1983, c. 126; 1986, c. 125; 1987, c. 488.</p>
