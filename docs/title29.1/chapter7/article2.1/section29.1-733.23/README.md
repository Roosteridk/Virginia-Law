# § 29.1-733.23 Rights of purchaser other than secured party.

<p>A. A buyer in ordinary course of business has the protections afforded by subsection (2) of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2-403/'>8.2-403</a> and subsection (a) of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.9A-320/'>8.9A-320</a> even if an existing certificate of title was not signed and delivered to the buyer or a new certificate listing the buyer as owner of record was not created.</p><p>B. Except as otherwise provided in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-733.17/'>29.1-733.17</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-733.24/'>29.1-733.24</a>, the rights of a purchaser of a watercraft that is not a buyer in ordinary course of business or a lien creditor are governed by the Uniform Commercial Code.</p><p>2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0787'>787</a>.</p>
