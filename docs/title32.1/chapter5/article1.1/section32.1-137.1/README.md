# § 32.1-137.1 Definitions.

<p>As used in this and the following article, unless the context indicates otherwise:</p><p>"Agent" or "insurance agent," when used without qualification, means an individual, partnership, limited liability company, or corporation that solicits, negotiates, procures or effects contracts of insurance or annuity in this Commonwealth.</p><p>"Bureau of Insurance" means the State Corporation Commission acting pursuant to Title 38.2.</p><p>"Complaint" means any written communication from a covered person primarily expressing a grievance.</p><p>"Covered person" means an individual residing in the Commonwealth, whether a policyholder, subscriber, enrollee, or member of a managed care health insurance plan, who is entitled to health care services or benefits provided, arranged for, paid for or reimbursed pursuant to a managed care health insurance plan under Title 38.2.</p><p>"Managed care health insurance plan" means an arrangement for the delivery of health care in which a health carrier as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5800/'>38.2-5800</a> undertakes to provide, arrange for, pay for, or reimburse any of the costs of health care services for a covered person on a prepaid or insured basis which (i) contains one or more incentive arrangements, including any credentialing requirements intended to influence the cost or level of health care services between the health carrier and one or more providers with respect to the delivery of health care services; and (ii) requires or creates benefit payment differential incentives for covered persons to use providers that are directly or indirectly managed, owned, under contract with or employed by the health carrier. Any health maintenance organization as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-4300/'>38.2-4300</a> or health carrier that offers preferred provider contracts or policies as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-3407/'>38.2-3407</a> or preferred provider subscription contracts as defined in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-4209/'>38.2-4209</a> shall be deemed to be offering one or more managed care health insurance plans. For the purposes of this definition, the prohibition of balance billing by a provider shall not be deemed a benefit payment differential incentive for covered persons to use providers who are directly or indirectly managed, owned, under contract with or employed by the health carrier. A single managed care health insurance plan may encompass multiple products and multiple types of benefit payment differentials; however, a single managed care health insurance plan shall encompass only one provider network or set of provider networks.</p><p>"Managed care health insurance plan licensee" means a health carrier subject to licensure by the Bureau of Insurance under Title 38.2 who is responsible for a managed care health insurance plan in accordance with Chapter 58 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/38.2-5801/'>38.2-5801</a> et seq.) of Title 38.2.</p><p>"Person" means any association, aggregate of individuals, business, company, corporation, individual, joint-stock company, Lloyds type of organization, other organization, partnership, receiver, reciprocal or inter-insurance exchange, trustee or society.</p><p>1998, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0891'>891</a>.</p>
