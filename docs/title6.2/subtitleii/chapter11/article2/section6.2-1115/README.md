# § 6.2-1115 Formation of state savings institutions.

<p>A. A stock savings and loan association may be incorporated as provided in the Virginia Stock Corporation Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-601/'>13.1-601</a> et seq.). A mutual savings and loan association may be incorporated as provided in the Virginia Nonstock Corporation Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-801/'>13.1-801</a> et seq.).</p><p>B. A stock savings bank may be formed by being incorporated as provided in the Virginia Stock Corporation Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-601/'>13.1-601</a> et seq.). A mutual savings bank may be formed by being incorporated as provided in the Virginia Nonstock Corporation Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-801/'>13.1-801</a> et seq.).</p><p>Code 1950, § 6-201.8; 1960, c. 402; 1966, c. 584, § 6.1-133; 1972, c. 796, § 6.1-195.7; 1985, c. 425, § 6.1-194.9; 1991, c. 230, § 6.1-194.111; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
