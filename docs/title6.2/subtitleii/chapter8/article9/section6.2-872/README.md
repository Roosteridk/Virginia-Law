# ยง 6.2-872 For what purpose banks may purchase, hold, and convey real estate.

<p>A. In addition to the authority provided in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-873/'>6.2-873</a>, every bank incorporated under the laws of the Commonwealth may purchase, hold, and convey the following real estate for the purposes stated and for no other:</p><p>1. Real estate it purchased at sales under judgments, decrees, mortgages, or deeds of trust held by it, in whole or in part, or purchased to secure debts due to it.</p><p>B.
