# § 6.2-808 Incorporation; corporate powers.

<p>A. A bank may be incorporated under the Virginia Stock Corporation Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-601/'>13.1-601</a> et seq.), but need not comply with the provisions of subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-630/'>13.1-630</a>.</p><p>B. Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, a bank shall:</p><p>1. Have all the powers conferred on corporations, and be subject to all restrictions imposed on corporations, by the Virginia Stock Corporation Act;</p><p>2. Not issue its shares for any consideration except money at least equal in amount to the par value of its shares; and</p><p>3. Not issue no-par stock.</p><p>Code 1950, § 6-10; 1956, c. 433; 1966, c. 584, § 6.1-6; 1987, c. 556; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
