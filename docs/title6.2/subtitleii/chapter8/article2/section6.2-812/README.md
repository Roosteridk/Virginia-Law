# § 6.2-812 Inspection of records, reports, and information of insured banks.

<p>A. As used in this section, "insured bank" has the meaning assigned to it in § 12-B of the Federal Reserve Act (12 U.S.C. § 1813 (h)), as amended.</p><p>B. All records, reports, reports of examinations, and information relating to insured banks shall be open to the inspection of, and made available to, the officers and duly accredited agents of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation so long as like records, reports, and information in the possession or under the control of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation are, by federal statute, made available and subject to inspection by the Commission.</p><p>Code 1950, § 6-25; 1966, c. 584, § 6.1-9; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
