# ยง 6.2-835 Banking facilities in certain hospitals or federal areas.

<p>A. The Commission, when in its discretion banking facilities are required (i) for patients in, students at, or employees of hospitals operated by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs or by the Commonwealth or (ii) for members of the armed forces at any military or naval federal area in the Commonwealth, may permit any bank that is authorized to do business in the Commonwealth to establish and operate such banking facilities as are required in any such hospital or federal area.</p><p>B. The banking facilities so established shall be operated in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth relating thereto.
