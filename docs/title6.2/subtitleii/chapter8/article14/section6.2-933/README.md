# ยง 6.2-933 Distribution of assets.

<p>A. All claims against the bank's estate, proved to the satisfaction of the FDIC as receiver or approved by the receivership court, shall be paid in the following order:</p><p>1. Administration expenses of the liquidation;</p><p>2. Claims given priority under other provisions of state or federal law;</p><p>3. Deposit obligations;</p><p>4. Other general liabilities;</p><p>5. Debt subordinated to the claims of depositors and general creditors; and</p><p>6. Equity capital securities.</p><p>B. No interest on any claim shall be paid until all claims within the same class have received the full principal amount of claim.</p><p>1983, c. 507, ยง 6.1-110.9; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
