# § 6.2-849 Definitions.

<p>As used in this article, unless a different meaning is required:</p><p>"Bank" has the meaning assigned to it in 12 U.S.C. § 1813 (a) (1) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Company Act of 1956 (12 U.S.C. § 1811 et seq.), as amended.</p><p>"Home state" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-836/'>6.2-836</a>.</p><p>"Host state" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-836/'>6.2-836</a>.</p><p>"Interstate merger transaction" means:</p><p>1. The merger or consolidation of banks with different home states, and the conversion of branches of any bank involved in the merger or consolidation to branches of the resulting bank; or</p><p>2. The purchase of all, or substantially all, of the assets of a bank whose home state is different than the home state of the acquiring bank.</p><p>"Out-of-state bank" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-836/'>6.2-836</a>.</p><p>"Out-of-state state bank" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-836/'>6.2-836</a>.</p><p>"Resulting bank" means a bank that has resulted from an interstate merger transaction under this article.</p><p>"Virginia bank" means a bank whose home state is Virginia.</p><p>"Virginia state bank" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-836/'>6.2-836</a>.</p><p>1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0301'>301</a>, § 6.1-44.16; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
