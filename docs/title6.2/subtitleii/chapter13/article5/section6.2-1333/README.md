<p>A. Nine or more individuals, all of whom are duly authorized representatives, respectively, of nine or more credit unions may, pursuant to the provisions of the Virginia Nonstock Corporation Act (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-801/'>13.1-801</a> et seq.), establish a corporation for the purpose of:</p><p>1.
