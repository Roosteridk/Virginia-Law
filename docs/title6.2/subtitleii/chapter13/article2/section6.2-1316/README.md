# ยง 6.2-1316 Offenses; penalty.

<p>Any officer, director, employee, receiver, or agent of a credit union who willfully does any of the following is guilty of a Class 6 felony:</p><p>1. With the intent to deceive, falsifies any book of account, report, statement, record, or other document of a credit union, whether by alteration, false entry, omission, or otherwise;</p><p>2. Signs, issues, publishes, or transmits to a government agency any book of account, report, statement, record, or other document that he knows to be false;</p><p>3. By means of deceit, obtains a signature to a writing that is a subject of forgery;</p><p>4. With intent to deceive, destroys any credit union book of account, report, statement, record, or other document; or</p><p>5. With the intent to defraud, shares or receives directly or indirectly any money, property, or benefits through any transaction of the credit union.</p><p>1985, c. 363, ยง 6.1-223.2; 1990, c. 373, ยง 6.1-225.62; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
