# § 6.2-1323 Amendments to articles of incorporation and bylaws.

<p>A. Subject to the provisions of subsection B and §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-886/'>13.1-886</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-892/'>13.1-892</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-893/'>13.1-893</a>, the articles of incorporation or the bylaws of a credit union may be amended as provided in the articles and bylaws, as the case may be. Amendments to the articles of incorporation shall be accomplished as provided in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-888/'>13.1-888</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-889/'>13.1-889</a>.</p><p>B. If proposed amendments to the articles of incorporation or bylaws include an amendment to expand the field of membership of a credit union, then the amendments shall be submitted to the Commissioner, who shall approve or disapprove them within 30 days. No amendments to the articles of incorporation or bylaws that include an amendment to expand the field of membership shall be effective until such amendment has been approved by the Commissioner, who may extend the period for approval as he may deem necessary for as much as an additional 30 days.</p><p>Code 1950, § 6-208; 1966, c. 584, § 6.1-199; 1990, c. 373, § 6.1-225.16; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0063'>63</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0044'>44</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0560'>560</a>.</p>
