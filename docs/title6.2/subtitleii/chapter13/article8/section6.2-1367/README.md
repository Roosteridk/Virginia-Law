# § 6.2-1367 Application of provisions to federal credit unions.

<p>The provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1365/'>6.2-1365</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-601/'>64.2-601</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/64.2-602/'>64.2-602</a> shall apply to federal credit unions operating in the Commonwealth to the extent that the same are not inconsistent with any federal law applicable to such credit unions.</p><p>1968, c. 560, § 6.1-208.5; 1979, c. 407; 1990, c. 373, § 6.1-225.50; 2010, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0269'>269</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>; 2013, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0007'>7</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0095'>95</a>.</p>
