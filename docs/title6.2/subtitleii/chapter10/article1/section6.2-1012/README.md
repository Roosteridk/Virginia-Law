# § 6.2-1012 Suspension or prohibition of trust institutions.

<p>The Commission may prohibit or suspend from engaging in trust business any (i) trust company that fails to comply with any of the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1005/'>6.2-1005</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1006/'>6.2-1006</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1008/'>6.2-1008</a>; (ii) bank doing a trust business that fails to comply with any of the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-821/'>6.2-821</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1005/'>6.2-1005</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1006/'>6.2-1006</a>, or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1008/'>6.2-1008</a>; or (iii) trust subsidiary that fails to comply with the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1006/'>6.2-1006</a> or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1008/'>6.2-1008</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 6-103; 1966, c. 584, § 6.1-32; 1974, c. 665; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
