# ยง 6.2-1016 Bond required.

<p>A. No applicant shall obtain a certificate without filing with the Commission, and maintaining continuously thereafter, a surety bond in such amount as the Commission may from time to time require.</p><p>B. In no event shall the amount of the surety bond be less than $1 million.</p><p>C. The surety bond required by this section shall be for the benefit of:</p><p>1. Any person damaged as a result of a violation of the provisions of, or any regulation adopted pursuant to, this chapter;</p><p>2. Any person damaged by the negligence, fraud, or embezzlement of a trust company organized under this article or its directors, officers, or employees; and</p><p>3. Any person damaged by any other breach of trust of any trust company organized under this article or its directors, officers, or employees.</p><p>D. The Commission may revoke the certificate of any trust company that the Commission finds has failed to maintain a bond as required by this section.</p><p>1993, c. 432, ยง 6.1-32.17; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
