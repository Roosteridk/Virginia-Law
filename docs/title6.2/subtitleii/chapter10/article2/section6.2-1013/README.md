# § 6.2-1013 Definitions.

<p>As used in this article, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Agent" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-501/'>13.1-501</a> of the Virginia Securities Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-501/'>13.1-501</a> et seq.).</p><p>"Broker-dealer" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-501/'>13.1-501</a> of the Virginia Securities Act.</p><p>"Control" means (i) ownership by a person of 25 percent or more of the voting stock of a trust company; (ii) control as defined in the Bank Holding Company Act of 1956 (12 U.S.C. § 1841 et seq.); or (iii) as determined by the Commission, the exercise of a controlling influence over the management and policies of a trust company.</p><p>"Fiduciary" means executor, administrator, conservator, guardian of a minor, committee, or trustee.</p><p>"Investment advisor" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-501/'>13.1-501</a> of the Virginia Securities Act.</p><p>"Investment advisor representative" has the meaning assigned to it in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-501/'>13.1-501</a> of the Virginia Securities Act.</p><p>"Investment company" has the meaning assigned to it in the Investment Company Act of 1940 (15 U.S.C. § 80a-1 et seq.).</p><p>"Operating plan" means a plan submitted by an applicant for a certificate of authority, which plan establishes the policies and procedures a trust company will have in effect when the institution opens for business and thereafter (i) to avoid or resolve conflicts of interests, (ii) to prevent improper influences from affecting the actions of the trustee, (iii) to ensure that trust accounts are handled in accordance with recognized standards of fiduciary conduct, and (iv) to assure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.</p><p>"Principal" means any person who, directly or indirectly, owns or controls (i) 10 percent or more of the outstanding stock of a stock corporation or (ii) a 10 percent or greater interest in a nonstock corporation or a limited liability company.</p><p>1993, c. 432, § 6.1-32.11;
