# § 6.2-1024 Restrictions on control, officers and directors.

<p>A. None of the following individuals or entities shall acquire control of any trust company under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1023/'>6.2-1023</a>:</p><p>1. An agent;</p><p>2. A broker-dealer;</p><p>3. An investment advisor;</p><p>4. An investment advisor representative;</p><p>5. An investment company; or</p><p>6. Any corporation, limited liability company, partnership, business trust, association, or similar organization.</p><p>B. Nothing in this section shall prohibit (i) the formation of a trust company holding company by a trust company, (ii) any officer, director, or employee of a trust company holding company or a subsidiary of a trust company holding company from owning, indirectly, five percent or more of any class of capital stock of an affiliated trust company, or (iii) the acquisition of a trust company pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1023/'>6.2-1023</a> by a bank holding company as defined in 12 U.S.C. § 1841 or by a corporation that controls a subsidiary authorized to engage in the trust business under federal law or the laws of any state.</p><p>1993, c. 432, § 6.1-32.20; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0524'>524</a>; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0140'>140</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0781'>781</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
