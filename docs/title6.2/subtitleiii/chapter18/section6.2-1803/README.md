# ยง 6.2-1803 Application for license; form; content; fee.

<p>A. An application for a license under this chapter shall be made in writing, under oath and on a form provided by the Commissioner.</p><p>B. The application shall set forth:</p><p>1. The name and address of the applicant;</p><p>2. If the applicant is a firm or partnership, the name and address of each member of the firm or partnership;</p><p>3. If the applicant is a corporation or a limited liability company, the name and address of each officer, director, registered agent, and each principal;</p><p>4. The addresses of the locations of the offices to be approved; and</p><p>5. Such other information concerning the financial responsibility, background, experience and activities of the applicant and its members, officers, directors, and principals as the Commissioner may require.</p><p>C. The application shall be accompanied by payment of an application fee of $500 or other reasonable amount that the Commission prescribes by regulation.</p><p>D. The application fee shall not be refundable in any event. The fee shall not be abated by surrender, suspension, or revocation of the license.</p><p>2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0897'>897</a>, ยง 6.1-447; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>; 2020, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1215'>1215</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1258'>1258</a>.</p>
