# ยง 6.2-1812 Other reporting requirements.

<p>A. A licensee shall file a written report with the Commissioner within 15 days following the occurrence of any of the following:</p><p>1. The filing of bankruptcy, reorganization or receivership proceedings by or against the licensee;</p><p>2. The institution of administrative or regulatory proceedings against the licensee by any governmental authority;</p><p>3. Any felony indictments of the licensee or any of its members, partners, directors, officers, or principals;</p><p>4. Any felony conviction of the licensee or any of its members, partners, directors, officers, or principals; and</p><p>5. Such other event as the Commission may prescribe by regulation.</p><p>B. The report shall be in writing and describe the event and its expected impact on the business of the licensee.</p><p>2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0897'>897</a>, ยง 6.1-455; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
