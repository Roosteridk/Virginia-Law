# ยง 6.2-2102 Exempt persons.

<p>This chapter shall not apply to:</p><p>1. Any person who:</p><p>a. Does not hold itself out to be a check cashing service;</p><p>b. Is principally engaged in the bona fide retail sale of goods or services;</p><p>c. Either as an incident to or independently of such a retail sale from time to time cashes items; and</p><p>d. Charges a fee or other consideration for the service that does not exceed the greater of $2 or two percent of the amount of the item; or</p><p>2. Any person authorized to engage in business as a bank, savings institution, or credit union under the laws of the United States or any state.</p><p>1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0221'>221</a>, ยง 6.1-434; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
