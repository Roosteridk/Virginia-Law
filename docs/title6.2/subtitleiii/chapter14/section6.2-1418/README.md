# ยง 6.2-1418 Suspension or revocation of authority.

<p>A. The Commission may suspend or revoke the authority of an association to do business upon any of the following grounds:</p><p>1. Any violation of the provisions of this chapter or regulations adopted by the Commission pursuant thereto, or a violation of any other law or regulation applicable to the conduct of its business;</p><p>2. A course of conduct consisting of failure to perform written agreements with borrowers;</p><p>3. Failure to account for funds received or disbursed to the satisfaction of the person supplying or receiving such funds;</p><p>4. Failure to disburse funds in accordance with any agreement connected with, and promptly upon closing of, a mortgage loan, taking into account any applicable right of rescission;</p><p>5. Conviction of any felony or misdemeanor involving fraud, misrepresentation, or deceit;</p><p>6. Entry of judgment against such association involving fraud, misrepresentation, or deceit;</p><p>7. Entry of a federal or state administrative order against such association for violation of any law or regulation applicable to the conduct of its business;</p><p>8. Refusal to permit an investigation or examination by the Commission;</p><p>9. Failure to pay any fee or assessment imposed by this chapter; or</p><p>10. Failure to comply with any order of the Commission.</p><p>B. For the purposes of this section, acts of any officer, director, or principal stockholder shall be deemed acts of the association.</p><p>1993, c. 419, ยง 6.1-237.8; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
