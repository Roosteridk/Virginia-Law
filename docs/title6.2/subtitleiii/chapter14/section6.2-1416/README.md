# ยง 6.2-1416 Prohibited practices.

<p>A. No association shall:</p><p>1. Obtain any agreement or instrument in which blanks are left to be filled in after execution;</p><p>2. Take an interest in collateral other than the real estate or residential property, including fixtures and appliances thereon, securing a mortgage loan; however, an interest in collateral other than real estate may be taken if the real estate taken as collateral does not have sufficient equity to secure the mortgage loan;</p><p>3. Obtain any exclusive dealing or exclusive agency agreement from any borrower;</p><p>4. Delay closing of any mortgage loan for the purpose of increasing interest, costs, fees, or charges payable by the borrower;</p><p>5. Obtain any agreement or instrument executed by the borrower which contains an acceleration clause permitting the unpaid balance of a mortgage loan to be declared due for any reason other than failure to make timely payments of interest and principal or to perform other obligations undertaken in the agreement or instrument; or</p><p>6. If acting as a mortgage lender, fail to require the person closing the mortgage loan to provide the borrower, prior to closing of the mortgage loan, with a (i) settlement statement and (ii) disclosure which conforms to that required by the provisions of 15 U.S.C. ยง 1601 et seq. and Consumer Financial Protection Bureau Regulation Z (12 C.F.R. Part 1026).</p><p>B. No association, when acting as a mortgage broker, shall:</p><p>1. Except for documented costs of a credit report and appraisals, receive compensation from a borrower until a written commitment to make a mortgage loan is given to the borrower by a mortgage lender;</p><p>2. Receive compensation from a mortgage lender of which it is a principal, partner, trustee, director, officer, or employee;</p><p>3. Receive compensation from a borrower in connection with any mortgage loan transaction in which it is the lender or a principal, partner, trustee, director, or officer of the lender;</p><p>4. Receive compensation from a borrower other than that specified in a written agreement signed by the borrower; or</p><p>5. Receive compensation for negotiating, placing or finding a mortgage loan where such association, or any person affiliated with such association, has otherwise acted as a real estate broker, agent, or salesman in connection with the real estate which secures the mortgage loan, and such association or affiliated person has received or will receive any other compensation or thing of value from the lender, borrower, seller, or any other person, unless the borrower is given the following notice in writing at the time the mortgage broker services are first offered to the borrower:</p><p>NOTICE</p><p>WE HAVE OFFERED TO ASSIST YOU IN OBTAINING A MORTGAGE LOAN. IF WE ARE SUCCESSFUL IN OBTAINING A LOAN FOR YOU, WE WILL CHARGE AND COLLECT FROM YOU A FEE NOT TO EXCEED _____ % OF THE LOAN AMOUNT.</p><p>WE DO NOT REPRESENT ALL OF THE LENDERS IN THE MARKET AND THE LENDERS WE DO REPRESENT MAY NOT OFFER THE LOWEST INTEREST RATES OR BEST TERMS AVAILABLE TO YOU. YOU ARE FREE TO SEEK A LOAN WITHOUT OUR ASSISTANCE, IN WHICH EVENT YOU WILL NOT BE REQUIRED TO PAY US A FEE FOR THAT SERVICE.</p><p>IF YOU ARE A MEMBER OF A CREDIT UNION, YOU SHOULD COMPARE OUR INTEREST RATES AND TERMS WITH THE MORTGAGE LOANS AVAILABLE THROUGH YOUR CREDIT UNION.</p><p>______________________________<br>BORROWER'S SIGNATURE</p><p>______________________________<br>BORROWER'S SIGNATURE</p><p>The foregoing notice shall be in at least 10-point type, and the prospective borrower shall acknowledge receipt of the written notice.</p><p>1993, c. 419, ยง 6.1-237.6; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0501'>501</a>.</p>
