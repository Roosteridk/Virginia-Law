# § 6.2-1707 Other conditions for mortgage loan originator licensing.

<p>In addition to the findings required by § <a href='/vacode/6.2-1706/'>6.2-1706</a>, the Commission shall not issue a mortgage loan originator license unless it finds that:</p><p>1. The applicant has never had a mortgage loan originator license revoked by any governmental authority;</p><p>2. The applicant has not been convicted of, or pled guilty or nolo contendere to, a felony in a domestic, foreign, or military court (i) during the seven-year period preceding the application for licensing and registration; or (ii) at any time preceding such date of application if such felony involved an act of fraud, dishonesty, breach of trust, or money laundering;</p><p>3. The applicant has completed the pre-licensing education requirement described in § <a href='/vacode/6.2-1708/'>6.2-1708</a>;</p><p>4. The applicant has passed a written test that meets the test requirement described in § <a href='/vacode/6.2-1709/'>6.2-1709</a>; and</p><p>5. The applicant has become registered through, and obtained a unique identifier from, the Registry.</p><p>2009, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0273'>273</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0453'>453</a>, § 6.1-431.8; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>; 2014, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0295'>295</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0343'>343</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0740'>740</a>.</p>
