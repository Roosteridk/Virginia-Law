# ยง 6.2-1621 Filing of written report with Commissioner; events affecting activities of licensee.

<p>A. A licensee shall file a written report with the Commissioner within 15 days of becoming aware of the occurrence of any of the following:</p><p>1. The filing for bankruptcy or reorganization by the licensee;</p><p>2. The institution of revocation or suspension proceedings against the licensee by any state or governmental authority;</p><p>3. The denial of the opportunity of the licensee to engage in business by any state or governmental authority;</p><p>4. Any felony indictment of the licensee or any of its employees, officers, directors, or principals;</p><p>5. Any felony conviction of the licensee or any of its employees, officers, directors, or principals; and</p><p>6. Such other events as the Commissioner may determine and identify by regulation.</p><p>B. The report shall be in writing and describe the event and its expected impact on the activities of the licensee in the Commonwealth.</p><p>2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0386'>386</a>, ยง 6.1-425.2; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
