# ยง 6.2-1603 Application for license; form; content; fee.

<p>A. An application for a license under this chapter shall be made in writing, under oath and on a form provided by the Commissioner.</p><p>B. The application shall set forth:</p><p>1. The name and address of the applicant;</p><p>2. If the applicant is a firm or partnership, the name and address of each member of the firm or partnership;</p><p>3. If the applicant is a corporation or a limited liability company, the name and address of each officer, director, registered agent and each principal;</p><p>4. The address of each office at which the business to be licensed is to be conducted;</p><p>5. Whether the applicant seeks a license to act as a mortgage lender, mortgage broker, or both; and</p><p>6. Such other information concerning the financial responsibility, background, experience and activities of the applicant and its members, officers, directors, and principals as the Commissioner may require.</p><p>C. The application shall be accompanied by payment of the following fees:</p><p>1. A license application fee of:</p><p>a. In the case of an application for a license to act as a mortgage lender or a mortgage broker, $500; or</p><p>b. In the case of an application for a license to act as both mortgage lender and mortgage broker, $1,000; and</p><p>2. An application fee of $150 for each office at which the business to be licensed is to be conducted.</p><p>D. The application fees shall not be refundable in any event. The fees shall not be abated by surrender, suspension or revocation of the license.</p><p>1987, c. 596, ยง 6.1-412; 1989, c. 411; 1992, c. 283; 1993, c. 183; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0435'>435</a>.</p>
