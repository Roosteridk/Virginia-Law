# ยง 6.2-2211 Other reporting requirements.

<p>Within 15 days following the occurrence of any of the following events, a licensee shall file a written report with the Commission describing such event and its expected impact upon the business of the licensee:</p><p>1. The filing of bankruptcy, reorganization, or receivership proceedings by or against the licensee;</p><p>2. The institution of administrative or regulatory proceedings against the licensee by any governmental authority;</p><p>3. Any felony indictments of the licensee or any of its members, partners, directors, officers, or principals;</p><p>4. Any felony conviction of the licensee or any of its members, partners, directors, officers, or principals; and</p><p>5. Such other event as the Commission may prescribe by regulation.</p><p>2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0477'>477</a>, ยง 6.1-491.</p>
