# ยง 6.2-2219 Suspension or revocation of license.

<p>A. The Commission may suspend or revoke any license issued under this chapter upon any of the following grounds:</p><p>1. Any ground for denial of a license under this chapter;</p><p>2. Any violation of the provisions of this chapter or regulations adopted by the Commission pursuant thereto, or a violation of any other law or regulation applicable to the conduct of the licensee's business;</p><p>3. A course of conduct consisting of the failure to perform written agreements with borrowers;</p><p>4. Conviction of a felony or misdemeanor involving fraud, misrepresentation, or deceit;</p><p>5. Entry of a judgment against the licensee involving fraud, misrepresentation, or deceit;</p><p>6. Entry of a federal or state administrative order against the licensee for violation of any law or any regulation applicable to the conduct of his business;</p><p>7. Refusal to permit an investigation or examination by the Commission;</p><p>8. Failure to pay any fee or assessment imposed by this chapter; or</p><p>9. Failure to comply with any order of the Commission.</p><p>B. For the purposes of this section, acts of any officer, director, member, partner, trustee, beneficiary, or principal shall be deemed acts of the licensee.</p><p>2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0477'>477</a>, ยง 6.1-499.</p>
