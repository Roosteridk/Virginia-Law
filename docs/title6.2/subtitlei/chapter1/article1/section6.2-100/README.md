# § 6.2-100 Definitions.

<p>As used in this title, unless the context otherwise requires:</p><p>"Bureau" means the Bureau of Financial Institutions, a division of the Commission.</p><p>"Commission" means the State Corporation Commission.</p><p>"Commissioner" means the Commissioner of Financial Institutions.</p><p>"Commission's Rules" means the rules of practice and procedure prescribed by the Commission pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/12.1-25/'>12.1-25</a>.</p><p>"Entity" means any corporation, partnership, association, cooperative, limited liability company, trust, joint venture, government, political subdivision, or other legal or commercial entity.</p><p>"Finance charge" has the meaning assigned to it in Consumer Financial Protection Bureau Regulation Z, 12 C.F.R. § 1026.4, as amended.</p><p>"Financial institution" means any bank, trust company, savings institution, industrial loan association, consumer finance company, or credit union.</p><p>"Person" means any individual, corporation, partnership, association, cooperative, limited liability company, trust, joint venture, government, political subdivision, or other legal or commercial entity.</p><p>Code 1950, § 6-1; 1966, c. 584, § 6.1-1; 1970, c. 270, § 6.1-2.1; 1976, c. 658; 1978, c. 683; 1983, c. 491; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0016'>16</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0501'>501</a>.</p>
