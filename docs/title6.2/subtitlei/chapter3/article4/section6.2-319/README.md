# ยง 6.2-319 Loans by pension plans to participants.

<p>A. As used in this section, "pension plan" includes an "employee pension benefit plan" or "pension plan" as defined in ยง 3(2) of the federal Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (P.L. 93-406, 88 Stat. 829).</p><p>B. No such participating individual, by way of defense or otherwise, shall avail himself of the provisions of this chapter, or any other law relating to interest or usury, to avoid or defeat the payment of interest or any other sum on any loan made by the pension plan.
