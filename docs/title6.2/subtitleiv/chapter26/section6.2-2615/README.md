# ยง 6.2-2615 Suspension or revocation of license.

<p>A. The Commission may suspend or revoke any license issued under this chapter upon any of the following grounds:</p><p>1. Any ground for denial of a license under this chapter;</p><p>2. Any violation of the provisions of this chapter or regulations adopted by the Commission pursuant thereto, or a violation of any other law or regulation applicable to the conduct of the licensee's business;</p><p>3. A course of conduct consisting of the failure to perform written agreements with qualified education loan borrowers;</p><p>4. Failure to account for funds received or disbursed to the satisfaction of the person supplying or receiving qualified education loan funds;</p><p>5. Conviction of any felony or of a misdemeanor involving fraud, misrepresentation, or deceit;</p><p>6. Entry of a judgment against the licensee involving fraud, misrepresentation, or deceit;</p><p>7. Entry of a federal or state administrative order against the licensee for violation of any law or regulation applicable to the conduct of the licensee's business;</p><p>8. Refusal to permit an investigation or examination by the Commission;</p><p>9. Failure to pay any fee or assessment imposed by this chapter; or</p><p>10. Failure to comply with any order of the Commission.</p><p>B. For the purposes of this section, acts of any senior officer, director, member, partner, trustee, or principal shall be deemed acts of the licensee.</p><p>2020, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1198'>1198</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1250'>1250</a>.</p>
