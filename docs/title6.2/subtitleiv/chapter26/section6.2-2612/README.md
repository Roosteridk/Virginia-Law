# ยง 6.2-2612 Reporting requirements.

<p>A. Within 15 days following the occurrence of any of the following events, a licensee shall file a written report with the Commission describing such event and its expected impact upon the business of the licensee:</p><p>1. The filing of bankruptcy, reorganization, or receivership proceedings by or against the licensee;</p><p>2. The institution of administrative or regulatory proceedings against the licensee by any governmental authority;</p><p>3. Any felony indictment of the licensee or any of its members, directors, senior officers, trustees, or principals;</p><p>4. Any felony conviction of the licensee or any of its members, directors, senior officers, trustees, or principals; and</p><p>5. Such other events as the Commission may prescribe by regulation.</p><p>B. Each licensee shall file periodic written reports with the Commissioner or the Registry containing such information as the Commissioner may require concerning the licensee's business and operations. Reports shall be in the form and be submitted with such frequency and by such dates as may be prescribed by the Commissioner.</p><p>2020, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1198'>1198</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP1250'>1250</a>.</p>
