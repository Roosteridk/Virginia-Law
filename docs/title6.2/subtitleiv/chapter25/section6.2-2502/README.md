# ยง 6.2-2502 Prohibited activities.

<p>Any facilitator who offers to facilitate, or who facilitates, a refund anticipation loan shall not:</p><p>1. Require a customer to enter into a loan arrangement in order to complete a tax return;</p><p>2. Misrepresent a material factor or condition of a refund anticipation loan;</p><p>3. Fail to process the application for a refund anticipation loan promptly after an applicant applies for the loan; or</p><p>4. Engage in any transaction, practice, or course of business that operates a fraud upon any person in connection with a refund anticipation loan.</p><p>2006, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?061+ful+CHAP0399'>399</a>, ยง 6.1-476; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
