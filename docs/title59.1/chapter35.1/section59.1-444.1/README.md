# § 59.1-444.1 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter:</p><p>"Consumer" means an individual who is also a resident of this state.</p><p>"Consumer reporting agency" has the same meaning as in § 603(f) of the Fair Credit Reporting Act (15 U.S.C. § 1681a(f)).</p><p>"Credit report" means a "consumer report," as defined in § 603(d) of the Fair Credit Reporting Act (15 U.S.C. § 1681a(d)); provided, however, that for purposes of this chapter, a credit report is limited to information that a consumer reporting agency furnishes to a person that it has reason to believe intends to use the information as a factor in establishing the consumer's eligibility for credit to be used primarily for personal, family or household purposes.</p><p>"Proper identification" means proper identification as defined in 15 U.S.C. § 1681h(a)(1).</p><p>2008, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0480'>480</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0496'>496</a>; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0570'>570</a>.</p>
