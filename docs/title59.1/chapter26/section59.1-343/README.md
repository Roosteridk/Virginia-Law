# ยง 59.1-343 Time of taking effect.

<p>This chapter shall become effective on July 1, 1986, and shall not apply to misappropriation occurring prior to the effective date. With respect to a continuing misappropriation that began prior to the effective date, the chapter also shall not apply to misappropriation that occurs after the effective date.</p><p>1986, c. 210.</p>
