# § 59.1-199 Exclusions.

<p>Nothing in this chapter shall apply to:</p><p>A. Any aspect of a consumer transaction which aspect is authorized under laws or regulations of this Commonwealth or the United States, or the formal advisory opinions of any regulatory body or official of this Commonwealth or the United States.</p><p>B. Acts done by the publisher, owner, agent or employee of a newspaper, periodical, or radio or television station, or other advertising media such as outdoor advertising and advertising agencies, in the publication or dissemination of an advertisement in violation of § <a href='/vacode/59.1-200/'>59.1-200</a>, unless it be proved that such person knew that the advertisement was of a character prohibited by § <a href='/vacode/59.1-200/'>59.1-200</a>.</p><p>C. Those aspects of a consumer transaction which are regulated by the Federal Consumer Credit Protection Act, 15 U.S.C. § 1601 et seq.</p><p>D. Banks, savings institutions, credit unions, small loan companies, public service corporations, mortgage lenders as defined in § <a href='/vacode/6.2-1600/'>6.2-1600</a>, broker-dealers as defined in § <a href='/vacode/13.1-501/'>13.1-501</a>, gas suppliers as defined in subsection E of § <a href='/vacode/56-235.8/'>56-235.8</a>, and insurance companies regulated and supervised by the State Corporation Commission or a comparable federal regulating body.</p><p>E. Any aspect of a consumer transaction which is subject to the Virginia Residential Landlord and Tenant Act (§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-1200/'>55.1-1200</a> et seq.) or Chapter 14 (§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-1400/'>55.1-1400</a> et seq.) of Title 55.1, unless the act or practice of a landlord constitutes a misrepresentation or fraudulent act or practice under § <a href='/vacode/59.1-200/'>59.1-200</a>.</p><p>F. Real estate licensees who are licensed under Chapter 21 (§ <a href='/vacode/54.1-2100/'>54.1-2100</a> et seq.) of Title 54.1.</p><p>1977, c. 635; 1987, c. 464; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0400'>400</a>; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0703'>703</a>; 1996, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0061'>61</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0077'>77</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0179'>179</a>; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0494'>494</a>; 2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0691'>691</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0706'>706</a>.</p>
