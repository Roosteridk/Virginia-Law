# ยง 59.1-267 Contracts required to be in writing; contents.

<p>A. Every business opportunity sales contract shall be in writing and a copy shall be given to the purchaser at the time he signs the contract.</p><p>B. Every contract for the sale of a business opportunity shall include the following:</p><p>1. The terms and conditions of payment;</p><p>2. A full and detailed description of the acts or services that the business opportunity seller undertakes to perform for the purchaser;</p><p>3. The seller's principal business address and the name and address of its agent in the Commonwealth of Virginia authorized to receive service of process;</p><p>4. A full and detailed description of any product, products, equipment or supplies the business opportunity seller is to deliver to the purchaser;</p><p>5. The approximate delivery date of any product, products, equipment, or supplies the business opportunity seller is to deliver to the purchaser.</p><p>1979, c. 523.</p>
