# ยง 59.1-210 Standard provisions for cover notice.

<p>Every contract for invention development services shall have a conspicuous and legible cover sheet attached with the following notice imprinted thereon in boldface type of not less than 10-point size:</p><p>1. "This contract between you and an invention developer is regulated by Chapter 18 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-208/'>59.1-208</a> et seq.) of Title 59.1. You are not permitted or required to make any payments under this contract until four working days after you sign this contract and receive a completed copy of it."</p><p>2. A statement that the contract is a fee-for-service contract and that the invention developer makes no guarantees as to the success of the invention.</p><p>3. Information as to how a customer who feels that his rights have been violated pursuant to this chapter may lodge a complaint with the Consumer Protection Division at the Office of the Attorney General, including the Division's telephone number and directions as to how to file an online consumer complaint.</p><p>Such cover sheet shall contain only the notice required by this section.</p><p>1977, c. 649; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0759'>759</a>.</p>
