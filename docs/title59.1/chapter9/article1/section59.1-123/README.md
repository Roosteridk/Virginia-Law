# § 59.1-123 Exemptions from article.

<p>The provisions of this article shall not apply to:</p><p>1. The sale of secondhand material mentioned in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-117/'>59.1-117</a> taken from premises occupied by the owner, when sold by such owner on the premises, or the sale of such articles when purchased from a public utility corporation at its place of business or a governmental agency;</p><p>2. Scrap metal purchasers as provided in Article 4 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-136.1/'>59.1-136.1</a> et seq.);</p><p>3. Authorized scrap sellers;</p><p>4. Public utilities;</p><p>5. Public transportation companies;</p><p>6. Peddlers permitted under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-118/'>59.1-118</a>;</p><p>7. Industrial and manufacturing companies;</p><p>8. Marine, automobile, and aircraft salvage and wrecking companies;</p><p>9. Governmental entities; or</p><p>10. The donation of secondhand material mentioned in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-117/'>59.1-117</a> by the material's owner or the owner's contractor or subcontractor to a nonprofit corporation as defined in § 501(c)(3) of the U.S. Internal Revenue Code or the sale of such donated material by such a nonprofit corporation.</p><p>Code 1950, § 59-151; 1968, c. 439; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0917'>917</a>; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0836'>836</a>; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0414'>414</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0626'>626</a>.</p>
