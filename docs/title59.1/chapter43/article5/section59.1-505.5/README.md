# ยง 59.1-505.5 Performance by delegate; subcontract.

<p>(a) A party may perform its contractual duties or exercise its contractual rights through a delegate or a subcontract unless:</p><p>(1) the contract prohibits delegation or subcontracting; or</p><p>(2) the other party has a substantial interest in having the original promisor perform or control the performance.</p><p>(b) Delegating or subcontracting performance does not relieve the delegating party of a duty to perform or of liability for breach.</p><p>(c) An attempted delegation that violates a term prohibiting delegation is not effective.</p><p>2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0101'>101</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0996'>996</a>.</p>
