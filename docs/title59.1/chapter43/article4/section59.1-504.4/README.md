# ยง 59.1-504.4 Implied warranty; informational content.

<p>(a) Unless the warranty is disclaimed or modified, a merchant that, in a special relationship of reliance with a licensee, collects, compiles, processes, provides, or transmits informational content warrants to that licensee that there is no inaccuracy in the informational content caused by the merchant's failure to perform with reasonable care.</p><p>(b) A warranty does not arise under subsection (a) with respect to:</p><p>(1) subjective characteristics of the informational content, such as the aesthetics, appeal, and suitability to taste;</p><p>(2) published informational content; or</p><p>(3) a person that acts as a conduit or provides no more than editorial services in collecting, compiling, distributing, processing, providing, or transmitting informational content that under the circumstances can be identified as that of a third person.</p><p>(c) The warranty under this section is not subject to the preclusion in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-501.15/'>59.1-501.15</a> (b) (1) on disclaiming obligations of diligence, reasonableness, or care.</p><p>2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0101'>101</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0996'>996</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
