# ยง 59.1-501.14:1 Pretransaction disclosures in Internet-type transactions.

<p>This section applies to a licensor that makes its computer information available to a licensee by electronic means from its Internet or similar electronic site. In such a case, the licensor affords an opportunity to review the terms of a standard form license, which opportunity satisfies ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-501.13:1/'>59.1-501.13:1</a> with respect to a licensee that acquires the information from that site, if the licensor:</p><p>(1) makes the standard terms of the license readily available for review by the licensee before the information is delivered or the licensee becomes obligated to pay, whichever occurs first, by:</p><p>(A) displaying prominently and in close proximity to a description of the computer information, or to instructions or steps for acquiring it, the standard terms or a reference to an electronic location from which they can be readily obtained; or</p><p>(B) disclosing the availability of the standard terms in a prominent place on the site from which the computer information is offered and promptly furnishing a copy of the standard terms on request before the transfer of the computer information; and</p><p>(2) does not take affirmative acts to prevent printing or storage of the standard terms for archival or review purposes by the licensee.</p><p>Failure to provide an opportunity to review under this section does not preclude a person from providing a person an opportunity to review by other means pursuant to ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-501.13:1/'>59.1-501.13:1</a> or law other than this chapter.</p><p>2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>.</p>
