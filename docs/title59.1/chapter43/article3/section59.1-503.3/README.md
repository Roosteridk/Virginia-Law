# § 59.1-503.3 Modification and rescission.

<p>(a) An agreement modifying a contract subject to this chapter needs no consideration to be binding.</p><p>(b) An authenticated record that precludes modification or rescission except by an authenticated record may not otherwise be modified or rescinded. In a standard form supplied by a merchant to a consumer, a term requiring an authenticated record for modification of the contract is not enforceable unless the consumer manifests assent to the term.</p><p>(c) A modification of a contract and the contract as modified must satisfy the requirements of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-502.1/'>59.1-502.1</a> (a) and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-503.7/'>59.1-503.7</a> (f) if the contract as modified is within those provisions.</p><p>(d) An attempt at modification or rescission which does not satisfy subsection (b) or (c) may operate as a waiver if § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-507.2/'>59.1-507.2</a> is satisfied.</p><p>2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0101'>101</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0996'>996</a>.</p>
