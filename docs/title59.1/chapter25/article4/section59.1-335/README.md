# § 59.1-335 Penalties.

<p>A. Any person violating any of the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-317/'>59.1-317</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-318/'>59.1-318</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-326/'>59.1-326</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-327/'>59.1-327</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-328/'>59.1-328</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-329/'>59.1-329</a> or a cease and desist or temporary order issued pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-324/'>59.1-324</a> shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor and any person violating any of the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-332/'>59.1-332</a> shall be guilty of a Class 3 misdemeanor. Other than with reference to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-332/'>59.1-332</a> each violation shall be deemed a separate offense.</p><p>B. Any violation of the provisions of this chapter shall constitute a prohibited practice pursuant to the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-200/'>59.1-200</a> and shall be subject to any and all of the enforcement provisions of the Virginia Consumer Protection Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-196/'>59.1-196</a> et seq.) of this title.</p><p>1985, c. 409; 1992, c. 545.</p>
