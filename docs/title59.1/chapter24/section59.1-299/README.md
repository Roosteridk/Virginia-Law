# ยง 59.1-299 Duration of contract.

<p>No health club contract shall have a duration for a period longer than thirty-six months, including any renewal period; however, a health club contract may exceed 36 months provided that:</p><p>1. Any initiation fee does not exceed 10 times the initial monthly fee;</p><p>2. All payments for health club services, other than the initiation fee, are collected as monthly fees on a monthly basis;</p><p>3. After an initial term of not more than 12 months, either party may cancel the health club contract upon not more than 30 days' notice; and</p><p>4. The monthly fee is never reduced below 80 percent of the monthly fee at the time the contract is initially executed.</p><p>1984, c. 738; 1990, cc. 392, 433; 1992, c. 117; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0459'>459</a>.</p>
