# § 59.1-92.22 Use of name, logo, or symbol of a bank, trust company, savings institution, or credit union.

<p>Any bank, trust company, savings institution, or credit union whose name, logo, or symbol, or any combination thereof, or any name, logo, or symbol, or any combination thereof that is deceptively similar thereto, is used by a person in a manner prohibited by §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-941/'>6.2-941</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1043/'>6.2-1043</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1105/'>6.2-1105</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/6.2-1307/'>6.2-1307</a>, is entitled to the remedies that are available to a registrant under subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-92.13/'>59.1-92.13</a>.</p><p>2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0240'>240</a>.</p>
