# § 60.2-536 Review of decision under § 60.2-535.

<p>A. Any person aggrieved by a decision of the Commission under the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-535/'>60.2-535</a> shall have the right to review before the Commission. Such review before the Commission shall be instituted by a request filed by the aggrieved party with the Commission within thirty days from the date of mailing of the decision.</p><p>B. Any party aggrieved by the Commission decision on review may secure judicial review of any decision pursuant to the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-500/'>60.2-500</a>, such provisions applying mutatis mutandis.</p><p>1974, c. 198, § 60.1-88.1; 1981, c. 252; 1986, c. 480; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0079'>79</a>.</p>
