# ยง 60.2-118 Civil action to enforce title; actions on behalf of other states.

<p>A. In any civil action to enforce the provisions of this title the Commission and the Commonwealth may be represented by the Office of the Attorney General.</p><p>B. The Office of the Attorney General may commence actions in this Commonwealth as agent for, or on behalf of, any other state:</p><p>1. To enforce judgments and liability for unemployment insurance taxes due such other state; or</p><p>2. To collect unemployment benefit overpayments of such state if such state extends like comity to this Commonwealth.</p><p>C. Venue for such actions shall be the same as for actions to enforce the provisions of this title.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 60-109; 1968, c. 738, ยง 60.1-126; 1972, c. 764; 1977, c. 445; 1986, c. 480.</p>
