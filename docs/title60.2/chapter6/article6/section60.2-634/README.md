# ยง 60.2-634 Receiving back pay after reinstatement.

<p>Whenever the Commission finds that a discharged employee has received back pay at his customary wage rate from his employer after reinstatement such employee shall be liable to repay any benefits paid to such person during the time he was unemployed. When such an employee is liable to repay benefits to the Commission, such sum shall be collectible without interest by civil action in the name of the Commission.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 60-116; 1962, c. 138; 1968, c. 738, ยง 60.1-133; 1974, c. 466; 1986, c. 480.</p>
