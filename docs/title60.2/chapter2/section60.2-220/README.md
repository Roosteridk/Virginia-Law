# ยง 60.2-220 Institution of higher education.

<p>"Institution of higher education," for the purposes of this title, means an educational institution which:</p><p>1. Admits as regular students only individuals having a certificate of graduation from a high school, or the recognized equivalent of such a certificate;</p><p>2. Is legally authorized in this Commonwealth to provide a program of education beyond high school;</p><p>3. Provides an educational program for which it awards a bachelor's or higher degree, or provides a program which is acceptable for full credit toward such a degree, a program of postgraduate or postdoctoral studies, or a program of training to prepare students for gainful employment in a recognized occupation;</p><p>4. Is a public or other nonprofit institution; and</p><p>5. Notwithstanding any of the foregoing provisions of this subsection, all colleges and universities in this Commonwealth are institutions of higher education for purposes of this section.</p><p>1971, Ex. Sess., c. 235, ยง 60.1-14.1; 1986, c. 480.</p>
