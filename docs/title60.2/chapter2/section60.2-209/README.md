# § 60.2-209 Taxes.

<p>"Taxes" means the money imposed by and collectible under this title and includes payments in lieu of taxes collectible under §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-501/'>60.2-501</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/60.2-507/'>60.2-507</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 60-11; 1968, c. 738, § 60.1-11; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 235; 1986, c. 480.</p>
