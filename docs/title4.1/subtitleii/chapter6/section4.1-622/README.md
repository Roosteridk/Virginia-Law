# ยง 4.1-622 Criminal history records check required on certain employees; reimbursement of costs.

<p>All persons hired by the Authority whose job duties involve access to or handling of the Authority's funds or merchandise shall be subject to a criminal history records check before, and as a condition of, employment.</p><p>The Board shall develop policies regarding the employment of persons who have been convicted of a felony or a crime involving moral turpitude.</p><p>The Department of State Police shall be reimbursed by the Authority for the cost of investigations conducted pursuant to this section.</p><p>2021, Sp. Sess. I, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0550'>550</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?212+ful+CHAP0551'>551</a>.</p>
