# § 4.1-229 Suspension or revocation of permits; grounds; notice and hearing; exception.

<p>A. The Board may suspend or revoke any permit. The suspension or revocation of any permit shall be in accordance with §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-225/'>4.1-225</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-227/'>4.1-227</a>.</p><p>B. This section shall not apply to temporary permits granted under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-212/'>4.1-212</a> which may be revoked summarily in the same manner as a temporary license may be revoked under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-211/'>4.1-211</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 4-26, 4-59, 4-61.2; 1950, p. 879; 1954, c. 351; 1968, c. 7, § 4-98.16; 1972, cc. 138, 717; 1976, c. 696; 1984, c. 53; 1986, c. 190; 1988, c. 786; 1990, cc. 442, 773; 1993, c. 866.</p>
