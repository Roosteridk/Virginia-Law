# ยง 4.1-103.1 Criminal history records check required on certain employees; reimbursement of costs.

<p>All persons hired by the Authority whose job duties involve access to or handling of the Authority's funds or merchandise shall be subject to a criminal history records check before, and as a condition of, employment.</p><p>The Board shall develop policies regarding the employment of persons who have been convicted of a felony or a crime involving moral turpitude.</p><p>The Department of State Police shall be reimbursed by the Authority for the cost of investigations conducted pursuant to this section.</p><p>1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0034'>34</a>; 2015, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0038'>38</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0730'>730</a>; 2017, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0698'>698</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0707'>707</a>.</p>
