# § 4.1-322 Possession or consumption of alcoholic beverages by interdicted persons; penalty.

<p>No person who has been interdicted pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-333/'>4.1-333</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-334/'>4.1-334</a> shall possess any alcoholic beverages, except those acquired in accordance with subdivisions 1 through 5 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/4.1-200/'>4.1-200</a>, nor be drunk in public in violation of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-388/'>18.2-388</a>.</p><p>Any interdicted person found to be in violation of this section shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.</p><p>Code 1950, § 4-52; 1954, c. 484; 1982, c. 66; 1993, c. 866; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0717'>717</a>.</p>
