# ยง 54.1-4202 Penalties for violation of the provisions of this chapter.

<p>Any person convicted of a first offense for willfully violating the provisions of this chapter shall be guilty of a Class 2 misdemeanor. Any person convicted of a second or subsequent offense under the provisions of this chapter shall be guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.</p><p>1989, c. 490.</p>
