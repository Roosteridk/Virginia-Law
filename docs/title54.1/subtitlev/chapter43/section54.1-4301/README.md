# ยง 54.1-4301 Records to be kept.

<p>Every itinerant merchant shall keep an accurate and legible record of his acquisition of the new merchandise. The records of such acquisition shall be retained by the itinerant merchant for at least twelve months from the display, sale or offer for sale of new merchandise and shall set forth the following:</p><p>1. A complete description of the new merchandise, including but not limited to product name and quantity of the new merchandise;</p><p>2. The time, date, and place of the acquisition of the new merchandise;</p><p>3. The amount of money paid for the new merchandise; and</p><p>4. Evidence of the legitimate purchase of the new merchandise, including but not limited to a receipt or bill of lading.</p><p>1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0701'>701</a>.</p>
