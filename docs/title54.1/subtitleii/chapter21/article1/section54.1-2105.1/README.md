# § 54.1-2105.1 (Effective until January 1, 2023) Other powers and duties of the Real Estate Board.

<p>In addition to the provisions of §§ <a href='/vacode/54.1-2105.01/'>54.1-2105.01</a> through <a href='/vacode/54.1-2105.04/'>54.1-2105.04</a>, the Board shall:</p><p>1. Develop a residential property disclosure statement form for use in accordance with the provisions of the Virginia Residential Property Disclosure Act (§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-700/'>55.1-700</a> et seq.) and maintain such statement on its website. The Board shall also develop and maintain on its website a one-page form to be signed by the parties acknowledging that the purchaser has been advised of the disclosures listed in the residential property disclosure statement located on the Board's website; and</p><p>2. Inform licensed brokers, in a manner deemed appropriate by the Board, of the broker's ability to designate an agent pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/54.1-2109/'>54.1-2109</a> in the event of the broker's death or disability.</p><p>1993, c. 958; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0759'>759</a>; 2007, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0265'>265</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0809'>809</a>; 2008, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0851'>851</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0871'>871</a>; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0461'>461</a>; 2014, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0024'>24</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0705'>705</a>; 2018, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0060'>60</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0086'>86</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0749'>749</a>.</p>
