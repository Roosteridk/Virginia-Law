# ยง 54.1-1505 Return of hearing aid by purchaser or lessee.

<p>A. Within thirty days of the date of delivery, any purchaser or lessee of a hearing aid shall be entitled to return the hearing aid for any reason, provided such aid is returned in satisfactory condition. Such purchaser or lessee shall be entitled to a replacement or a refund of all charges paid, less a reasonable charge for medical, audiological, and hearing aid evaluation services provided by the hearing aid specialist.</p><p>B. The right of a purchaser or lessee to return a hearing aid and the charges to be imposed upon the return of such hearing aid, as provided in subsection A of this section, shall be explained and given in writing in at least ten-point, bold-faced type to such purchaser or lessee by the hearing aid specialist.</p><p>C. The provisions of this section shall be subject to the provisions of the Virginia Consumer Protection Act (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-196/'>59.1-196</a> et seq.).</p><p>1990, c. 584.</p>
