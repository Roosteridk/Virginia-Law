# ยง 54.1-517.2 Requirements for licensure.

<p>A. The Board shall issue a license to practice as a home inspector in the Commonwealth to:</p><p>1. An individual who holds an unexpired certificate as a home inspector issued prior to June 30, 2017; or</p><p>2. An applicant who has successfully:</p><p>a. Completed the educational requirements as required by the Board;</p><p>b. Completed the experience requirements as required by the Board; and</p><p>c. Passed the examination approved by the Board.</p><p>B. The Board shall issue a license with the new residential structure endorsement to any applicant who completes a training module developed by the Board in conjunction with the Department of Housing and Community Development based on the International Residential Code component of the Virginia Uniform Statewide Building Code.</p><p>2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0723'>723</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0411'>411</a>; 2016, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0161'>161</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0436'>436</a>.</p>
