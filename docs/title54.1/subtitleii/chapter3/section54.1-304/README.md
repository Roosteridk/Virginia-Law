# ยง 54.1-304 Powers and duties of the Director with respect to regulatory boards.

<p>Each of the regulatory boards within the Department shall be a separate board. All of the administrative functions of the regulatory boards shall be under the direction and supervision of the Director.</p><p>In the performance and discharge of his duties with respect to the regulatory boards, the Director shall:</p><p>1. Be the secretary of each board;</p><p>2. Maintain all records for each board;</p><p>3. Collect and account for all fees prescribed to be paid into each board and account for and deposit the moneys so collected into a special fund from which the expenses of the Board, regulatory boards and Department shall be paid;</p><p>4. Enforce all statutes and regulations the Director is required to administer;</p><p>5. Exercise other powers necessary to function as the sole administrative officer of each of such boards; and</p><p>6. Perform any additional administrative functions prescribed by the Board.</p><p>1979, c. 408, ยง 54-1.33; 1984, c. 734; 1988, c. 765.</p>
