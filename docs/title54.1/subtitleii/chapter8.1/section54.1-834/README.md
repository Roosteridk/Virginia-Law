# ยง 54.1-834 Prohibited activities; penalties.

<p>A. No betting or wagering shall be permitted at an event or exhibition before, during, or after the event in the building where the event is held.</p><p>B. No person shall participate in a sham or fake boxing or martial arts contest. The Department shall have the authority to order, without a hearing, the person controlling the purse to hold the distribution to contestants, promoters, and trainers pending a public hearing by the Department. The Department shall, simultaneously with the issuance of such order to retain the share or purse, institute proceedings for a hearing to determine whether a sham or fake boxing or martial arts contest has occurred.</p><p>C. It shall be a Class 1 misdemeanor for any person to violate this section or any statute or regulation governing the persons or activities regulated pursuant to this chapter.</p><p>D. The third or any subsequent conviction for violating any provision of this section during a 36-month period shall constitute a Class 6 felony.</p><p>1998, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0895'>895</a>; 2015, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0216'>216</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0264'>264</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0756'>756</a>.</p>
