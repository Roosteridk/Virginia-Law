# § 54.1-2342 Penalties.

<p>Any person who violates any of the provisions of this chapter shall be subject to the sanctions provided in Chapter 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-100/'>54.1-100</a> et seq.) and Chapter 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-200/'>54.1-200</a> et seq.) and in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2314/'>54.1-2314</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2317/'>54.1-2317</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2326/'>54.1-2326</a>. Any person who is convicted of willfully violating any of the provisions of this chapter is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor. Any person convicted of violating, or failing to comply with, any of the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2317/'>54.1-2317</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2319/'>54.1-2319</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2321/'>54.1-2321</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2322/'>54.1-2322</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2325/'>54.1-2325</a> or <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2326/'>54.1-2326</a> with the intent to defraud is guilty of a Class 6 felony.</p><p>For the purposes of this section, "person" includes any individual, corporation, partnership, association, cooperative, limited liability company, trust, joint venture, government, political subdivision, or any other legal or commercial entity and any successor, representative, agent, agency, instrumentality, officer or employee thereof.</p><p>1998, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0708'>708</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0721'>721</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0691'>691</a>.</p>
