# § 54.1-531 Suspension, revocation, or refusal to renew registration.

<p>A. The Director may limit, suspend, revoke, or refuse to renew a registration of an individual registered under subsection A of § <a href='/vacode/54.1-530/'>54.1-530</a> for conduct that would have justified refusal to issue a certificate of registration under subsection B of § <a href='/vacode/54.1-530/'>54.1-530</a>.</p><p>B. The Director may suspend or revoke the registration of an individual registered under subsection C of § <a href='/vacode/54.1-529/'>54.1-529</a> or renewed under subsection E of § <a href='/vacode/54.1-530/'>54.1-530</a> for any reason for which the Director could have refused to grant or renew registration or for conduct that would justify refusal to issue a certificate of registration under subsection B of § <a href='/vacode/54.1-530/'>54.1-530</a>.</p><p>2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0481'>481</a>, § 54.1-524.</p>
