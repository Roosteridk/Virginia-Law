# § 54.1-2400.8 Immunity for reporting.

<p>In addition to the immunity for reporting as provided by §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2400.6/'>54.1-2400.6</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2400.7/'>54.1-2400.7</a>, any person (i) making a report regarding the conduct or competency of a health care practitioner as required by law or regulation, (ii) making a voluntary report to the appropriate regulatory board or to the Department of Health Professions regarding the unprofessional conduct or competency of any practitioner licensed, certified, or registered by a health regulatory board, or (iii) providing information pursuant to an investigation or testifying in a judicial or administrative proceeding as a result of such reports shall be immune from any civil liability resulting therefrom unless such person acted in bad faith or with malicious intent.</p><p>2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0064'>64</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0932'>932</a>.</p>
