# ยง 54.1-3022 Use of the title "Certified Nurse Aide" or "C.N.A.".

<p>No person shall use or assume the title "Certified Nurse Aide" or abbreviation "C.N.A." or any words, letters, signs, or devices to indicate that person is a certified nurse aide unless certified by the Board.</p><p>1989, c. 278.</p>
