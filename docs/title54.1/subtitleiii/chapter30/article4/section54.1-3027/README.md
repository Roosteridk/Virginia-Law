# ยง 54.1-3027 Exclusions.

<p>This article shall not be construed to affect or apply to:</p><p>1. The gratuitous care of friends or family members;</p><p>2. A person for hire who does not represent himself as or hold himself out to the public as a certified nurse aide. However, a person for hire who is not a certified nurse aide in accordance with this article shall not hold himself out as a certified nurse aide or be employed as a certified nurse aide.</p><p>1989, c. 278.</p>
