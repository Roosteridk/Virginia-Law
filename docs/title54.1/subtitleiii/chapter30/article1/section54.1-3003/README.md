# ยง 54.1-3003 Qualifications of members.

<p>A. Each professional nurse appointed to the Board shall:</p><p>1. Be a citizen of the United States of America;</p><p>2. Be a resident of this Commonwealth;</p><p>3. Have been graduated from an educational program approved by a state board of nursing;</p><p>4. Be licensed in this Commonwealth as a registered nurse;</p><p>5. Have had at least five years' experience in nursing, nursing administration or teaching in a nursing education program; and</p><p>6. Have been actively engaged in professional nursing in this Commonwealth for at least three years preceding appointment or reappointment.</p><p>B. Each licensed practical nurse appointed to the Board shall:</p><p>1. Be a citizen of the United States of America;</p><p>2. Be a resident of this Commonwealth;</p><p>3. Have been graduated from a high school or the equivalent;</p><p>4. Have been graduated from a practical nursing program approved by a state board of nursing;</p><p>5. Be licensed in this Commonwealth as a practical nurse;</p><p>6. Have had at least five years' experience in nursing; and</p><p>7. Have been actively engaged in practical nursing in this Commonwealth for at least three years preceding appointment or reappointment.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 54-333; 1970, c. 116; 1985, c. 305, ยง 54-367.9; 1988, c. 765.</p>
