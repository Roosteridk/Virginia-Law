# ยง 54.1-3020 Qualifications of applicant for practical nurse's license.

<p>A. An applicant for a license to practice as a practical nurse shall furnish evidence satisfactory to the Board that the applicant:</p><p>1. Has completed two years of high school or its equivalent;</p><p>2. Has received a diploma from an approved practical nursing program;</p><p>3. Has passed a written examination as required by the Board; and</p><p>4. Has committed no acts which are grounds for disciplinary action as set forth in this chapter.</p><p>B. The Board shall consider and may accept relevant practical experience and didactic and clinical components of education and training completed by an applicant for licensure as a practical nurse during his service as a member of any branch of the armed forces of the United States as evidence of the satisfaction of the educational requirements for licensure.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 54-348; 1970, c. 116; 1979, c. 5, ยง 54-367.19; 1982, c. 598; 1988, c. 765; 2011, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0390'>390</a>.</p>
