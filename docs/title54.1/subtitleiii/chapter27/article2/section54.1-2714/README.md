# ยง 54.1-2714 Restricted licenses to teach dentistry for foreign dentists.

<p>A. The Board may grant, without examination, a restricted license for a temporary appointment to teach dentistry at a dental school in this Commonwealth to any person who:</p><p>1. Is a resident of a foreign country;</p><p>2. Is licensed to practice dentistry in a foreign country;</p><p>3. Holds a faculty appointment in a dental school in a foreign country;</p><p>4. Is a graduate of a foreign dental school or college or the dental department of a foreign institution of higher education;</p><p>5. Is not licensed to practice dentistry in Virginia;</p><p>6. Has not failed an examination for a license to practice dentistry in this Commonwealth;</p><p>7. Has received a temporary appointment to the faculty of a dental school in this Commonwealth to teach dentistry;</p><p>8. Is, in the opinion of the Board, qualified to teach dentistry; and</p><p>9. Submits a completed application, the supporting documents the Board deems necessary to determine his qualifications, and the prescribed fee.</p><p>B. A restricted license shall entitle the licensee to perform all operations which a person licensed to practice dentistry may perform but only for the purpose of teaching. No person granted a restricted license shall practice dentistry intramurally or privately or receive fees for his services.</p><p>C. A restricted license granted pursuant to this section shall expire 24 months from the date of issuance and may not be renewed or reissued.</p><p>1977, c. 349, ยง 54-175.2; 1988, c. 765; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0020'>20</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0116'>116</a>.</p>
