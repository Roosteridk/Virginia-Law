# § 54.1-3605 Powers and duties of the Board.

<p>In addition to the powers granted in other provisions of this title, the Board shall have the following specific powers and duties:</p><p>1. To cooperate with and maintain a close liaison with other professional boards and the community to ensure that regulatory systems stay abreast of community and professional needs.</p><p>2. To conduct inspections to ensure that licensees conduct their practices in a competent manner and in conformance with the relevant regulations.</p><p>3. To designate specialties within the profession.</p><p>4. To issue a temporary license for such periods as the Board may prescribe to practice psychology to persons who are engaged in a residency or pursuant to subdivision 7 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3601/'>54.1-3601</a>.</p><p>5. To promulgate regulations for the voluntary certification of licensees as sex offender treatment providers.</p><p>6. To administer the mandatory certification of sex offender treatment providers for those professionals who are otherwise exempt from licensure under subdivision 4 of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3501/'>54.1-3501</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3601/'>54.1-3601</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3701/'>54.1-3701</a> and to promulgate regulations governing such mandatory certification. The regulations shall include provisions for fees for application processing, certification qualifications, certification issuance and renewal and disciplinary action.</p><p>7. To promulgate regulations establishing the requirements for licensure of clinical psychologists that shall include appropriate emphasis in the diagnosis and treatment of persons with moderate and severe mental disorders.</p><p>1976, c. 608, §§ 54-929, 54-931; 1983, c. 115; 1986, cc. 64, 100, 464; 1988, c. 765; 1993, c. 767; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0778'>778</a>; 1996, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0937'>937</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0980'>980</a>; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0556'>556</a>; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0630'>630</a>; 2001, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0186'>186</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0198'>198</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0011'>11</a>.</p>
