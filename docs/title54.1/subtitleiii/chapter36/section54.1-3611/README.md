# § 54.1-3611 Restriction of practice; use of titles.

<p>No person, including licensees of the Boards of Counseling; Medicine; Nursing; Psychology; or Social Work, shall claim to be a certified sex offender treatment provider unless he has been so certified. No person who is exempt from licensure under subdivision 4 of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3501/'>54.1-3501</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3601/'>54.1-3601</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3701/'>54.1-3701</a> shall hold himself out as a provider of sex offender treatment services unless he is certified as a sex offender treatment provider by the Board of Psychology.</p><p>1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0778'>778</a>; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0630'>630</a>; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0473'>473</a>.</p>
