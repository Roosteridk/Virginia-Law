# § 54.1-2602 Board membership; officers; duties of Director of Department.

<p>The Board of Audiology and Speech-Language Pathology shall consist of seven members as follows: two licensed audiologists, two licensed speech-language pathologists, one otolaryngologist, and two citizen members. The terms of Board members shall be four years. All professional members of the Board shall have actively practiced their professions for at least two years prior to their appointments.</p><p>The Board shall elect annually a chairman and a vice-chairman.</p><p>The Board shall be authorized to promulgate canons of ethics under which the professional activities of persons regulated shall be conducted.</p><p>1972, c. 181, §§ 54-83.1:8, 54-83.1:10; 1978, c. 34; 1979, c. 296; 1981, c. 447; 1987, c. 686, § 54-83.1:8.1; 1988, c. 765; 1992, c. 706; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0436'>436</a>.</p>
