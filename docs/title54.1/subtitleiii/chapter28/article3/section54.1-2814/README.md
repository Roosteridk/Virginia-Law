# § 54.1-2814 Examination.

<p>Each applicant for license for the practice of funeral service shall be examined in writing on:</p><p>1. Basic and health sciences including anatomy, chemistry, bacteriology, pathology, hygiene and public health;</p><p>2. Funeral service arts and sciences including embalming and restorative art;</p><p>3. Funeral service administration including accounting, funeral law, psychology, and funeral principles, directing and management.</p><p>The Board may recognize other examinations that it considers equivalent to its examination.</p><p>Code 1950, § 54-260.70; Code 1950, §§ 54-260.22 through 54-260.30, 54-260.32; 1956, c. 220; 1966, c. 284, § 54-260.70; 1972, c. 797; 1973, c. 296; 1974, c. 686; 1978, c. 849; 1986, c. 43; 1988, c. 765; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0773'>773</a>.</p>
