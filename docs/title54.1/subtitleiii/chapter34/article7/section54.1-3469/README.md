# ยง 54.1-3469 Storage, usage, and disposition of controlled paraphernalia.

<p>Each person, association or corporation which has lawfully obtained possession of any of the controlled paraphernalia mentioned in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-3467/'>54.1-3467</a> shall exercise reasonable care in the storage, usage and disposition of such devices or substances to ensure that they are not diverted for reuse for any purposes other than those for which they were lawfully obtained.
