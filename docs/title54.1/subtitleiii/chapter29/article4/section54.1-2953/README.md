# § 54.1-2953 Renewal, revocation, suspension, and refusal.

<p>The Board may revoke, suspend, or refuse to renew a license to practice as a physician assistant for any of the following:</p><p>1. Any action by a physician assistant constituting unprofessional conduct pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/54.1-2915/'>54.1-2915</a>;</p><p>2. Practice by a physician assistant other than as part of a patient care team, including practice without entering into a practice agreement with one or more patient care team physicians or patient care team podiatrists;</p><p>3. Failure of the physician assistant to practice in accordance with the requirements of his practice agreement;</p><p>4. Negligence or incompetence on the part of the physician assistant or other member of the patient care team;</p><p>5. Violation of or cooperation in the violation of any provision of this chapter or the regulations of the Board; or</p><p>6. Failure to comply with any regulation of the Board required for licensure of a physician assistant.</p><p>1973, c. 529, §§ 54-281.8, 54-281.9; 1985, c. 316; 1988, c. 765; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0144'>144</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0450'>450</a>; 2019, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0092'>92</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0137'>137</a>; 2021, Sp.
