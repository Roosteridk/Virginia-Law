# ยง 54.1-2957.7 Licensed midwife and practice of midwifery; definitions.

<p>"Midwife" means any person who provides primary maternity care by affirmative act or conduct prior to, during, and subsequent to childbirth, and who is not licensed as a doctor of medicine or osteopathy or certified nurse midwife.</p><p>"Practicing midwifery" means providing primary maternity care that is consistent with a midwife's training, education, and experience to women and their newborns throughout the childbearing cycle, and identifying and referring women or their newborns who require medical care to an appropriate practitioner.</p><p>2005, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0719'>719</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0917'>917</a>.</p>
