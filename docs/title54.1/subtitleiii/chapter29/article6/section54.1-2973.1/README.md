# § 54.1-2973.1 Practice of laser hair removal.

<p>The practice of laser hair removal shall be performed by a properly trained person licensed to practice medicine or osteopathic medicine or a physician assistant as authorized pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2952/'>54.1-2952</a> or a nurse practitioner as authorized pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2957/'>54.1-2957</a> or by a properly trained person under the direction and supervision of a licensed doctor of medicine or osteopathic medicine or a physician assistant as authorized pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2952/'>54.1-2952</a> or a nurse practitioner as authorized pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2957/'>54.1-2957</a> who may delegate such practice in accordance with subdivision A 6 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/54.1-2901/'>54.1-2901</a>.</p><p>2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0390'>390</a>.</p>
