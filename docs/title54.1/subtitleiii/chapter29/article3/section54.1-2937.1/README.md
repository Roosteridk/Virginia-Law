# ยง 54.1-2937.1 Retiree license.

<p>A. The Board may issue a retiree license to any doctor of medicine, osteopathy, podiatry, or chiropractic who holds an unrestricted, active license to practice in the Commonwealth upon receipt of a request and submission of the fee required by the Board. A person to whom a retiree license has been issued shall not be required to meet continuing competency requirements for the first biennial renewal of such license.</p><p>B. A person to whom a retiree license has been issued shall only engage in the practice of medicine, osteopathy, podiatry, or chiropractic for the purpose of providing (i) charity care, as defined in ยง <a href='/vacode/32.1-102.1/'>32.1-102.1</a>, and (ii) health care services to patients in their residence for whom travel is a barrier to receiving medical care.</p><p>2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0379'>379</a>.</p>
