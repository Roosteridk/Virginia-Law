<p>A. The absence of an advance directive by an adult patient shall not give rise to any presumption as to his intent to consent to or refuse any particular health care.</p><p>B. The provisions of this article shall not apply to authorization of nontherapeutic sterilization, abortion, or psychosurgery.</p><p>C. If any provision of a patient's advance directive conflicts with the authority conferred by any emergency custody, temporary detention, involuntary admission, and mandatory outpatient treatment order set forth in Chapter 8 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-800/'>37.2-800</a> et seq.) of Title 37.2 or by any other provision of law, the provisions of the patient's advance directive that create the conflict shall have no effect. However, a patient's advance directive shall otherwise be given full effect.</p><p>D. The provisions of this article, if otherwise applicable, may be used to authorize admission of a patient to a facility, as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-100/'>37.2-100</a>, only if the admission is otherwise authorized under Chapter 8 (ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-800/'>37.2-800</a> et seq.) of Title 37.2.</p><p>2009, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0211'>211</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0268'>268</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0792'>792</a>.</p>
