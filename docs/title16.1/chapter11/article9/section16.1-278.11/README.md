# § 16.1-278.11 Mental illness and intellectual disability.

<p>In cases involving a person who is involuntarily admitted because of a mental illness or is judicially certified as eligible for admission to a training center for persons with intellectual disability, disposition shall be in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 8 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/37.2-800/'>37.2-800</a> et seq.) of Title 37.2. A child shall not be committed pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-278.2/'>16.1-278.2</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-278.8/'>16.1-278.8</a> or the provisions of Title 37.2 to a maximum security unit within any state hospital where adults determined to be criminally insane reside.</p><p>1991, c. 534; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0716'>716</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0476'>476</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0507'>507</a>.</p>
