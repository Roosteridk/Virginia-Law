# § 16.1-69.48:3 Fees charged to drug offenders.

<p>Whenever in a general district court the costs provided for in subsection C of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/'>16.1-69.48:1</a> are assessed for a violation of any provision of Article 1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/18.2-247/'>18.2-247</a> et seq.) of Chapter 7 of Title 18.2, a portion of the costs, as specified in subsection C of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/'>16.1-69.48:1</a>, shall be included in the taxed costs and paid into the Drug Offender Assessment and Treatment Fund.</p><p>1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0463'>463</a>, § 14.1-134.1; 1998, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0783'>783</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0840'>840</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0872'>872</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0831'>831</a>; 2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP1004'>1004</a>.</p>
