# ยง 55.1-1315 Eviction of tenant.

<p>A landlord may evict a tenant only for:</p><p>1. Nonpayment of rent;</p><p>2. Violation of the applicable building and housing code caused by a lack of reasonable care by the tenant, a member of the tenant's household, or a guest or invitee of the tenant;</p><p>3. Violation of a federal, state, or local law or ordinance that is detrimental to the health, safety, and welfare of other tenants in the manufactured home park;</p><p>4. Violation of any rule or provisions of the rental agreement materially affecting the health, safety, and welfare of the tenant or others; or</p><p>5. Two or more violations of any rule or provision of the rental agreement occurring within a six-month period.</p><p>1992, c. 709, ยง 55-248.50:1; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
