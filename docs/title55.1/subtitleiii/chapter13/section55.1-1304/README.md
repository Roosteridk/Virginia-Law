# ยง 55.1-1304 Tenant's obligations.

<p>In addition to the provisions of the rental agreement, the tenant shall:</p><p>1. Comply with applicable laws affecting manufactured home owners and tenants;</p><p>2. Keep and maintain the exterior of the tenant's manufactured home and manufactured home lot as clean and safe as conditions permit;</p><p>3. Place all garbage and other waste in the appropriate receptacles, which shall be provided by the tenant when door-to-door garbage and waste pickup is provided;</p><p>4. Use in a reasonable and orderly manner all facilities and appliances in the manufactured home park and require any guest or invitee to do so;</p><p>5. Conduct himself and require any guest or invitee to conduct himself in a manner that will not disturb the tenant's neighbors' peaceful enjoyment of the premises;</p><p>6. Abide by all reasonable rules and regulations imposed by the landlord; and</p><p>7. In the absence of express written agreement to the contrary, occupy the tenant's manufactured home only as a dwelling unit.</p><p>1975, c. 535, ยง 55-248.44; 1992, c. 709; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
