# ยง 55.1-1995 Exceptions to disclosure requirements.

<p>A. The resale certificate required by this article need not be provided in the case of:</p><p>1. A disposition of a unit by gift;</p><p>2. A disposition of a unit pursuant to court order if the court so directs;</p><p>3. A disposition of a unit by foreclosure or deed in lieu of foreclosure; or</p><p>4. A disposition of a unit by a sale at auction when the resale certificate was made available as part of the auction package for prospective purchasers prior to the auction.</p><p>B. In any transaction in which a resale certificate is required and a trustee acts as the seller in the sale or resale of a unit, the trustee shall obtain the resale certificate from the unit owners' association and provide the resale certificate to the purchaser.</p><p>1974, c. 416, ยง 55-79.97;
