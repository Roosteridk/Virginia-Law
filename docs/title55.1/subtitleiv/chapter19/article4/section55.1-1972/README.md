# § 55.1-1972 Exemptions from certain provisions of article.

<p>A. Unless the method of offer or disposition is adopted for the purpose of evasion of this chapter, the provisions of §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-1974/'>55.1-1974</a> through <a href='/vacode/55.1-1979/'>55.1-1979</a>, subsections B and D of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-1982/'>55.1-1982</a>, and §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-1990/'>55.1-1990</a> and <a href='/vacode/55.1-1991/'>55.1-1991</a> do not apply to:</p><p>1. Dispositions pursuant to court order;</p><p>2. Dispositions by any government or government agency;</p><p>3. Offers by the declarant on nonbinding reservation agreements;</p><p>4. Dispositions in a residential condominium in which there are three or fewer units, so long as the condominium instruments do not reserve to the declarant the right to create additional condominium units; or</p><p>5. A disposition of a unit by a sale at an auction where a current public offering statement or resale certificate was made available as part of an auction package for prospective purchasers prior to the auction sale.</p><p>B. In cases of dispositions in a condominium where all units are restricted to nonresidential use, the provisions of §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-1974/'>55.1-1974</a> through <a href='/vacode/55.1-1983/'>55.1-1983</a> shall not apply, unless the method of offer or disposition is adopted for the purpose of evasion of this chapter.</p><p>1974, c. 416, § 55-79.87; 1975, c. 415; 1984, c. 427; 1993, c. 667; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0266'>266</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0325'>325</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0277'>277</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
