# § 55.1-1904 Association charges.

<p>Except as expressly authorized in this chapter, in the condominium instruments, or as otherwise provided by law, no unit owners' association may make an assessment or impose a charge against a unit owner unless the charge is (i) authorized under § <a href='/vacode/55.1-1964/'>55.1-1964</a>, (ii) a fee for services provided, or (iii) related to the provisions set out in § <a href='/vacode/55.1-1992/'>55.1-1992</a>. The Common Interest Community Board may assess a monetary penalty for a violation of this section against any (a) unit owners' association pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/54.1-2351/'>54.1-2351</a> or (b) common interest community manager pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/54.1-2349/'>54.1-2349</a> and may issue a cease and desist order pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/54.1-2352/'>54.1-2352</a>.</p><p>2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0277'>277</a>, § 55-79.42:1; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0592'>592</a>.</p>
