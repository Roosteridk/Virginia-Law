# § 55.1-2252 Penalties.

<p>A. Any person who willfully violates any of the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-2217/'>55.1-2217</a>, <a href='/vacode/55.1-2218/'>55.1-2218</a>, <a href='/vacode/55.1-2219/'>55.1-2219</a>, <a href='/vacode/55.1-2220/'>55.1-2220</a>, <a href='/vacode/55.1-2221/'>55.1-2221</a>, <a href='/vacode/55.1-2229/'>55.1-2229</a>, <a href='/vacode/55.1-2233/'>55.1-2233</a>, or <a href='/vacode/55.1-2238/'>55.1-2238</a>, or any order issued pursuant to §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-2247/'>55.1-2247</a> through <a href='/vacode/55.1-2250/'>55.1-2250</a> is guilty of a Class 5 felony.</p><p>Any person who willfully violates any of the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-2226/'>55.1-2226</a>, <a href='/vacode/55.1-2228/'>55.1-2228</a>, or <a href='/vacode/55.1-2244/'>55.1-2244</a> or any order issued pursuant to §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-2247/'>55.1-2247</a> through <a href='/vacode/55.1-2250/'>55.1-2250</a> regarding a violation of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-2226/'>55.1-2226</a>, <a href='/vacode/55.1-2228/'>55.1-2228</a>, or <a href='/vacode/55.1-2244/'>55.1-2244</a> is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.</p><p>Each violation shall be deemed a separate offense.</p><p>B. Any developer, member, agent or affiliate of any developer of time-shares registered pursuant to § <a href='/vacode/55.1-2241/'>55.1-2241</a>, or any reseller, who violates any provision of this chapter or regulations promulgated pursuant to this chapter, and who is not criminally prosecuted, may be subject to a civil penalty. If it has been determined by the Board upon or after a hearing that a respondent has violated this chapter or the Board's rules and regulations, the Board shall proceed to determine the amount of the civil penalty for such violation, which shall not exceed $2,000 for each violation. Such penalty may be sued for and recovered in the name of the Commonwealth.</p><p>1981, c. 462, § 55-400; 1983, c. 59; 1985, c. 517; 1991, c. 704; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0751'>751</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
