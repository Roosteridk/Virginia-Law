# ยง 55.1-2213 Time-share estate owners' association annual report.

<p>A. Commencing with the time-share estate program and within 180 days after the close of each fiscal year thereafter, an annual report shall be prepared and distributed to all time-share estate owners. Such annual report shall be prepared and distributed for each time-share estate project registered with the Board. During the developer control period, the annual report shall be prepared and distributed to all time-share purchasers by the developer or its designated managing entity. After the developer control period, such annual report shall be prepared and distributed by the association.</p><p>B. The annual report shall contain the following:</p><p>1. The full legal name of the time-share project and its address;</p><p>2. The full legal name of the association;</p><p>3. A list of the names and mailing addresses of the members of the association's board of directors and the name of the person who prepared the report;</p><p>4. The managing entity's name, address, and contact person, if any, for the project;</p><p>5. A statement of whether or not the developer control period has terminated for the time-share estate project;</p><p>6. Financial statements of the association audited by an independent certified public accounting firm of the association that contain at least the following:</p><p>a. A balance sheet as of the end of the fiscal year;</p><p>b. An income statement as of the end of the fiscal year; and</p><p>c. A statement of the net changes in the financial position of the association for the fiscal year just ended;</p><p>7. A statement of the time-share estates occupancy expenses, the regular assessment, and any special assessments or other charges due for the current year from each time-share estate owner;</p><p>8. A copy of the current budget reflecting the anticipated time-share estate occupancy expenses along with:</p><p>a. A statement as to who prepared the budget;</p><p>b. A statement of the budgetary assumptions concerning occupancy factors;</p><p>c. A description of any provision made in the budget for reserves for repairs and replacement;</p><p>d. A statement of any other reserves;</p><p>e. The projected financial liability for each time-share estate owner, including a statement of (i) the nature of all charges, assessments, maintenance fees, and other expenses that may be assessed; (ii) the current amounts assessed; and (iii) the method and formula for changing any such assessments; and</p><p>f. A statement of any services not reflected in the budget that the developer provides, or expenses that it pays, that the association expects may become a time-share expense at any subsequent time, and the projected time-share expense assessment attributable to each of those services or expenses for the association and for each time-share; and</p><p>9. A statement of the location of the books and records of the association along with the name and contact address of the custodian of such books and records.</p><p>C. In lieu of the annual report required by subsection A, during the first 12 months of the time-share program, the developer or the association shall prepare a budget that shall contain the information contained in subdivision B 8.</p><p>1985, c. 517, ยง 55-370.1; 1991, c. 704; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0580'>580</a>; 1998, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0460'>460</a>; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0533'>533</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
