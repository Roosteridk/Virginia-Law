# ยง 55.1-2153 Applicability; waiver.

<p>A. This article applies to all cooperative interests subject to this chapter, except as provided in subsection B or as modified or waived by agreement of purchasers of cooperative interests in a cooperative in which all units are restricted to nonresidential use.</p><p>B. Neither a public offering statement nor a resale certificate need be prepared or delivered in the case of:</p><p>1. A gratuitous disposition of a cooperative interest;</p><p>2. A disposition pursuant to court order;</p><p>3. A disposition by a government or governmental agency;</p><p>4. A disposition by foreclosure or transfer in lieu of foreclosure;</p><p>5. A disposition to a person in the business of selling cooperative interests who intends to offer those cooperative interests to purchasers; or</p><p>6. A disposition that may be canceled at any time and for any reason by the purchaser without penalty.</p><p>1982, c. 277, ยง 55-476; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
