# § 55.1-1807 Statement of lot owner rights.

<p>Every lot owner who is a member in good standing of a property owners' association shall have the following rights:</p><p>1. The right of access to all books and records kept by or on behalf of the association according to and subject to the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-1815/'>55.1-1815</a>, including records of all financial transactions;</p><p>2. The right to cast a vote on any matter requiring a vote by the association's membership in proportion to the lot owner's ownership interest, unless the declaration provides otherwise;</p><p>3. The right to have notice of any meeting of the board of directors, to make a record of any such meeting by audio or visual means, and to participate in any such meeting in accordance with the provisions of subsection G of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-1815/'>55.1-1815</a> and § <a href='/vacode/55.1-1816/'>55.1-1816</a>;</p><p>4. The right to have (i) notice of any proceeding conducted by the board of directors or other tribunal specified in the declaration against the lot owner to enforce any rule or regulation of the association and (ii) the opportunity to be heard and represented by counsel at such proceeding, as provided in § <a href='/vacode/55.1-1819/'>55.1-1819</a>, and the right of due process in the conduct of that hearing; and</p><p>5. The right to serve on the board of directors if duly elected and a member in good standing of the association, unless the declaration provides otherwise.</p><p>The rights enumerated in this section shall be enforceable by any such lot owner pursuant to the provisions of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-1828/'>55.1-1828</a>.</p><p>2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0286'>286</a>, § 55-509.3:2; 2018, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0663'>663</a>; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
