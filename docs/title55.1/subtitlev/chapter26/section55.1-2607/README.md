# § 55.1-2607 Status of property loaned to or deposited with museum prior to July 1, 2002.

<p>Except as otherwise provided in a written agreement between a lender and a museum, property loaned to or deposited with a museum prior to July 1, 2002, may be discarded or transferred to another museum located in Virginia, provided that (i) the notice provisions of §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-2604/'>55.1-2604</a> and <a href='/vacode/55.1-2605/'>55.1-2605</a> have been complied with and (ii) such property is held by the museum receiving the transfer for at least three years before it sells or disposes of such property.</p><p>2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0480'>480</a>, § 55-210.38; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
