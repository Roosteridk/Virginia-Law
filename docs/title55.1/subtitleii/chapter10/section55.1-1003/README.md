# § 55.1-1003 Persons who may act as a settlement agent.

<p>A. A person shall not act in the capacity of a settlement agent, and a lender, seller, purchaser or borrower may not contract with any person to act in the capacity of a settlement agent, with respect to real estate settlements in the Commonwealth unless the person has not been convicted of a felony, unless such person has had his civil rights restored by the Governor or been granted a writ of actual innocence, and is either:</p><p>1. Licensed as an attorney under Chapter 39 (§ <a href='/vacode/54.1-3900/'>54.1-3900</a> et seq.) of Title 54.1;</p><p>2. Licensed as a title insurance company under Title 38.2;</p><p>3. Licensed as a title insurance agent under Title 38.2 and is appointed by a title insurance company licensed in the Commonwealth pursuant to Chapter 18 (§ <a href='/vacode/38.2-1800/'>38.2-1800</a> et seq.) of Title 38.2;</p><p>4. Licensed as a real estate broker under Chapter 21 (§ <a href='/vacode/54.1-2100/'>54.1-2100</a> et seq.) of Title 54.1;</p><p>5. A financial institution authorized to do business in the Commonwealth under any of the provisions of Title 6.2 or under federal law; or</p><p>6. A subsidiary or affiliate of a financial institution described in subdivision 5.</p><p>Any person described in subdivisions 1 through 6 not acting in the capacity of a settlement agent shall not be subject to the provisions of this chapter.</p><p>B. Notwithstanding any rule of court to the contrary, a settlement agent operating in compliance with the requirements of this chapter or a party to the real estate transaction may provide escrow, closing, or settlement services and receive compensation for such services.</p><p>1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0716'>716</a>, § 6.1-2.21; 1998, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?981+ful+CHAP0069'>69</a>; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0549'>549</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0464'>464</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0898'>898</a>; 2008, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?081+ful+CHAP0092'>92</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0794'>794</a>, § 55-525.19; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
