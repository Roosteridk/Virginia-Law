# § 55.1-125 When nonvested property interest or power of appointment created.

<p>A. Except as provided in subsections B and C and in § <a href='/vacode/55.1-128/'>55.1-128</a>, the time of creation of a nonvested property interest or a power of appointment is determined under general principles of property law.</p><p>B. For the purposes of §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-124/'>55.1-124</a> through <a href='/vacode/55.1-129/'>55.1-129</a>, if there is a person who alone can exercise a power created by a governing instrument to become the unqualified beneficial owner of (i) a nonvested property interest or (ii) a property interest subject to a power of appointment described in subsection B or C in § <a href='/vacode/55.1-124/'>55.1-124</a>, the nonvested property interest or power of appointment is created when the power to become the unqualified beneficial owner terminates.</p><p>C. For the purposes of §§ <a href='/vacode/55.1-124/'>55.1-124</a> through <a href='/vacode/55.1-129/'>55.1-129</a>, a nonvested property interest or a power of appointment arising from a transfer of property to a previously funded trust or other existing property arrangement is created when the nonvested property interest or power of appointment in the original contribution was created.</p><p>2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0714'>714</a>, § 55-12.2; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
