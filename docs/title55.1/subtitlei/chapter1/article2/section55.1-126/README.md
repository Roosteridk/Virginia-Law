# § 55.1-126 Reformation.

<p>Upon the petition of an interested person, a circuit court in the county or city in which the affected property or the greater part of such property is located shall reform a disposition in the manner that most closely approximates the transferor's manifested plan of distribution and is within the 90 years allowed by subdivision A 2, B 2, or C 2 of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-124/'>55.1-124</a> if:</p><p>1. A nonvested property interest or a power of appointment becomes invalid under § <a href='/vacode/55.1-124/'>55.1-124</a>;</p><p>2. A class gift is not but might become invalid under § <a href='/vacode/55.1-124/'>55.1-124</a> and the time has arrived when the share of any class member is to take effect in possession or enjoyment; or</p><p>3. A nonvested property interest that is not validated by subdivision A 1 of § <a href='/vacode/55.1-124/'>55.1-124</a> can vest but not within 90 years after its creation.</p><p>2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0714'>714</a>, § 55-12.3; 2019, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?191+ful+CHAP0712'>712</a>.</p>
