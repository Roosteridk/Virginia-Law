# § 52-53 Definitions.

<p>As used in this chapter, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Coordinator" means the position of the Gaming Enforcement Coordinator established pursuant to § <a href='https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/52-54/'>52-54</a>.</p><p>"Department" means the Department of State Police.</p><p>"Gaming laws" means the laws regulating gambling under Article 1 (§ <a href='/vacode/18.2-325/'>18.2-325</a> et seq.) of Chapter 8 of Title 18.2, charitable gaming under Article 1.1:1 (§ <a href='/vacode/18.2-340.15/'>18.2-340.15</a> et seq.) of Chapter 8 of Title 18.2, lottery games under Article 1 (§ <a href='/vacode/58.1-4000/'>58.1-4000</a> et seq.) of Chapter 40 of Title 58.1, sports betting under Article 2 (§ <a href='/vacode/58.1-4030/'>58.1-4030</a> et seq.) of Chapter 40 of Title 58.1, casino gaming under Chapter 41 (§ <a href='/vacode/58.1-4100/'>58.1-4100</a> et seq.) of Title 58.1, fantasy contests under Chapter 51 (§ <a href='/vacode/59.1-556/'>59.1-556</a> et seq.) of Title 59.1, horse racing and pari-mutuel wagering under Chapter 29 (§ <a href='/vacode/59.1-364/'>59.1-364</a> et seq.) of Title 59.1, any regulations promulgated pursuant to such laws, and any other federal, state, or local laws the Gaming Enforcement Coordinator deems relevant.</p><p>"Superintendent" means the Superintendent of State Police.</p><p>2022, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0721'>721</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?221+ful+CHAP0768'>768</a>.</p>
