# ยง 52-6 Appointment of additional police officers.

<p>The Superintendent of State Police, with the approval of the Governor, shall have authority to appoint additional police officers, who may or may not be residents of this Commonwealth, to serve for specified periods of time. The police officers so appointed shall receive such compensation as the Superintendent shall approve. Such police officers shall have the same powers and perform the same duties as the regular police officers appointed by the Superintendent.</p><p>1932, p. 618; Michie Code 1942, ยง 2154(54); R.
