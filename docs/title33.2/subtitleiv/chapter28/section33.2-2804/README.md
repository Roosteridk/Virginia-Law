<p>The Authority shall be governed by a board of directors, for purposes of this chapter referred to as the "Authority Board," which shall consist of the following:</p><p>1. Two directors representing the County of Albemarle, each of whom shall be a member of the governing body of the county;</p><p>2. Two directors representing the City of Charlottesville, each of whom shall be a member of the governing body of the city;</p><p>3. One director representing each county that joins the Authority pursuant to ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-2803/'>33.2-2803</a>, each of whom shall be a member of the governing body of each respective county; and</p><p>4. Up to four additional directors, who shall be nonvoting, representing the interests of such agencies, institutions, and facilities described in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-2803/'>33.2-2803</a> that join the Authority.</p><p>All members of the Authority Board shall serve terms coincident with their terms of office. Vacancies shall be filled in the same manner as the original appointments.</p><p>The Authority Board shall appoint a chair and vice-chair from among its members.</p><p>2009, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0645'>645</a>, ยง 15.2-7027; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805'>805</a>.</p>
