# § 33.2-228 Agreements between Commissioner of Highways and certain localities.

<p>Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-209/'>33.2-209</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-214/'>33.2-214</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-221/'>33.2-221</a>, the Commissioner of Highways, pursuant to a resolution adopted by the Board and following receipt of a resolution adopted by the governing body of a locality, may enter into an agreement with any such locality pursuant to which the locality assumes responsibility for the design, right-of-way acquisition, and construction of highways or portions thereof in such locality, using funds allocated pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-371/'>33.2-371</a>.</p><p>2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0623'>623</a>, § 33.1-12.1; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805'>805</a>; 2015, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?151+ful+CHAP0684'>684</a>.</p>
