# § 33.2-502 Designation of HOT lanes.

<p>The Board may designate one or more lanes of any highway, including lanes that may previously have been designated HOV lanes under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-501/'>33.2-501</a>, in the Interstate System, primary state highway system, or National Highway System, or any portion thereof, as HOT lanes. In making HOT lanes designations, the Board shall also specify the high-occupancy requirement and conditions for use of such HOT lanes or may authorize the Commissioner of Highways to make such determination consistent with the terms of a comprehensive agreement executed pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-1808/'>33.2-1808</a>. The high-occupancy requirement for a HOT lanes facility constructed or operated as a result of the Public-Private Transportation Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/33.2-1800/'>33.2-1800</a> et seq.) shall not be less than three.</p><p>2004, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?041+ful+CHAP0783'>783</a>, § 33.1-56.2; 2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0805'>805</a>.</p>
