# ยง 28.2-1313 When Commission to modify, remand, or reverse decision of wetlands board.

<p>The Commission shall modify, remand, or reverse the decision of the wetlands board if:</p><p>1. The wetlands board, in reaching its decision, failed to fulfill its responsibilities under the wetlands zoning ordinance; or</p><p>2. The substantial rights of the appellant or the applicant have been prejudiced because the findings, conclusions, or decisions of the board are:</p><p>a. In violation of constitutional provisions;</p><p>b. In excess of statutory authority or jurisdiction of the wetlands board;</p><p>c. Made upon unlawful procedure;</p><p>d. Affected by other error of law;</p><p>e. Unsupported by the evidence on the record considered as a whole; or</p><p>f. Arbitrary, capricious, or an abuse of discretion.</p><p>1972, c. 711, ยง 62.1-13.13; 1975, c. 467; 1992, c. 836.</p>
