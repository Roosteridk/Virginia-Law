# ยง 28.2-810 Removal, transportation, etc., from polluted ground; penalty.

<p>A. It is unlawful for any person to take, catch, transport, sell, offer for sale, remove, receive, keep or store shellfish from condemned areas, or relay shellfish taken from such areas, until the Commissioner of Marine Resources or his designee has issued a special permit. The permittee shall carry the permit when engaged in such operation.</p><p>B. It is unlawful for any person to take or remove shellfish from private grounds in condemned areas without written authority in his possession from the owner or lessee, in addition to the permit required by subsection A of this section.</p><p>C. It is unlawful for any person to transport, relay, or move shellfish from condemned areas after sunset or before sunrise, except by motor vehicle properly sealed as required by ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-812/'>28.2-812</a>. It is unlawful to mix clean shellfish and shellfish from condemned areas in the same cargo.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 28-162.1; 1960, c. 517; 1962, c. 406, ยง 28.1-179; 1966, c. 684; 1968, c. 745; 1979, c. 274; 1981, c. 52; 1986, c. 184; 1988, c. 600; 1992, c. 836.</p>
