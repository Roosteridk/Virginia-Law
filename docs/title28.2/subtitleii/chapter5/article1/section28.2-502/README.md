# § 28.2-502 License fees for taking oysters or clams in tidal waters.

<p>Every resident who applies for a license to take or catch oysters and clams shall pay as follows or as subsequently revised by the Commission pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-201/'>28.2-201</a>:</p><p>1. For each person taking or catching oysters by hand or with ordinary tongs, $10;</p><p>2. For each single-rigged patent tong boat taking oysters, $35;</p><p>3. For each double-rigged patent tong boat taking oysters, $70;</p><p>4. For each person taking or catching clams by hand or with ordinary tongs, $15;</p><p>5. For each single-rigged patent tong boat taking clams, $35;</p><p>6. For each double-rigged patent tong boat taking clams, $70; and</p><p>7. For each dredge used for taking or catching clams, $50.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 28-137, 28-157; 1960, c. 517; 1962, c. 406, §§ 28.1-120, 28.1-133; 1964, c. 393; 1966, c. 684; 1970, c. 726; 1979, c. 274; 1991, c. 285; 1992, c. 836; 1993, c. 11; 2009, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0009'>9</a>.</p>
