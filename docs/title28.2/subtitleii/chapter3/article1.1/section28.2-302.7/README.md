# § 28.2-302.7 Recreational boat; reporting of catch.

<p>A. The owner or operator of a recreational boat used for saltwater recreational fishing shall have the option of purchasing a saltwater recreational fishing license which covers his passengers and himself to meet the licensing requirements set forth in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-302.1/'>28.2-302.1</a>. The cost of the license shall be $30 or as subsequently revised by the Commission pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-201/'>28.2-201</a> a year for boats; however, notwithstanding subdivision 4 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-201/'>28.2-201</a>, the Commission may institute a one-time fee increase of no more than $10 to purchase the license issued pursuant to this section. If the owner or operator of the boat does not purchase a license which covers all his passengers, individuals shall still purchase a license as required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-302.1/'>28.2-302.1</a>. Purchasers of licenses pursuant to this section will be issued an individual license, at no additional cost, as required in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-302.1/'>28.2-302.1</a>.</p><p>B. Holders of licenses issued pursuant to subsection A shall report catch and other data as are deemed necessary by the Commission for effective fisheries management.</p><p>C. All funds collected pursuant to this section shall be paid into the state treasury to the credit of the Virginia Saltwater Recreational Fishing Development Fund, as established by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/28.2-302.3/'>28.2-302.3</a>.</p><p>1992, c. 895; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0559'>559</a>; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0106'>106</a>; 2009, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0009'>9</a>; 2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0484'>484</a>.</p>
