# § 8.2A-104 Leases subject to other law.

<p>(1) A lease, although subject to this title, is also subject to any applicable:</p><p>(a) Certificate of title statute of this Commonwealth such as Article 2 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/46.2-616/'>46.2-616</a> et seq.) of Chapter 6 of Title 46.2 and Article 2.1 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/29.1-733.2/'>29.1-733.2</a> et seq.) of Chapter 7 of Title 29.1;</p><p>(b) Certificate of title statute of another jurisdiction (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-105/'>8.2A-105</a>); or</p><p>(c) Consumer protection statute of this Commonwealth, such as Chapter 17 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/59.1-196/'>59.1-196</a> et seq.) of Title 59.1, or final consumer protection decision of a court of this Commonwealth existing on the effective date of this title.</p><p>(2) In case of conflict between this title, other than §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-105/'>8.2A-105</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-304/'>8.2A-304</a> (3) and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.2A-305/'>8.2A-305</a> (3), and a statute or decision referred to in subsection (1) of this section, the statute or decision controls.</p><p>(3) Failure to comply with an applicable law has only the effect specified therein.</p><p>1991, c. 536; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0787'>787</a>.</p>
