# ยง 8.2A-407 Irrevocable promises: finance leases.

<p>(1) In the case of a finance lease that is not a consumer lease the lessee's promises under the lease contract become irrevocable and independent upon the lessee's acceptance of the goods.</p><p>(2) A promise that has become irrevocable and independent under subsection (1) of this section is:</p><p>(a) Effective and enforceable between the parties, and by or against third parties including assignees of the parties; and</p><p>(b) Not subject to cancellation, termination, modification, repudiation, excuse, or substitution without the consent of the party to whom the promise runs.</p><p>(3) This section does not affect the validity under any other law or a covenant in any lease contract making the lessee's promises acceptance of the goods.</p><p>1991, c. 536.</p>
