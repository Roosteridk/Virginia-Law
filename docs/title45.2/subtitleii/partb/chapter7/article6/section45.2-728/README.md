# § 45.2-728  Number of miners in openings.

<p>Until the two travel ways are made as required by § <a href='/vacode/45.2-726/'>45.2-726</a>, no more than 20 miners shall work underground in the mine at one time. No additional development shall be permitted until the connection is made to the second opening. In a mine in which final pillar removal operations necessitate closing the second opening, no more than 20 miners shall be permitted to work in the mine.</p><p>Code 1950, § 45-71; 1954, c. 191; 1966, c. 594, § 45.1-94; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0028'>28</a>, § 45.1-161.164; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0774'>774</a>; 2021, Sp.
