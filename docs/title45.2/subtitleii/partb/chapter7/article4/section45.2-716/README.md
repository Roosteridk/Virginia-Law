# § 45.2-716  Mapping of roof falls.

<p>Any unplanned roof fall that is required to be reported in accordance with § <a href='/vacode/45.2-556/'>45.2-556</a> shall be marked on a map maintained at the mine to indicate the specific location of the fall.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 45-61, 45-68.1; 1954, c. 191; 1966, c. 594, § 45.1-42; 1976, c. 598; 1978, c. 118; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0028'>28</a>, § 45.1-161.117; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0256'>256</a>; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0003'>3</a>; 2021, Sp.
