# ยง 45.2-756  Riding on cars.

<p>A. No person other than the motorman and the trip rider shall ride on a locomotive unless authorized by the mine foreman.</p><p>B. No person shall ride on a loaded car or between cars of any trip.</p><p>C. No person shall get on or off a moving locomotive or a car that is being moved by a locomotive.</p><p>D.
