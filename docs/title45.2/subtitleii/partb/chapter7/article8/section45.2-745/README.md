# § 45.2-745  Track switches and rails.

<p>A. Every track switch shall be provided with a properly installed throw and properly installed latches and bridle bars.</p><p>B. Every track switch, other than a switch in a room or entry development, shall be equipped with properly installed guardrails.</p><p>C. Every switch throw and stand shall be installed on the side of the track where clearance is provided.</p><p>D. Every rail shall be secured at all joints by plates or welds.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 45-69.1, 45-69.4; 1954, c. 191; 1966, c. 594, §§ 45.1-70, 45.1-73; 1979, c. 315; 1983, c. 70; 1985, c. 302; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0028'>28</a>, § 45.1-161.134; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0256'>256</a>; 2021, Sp.
