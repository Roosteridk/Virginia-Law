# ยง 45.2-840  Crosscuts.

<p>A. Crosscuts shall be made between entries and between rooms as provided in the approved roof control plan.</p><p>B. Every crosscut between an intake and a return air course shall be closed, except the one nearest the face. A crosscut between rooms shall be closed where necessary to provide adequate ventilation at the working face.</p><p>C. Where practicable, a crosscut shall be provided at or near the face of each entry or room before the place is abandoned.</p><p>D.
