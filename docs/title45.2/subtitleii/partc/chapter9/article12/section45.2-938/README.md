# ยง 45.2-938  Safety precautions.

<p>A. No person shall enter an auger hole or highwall miner entry without prior approval from the Chief.</p><p>B. Every auger hole or highwall miner entry shall be blocked with highwall spoil or other suitable material before it is abandoned.</p><p>C. Every auger or highwall mining machine that is exposed to any highwall or explosion hazard shall be provided with worker protection from falling material and a mine explosion.</p><p>D. At least one person shall be assigned to observe the highwall for possible movement while ground personnel are working in a high-risk area in close proximity to the highwall.</p><p>E. All persons shall stay clear of any moving auger or highwall miner train, and no person shall pass over or under a moving train unless adequate crossing facilities are provided.</p><p>F. The ground control plan shall specify any spacing of holes, web design, and use of alignment control devices.</p><p>G. The ground control plan shall include other administrative, engineering, and source controls that are to be provided for safe operations.</p><p>1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0028'>28</a>, ยง 45.1-161.291; 2011, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0826'>826</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0862'>862</a>; 2021, Sp.
