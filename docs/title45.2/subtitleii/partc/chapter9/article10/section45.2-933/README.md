# § 45.2-933  Minimum blasting practices.

<p>A. If explosives are in use on the surface and an electrical storm approaches, all persons shall be removed from the blast area until the storm has passed.</p><p>B. In accordance with the standards set forth in § <a href='/vacode/45.2-902/'>45.2-902</a>, the Chief shall adopt regulations regarding the safe storage, transportation, handling, and use of blasting agents and other explosives.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 45-53.1, 45-53.5; 1954, c. 191; 1966, c. 594, §§ 45.1-48, 45.1-53.1; 1978, c. 729; 1981, c. 179; 1984, c. 229; 1993, c. 442; 1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0028'>28</a>, § 45.1-161.286; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0774'>774</a>; 2021, Sp.
