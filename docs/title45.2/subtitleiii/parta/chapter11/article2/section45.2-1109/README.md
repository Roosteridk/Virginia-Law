# § 45.2-1109  Appointment and general qualifications of mine inspectors.

<p>A. Each mine inspector shall be appointed by the Director.</p><p>B. Each mine inspector shall (i) be at least 25 years of age; (ii) be of good moral character and temperate habits; (iii) hold a certificate as a mine foreman; and (iv) hold a certificate as a mine inspector issued prior to July 1, 2012, by the Board of Mineral Mining Examiners or on or after July 1, 2012, by the Department.</p><p>1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0390'>390</a>, §§ 45.1-161.292:10, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0045'>45</a>.1-161.292:11; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0803'>803</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0835'>835</a>; 2021, Sp.
