# ยง 45.2-1602  Construction; purposes.

<p>The provisions of this chapter shall be liberally construed so as to effectuate the following purposes:</p><p>1. To foster, encourage, and promote the safe and efficient exploration for and development, production, utilization, and conservation of the Commonwealth's gas and oil resources;</p><p>2. To provide a method of gas and oil conservation for maximizing exploration, development, production, and utilization of gas and oil resources;</p><p>3. To recognize and protect the rights of any person owning an interest in gas or oil resources contained within a pool;</p><p>4. To ensure the safe recovery of coal and other minerals;</p><p>5. To maximize the production and recovery of coal without substantially affecting the right of a gas or oil owner proposing to drill a gas or oil well to explore for and produce gas or oil;</p><p>6. To protect the citizens and the environment of the Commonwealth from the public safety and environmental risks associated with the development and production of gas or oil; and</p><p>7. To recognize that the use of the surface for gas or oil development shall be only the use that is reasonably necessary to obtain the gas or oil.</p><p>1982, c. 347, ยง 45.1-290; 1990, c. 92, ยง 45.1-361.3; 2021, Sp.
