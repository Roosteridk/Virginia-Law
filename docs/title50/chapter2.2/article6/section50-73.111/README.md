# § 50-73.111 Effect of partner's dissociation.

<p>A. If a partner's dissociation results in a dissolution and winding up of the partnership business, Article 8 applies; otherwise, Article 7 applies.</p><p>B. Upon a partner's dissociation:</p><p>1. The partner's right to participate in the management and conduct of the partnership business terminates, except as otherwise provided in § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.119/'>50-73.119</a>;</p><p>2. The partner's duty of loyalty under subdivision B 3 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.102/'>50-73.102</a> terminates; and</p><p>3. The partner's duty of loyalty under subdivisions B 1 and B 2 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.102/'>50-73.102</a> and duty of care under subsection C of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.102/'>50-73.102</a> continue only with regard to matters arising and events occurring before the partner's dissociation, unless the partner participates in winding up the partnership's business pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.119/'>50-73.119</a>.</p><p>1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0292'>292</a>.</p>
