# ยง 50-73.131 Statement of merger.

<p>A. After a plan of merger is approved, the surviving partnership or limited partnership shall file with the Commission a statement of merger on behalf of the partnerships that have filed either a statement of partnership authority or a statement of registration as a registered limited liability partnership that is not canceled.</p><p>B. A statement of merger executed by each party to the merger shall contain:</p><p>1. The name of each partnership or limited partnership that is a party to the merger;</p><p>2. The name of the surviving entity into which the other partnerships or limited partnership were merged;</p><p>3. The identification number issued by the Commission to each partnership that has filed a statement of partnership authority that has not been canceled;</p><p>4. The street address of the surviving entity's principal office and of an office in this Commonwealth, if any; and</p><p>5. Whether the surviving entity is a partnership or a limited partnership.</p><p>C. Except as otherwise provided in subsection D, for the purposes of ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.92/'>50-73.92</a>, property of the surviving partnership or limited partnership which before the merger was held in the name of another party to the merger is property held in the name of the surviving entity upon filing a statement of merger.</p><p>D. For the purposes of ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.92/'>50-73.92</a>, real property of the surviving partnership or limited partnership which before the merger was held in the name of another party to the merger is property held in the name of the surviving entity upon recording a certified copy of the statement of merger in the office for recording transfers of that real property.</p><p>E. A filed and, if appropriate, recorded statement of merger, executed and declared to be accurate pursuant to subsection C of ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/50-73.83/'>50-73.83</a>, stating the name of a partnership or limited partnership that is a party to the merger in whose name property was held before the merger and the name of the surviving entity, but not containing all of the other information required by subsection B, operates with respect to the partnerships or limited partnerships named to the extent provided in subsections C and D.</p><p>1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0292'>292</a>; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0058'>58</a>; 2003, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?031+ful+CHAP0567'>567</a>; 2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0631'>631</a>; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0018'>18</a>.</p>
