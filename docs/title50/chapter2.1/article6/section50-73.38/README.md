# ยง 50-73.38 Withdrawal of limited partner.

<p>A limited partner may withdraw from a limited partnership only at the time or upon the happening of events specified in writing in the partnership agreement.</p><p>1985, c. 607; 1987, c. 702; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0188'>188</a>.</p>
