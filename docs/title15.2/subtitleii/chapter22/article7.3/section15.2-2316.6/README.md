# ยง 15.2-2316.6 Definitions.

<p>As used in this article, unless the context requires a different meaning:</p><p>"Energy storage facilities" means the energy storage equipment and technology within an energy storage project that is capable of absorbing energy, storing such energy for a period of time, and redelivering such energy after it has been stored.</p><p>"Energy storage project" means the energy storage facilities within the project site.</p><p>"Host locality" means any locality within the jurisdictional boundaries of which construction of a commercial solar project or an energy storage project is proposed.</p><p>"Solar facilities" means commercial solar photovoltaic (electric energy) generation facilities. "Solar facilities" does not include any solar project that is (i) described in ยง <a href='/vacode/56-594/'>56-594</a>, <a href='/vacode/56-594.01/'>56-594.01</a>, <a href='/vacode/56-594.02/'>56-594.02</a>, or <a href='/vacode/56-594.2/'>56-594.2</a>, or (ii) five megawatts or less.</p><p>"Solar project" means the solar facilities, subject to this chapter, that are within the project site.</p><p>2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0802'>802</a>; 2021, Sp.
