# ยง 15.2-1523 Record of qualification.

<p>When an officer qualifies and gives bond, the judge shall certify the fact and the bond and certificate shall be returned to the clerk of the circuit court, and the certificate shall be entered in the order book of the court on the law side thereof and such bond shall be recorded by the clerk. When the officer qualifies and gives bond before the clerk, the clerk shall enter the fact of such qualification in the order book of the court, on the law side thereof, and record the bond.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 15-476; 1962, c. 623, ยง 15.1-39; 1997, c. 587.</p>
