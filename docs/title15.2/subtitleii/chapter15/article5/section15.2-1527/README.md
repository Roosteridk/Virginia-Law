# § 15.2-1527 Bonds of officers.

<p>Every treasurer or director of finance, sheriff, clerk of a circuit court, commissioner of the revenue, and other persons in the offices of constitutional officers required to give bond shall, at the time he qualifies, give such bond as is required by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/49-12/'>49-12</a>. Bonds for a treasurer or director of finance, sheriff, clerk of the circuit court and commissioner of the revenue shall be provided through the state Department of the Treasury, Division of Risk Management pursuant to subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1840/'>2.2-1840</a>. The penalty of the bond of each officer shall be determined by the court or clerk before whom he qualifies, within the limits prescribed in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1528/'>15.2-1528</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1529/'>15.2-1529</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1530/'>15.2-1530</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 15-478; 1962, c. 623, § 15.1-41; 1964, c. 278; 1971, Ex. Sess., c. 155; 1972, c. 549; 1997, c. 587; 2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0618'>618</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0632'>632</a>.</p>
