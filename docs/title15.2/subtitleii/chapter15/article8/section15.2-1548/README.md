# § 15.2-1548 Finance boards or alternatives thereto to manage the assets of trusts or equivalent arrangements to fund postemployment benefits other than pensions and provide annual reports; exemption from Public Procurement Act.

<p>Except as otherwise provided herein, any finance board or any other person or entity serving as an alternative thereto pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1547/'>15.2-1547</a> shall retain the services of an investment manager, invest its funds in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1549/'>15.2-1549</a>, maintain records of all of its proceedings, make such records available for inspection by the public, invest the assets of the trust or equivalent arrangement in accordance with the provisions of this article, and provide the governing body that created it an annual report on the fund's performance and financial status. In lieu of any finance board or other person or entity serving as an alternative thereto pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1547/'>15.2-1547</a> serving as trustee, the investment manager may serve as trustee of the funds. The selection of services related to the management, purchase, or sale of authorized investments, including but not limited to actuarial services, shall not be subject to the provisions of the Virginia Public Procurement Act (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-4300/'>2.2-4300</a> et seq.).</p><p>2007, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0710'>710</a>.</p>
