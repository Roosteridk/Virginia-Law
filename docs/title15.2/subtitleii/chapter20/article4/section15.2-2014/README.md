# ยง 15.2-2014 Temporary closing of rights-of-way in certain circumstances.

<p>The chief administrative officer of any locality or, if there is none, then the chairman or mayor, may temporarily close any public right-of-way in the locality when in his judgment the public safety so requires. Such temporary closing shall not extend past the time of the next meeting of the governing body.</p><p>1970, c. 529, ยง 15.1-889.1; 1997, c. 587.</p>
