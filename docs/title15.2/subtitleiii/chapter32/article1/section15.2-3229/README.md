# ยง 15.2-3229 Annexation of whole town.

<p>The provisions of this article shall apply to the annexation by a city or town of an adjoining town. No part of a town shall be annexed unless the whole town is annexed. The annexing city or town shall assume all the indebtedness of the town annexed, and shall own all the corporate property, franchises and rights thereof.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 15-152.27; 1952, c. 328; 1962, c. 623, ยง 15.1-1057; 1997, c. 587.</p>
