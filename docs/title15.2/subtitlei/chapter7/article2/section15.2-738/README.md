# § 15.2-738 Modification of grievance procedure.

<p>Notwithstanding the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1202.1/'>2.2-1202.1</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1506/'>15.2-1506</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1507/'>15.2-1507</a> to the contrary, in any county which has the county manager plan of government provided for in this chapter, a grievance procedure may be established which permits an Equal Employment Opportunity officer, except the Director of the Department of Human Resource Management appointed pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-1200/'>2.2-1200</a> and any employees thereof, to be present at any step of a grievance procedure established under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-1506/'>15.2-1506</a>. Such officer shall not be an advocate or representative on behalf of either the grievant or management.</p><p>1989, c. 622, § 15.1-687.16; 1991, c. 143; 1995, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0770'>770</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0818'>818</a>; 1996, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0164'>164</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0869'>869</a>; 1997, c. 587; 2000, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP0947'>947</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1006'>1006</a>; 2012, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0803'>803</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0835'>835</a>.</p>
