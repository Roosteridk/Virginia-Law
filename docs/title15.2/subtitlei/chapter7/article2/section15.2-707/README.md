# ยง 15.2-707 Bonds of county officers and employees.

<p>The county officers shall give such bonds as are now required by general law, except that the bond of the treasurer shall be in such penalty as the court or judge requires, but not less than fifteen percent of the amount to be received annually by him. In addition, the board may fix and require bonds in excess of the amounts so required, and may require bonds of other county officers and employees in the board's discretion, conditioned on the faithful discharge of their duties and the proper accounting for all funds coming into their possession.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 15-352.1; 1962, c. 623, ยง 15.1-678; 1997, c. 587.</p>
