# ยง 15.2-406 Appointment, compensation and removal of county administrator.

<p>A. The board shall appoint a county administrator and fix his compensation. He shall be appointed with due regard to merit only, and need not be a resident of the county at the time of his appointment. No board member shall, during the time for which he is elected, be chosen county administrator.</p><p>B. The county administrator may be removed at the pleasure of the board.</p><p>C. In case of the absence or disability of the county administrator, the board may designate some responsible person to perform the duties of the office.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 15-369; 1962, c. 623, ยง 15.1-704; 1973, c. 163; 1997, c. 587.</p>
