# § 15.2-5139 Lien for charges.

<p>An authority may place a lien upon the real property of an owner only in the same manner provided by § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2119/'>15.2-2119</a>, and such lien may only be processed, recorded, and released in accordance therewith. An authority may only provide services to lessees or tenants of property owners in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-2119.4/'>15.2-2119.4</a>.</p><p>An authority may contract with a locality to collect amounts due on properly recorded utility liens in the same manner as unpaid real estate taxes due the locality.</p><p>Code 1950, § 15-764.25; 1950, p. 1327; 1958, c. 97; 1962, c. 623, § 15.1-1263; 1976, c. 243; 1983, c. 422; 1987, c. 307; 1993, c. 383; 1994, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0599'>599</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0602'>602</a>; 1997, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0587'>587</a>; 2009, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0420'>420</a>; 2011, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0529'>529</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?111+ful+CHAP0580'>580</a>; 2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0766'>766</a>; 2017, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?171+ful+CHAP0736'>736</a>.</p>
