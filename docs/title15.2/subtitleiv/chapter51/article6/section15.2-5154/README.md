# § 15.2-5154 Contents of petition.

<p>A petition for the creation of a community development authority shall:</p><p>1. Set forth the name and describe the boundaries of the proposed district, including any provisions for adjusting the community development authority district boundaries pursuant to subsection A of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-5155/'>15.2-5155</a>;</p><p>2. Describe the services and facilities proposed to be undertaken by the community development authority within the district;</p><p>3. Describe a proposed plan for providing and financing such services and facilities within the district;</p><p>4. Describe the benefits which can be expected from the provision of such services and facilities by the community development authority;</p><p>5. Provide that the board members of the community development authority shall be selected under the applicable provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-5113/'>15.2-5113</a>; and</p><p>6. Request the local governing body to establish the proposed community development authority for the purposes set forth in the petition.</p><p>Such petition may provide that the board members of the community development authority appointed pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/15.2-5113/'>15.2-5113</a> shall consist of a majority of the petitioning landowners or their designees or nominees.</p><p>Code 1950, § 15-764.3; 1950, p. 1315; 1962, c. 623, § 15.1-1241; 1972, c. 370; 1973, c. 478; 1993, c. 850; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0402'>402</a>; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0897'>897</a>; 1997, c. 587; 2009, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?091+ful+CHAP0473'>473</a>.</p>
