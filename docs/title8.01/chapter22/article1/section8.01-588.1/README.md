# ยง 8.01-588.1 Bonds apportioned to funds under control; annual reports.

<p>The general receiver shall obtain bond through the Department of the Treasury's Division of Risk Management. No later than October 1 of each year, he shall report to the Division the amount of moneys under his control pursuant to ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-582/'>8.01-582</a> as of June 30 of the current year and shall report the amount he expects to come under his control for the year ending on June 30 of the following year. The cost of the bond shall be apportioned among the funds under his control as of the billing date based on the amount of each owner's or beneficiary's moneys.
