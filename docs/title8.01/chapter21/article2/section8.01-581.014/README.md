# ยง 8.01-581.014 Court; jurisdiction.

<p>The term "court" means a circuit court or general district court of the Commonwealth having jurisdiction over the subject matter of the controversy.</p><p>1986, c. 614; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0342'>342</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0181'>181</a>.</p>
