# ยง 8.01-465.13:2 Applicability.

<p>A. Except as otherwise provided in subsection B, this chapter applies to a foreign-country judgment to the extent that the judgment:</p><p>1. Grants or denies recovery of a sum of money; and</p><p>2. Under the law of the foreign country where rendered, is final, conclusive, and enforceable.</p><p>B. This chapter does not apply to a foreign-country judgment, even if the judgment grants or denies recovery of a sum of money, to the extent that the judgment is:</p><p>1. A judgment for taxes;</p><p>2. A fine or other penalty; or</p><p>3. A judgment for divorce, support, or maintenance, or other judgment rendered in connection with domestic relations.</p><p>C. A party seeking recognition of a foreign-country judgment has the burden of establishing that this chapter applies to the foreign-country judgment.</p><p>2014, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?141+ful+CHAP0462'>462</a>.</p>
