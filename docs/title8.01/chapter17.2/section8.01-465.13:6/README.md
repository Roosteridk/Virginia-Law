# ยง 8.01-465.13:6 Effect of recognition of foreign-country judgment.

<p>If the court in a proceeding under ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-465.13:5/'>8.01-465.13:5</a> finds that the foreign-country judgment is entitled to recognition under this chapter then, to the extent that the foreign-country judgment grants or denies recovery of a sum of money, the foreign-country judgment is:</p><p>1.
