# § 8.01-506.2 Proceedings in court of county or city where execution debtor resides.

<p>When pursuant to subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-506/'>8.01-506</a>, a summons requires the execution debtor to appear before a court of the county or city in which the execution debtor resides, or of a county or city contiguous thereto, the execution creditor may have the case filed or docketed in that court as follows:</p><p>1. The execution creditor shall file with that court an abstract of the judgment rendered.</p><p>2. The execution creditor shall pay a fee to that court in accordance with the provisions of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:2/'>16.1-69.48:2</a> or subdivision 17 of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275/'>17.1-275</a>.</p><p>3. After docketing or filing the abstract of judgment and payment of any fees, the court shall issue the summons and any subsequent executions on the filed or docketed judgment, including a subpoena duces tecum pursuant to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-506.1/'>8.01-506.1</a>, and shall conduct such hearings and enter such orders pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-507/'>8.01-507</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-507.1/'>8.01-507.1</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-508/'>8.01-508</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-509/'>8.01-509</a>, and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-510/'>8.01-510</a> as may be required.</p><p>4. The execution creditor shall file in both courts any releases or satisfactions of judgment.</p><p>2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0726'>726</a>.</p>
