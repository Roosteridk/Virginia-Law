# ยง 8.01-84 Application of proceeds of sale to payment of lien.

<p>When there are liens on the interest of any party in the subject so sold, the court may, on the petition of any person holding a lien, ascertain the liens, and apply the dividend of such party in the proceeds of sale to the discharge thereof, so far as the same may be necessary.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 8-693; 1977, c. 617.</p>
