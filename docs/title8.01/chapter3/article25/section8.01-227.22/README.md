# ยง 8.01-227.22 Failure to fulfill duty or responsibility not negligence per se.

<p>An operator's or participant's failure to abide by or fulfill a duty or responsibility under this article shall not constitute negligence per se.</p><p>2012, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?121+ful+CHAP0713'>713</a>.</p>
