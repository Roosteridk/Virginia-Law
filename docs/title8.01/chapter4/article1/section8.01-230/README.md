# § 8.01-230 Accrual of right of action.

<p>In every action for which a limitation period is prescribed, the right of action shall be deemed to accrue and the prescribed limitation period shall begin to run from the date the injury is sustained in the case of injury to the person or damage to property, when the breach of contract occurs in actions ex contractu and not when the resulting damage is discovered, except where the relief sought is solely equitable or where otherwise provided under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-233/'>8.01-233</a>, subsection C of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-245/'>8.01-245</a>, §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-249/'>8.01-249</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-250/'>8.01-250</a> or other statute.</p><p>1977, c. 617; 1996, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?961+ful+CHAP0328'>328</a>.</p>
