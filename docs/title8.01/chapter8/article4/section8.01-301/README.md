# § 8.01-301 How process served on foreign corporations generally.

<p>Subject to § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-286.1/'>8.01-286.1</a>, service of process on a foreign corporation may be effected in the following manner:</p><p>1. By personal service on any officer, director or on the registered agent of a foreign corporation which is authorized to do business in the Commonwealth, and by personal service on any agent of a foreign corporation transacting business in the Commonwealth without such authorization, wherever any such officer, director, or agents be found within the Commonwealth;</p><p>2. By substituted service on a foreign corporation in accordance with §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-766/'>13.1-766</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/13.1-928/'>13.1-928</a>, if such corporation is authorized to transact business or affairs within the Commonwealth;</p><p>3. By substituted service on a foreign corporation in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-329/'>8.01-329</a> or by service in accordance with § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-320/'>8.01-320</a>, where jurisdiction is authorized under § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-328.1/'>8.01-328.1</a>, regardless of whether such foreign corporation is authorized to transact business within the Commonwealth; or</p><p>4. By order of publication in accordance with §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-316/'>8.01-316</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/8.01-317/'>8.01-317</a> where jurisdiction in rem or quasi in rem is authorized, regardless of whether the foreign corporation so served is authorized to transact business within the Commonwealth.</p><p>This section does not prescribe the only means, or necessarily the required means, of serving a foreign corporation.</p><p>Code 1950, § 8-60; 1977, c. 617; 1991, c. 672; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0866'>866</a>; 2013, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?131+ful+CHAP0113'>113</a>.</p>
