# § 8.01-299 How process served on domestic corporations and limited liability companies generally.

<p>Except as prescribed in § <a href='/vacode/8.01-300/'>8.01-300</a> as to municipal and quasi-governmental corporations, and subject to § <a href='/vacode/8.01-286.1/'>8.01-286.1</a>, process may be served on a corporation or limited liability company created by the laws of the Commonwealth as follows:</p><p>1. By personal service on any officer, director, or registered agent of such corporation or on the registered agent of such limited liability company;</p><p>2. By substituted service on stock corporations in accordance with § <a href='/vacode/13.1-637/'>13.1-637</a>, on nonstock corporations in accordance with § <a href='/vacode/13.1-836/'>13.1-836</a>, and on limited liability companies in accordance with § <a href='/vacode/13.1-1018/'>13.1-1018</a>; or</p><p>3. If the address of the registered office of the corporation or limited liability company is a single-family residential dwelling, by substituted service on the registered agent of the corporation or limited liability company in the manner provided by subdivision 2 of § <a href='/vacode/8.01-296/'>8.01-296</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 8-59; 1954, c. 23; 1956, c. 432; 1958, c. 13; 1976, c. 395; 1977, c. 617; 1991, c. 672; 2005, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?051+ful+CHAP0866'>866</a>; 2016, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?161+ful+CHAP0270'>270</a>; 2018, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?181+ful+CHAP0475'>475</a>.</p>
