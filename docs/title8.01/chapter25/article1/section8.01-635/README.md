# ยง 8.01-635 Common-law writ of quo warranto and information in the nature of writ of quo warranto abolished; statutory writ of quo warranto established.

<p>The common-law writ of quo warranto and information in the nature of writ of quo warranto is hereby abolished and superseded by the statutory writ of quo warranto.</p><p>1977, c. 617.</p>
