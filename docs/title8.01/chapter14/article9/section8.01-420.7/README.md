# ยง 8.01-420.7 Attorney-client privilege and work product protection; limitations on waiver.

<p>A. When disclosure of a communication or information covered by the attorney-client privilege or work product protection made in a proceeding or to any public body as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3701/'>2.2-3701</a> operates as a waiver of the privilege or protection, the waiver extends to an undisclosed communication or information only if:</p><p>1. The waiver is intentional;</p><p>2. The disclosed and undisclosed communications or information concern the same subject matter; and</p><p>3. The disclosed and undisclosed communications or information ought in fairness be considered together.</p><p>B. Disclosure of a communication or information covered by the attorney-client privilege or work product protection made in a proceeding or to any public body as defined in ยง <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/2.2-3701/'>2.2-3701</a> does not operate as a waiver of the privilege or protection if:</p><p>1. The disclosure is inadvertent;</p><p>2. The holder of the privilege or protection took reasonable steps to prevent disclosure; and</p><p>3. The holder promptly took reasonable steps to rectify the error, including, if applicable, complying with the provisions of subdivision (b) (6) (ii) of Rule 4:1 of the Rules of the Supreme Court.</p><p>C. A court may order that the privilege or protection is not waived by the disclosure connected with the litigation pending before the court, in which case the disclosure does not operate as a waiver in any other proceeding.</p><p>D. An agreement on the effect of the disclosure in a proceeding is binding only on the parties to the agreement, unless it is incorporated into a court order.</p><p>E. This section shall not limit any otherwise applicable waiver of attorney-client privilege or work product protection by an inmate who files an action challenging his conviction or sentence.</p><p>2010, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?101+ful+CHAP0350'>350</a>.</p>
