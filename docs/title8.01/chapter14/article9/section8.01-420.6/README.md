# ยง 8.01-420.6 Number of witnesses whose depositions may be taken.

<p>Notwithstanding any other provision of law or rule of court, there shall be no limit on the number of witnesses whose depositions may be taken by a party except by order of the court for good cause shown.</p><p>2001, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?011+ful+CHAP0595'>595</a>.</p>
