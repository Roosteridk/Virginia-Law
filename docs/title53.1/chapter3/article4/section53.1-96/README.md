# ยง 53.1-96 County and city farms; persons who may be confined.

<p>The governing body of any county or city may, within its respective jurisdiction or elsewhere, establish and maintain a farm where any person convicted and sentenced to confinement in the jail of such county or city, or sentenced to a state correctional institution, may be confined and required to do such work as may be assigned him during the term of his sentence. A local jail farm may be used to hold or confine any person who could be lawfully held or confined in a jail operated by the county or city.</p><p>The governing body or the farm board appointed to supervise and manage the farm may prescribe rules and regulations to govern the operation of the farm.</p><p>Code 1950, ยง 53-195; 1954, c. 193; 1970, c. 648; 1979, c. 700; 1982, c. 636; 2007, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0095'>95</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?071+ful+CHAP0106'>106</a>.</p>
