# ยง 53.1-71.5 Board to promulgate regulations.

<p>The Board shall make, adopt and promulgate regulations governing the following aspects of private management and operation of regional jail facilities:</p><p>1. Minimum standards for the construction, equipment, administration and operation of the facilities; however, the standards shall be at least as stringent as those established for local correctional facilities;</p><p>2. Contingency plans for operation of a contractor-operated facility in the event of a termination of the contract;</p><p>3. Use of deadly and nondeadly force by regional jail facility contractors' security personnel;</p><p>4. Methods of monitoring a contractor-operated facility by an appropriate state or local governmental entity or entities;</p><p>5. Public access to a contractor-operated facility; and</p><p>6. Such other regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this article.</p><p>1994, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0715'>715</a>.</p>
