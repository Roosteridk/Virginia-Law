# ยง 53.1-265 Powers and duties not delegable to contractor.

<p>No contract for correctional services shall authorize, allow, or imply a delegation of authority or responsibility of the Director to a prison contractor for any of the following:</p><p>1. Developing and implementing procedures for calculating inmate release and parole eligibility dates;</p><p>2. Developing and implementing procedures for calculating and awarding sentence credits;</p><p>3. Approving inmates for furlough and work release;</p><p>4. Approving the type of work inmates may perform and the wages or sentence credits which may be given the inmates engaging in such work;</p><p>5. Granting, denying, or revoking sentence credits;</p><p>6. Classifying inmates or placing inmates in less restrictive custody or more restrictive custody;</p><p>7. Transferring an inmate; however, the contractor may make written recommendations regarding the transfer of an inmate or inmates;</p><p>8. Formulating rules of inmate behavior, violations of which may subject inmates to sanctions; however, the contractor may propose such rules to the Director for his review and adoption, rejection, or modification as otherwise provided by law or regulation; and</p><p>9. Disciplining inmates in any manner which requires a discretionary application of rules of inmate behavior or a discretionary imposition of a sanction for violations of such rules.</p><p>1991, c. 705; 1992, c. 654; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0694'>694</a>.</p>
