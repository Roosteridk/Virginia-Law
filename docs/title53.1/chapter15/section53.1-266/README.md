# ยง 53.1-266 Department shall promulgate regulations.

<p>The Director shall make, adopt and promulgate regulations governing the following aspects of private management and operation of prison facilities:</p><p>1. Contingency plans for state operation of a contractor-operated facility in the event of a termination of the contract;</p><p>2. Use of deadly and nondeadly force by prison contractors' security personnel;</p><p>3. Methods of monitoring a contractor-operated facility by the Department;</p><p>4. Public access to a contractor-operated facility; and</p><p>5. Such other regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.</p><p>1991, c. 705; 1992, c. 654; 1995, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?951+ful+CHAP0694'>694</a>; 2020, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?201+ful+CHAP0759'>759</a>.</p>
