# § 53.1-198 Certain persons to choose good conduct system.

<p>Every person who, on or before June 30, 1981, was convicted of a felony and every person convicted of a misdemeanor, and to whom the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-151/'>53.1-151</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-152/'>53.1-152</a> or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-153/'>53.1-153</a> apply, may choose the system of good conduct allowances established in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-199/'>53.1-199</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-202/'>53.1-202</a> to govern the computing of his discharge date and eligibility for parole. A person who chooses the system established in this article may not thereafter be governed by the laws establishing good conduct allowances in effect prior to July 1, 1981.</p><p>Code 1950, § 53-209.1; 1981, c. 392; 1982, c. 636.</p>
