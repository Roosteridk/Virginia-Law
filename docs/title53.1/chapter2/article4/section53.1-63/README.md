<p>A. The Department shall establish, staff and maintain, at any state correctional facility designated by the Director, programs and housing for the rehabilitation, training and confinement of persons committed to the Department under the provisions of Article 2 (ยง <a href='/vacode/19.2-311/'>19.2-311</a> et seq.) of Chapter 18 of Title 19.2.
