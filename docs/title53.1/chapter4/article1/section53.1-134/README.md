# § 53.1-134 Creation of Parole Board; appointment of members.

<p>There shall be a Parole Board which shall consist of up to five members appointed by the Governor and subject to confirmation by the General Assembly, if in session when such appointment is made, and if not in session, then at its next succeeding session. At least one member of the Parole Board shall be a representative of a crime victims' organization or a victim of crime as defined in subsection B of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/19.2-11.01/'>19.2-11.01</a>.</p><p>The members of the Parole Board shall serve at the pleasure of the Governor.</p><p>Code 1950, §§ 53-230, 53-231, 53-232, 53-233, 53-234, 53-235, 53-236; 1966, c. 638; 1970, c. 648; 1973, c. 253; 1974, cc. 44, 45; 1975, c. 316; 1982, c. 636; 1984, c. 584; 1997, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0795'>795</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?971+ful+CHAP0883'>883</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0569'>569</a>.</p>
