# § 53.1-150 Contributions by persons on parole, probation, and work release.

<p>A. Any person who has costs assessed against him pursuant to §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.1/'>17.1-275.1</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.2/'>17.1-275.2</a>, <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.7/'>17.1-275.7</a>, or § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/17.1-275.8/'>17.1-275.8</a>, or subsection B or C of § <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/16.1-69.48:1/'>16.1-69.48:1</a> shall be required to pay, as specified in those sections, a sentencing/supervision fee to be deposited in the general fund of the state treasury.</p><p>All fees assessed pursuant to this section shall be paid to the clerk of the sentencing court.</p><p>B. Except when the fee referenced in subsection A has been previously assessed, any person (i) who is granted parole or (ii) who participates in a work release program pursuant to the provisions of §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-60/'>53.1-60</a> and <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-131/'>53.1-131</a> shall be required to pay a fee of fifty dollars as a condition of parole or work release.</p><p>Code 1950, § 53-19.40; 1981, c. 634; 1982, cc. 492, 636; 1984, c. 668; 1988, c. 824; 1990, cc. 511, 816; 1992, c. 529; 1993, c. 195; 1994, cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0613'>613</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?941+ful+CHAP0638'>638</a>; 1994, 2nd Sp. Sess., cc. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?943+ful+CHAP0001'>1</a>, <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?943+ful+CHAP0002'>2</a>; 1999, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?991+ful+CHAP0009'>9</a>; 2000, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?001+ful+CHAP1040'>1040</a>; 2002, c. <a href='http://lis.virginia.gov/cgi-bin/legp604.exe?021+ful+CHAP0831'>831</a>.</p>
