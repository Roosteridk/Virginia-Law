# § 53.1-173 Preliminary hearings for out-of-state parolees in Virginia.

<p>In any case in which any person released on parole from another state is present in Virginia, if such person is not present in Virginia pursuant to the provisions of Article 4 (§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-166/'>53.1-166</a> et seq.), upon request by the duly constituted judicial or administrative authorities of such other state, the compact administrator of Virginia or his designee shall cause to be conducted a preliminary hearing to determine probable cause for violation of conditions of parole. Such preliminary hearing shall be substantially similar to the hearing provided for in §§ <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-168/'>53.1-168</a> through <a href='http://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacode/53.1-172/'>53.1-172</a>.</p><p>Code 1950, § 53-290.7; 1975, c. 39; 1977, c. 106; 1982, c. 636.</p>
